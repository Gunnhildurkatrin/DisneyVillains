"[[File:Janelle.png|thumb]]\n'''Janelle''' is a cashier at the Mellow*Mart, from the show [[Kick Buttowski: Suburban Daredevil]]. Her first and only speaking appearance is in \"[[Fistful of Ice Cream]],\" where [[Brad Buttowski|Brad]] starts flirting with her.
