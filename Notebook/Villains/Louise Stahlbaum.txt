"{{Infobox_character\n|image = Louise Stahlbaum.png\n|films = ''[[The Nutcracker and the Four Realms]]''\n|actress = Ellie Bamber\n|home = [[London]], [[England]]\n|likes = Dresses, Christmas, balls, things tidy, being the oldest in the family,\n|dislikes = Messes, the loss of Marie, her younger sister and brother being in the attic, family tension, Fritz acting immaturely and mentioning their mother's loss\n|parents = [[Benjamin Stahlbaum]] (father)<br>[[Marie Stahlbaum]] \u2020 (mother)\n|siblings = [[Clara Stahlbaum|Clara]] (sister)<br>[[Fritz Stahlbaum|Fritz]] (brother)}}\n\n'''Louise Stahlbaum''' is a character of the 2018 Disney film, ''[[The Nutcracker and the Four Realms|The Nutcracker and The Four Realms]]''. She is the elder sister of [[Clara Stahlbaum|Clara]] and [[Fritz Stahlbaum]] and is played by Ellie Bamber.\n\n==Background==\n===Personality===\nLouise is a kind, polite, girly, and tidy girl.\n\n===Physical Appearance===\nLouise is a fair skinned girl with brown hair.\n\n==Role in the film==\nClara and Fritz encounter Louise, telling Fritz that Clara is planning on catching mice on Christmas Eve. She, Clara, and Fritz approach their father, [[Benjamin Stahlbaum|Benjamin]] who gives them presents from their late mother, [[Marie Stahlbaum|Marie]], to which Louise receives her favorite dress. Upon wearing it, she interrupts Benjamin's discussion with Clara in a plan to go to the party, much to Clara's frustration in an attempt to open Marie's last gift.\n\nJust as the Stahlbaum family leaves for [[Drosselmeyer]]'s mansion, Benjamin tells Louise that she should try to make new friends while dancing. She and her relatives are accepted into the invitation.\n\nAfter Clara returns home, Louise happily reunites with her family upon seeing Benjamin dancing with her together just as Louise and Fritz dance together in couples.
