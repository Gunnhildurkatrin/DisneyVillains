She sometimes engages [[Clover]] in duels of witty comebacks and often wins.\n\nEven so, she cares deeply for him and all her friends and looks out for Sofia like a mother-hen.\n\nShe has been described as having been around the woods a few times, making her care for Sofia, like Baileywick's, be due to similar and often hard lives.\n\n===Physical Appearance===\nRobin has red, brown, tan, and white feather colors.
