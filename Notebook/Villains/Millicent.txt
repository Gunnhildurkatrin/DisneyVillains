His idol is [[Scrooge McDuck]], Uncle Donald, trying to find whenever visit his nephew [[Donald Duck]]. At first, she hated being called \"Cent\" to name, but after Scrooge he pointed out that every cent is a step toward infinity, she changed her mind.
