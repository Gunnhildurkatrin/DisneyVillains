She is a brave and resourceful commoner who becomes the royal detective after solving a mystery that saved the kingdom's young prince.\nAs the royal detective, Mira travels throughout the kingdom helping royals and commoners alike, and will stop at nothing to solve the case.\n\n==Background==\n===Physical appearance===\nMira has olive skin, hazel eyes, and long black hair. She is around eight years of age. Her outfit is a blue dress, magenta pants, pink earrings, and blue shoes. She wears a gold magnifying glass.\n\n=== Personality ===\nMira is a kind and intelligent girl, and she's willing to help with any case that comes her way whether it's searching for a missing bicycle or the reason for something strange to happen. When it comes to solving a case, she's not one to jump to conclusions, and she won't come to a conclusion until she gets all the facts.\n\n==Trivia==\n*Mira's mother is first mentioned in \"[[The Great Diwali Mystery]]\", and it's hinted that she passed away prior to the series.\n\n==Gallery==\n<gallery position=\"center\" captionalign=\"center\" spacing=\"small\" widths=\"150\">\nMira, Royal Detective.png\nMira, Royal detective concept.jpg|Mira concept design\nMira Royal Detective -
