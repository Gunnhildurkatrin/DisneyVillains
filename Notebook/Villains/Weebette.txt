"{{Infobox_character\n|image=Weebette.jpeg\n|name=Weebette\n|films=[[Flubber]]\n|voice=Julie Morrison\n|inspiration=[[Weebo]]\n|dislikes=Flubber (sometimes)\n|parents=[[Professor Brainard|Philip Brainard]] (father)<br>[[Dr. Sara Jean Reynolds|Sara Jean Reynolds]] (mother)\n|siblings=[[Flubber (character)|Flubber]]\n|status = Active}}\n\n'''Weebette''' is a minor character in Disney's ''[[Flubber]]''. She only appeared at the end, as the daughter of [[Weebo]], who unlike the latter, is very sarcastic around the humans, to the point of insulting [[Flubber (character)|Flubber]]. Weebette was voiced by the voice actress Julie Morrison.\n\n==Appearance==\nWeebette was created from blueprints designed by [[Weebo]]. As such, she can be considered a streamlined version of her mother. She is mainly red with a similar LCD screen.\n\n==Personality==\nUnlike her mother, Weebette behaves like a teenage girl with a bad attitude.\n\nShe's very sarcastic around humans, though, he once referred Sara Jean Reynolds as mother, since she was made from Sara's husband, Philip Brainard, implying she refers him as father.\n\nAlso, despite Flubber being friends with her father and late mother, Weebo, Weebette insults him, seeing him as a brother.\n\n{{CharacterStub}}\n{{Flubber}}\n[[Category:Robots]]\n[[Category:Objects]]\n[[Category:Disney characters]]\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:Flubber characters]]\n[[Category:Live-action characters]]\n[[Category:Characters who fly]]\n[[Category:Females]]"
