"{{Infobox character\n|image = Nate appearance.png\n|name = Nate\n|shows = ''[[Gravity Falls]]''\n|voice = [[Scott Menville]] (season 1)\n[[Alex Hirsch]] (season 2)\n|occupation = Usher at The Royal Ragtime Theater\n|home = [[Gravity Falls (location)|Gravity Falls, Oregon]]\n|friends = [[Wendy Corduroy]], [[Robbie Valentino|Robbie]], [[Lee]], [[Tambry]], [[Thompson]], [[Dipper Pines]], [[Mabel Pines]]\n|family = Unnamed Girlfriend\n|enemies = [[Pa and Ma Duskerton]], [[Bill Cipher]]\n|likes = Having fun, dares, parties\n|dislikes = Getting trapped\n|alignment=Neutral}}'''Nate'''\u00a0is one of\u00a0[[Dipper Pines|Dipper]]'s friends and\u00a0[[Lee]]'s best friend, and is usually seen with him. He appears in the episode \"[[The Inconveniencing]]\".\n\n==History==\nNate is first seen with the rest of [[Wendy Corduroy|Wendy's]] friends. He comes along to the abandoned\u00a0Dusk 2 Dawn\u00a0store. He gives\u00a0[[Dipper Pines|Dipper]]\u00a0the name \"Dr. Funtimes\" after he opens the door. Later, when he is headed out the door and the door closes, he yells at the\u00a0ghosts\u00a0to let him out. Then the ghosts turn him into a hot dog. He is the fourth of Wendy's friends to have something happen to. He is rescued by Dipper along with the rest of Wendy's friends. Lee and Nate do not have enough money to leave.\n\nIn\u00a0\"[[Double Dipper]]\", he and\u00a0Lee\u00a0are seen attempting to escape from\u00a0Stan's\u00a0party. They get trapped, due to not having enough cash to pay Stan's \"exit fee\". Nate can be seen celebrating\u00a0Pioneer Day\u00a0when\u00a0[[Quentin Trembley]]\u00a0returned to Gravity Falls.\u00a0<sup class=\"reference\" id=\"cite_ref-0\">[http://gravityfalls.wikia.com/wiki/Nate#cite_note-0 [1]]</sup>\n\nIn \"[[The Time Traveler's Pig]]\", Nate is seen riding the Ferris wheel with Lee at the\u00a0Mystery Fair.\n\n==Personality==\nNate is a stereotypical carefree, laid back teenager. He can be sarcastic and loves partying and doing stupid things with his friends.\n\n==Appearance==\nHe has dark skin, brown hair, a small beard, and dark aqua green and white cap and earrings. He wears a black shirt.
