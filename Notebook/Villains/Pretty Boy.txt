"{{Infobox character\n|name= Pretty Boy\u00a0\n|image= Pretty_Boy.jpg\n|films= ''[[Teacher's Pet: The Movie]]''\n|shows= ''[[Teacher's Pet]]''\n|games= \n|rides= \n|actor= \n|actress= \n|voice= [[Jerry Stiller]]\n|animator= \n|model= \n|designer= \n|inspiration=[[Iago]] from ''Disney's Aladdin'' \n|awards= \n|fullname= \n|alias= \n|occupation= Leonard's bird\n|home=Leonard Helperman's house \n|family= \n|friends= [[Mr. Jolly]], [[Leonard Helperman]], [[Spot Helperman/Scott Leadready II|Spot Helperman]]|minions= [[Mr. Jolly]]\n|enemies= [[Dr. Ivan Krank]], Tallulah, everybody else\n|likes= Seedbells, soaps, his friends\n|dislikes= Being kissed by Mr. Jolly\n|powers=\n|status =\n|quote= \"I ain't no fairy.\"}}\n\n'''Pretty Boy''' is [[Leonard Helperman|Leonard]]'s pet bird who stays at home with [[Mr. Jolly]]. He's a grumpy, mean-spirited, sarcastic bird. He does whatever he can to help his friends,\u00a0and is an honest guy.\n\n==Personality==\nPretty Boy is a bossy, big-mouthed canary with a Napoleon complex. He yells at everyone for everything. He loves seed bells and soaps. He also has a wonderful singing voice.\n\n==Appearance==\nPretty Boy is a canary with green feathers and an orange beak.\n\n==Role in the series==\nPretty Boy is featured in the subplots of the series along with [[Mr. Jolly]] and they have adventures of their own. They however left the house\u00a0twice in the series, when they thought they were each missing and when [[Leonard Helperman|Leonard]] and [[Spot Helperman/Scott Leadready II|Scott]] brought them on pet day. In one episode, Pretty Boy migrated and Scott and Leonard went to get him back.\n\n==Role in the film==\nIn the movie, he stays at home with Spot and Jolly while Leonard and his mom are on vacation. After spot leaves to see Dr. Ivan Krank, he and Jolly cover for him. Later, they find out that Dr. Krank turns animals into mutants and go to save Spot. After\u00a0hitching a ride on a plane,\u00a0hitch a ride\u00a0with a biker gang, and getting lost\u00a0in Cuba, they get to Florida by a garbage truck and find Leonard and they find out about what happened to Spot. Spot (who is now a man) arrives and they warn him that Leonard's gonna turn into a dog. When they get to the lab, a battle and an explosion begin turning Krank into a mouse and Spot back to a dog. Afterward, they hid under the RV's beds to get home as part of Spot's plan.\n\n==Trivia==\n*In one episode, Pretty Boy gets the last line: \"When we get home, I'm gonna give him such a peck on the--\".\n*In the show, Pretty Boy sings operatically, but in the movie, he sings in his normal voice.\n\n==Gallery==\n<gallery widths=\"164\" columns=\"dynamic\" position=\"center\" spacing=\"small\">\nChar_32636_thumb.jpg\nPretty Boy.png\nPretty_Boy1.jpg\nTumblr ma0e6nH44w1r3jtxx.png\nScreen Shot 2013-02-26 at 7.51.21 PM.png\nScreen Shot 2013-01-28 at 8.54.28 PM.png\nScreen Shot 2013-02-26 at 7.54.13 PM.png\nScreen Shot 2013-03-15 at 8.14.47 PM.png\nScreen Shot 2013-03-01 at 6.45.19 PM.png\nScreen Shot 2013-03-16 at 9.05.43 AM.png\nScreen Shot 2013-03-25 at 4.13.37 PM.png\nScreen Shot 2013-02-01 at 8.10.40 PM.png\nScreen Shot 2013-03-18 at 9.11.52 PM.png\nScreen Shot 2013-03-18 at 9.12.03 PM.png\nScreen Shot 2013-03-30 at 3.44.08 PM.png\nScreen Shot 2013-03-28 at 7.03.13 PM.png\nScreen Shot 2013-02-09 at 7.52.28 PM.png\nScreen Shot 2013-02-09 at 7.48.41 PM.png\nScreen Shot 2013-04-30 at 6.43.51 PM.png\nAaahh.JPG\nWelcome to cubs.JPG\nScream silently.JPG\nKrank's defeat.JPG\nBathtub.JPG\nGary baseman.JPG\nCome on.JPG\n</gallery>\n\n{{Teacher's Pet}}\n[[Category:Teacher's Pet characters]]\n[[Category:Disney characters]]\n[[Category:Passeriformes]]\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:TV Animation characters]]\n[[Category:Pets]]\n[[Category:Males]]\n[[Category:Heroes]]\n[[Category:Anti-heroes]]\n[[Category:Transformed characters]]\n[[Category:Animated characters]]\n[[Category:Singing characters]]\n[[Category:Characters who break the Fourth Wall]]"
