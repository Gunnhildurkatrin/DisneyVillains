"[[File:ThirdEye.png|thumb]]'''Princess Iris''' is the princess of Cornea in the show ''[[Pair of Kings]]''. She is a three eyed girl.\n==History==\nPrincess Iris is a three eyed girl who can see out of her third eye. With her third eye, she can do two things at once, like watching TV or texting while talking to someone.\nKing Boomer and King Brady both like Princess Iris and fight over her. They are forced to compete in a 'third wheel' duel for her.\n\nEventually, Iris stands up to her father, the King, and says she does not want an arranged boyfriend.
