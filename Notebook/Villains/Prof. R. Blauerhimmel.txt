Blauerhimmel''' is a pilot and member of the [[Society of Explorers and Adventurers]].\n\n==Background==\nLittle information is available about Blauerhimmel, having only made a few minor portrait appearances. It is known that he was an aviator who became a member of S.E.A. during the late 19th century and was active throughout the early 20th century.\n\n==Appearances==\n\n===[[Mystic Manor]]===\nAt Mystic Manor, his portrait is on display in the queue as well as appearing in the 1899 Society meeting painting alongside [[Henry Mystic|Lord Henry Mystic]], [[Harrison Hightower III]] (with [[Shiriki Utundu]]), [[Mary Oceaneer|Captain Mary Oceaneer]], and Dr. [[Dr. J.L. Baterista|J.L. Baterista]].\n\n===Bengal Barbecue===\nThe profile painting of Blauerhimmel can also be found at Disneyland's Bengal Barbecue. It seen with a photograph of Blauerhimmel in the cockpit of a biplane and a collection of postcards.\n\n===[[Skipper Canteen]]===\nA book in the ''[[Jungle Navigation Company|Jungle Navigation Co.]] Ltd. Skipper Canteen'' called \"Traditional native holiday d\u00e9cor\" is written by Blauerhimmel. This book's name is an apparent allusion to the seasonal [[Jingle Cruise]] overlay of the [[Jungle Cruise]] though it is unknown why this is affiliated with the professor.\n\n===[[The Tropical Hideaway|Tropical Hideaway]]===\nAt The Tropical Hideaway, one of the expedition paddles is marked with his name and displays a 1904 expedition on the Ucayali River in Peru.\n\n==Trivia==\n*Blauerhimmel is German for \"Blue Sky\", the term Imagineering uses for early \"no limits\" conceptual exploration for an attraction.\n*The professor is modelled after imagineer Robert Coltrin.\n\n{{Society of Explorers and Adventurers}}\n[[Category:Theme park characters]]\n[[Category:Society of Explorers and Adventurers]]\n[[Category:S.E.A. characters]]\n[[Category:Pilots]]\n[[Category:Males]]\n[[Category:Disney characters]]\n[[Category:The Haunted Mansion characters]]\n[[Category:Adults]]\n[[Category:Heroes]]\n[[Category:Professors]]\n[[Category:Jungle Cruise characters]]"
