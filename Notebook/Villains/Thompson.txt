"{{Infobox character\n|image = S1e5_thompson.png\n|shows = ''[[Gravity Falls]]''\n|voice = Michael Rianda\n|occupation = Manager at the Royal Ragtime Theater\n|family = Unnamed mother\n|friends = [[Wendy Corduroy|Wendy]], [[Tambry]], [[Nate (Gravity Falls)|Nate]], [[Lee]], [[Robbie Valentino|Robbie]]\n|enemies = [[Pa and Ma Duskerton]]\n|likes = Dancy Pants Revolution, hanging out with his friends\n|powers = Driving|fullname = Leon Thompson}}'''Thompson'''\u00a0is one of\u00a0[[Wendy Corduroy|Wendy]]'s friends who appears in \"[[The Inconveniencing]]\". He is usually seen for the pranks the guys play, such as putting ice in his pants and trying to get a jellybean in his belly button.\n\n==History==\nHe is seen with Wendy's friends, driving the van they use to get around\u00a0[[Gravity Falls (location)|town]]. He drives them,\u00a0[[Dipper Pines|Dipper]],\u00a0and\u00a0[[Mabel Pines|Mabel]]\u00a0to the\u00a0[[Dusk 2 Dawn]]\u00a0store to party. He cheers Dipper on when he gets the door open. In the store, he is seen letting the guys stuff his pants with ice and playing\u00a0Dancy Pants Revolution.\n\nWhen the\u00a0ghosts\u00a0attack, he is sucked into Dancy Pants Revolution, which starts piercing him with multicolored arrows and taunting him.\n\nThompson is seen again in \"[[Double Dipper]]\" and in one scene, he is dancing with someone. He appears again in \"[[Irrational Treasure]]\", watching the wagons circle around\u00a0[[Stan Pines|Grunkle Stan]]'s car at\u00a0Pioneer Day. In \"[[The Time Traveler's Pig]]\", Thompson is seen riding the Ferris wheel with\u00a0Tambry\u00a0at the\u00a0Mystery Fair.\n\n==Personality==\nThompson is a stereotypical teenager, laid-back, fun-loving, and friendly.
