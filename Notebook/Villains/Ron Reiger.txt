He is voiced by [[James Arnold Taylor]].\n\n==History==\nThe first time that he surfaced was in the episode \"[[Gorilla Fist]]\". According to Ron Stoppable, he regularly gets out of P.E., and stays home from school, as mentioned in that episode.\n\nHe appeared in this episode in \"[[Homecoming Upset]]\", when he waved at Bonnie. He also was using a computer in \"Homecoming Upset\", which is how the ballots were counted for Homecoming King and Queen.\n\nIn the series finale \"[[Graduation]]\", he was marching behind Bonnie and his cap got nailed by her and he was sitting next to [[Kim Possible (character)|Kim]].
