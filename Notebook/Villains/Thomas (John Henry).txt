"{{Infobox character\n|image = Thomas.jpg\n|name = Thomas\n|shorts = ''[[John Henry]]''\n|voice = David Murray\n|occupation = Railroad worker\n|alignment = Good\n|friends = [[John Henry (character)|John Henry]]<br>Other railroad workers\n|enemies = Steam drill operator\n|possessions = Sledgehammer}}'''Thomas''' is one of [[John Henry (character)|John Henry's]] co-workers on the railroad line in the [[2000]] animated short ''[[John Henry]]''. He was voiced by Dave Murray.\n\n==Role in the short film==\nThomas is one of the workers on the C&O Railroad who was worried when Mactavish tells the other workers that if they do not finish building the railroad on the other side of the mountain, their jobs could be lost. When John Henry helps railroad industry, Thomas and the other workers work together with John to help the industry and the workers to finish their task. When a steam drill arrives, Thomas and the other workers are upset when modern machinery arrives and John tells the other workers that he decides to challenge against the steam drill. Later after finishing the challenge, Thomas and the other workers, along with Polly, are very proud for John's victory. Later after John Henry collapses and dies, Polly, Thomas, Mactavish, and the other workers, mourn for his death.\n[[Category:Disney characters]]\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:Animated characters]]\n[[Category:Males]]\n[[Category:Adults]]\n[[Category:African American characters]]\n[[Category:Singing characters]]"
