"{{Infobox_character\n|image = Constance_Close_Up.jpg\n|name = Constance\n|films = ''[[The Three Musketeers]]''\n|actress = [[Julie Delpy]]\n|appearance = Blonde, beautiful\n|alliance = Good\n|home = [[Paris]], [[France]]\n|likes=[[D'Artagnan]] and serving the Queen\n|dislikes=The King and Queen in danger\n|friends = King Louis XIII, Queen Anne, and the musketeers\n|enemies=[[Cardinal Richelieu]] and his men\n|fate= Shares a kiss with D'Artagnan after he is finally made a musketeer.\n|quote=\"Ladies-in-waiting are forbidden to socialize with musketeers.\"\n|alignment = Good}}'''Constance''' is a minor character in [[Disney]]'s\u00a0[[1993]]\u00a0movie,\u00a0''[[The Three Musketeers]]''.\n\n==Role in the film==\nConstance is a lady-in-waiting and friend to Queen Anne. Queen Anne and Constance ride their horses with the royal bodyguards. [[D'Artagnan]] seeing them ride, defeats her traveling escorts by accident (believing them to be bandits), Constance is smitten by her \"rescuer.\" After discovering that he wants to be a Musketeer, she coyly tells him that Musketeers and Royal ladies-in-waiting are not permitted to see each other and tells him that he ''will'' become a great Musketeer due to his bravery and fighting skills. Later, Constance is instrumental in rescuing D'Artagnan in his final duel with [[Captain Rochefort|Rochefort]]\u00a0by giving him back his sword.
