Her family is unknown.\n\n==History==\nShe is friends with [[Bonnie Rockwaller]], [[Tara]], [[Hope]], and the other girls.\n\nBonnie notes in ''So the Drama'' that she is dating [[Steve Farley]] the baseball captain.\n\n==Appearance==\nJessica is blue-eyed with straight blonde hair and light freckles.
