She is the Headmaster of Sage Academy.\n==Appearance==\nLinda Knightslinger is a middle aged woman. She is a redhead and wears her hair styled into three large curls.\n\n==Personally==\nHeadmaster Knightslinger is a passionate and dedicated woman who takes great pride in her work and words diligently to ensure that every generation of mages is able to flourish.\n\nShe is willing to do what is necessary to protect her students and all of humanity, even if the circumstances are less than favorable.\n\nShe is also annoyed fairly easily.\n\n==History==\nHeadmaster Knightslinger introduces herself to the newest group of first year Sage Academy students. She welcomes them and tells them about the five types of magical talents and the five schools that make up Sage. Fuzzies, who can talk to animals; Flickers, who can pull things to them with a flick of their hand; Flares, who have power over the element of flames; Flyers, who can fly; and Fluxers, who can shapeshift into animals.\n\nKnightslinger, along with the Sage faculty, oversee a placement test to determine who among the new students qualify for the honors level classes, and who is a UDM.\n\n== Trivia ==\n* Headmaster Knightslinger is meant to replace [https://upside-down-magic.fandom.com/wiki/Dr._Stone_Horace Stone Horace] from the books in the film.\n* She is very powerful Flare.\n\n{{CharacterStub}}\n[[Category:Live-action characters]]\n[[Category:Disney characters]]\n[[Category:Teachers]]\n[[Category:Females]]\n[[Category:Adults]]\n[[Category:Upside-Down Magic characters]]\n[[Category:Magic Users]]"
