He also likes playing music using whatever objects he can find as instruments.\n\n===Physical appearance===\nD.J. is a Dalmatian puppy with black ears that have a single big white circular spot on both of them that resemble headphones and two spots above his right eyebrow. He wears red headphones and a red collar with a black square dog tag with a white circle on it.\n\n==Role in the series==\nD.J. first appeared in \"[[Boom Night]]\" at the very end of the episode, relaxing on the roof and watching the fireworks with his siblings.\n\nD.J. also appears in \"[[Winter Funderland]]\", entertaining guests at the indoor snow party by playing icicle drums with two soup ladles.\n\nIn \"[[Poetry Scam]]\", D.J. can be seen playing some bongos while [[Dylan]] recites his poem, and can also be seen dancing as [[Dolly (101 Dalmatian Street)|Dolly]] does her freestyle.\n\nIn \"[[Crushed Out]]\", D.J. provides the music on a keyboard for Dylan's date with Portia the Poodle.\n\nHis first vocal appearance is in \"[[The Woof Factor]]\", where Dolly and Dylan come to him, looking for a third pup to help fill in for [[Triple-D]] in an advert, as the actual Triple-D are double booked. However, D.J. is unable to, as he is planning on busking in Covent Garden.\n\nIn \"[[Dal-Martians]]\", he used a theremin to make alien sounds as Dolly and [[Dawkins]] were fooling Dylan into believing aliens have come to Camden.\n\nIn \"[[The Dog House]]\", he helped Dolly and Triple-D build a human by programming the sound it makes, under what Triple-D describe as humans constantly talking.\n\nIn \"A Summer To Remember\", D.J. is said to provide the music to the \"Summer Song\" musical number, as well as showing to provide the music for the beach party.\n\nIn \"Long Tongue Day\", he is shown with the other pups hiding from the Camden Kraken, before watching Dolly & Dylan do battle with their model of it later in the episode.\n\n==Trivia==\n*D.J.'s name obviously comes from the slang abbreviation of \"Disc Jockey\", a person who introduces and plays recorded popular music, especially on the radio or at social gatherings such as parties.\n\n\n==Gallery==\n<gallery position=\"center\" captionalign=\"center\">\nDJ Model SheetsDL.png|DJ Character Ref\nDJ's Picture.png\nScreen Shot 2019-04-03 at 2.21.00 AM.png|D.J's first appearance in \"Boom Night\"\nScreen Shot 2019-03-31 at 3.04.42 PM.png\nDimitrisDancing.png\nScreen Shot 2019-04-14 at 6.10.07 PM.png|D.J. setting the mood for his brother's date with Portia\nDJWoofFactor.png|\u201cI\u2019m busking to Covent Garden.\u201d\nTumblr abf691f3a7cf2541eb0eab13c3d97b45 2adab178 2048 (1).png|In a pup cage bottom on the right of [[Hunter De Vil]]\n</gallery>\n\n{{101 Dalmatians}}\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:101 Dalmatians characters]]\n[[Category:TV Animation characters]]\n[[Category:Animated characters]]\n[[Category:Disney characters]]\n[[Category:Musicians]]\n[[Category:Kids]]\n[[Category:Dogs]]\n[[Category:Males]]\n[[Category:Siblings]]\n[[Category:American characters]]\n[[Category:Pre-teens]]"
