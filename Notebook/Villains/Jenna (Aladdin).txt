She appears in the [[2006]] book, ''My Best Friend is Jasmine''.\n\n== Story ==\nJenna lives on the streets of Agrabah with her younger brother [[Rafi (Aladdin)|Rafi]], and younger sister Salima. One day, when looking for food, Jenna stumbles into the palace gardens, and twists her ankle. Princess [[Jasmine]] offers to let her and her siblings stay in the palace while her ankle heals. Jenna asks to help the princess with the palace animals, and Jasmine shows her a shy, lonley baby elephant that refueses to be near anybody. Over the time it takes for Jenna's ankle to heal, she slowly befriends the baby elephant, evetually earning his trust.
