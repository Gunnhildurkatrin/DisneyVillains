''He made his debut appearance in [[Vee Goes Viral]].\n\n== Background ==\nDragos is a funky vampire dancer who arises from time to time when 100 humans perform his favorite dance, the \u201cSpine-chiller\u201d which was recorded by Poppy\u2019s brother, Edgar when Vampirina and Poppy learned the dance.\n\n=== Physical Appearance ===\nDragos is a vampire with slicked back black and white hair, lavender skin and purple eyes. He also has pointed ears. He wears a high collared hot pink to purple disco shirt with some decals on it.
