"{{Infobox character\n|image = Andromeda.jpg\n|shows = ''[[Hercules: The Animated Series|Hercules]]''\n|alignment = Good\n|voice = [[Kath Soucie]]}}'''Andromeda''' is a character in the TV series ''[[Hercules: The Animated Series|Hercules]]''. She appeared in ''[[Hercules and the Big Lie]]''. She is a new student who attracts [[Hercules (character)|Hercules]]' attention. She is voiced by [[Kath Soucie]].\n== Trivia ==\n* In the original Greek mythology, Andromeda was the daughter of Cepheus and Cassiopeia. When the former claimed that her daughter was more beautiful than Nereids, [[Poseidon]] sent the sea monster Cetus to kill Andromeda who was chained to a rock.
