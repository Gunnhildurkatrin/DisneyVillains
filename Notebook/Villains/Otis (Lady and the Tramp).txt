"{{Infobox character\n|image=Otis.jpeg\n|name=Otis\n|films=''[[Lady and the Tramp II: Scamp's Adventure]]''\n|voice= [[Rob Paulsen]]\n|personality= Mad, insane, unkempt, funny, dim-witted\n|appearance= Slender Chinese Crested with light brown fur on his body and gray unkempt fur around his scalp\n|occupation= Stray dog\n|alignment= Neutral\n|affiliations= \n|goal= To escape from [[Dog Pound|the pound]] (failed)\n|home=[[Dog Pound]]\n|friends = Pound dogs (possibly)\n|enemies= [[The Dogcatcher]], [[Reggie]], [[Tramp]], [[Scamp]], [[Angel (Lady and the Tramp)|Angel]]\n|likes= Being freed from [[Dog Pound|the pound]], cauliflower, watching fights\n|dislikes= Not being rescued, [[the Dogcatcher]]\n|powers= \n|weapons=Teeth\n|fate=Yells at Tramp, Scamp and Angel to come back and save him\n|quote = }}'''Otis''' is a mad and unkempt Chinese Crested from Disney's\u00a0[[2001]] film ''[[Lady and the Tramp II: Scamp's Adventure]]''.\n\n==Appearances==\n===''[[Lady and the Tramp II: Scamp's Adventure]]''===\nOtis is first seen at the [[Dog Pound|dog pound]] when [[the Dogcatcher]] puts [[Scamp]] into the kennel with [[Reggie]], to which Otis laughs and tells Scamp that he is in the wrong cage. [[Tramp]] eventually bursts into the pound when Reggie is about to land a fatal blow on Scamp. After a prolonged fight, Otis laughs again when he sees Scamp knocked out and claims that he is done for. When Scamp later wakes up, the dogcatcher comes in to recapture him, but is defeated by [[Angel (Lady and the Tramp)|Angel]]. As Tramp, Scamp and Angel leave the pound, Otis is still laughing but then yells at them to come back and free him as well, which they don't do.
