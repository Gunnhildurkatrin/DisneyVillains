"{{Infobox character\n|image = Ms. Perumal.png\n|shows = ''[[The Mysterious Benedict Society]]''\n|actress = Gia Sandhu\n|inspiration = Ms. Perumal from the book series\n|occupation = Tutor\n|parents = Mrs. Perumal (mother)\n|likes = Reynie Muldoon<br>Seeing Reynie succeed\n|dislikes = Not getting direct answers }}\n'''Ms. Perumal''' is a major supporting character in the [[Disney+]] series ''[[The Mysterious Benedict Society]]''. She is [[Reynie Muldoon]]'s tutor from the orphanage who sees a lot of potential in the boy.\n\n==Background==\n\n===Personality===\nMs. Perumal is kind and headstrong. She can recognize greatness and shows nothing but support and care to those around her who show genuine talent. She sees a lot of potential in Reynie to the point that she develops a maternal bond with him. She is also shown to be a very tough and determined woman when the situation demands it.\n\n===Physical appearance===\nMs. Perumal has a light brown complexion to her and has neatly combed hair. She is typically adorned in pink clothing that consists of a long skirt and sweater.\n\n===Abilities===\nMs. Perumal is bilingual and is capable of speaking in both English and Tamil.\n\n==Trivia==\n*In the original book series, Miss Perumal was more of a minor character while the series expands her role and gives her more to do, such as when she discovered that Reynie was not at Boatwright Academy.\n\n==External links==\n\n*[[w:c:mysteriousbenedictsociety:Miss Perumal|Ms. Perumal]] at [[w:c:mysteriousbenedictsociety:The_Mysterious_Benedict_Society_Wiki|The Mysterious Benedict Society Wiki]]\n{{The Mysterious Benedict Society}}\n[[Category:The Mysterious Benedict Society]]\n[[Category:Adults]]\n[[Category:Females]]\n[[Category:Live-action characters]]\n[[Category:Disney characters]]"
