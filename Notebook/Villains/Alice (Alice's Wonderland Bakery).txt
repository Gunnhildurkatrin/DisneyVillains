She is the descendant of her great-grandmother [[Alice]], who also shares the same name as her, and is a young baker working at the Wonderland Bakery somewhere in Wonderland.<ref>https://d23.com/sweet-news-from-disney-junior-alices-wonderland-bakery-to-debut-in-2022/</ref>\n\n== Background ==\n=== Physical Appearance ===\nLike the original, Alice is a young, pretty girl living in what is assumed to be the Victorian era. She has big blue eyes, fair skin, and blonde, thick, medium-length hair held back by a blue checkered ribbon with a bow and a spiral button attached to it. Her dress was designed to be a blue cooking outfit by a chef coat with a small white apron that has a small pocket with a cupcake logo on it. Her sleeves and stockings are purple and pink-striped based on the [[Cheshire Cat]], and wears white and blue-checkered sneakers with pink outlining, blue laces, and two spiral buttons attached to each side of the shoes.\n\n==Gallery==\n<gallery position=\"center\" captionalign=\"center\" spacing=\"small\" widths=\"150\" orientation=\"landscape\">\nAlice Bakery concept art 1.jpg|Earliest concept drawings for Alice\nAlice Bakery concept art 2.jpg\nAlice Bakery concept art 3.jpg\nAlice Bakery concept art 4.jpg\nAlice Bakery concept art 5.jpg\nAlice Bakery concept art 6.jpg\nAlice Bakery concept art 7.jpg\n</gallery>\n\n==Trivia==\n*She is the second Disney Junior character to be a descendant of a Disney character after [[Kit Secord]] from ''[[The Rocketeer (TV series)|The Rocketeer]]''.\n*Concept art of Alice shows that she was designed by fan artist Luigi Lucarelli, who's known for making character designs like ''TMNT: Back to the 80s'' and a planned ''[[The Nightmare Before Christmas|Nightmare Before Christmas]]'' spinoff series entitled ''Halloween Town'' which was a suggestion he came up with.\n**Some of the concept art shows Alice looking almost exactly like her great-grandmother in the original film.\n\n==References==\n{{Reflist}}\n\n== Navigation ==\n{{Alice in Wonderland}}\n[[Category:Alice in Wonderland characters]]\n[[Category:Heroines]]\n[[Category:Females]]\n[[Category:Singing characters]]\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:Disney Junior characters]]\n[[Category:Kids]]\n[[Category:Upcoming characters]]\n[[Category:Animated characters]]\n[[Category:TV Animation characters]]\n[[Category:Disney characters]]\n[[Category:English characters]]"
