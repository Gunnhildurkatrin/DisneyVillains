"[[File:Neckbeard.png|thumb]]\n'''Neckbeard''' is a character in ''[[Wander Over Yonder]]''. He is voiced by Seth Morris.\n==Background==\nHe first appeared in the episode \"[[The Sky Guy]]\", where he runs a magical item shop then [[Wander]] and [[Sylvia]] come in to buy a magic mirror to stop Lord Dominator but Wander is distracted by a tiny isolated planet Neckbeard forbids to let him touch. He then fights Wander for the planet and offers the mirror for the Bitties but Sylvia throws the planet in the mirror to another galaxy being free to see the universe. Then Neckbeard charges them for the mirror and the planet.\n\nHe will join Wander and a group of rebels to fight [[Lord Dominator]] in \"[[The End of the Galaxy]]\".\n\n{{Wander Over Yonder}}\n{{DEFAULTSORT:Neckbeard}}\n[[Category:Wander Over Yonder characters]]\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:Neutral characters]]\n[[Category:Wizards]]\n[[Category:Magic Users]]\n[[Category:Animated characters]]\n[[Category:TV Animation characters]]\n[[Category:Disney characters]]\n[[Category:Disney XD characters]]\n[[Category:Males]]"
