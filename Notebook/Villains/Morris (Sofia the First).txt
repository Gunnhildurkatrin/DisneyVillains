"{{Infobox character\n|image = Morris.png\n|name = Morris\n|shows = ''[[Sofia the First]]''\n|voice = [[Andrew Rannells]]\n|personality = Charming, generous, kind, nice, polite, sweet, funny\n|alignment = Good\n|goal = To attend the Royal Summer Ball (Succeeded)\n|home = Isleworth\n|friends = [[Princess Charlotte]], [[Sofia the First (character)|Sofia]], [[Zinessa]]\n|enemies = [[Princess Charlotte]] (formerly)\n|likes = Collecting anything related to royalty, parties, peacocks, tea\n|dislikes = People hurting his feelings\n|fate = Becomes friends with Charlotte and dances with her at the ball\n}}'''Morris''' is a goblin who appears in the ''[[Sofia the First]]'' episode, \"[[Beauty is the Beast]]\". When [[Princess Charlotte]] is turned into a beast, she must befriend Morris in order to undo the curse.\n\nMorris is a nice, polite, and a generous goblin. He lives in a tree with a bright red door on it. Morris passion is to collect all things royals ranging from teacups and silver spoons to a replica of the Enchancia Flag. He opens his house every other Sunday and some holidays, so others can come and view his collection. He shares many interests with Princess Charlotte, who comes to realize he is a true friend and defends him when the palace guards believed that he kidnapped her, being the first person to do something like that for him and after Charlotte returns to herself, Morris forgives her and she invites him to the royal ball, the episode ends with Charlotte dancing with Morris in the ballroom.\n\n==Gallery==\n<gallery position=\"center\" captionalign=\"center\" widths=\"180\">\nSofia the First - More to Adore.jpg\nBecoming Friends.png\nDancing the waltz.jpg\n</gallery>\n\n{{Sofia the First}}\n{{DISPLAYTITLE:Morris}}\n[[pt-br:Morris (Princesinha Sofia)]]\n[[Category:Goblins]]\n[[Category:Males]]\n[[Category:Sofia the First characters]]\n[[Category:Disney characters]]\n[[Category:Animated characters]]\n[[Category:TV Animation characters]]\n[[Category:Monsters]]\n[[Category:Disney Junior characters]]\n[[Category:Singing characters]]"
