She is also a reporter/interviewer for the school's newscast.\n\n==Personality==\nNaturally, being a cheerleader, she is very chipper and bubbly, and smiles most of the time. Catering to the blonde stereotype, she may also be a little ditzy.\n\n==Appearance==\nShe is a caucasian girl with a blonde ponytail and blue eyes. She is most often seen in a mostly red cheerleader outfit.\n\n==History==\nNot only is she seen as a member of the school's cheerleading squad, but she has also been seen co-anchoring the school's news program with\u00a0[[Philsky Richards|Philsky]]. Such as covering a parade on the football field that includes Principal Folsom in\u00a0<ref>Cry, The Beloved Mascot</ref>\u00a0and an organization at the school called\u00a0B.A.G.A.S.T., in\u00a0<ref>Two Wheels, Full Throttle, No Breaks</ref>.\n\nWhen Ingrid is seen going through the school's hallway as a new student at X in\u00a0Ingrid Third, Public Enemy Number One, she meets Cheri, who is the first to greet her as part of the welcome bandwagon committee.\n\n==Episode Appearances==\n*\"[[Cry, the Beloved Mascot]]\"\n*\"[[Ingrid Third, Public Enemy Number One]]\"\n*\"[[Next Stop, Armageddon]]\"\n*\"[[Two Wheels, Full Throttle, No Breaks]]\"\n*\"[[The Shreds Fell Like Snowflakes]]\"\n\n==Gallery==\n<gallery spacing=\"small\" position=\"center\">\nNext Stop, Armageddon (16).jpg\nCry The Beloved Mascot (7).jpg\nCry The Beloved Mascot (8).jpg\nIngrid Third, Public Enemy (18).jpg\nIngrid Third, Public Enemy (19).jpg\nTwo Wheels, Full Throttle.
