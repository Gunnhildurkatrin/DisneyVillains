"{{Infobox character\n|image = Char 15088.jpg\n|name = Ollie\n|shows = ''[[The Little Mermaid (TV series)|The Little Mermaid]]''\n|voice = Whit Hertford\n|occupation = Gabriella's translator\n|alignment = Good\n|friends = [[Gabriella]], [[Ariel]], [[Sebastian]], [[Flounder]]\n|enemies = [[Ursula]] \u2020\n}}'''Ollie''' is a blue vocal octopus who appears in two episodes; \"[[Wish Upon a Starfish]]\" and \"[[Ariel's Treasures]]\".\n==Appearances==\n===''[[The Little Mermaid (TV series)|The Little Mermaid: The Series]]''===\nHe is the close friend and interpreter of [[Gabriella]], a deaf mermaid. He has spots on the sides of his head and a patch on his left eye. In his first appearance, he is shown with Gabriella, watching [[Ariel]] sing. After Ariel notices them, he explains Gabriella's disability and introduces himself. He is also the one to tell the tale of the [[The Magical Wishing Starfish|Magical Wishing Starfish]], and he accompanies them along the way. In his second appearance, he is visiting [[Atlantica]] with Gabriella and helps her stop [[Ursula]]'s plot to take over the kingdom.\n\n==Quotes==\n*\"Hello.
