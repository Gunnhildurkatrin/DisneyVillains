She is a competitive girl with a streak of overconfidence, and is also known as the \"girl of the lance\".\n\n== Appearance ==\nAstra\u2019s theme color is red. She is a teenage girl with fair skin, red eyes, lips, and hair held into a high ponytail. Her outfit consists of a red jacket trimmed with yellow and rolled up sleeves, a red dress underneath with the word, \u201cstar\u201d, white socks, and red sneakers. Her accessories are her star-charmed wristbands.\n\nOn Earth, Astra\u2019s appearance changes to look like a normal human. Her hair color changes to amber-brown with only a streak of red. Her skin loses its sparkle and her outfit changes to look more human.
