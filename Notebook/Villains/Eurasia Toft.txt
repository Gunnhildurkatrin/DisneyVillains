"{{Character\n|image = EurasiaToft.png\n|name = Eurasia Toft}}\n'''Eurasia Toft''' is a female anthropomorphic mouse created by Andrea \"Casty\" Castellan for the Italian Disney comics.\n\nShe's the spunky and resourceful niece of aging explorer Pangaea Toft, who works as an archeologist specializing in study of ancient ruins and civilizations. She has often crossed paths with [[Mickey Mouse|Mickey]] and [[Goofy]] during her adventures, becoming quite close to both these fellow adventurers.\n[[Category:Mickey Mouse universe characters]]\n[[Category:Comic characters]]\n[[Category:Characters introduced in comics]]\n[[Category:Italian characters]]\n[[Category:Anthropomorphic characters]]\n[[Category:Mice]]\n[[Category:Females]]\n[[Category:Disney characters]]"
