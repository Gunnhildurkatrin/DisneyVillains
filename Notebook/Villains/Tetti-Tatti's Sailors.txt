However, they become so appreciative about his singing that they refuse to kill him, knowing he didn't swallow a singer. Later, when Tetti-Tatti harpoons him, the sailors, enraged and horrified, attack and restrain him.\n\n===Physical Description===\nAll three look exactly the same\u2014barrel-chested with a large rounded jaw, shaven head, reddish-brown beards, wearing the typical sailor suits, with the typical buff male triangular body design.\n\n==Gallery==\n<gallery navigation=\"true\">\nMakeminemusic-disneyscreencaps com-5661.jpg|Sailors shushing their boss\nMakeminemusic-disneyscreencaps com-5627.jpg\nMakeminemusic-disneyscreencaps com-5614.jpg\nMakeminemusic-disneyscreencaps com-5589.jpg|Sailors trying to harpoon Willie\nMakeminemusic-disneyscreencaps com-5582.jpg\nmakeminemusic-disneyscreencaps_com-5785.jpg\nMakeminemusic-disneyscreencaps com-5802.jpg\nMakeminemusic-disneyscreencaps com-5688.jpg\nMakeminemusic-disneyscreencaps com-5666.jpg\nMakeminemusic-disneyscreencaps com-6001.jpg\nMakeminemusic-disneyscreencaps com-5873.jpg\nMakeminemusic-disneyscreencaps com-5840.jpg\n0109make23.jpg|Sailors|link=http://vegalleries.com/dis-opc/0109make23.jpg\nMakeminemusic-disneyscreencaps com-6311.jpg|The sailors, furious with Tetti-Tatti for shooting and killing Willie, beat him up.\n</gallery>\n\n{{Make Mine Music}}\n[[Category:Reformed characters]]\n[[Category:Henchmen]]\n[[Category:Silent characters]]\n[[Category:Disney characters]]\n[[Category:Make Mine Music characters]]\n[[Category:Animated characters]]\n[[Category:Adults]]\n[[Category:Males]]\n[[Category:Heroes]]\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:Italian characters]]\n[[Category:Character groups]]\n[[Category:Protagonists]]\n[[Category:Sailors]]\n[[Category:Characters in the Disney animated features canon]]"
