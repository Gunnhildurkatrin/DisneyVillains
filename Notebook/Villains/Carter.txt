"{{Infobox character\n|image = carter.jpg\n|name = Carter\n|films = ''Muppets from Space''<br>''[[Muppets Most Wanted]]'' (cameo)\n|shows = ''[[Muppets Tonight]]''<br>''[[Disney Drive-On with The Muppets]]''\n|actor = [[Kevin Clash]]\n|appearance = Elderly human muppet with long white hair and black sclerae\n|occupation = [[Howard Tubman|Howard's]] butler\n|alignment = Good\n|friends = [[Howard Tubman]], [[Kermit the Frog]], [[Walter]], other Muppets}}'''Carter''' is\u00a0[[Howard Tubman]]'s elderly, doddering butler seen in The Tubmans of Porksmith and Boarshead Revisited sketches on\u00a0''[[Muppets Tonight]]''. Carter also appeared in\u00a0''Muppets from Space'', as a resident of\u00a0the Muppet Boarding House\u00a0and makes a cameo appearance in\u00a0''[[Muppets Most Wanted]]''.\n\nThe puppet was later recycled as Faversham,\u00a0[[Miss Piggy]]'s\u00a0butler (played by\u00a0[[Steve Whitmire]]), in an appearance on\u00a0''Donna's Day''\u00a0on September 1, 2000. He converses via telephone with\u00a0[[Kermit the Frog]]\u00a0and informs him of Piggy's absence (and also passes on several kisses).\n\nIn the third installment of\u00a0[[Disney Drive-On with The Muppets|Disney Drive-On]], the Carter puppet appears as an elderly\u00a0Disney\u00a0animator, the 10th Old Man (in reference to the group of animators known as \"The Nine Old Men\"). Played by\u00a0[[Bruce Lanoil]], he's still at work animating the title character from\u00a0''[[Peter Pan (1953 film)|Peter Pan]]''\u00a0(1953, although the original animation drawings shown were done by [[Milt Kahl]]). He mistakes\u00a0[[Walter]]\u00a0for \"Mr. Disney\" and recalls how he was supposed to get\u00a0''[[Cinderella (1950 film)|Cinderella]]''\u00a0animator [[Marc Davis]]' office.\n\nThe puppet's design appears to be reworked from\u00a0an old lady\u00a0puppet that was used as part of\u00a0[[w:c:muppet:Geri and the Atrics|Geri and the Atrics]]\u00a0on\u00a0''The Muppet Show''.\n==Trivia==\n* Carter's film appearance in Muppets from Space is owned by Sony Pictures.\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:Disney characters]]\n[[Category:Males]]\n[[Category:Adults]]\n[[Category:Elderly characters]]\n[[Category:The Muppets characters]]\n[[Category:Silent characters]]\n[[Category:Acquired characters]]"
