"[[File:D2.png|thumb|209px]]'''Dustin Pitt''' is a one-time guest and minor character of [[Disney Channel Original Series|Disney Channel's Original Series]] sitcom, ''[[Dog With a Blog]]''. He is portrayed by Noah Harden.\n\n==Biography==\nAvery had a huge crush on him, so Nikki helped Avery get the attention of Dustin. After watching a movie together, Avery begins to worry that he doesn't like her, but the next day he asks her to be his girlfriend. They broke up not too long after, which was hard for Avery. He looks up to Tyler James very much, mostly because of his great bike riding skills.\n\n==Physical Description==\nDustin is about the same height as Avery, and has short, brown hair.\n\n==Relationships==\n===[[Avery Jennings]]===\nThey both have crushes on each other. In Avery's First Crush, Dustin asks Avery to be his girlfriend but then in Avery's First Breakup, he broke up with Avery. He is never mentioned after.\n\n===[[Tyler James]]===\nDustin looks up to Tyler because he is a good bike rider. He first didn't know that Tyler was Avery's brother.
