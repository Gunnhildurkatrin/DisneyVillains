"{{Infobox character\n|image = Alexander and Terry.png\n|shows = ''[[Big City Greens]]''\n|voice = John Early (Alexander)\n|alias = Two best friends\n|personality = '''Alexander:''' Loud, friendly, bossy\n----\n'''Terry:''' Quiet, introverted\n|appearance = '''Alexander:''' Slender, powder blue skin, blond hair that's shaved on the sides\n----\n'''Terry:''' Slender, magenta skin, shaved hair, black-framed glasses\n|alignment = Neutral\n|home = [[Big City]]\n|friends = [[Cricket Green]], [[Tilly Green]], Each other (boyfriends)\n|quote = \"Terry, oh my gosh, give [[Cricket Green|him]] a tip already!\"}}\n'''Alexander and Terry''' are two minor characters from the [[Disney Channel]] animated series ''[[Big City Greens]]''. They are a random best friends/boyfriends that the series protagonists [[Cricket Green|Cricket]] and [[Tilly Green]] keep encountering in strange circumstances. Alexander and Terry are almost polar opposites.\n\n\n\n==Background==\n===Personality===\n<tabber>Alexander=\n'''Alexander''' is a loud and curious person. While he is friendly, he can be bossy, especially to his boyfriend Terry who he loves being with, but seems to be constantly annoyed at.\n|-|Terry=\n'''Terry''' himself is quiet and never utters a word, making him an introvert. He seems to prefer doing quiet activities, except dancing which seems to make both men happy.\n</tabber>\n\n===Appearance===\nAlexander and Terry are both tall skinny men. Alexander has light blue skin and blonde hair that appears shaved down on the sides and combed back at the top. He usually wears an orange polo shirt and gray pants with matching black shoes. Terry has light violet skin and shaved down hair and wears glasses. His clothing is more laid back as he wears a navy blue hoodie and light brown cargo pants along with red sneakers.\n\n==Trivia==\n*Series creator, [[Chris Houghton]], confirmed that Alexander and Terry are in fact a couple.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://chrishoughton.tumblr.com/post/180830849052/would-you-ever-consider-making-an-lgbt-mainside|title=Chris Houghton Tumblr Post|website=Tumblr|date=December 5, 2018}}</ref>\n*Alexander and Terry might be wealthy as they are seen eating at Sea Fus\u00efon.
