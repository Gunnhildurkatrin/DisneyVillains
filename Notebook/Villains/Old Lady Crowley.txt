She is a grumpy old lady who is known to never smile, earning her the nickname \"Scowley Crowley\". She is often seen sweeping the castle floors. The one and only thing that puts a smile on Crowley's face is peace and quiet.\n==Role in the [[Tangled: The Series|series]]==\nIn \"[[Tangled: Before Ever After]]\", Crowley points out the mud that Rapunzel led into the castle.\n\nIn \"[[One Angry Princess]]\", Crowley talks to Rapunzel and says that she saw Attila picking flowers.
