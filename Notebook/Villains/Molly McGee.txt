She is a 13-year-old Thai-American girl who befriends a grumpy ghost named [[Scratch (The Ghost and Molly McGee)|Scratch]].\n\n==Background==\n===Personality===\nMolly is described to be an optimistic girl who lives to make the world a better place.\n\n===Physical appearance===\nMolly is a girl of small height. She has tan skin, dark brown hair with a reddish tint placed into a ponytail with a pink headband, and dark brown eyes.\n\nHer clothes consist of a white t-shirt with black edges covered with a sleeveless jean jacket, waistband bike shorts, a magenta-and-purple zig-zag patterned plaid skirt, white and mint socks, and yellow-and-pink sneakers.\n\n==Gallery==\n{{Gallerylink}}\n\n==Trivia==\n*Molly is the second main character from a Disney animated series to be a Thai-American, similar to [[Anne Boonchuy]] from ''[[Amphibia]]''.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://twitter.com/CAPEUSA/status/1388695881453502472|title=CAPE Post|website=Twitter|date=May 1, 2021}}</ref>\n**Both characters are also thirteen years old.\n*Molly is biracial, just like her voice actress, [[Ashly Burch]].\n**She is also the second main Disney Channel character after [[Jake Long]] from ''[[American Dragon: Jake Long]]'' to be a Eurasian whom the latter is of both Chinese (maternally) and European-American (paternally) descent.\n***Jake was also 13 (during season 1) and had a younger sister;
