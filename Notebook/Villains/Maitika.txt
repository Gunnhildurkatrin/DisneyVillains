"[[File:Snapshot_2_(5-20-2017_5-45_PM).png|thumb|250px]]\n\n'''Maitika''' is a minor character from ''[[Jungle 2 Jungle]]''. She is [[Mimi-Siku Cromwell|Mimi Siku]]'s pet tarantula. \n\nAt one point, she escapes from her cage and causes utter chaos until Mimi finds her. He then puts her back in her cage, calling her a \"bad girl\" and tells her to stay in her cage. \n\nDuring the film's climax, she aids Mimi in fighting off a gang of Russian mobsters when Mimi drops her on [[Alexei Jovanovic]]'s back, and she crawls up to the top of his head.\n\n{{Jungle 2 Jungle}}\n[[Category:Disney characters]]\n[[Category:Jungle 2 Jungle characters]]\n[[Category:Females]]\n[[Category:Adults]]\n[[Category:Spiders]]\n[[Category:Pets]]\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:Live-action characters]]\n[[Category:Silent characters]]\n[[Category:Heroines]]"
