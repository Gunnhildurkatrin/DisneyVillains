"[[File:Gloria Forrester.png|thumb]]\n'''Gloria Forrester''' is a minor character in ''[[Honey, I Shrunk the Kids]]''. She is the wife, sister, or cousin of [[Don Forrester]].\n\nGloria and Don arrive in their trailer at the Thompsons' house, and then when Don pretends to \"fish\" [[Russ Thompson, Sr.|Russ]] downstairs from the foundation, she greets Russ, and waves her dog Gigi's paw. Noticing [[Wayne Szalinski]] \"flying,\" she tries to get Don's attention and tell him, but he is busy talking to Russ. When Russ has told Don about Mae \"having not been feeling too well\", the Forresters just leave.\n\n{{Honey, I Shrunk the Kids}}\n[[Category:Honey, I Shrunk the Kids characters]]\n[[Category:Females]]\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:Live-action characters]]\n[[Category:Disney characters]]\n[[Category:Adults]]"
