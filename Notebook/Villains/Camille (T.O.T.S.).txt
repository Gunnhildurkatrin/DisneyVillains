"[[File:Camille_happy.png|thumb]]\n\n'''Camille''' is a baby camel who first appeared in the ''[[T.O.T.S.]]'' episode \"[[A Penguin in the Desert]]\".\n\n==Appearance==\nCamille is a light tan colored camel with a light brown tuft of curly hair on her head. She has green eyes and a light colored muzzle. She wears a light orange collar with the T.O.T.S. logo on it and a diaper with light orange trim and cactus prints on it.\n\n==Trivia==\n*She is voiced by Charlie Townsend, who also voiced [[Mia (T.O.T.S.)|Mia the Kitten]] and [[Octivia|Octivia the Octopus]],\n*She speaks in babbles and coos, but does say \"Mama, Dada\" like a lot of the other babies.\n{{T.O.T.S.}}\n[[Category:T.O.T.S. characters]]\n[[Category:Camels]]\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:Disney Junior characters]]\n[[Category:Females]]\n[[Category:Infants]]\n[[Category:Disney characters]]\n[[Category:Animated characters]]\n[[Category:TV Animation characters]]\n[[Category:Anthropomorphic characters]]"
