"[[File:Jeremy_Hunter.jpg|thumb]]'''Jeremy Hunter''' is a Fire captain who investigated the fire at Shake It Up, Chicago, and was the boyfriend/fianc\u00e9 of [[Georgia Jones]] for the first half of season three. He first appeared in Fire It Up, and immediately fell for Georgia. The romance continued to blossom for several episodes until \"Oh Brother It Up\" where they become engaged. The couple plan to wed in \"I Do It Up\", but they call off the marriage at the altar.
