"{{Infobox character\n|image = Recurring Raccoon.png\n|shows = ''[[Milo Murphy's Law]]''\n|alignment = Bad\n|home = [[Danville]]\n|likes = Stealing\n|fate = Deceased\n}}\n{{Quote|You've seen him before, you're gonna see him soon, he's the recurring raccoon.|Recurring Raccoon jingle}}\n\nThe '''Recurring Raccoon''' is a character who appears in the second season of ''[[Milo Murphy's Law]]''. The name implies that he makes recurring appearances in several episodes throughout the season. He will often show up unexpectedly, steal something, and then walk away along with a jingle playing in the background.\n\n==Role in the Series==\nThe Recurring Raccoon first appears in \"[[Picture Day (Milo Murphy's Law)|Picture Day]]\", in which he steals a camera and runs away with it, leaving [[Milo Murphy|Milo]], [[Zack Underwood|Zack]], [[Melissa Chase|Melissa]], and [[Amanda Lopez|Lopez]] staring blankly in confusion. He appears again in \"[[Game Night (Milo Murphy's Law)|Game Night]]\" when he steals some dice from Milo while looking for a board game containing dice. He notes that he knew he was going to come back.\n\nWhile watching TV in \"[[Cake 'Splosion!]]\", the Recurring Raccoon shows up an steals the TV remote from [[Martin and Brigette Murphy|Martin]] and [[Heinz Doofenshmirtz|Doofenshmirtz]]. He goes into the backyard and dunks it in the birdbath, causing the TV to malfunction. Doofenshmirtz tries to zap the raccoon with a inator, which will send him to a random location.
