"{{Dablink|the character Georgia|the state of Georgia|[[Georgia (state)]]}}\n{{Infobox character\n|image = Profile_-_Georgia.jpg\n|films = ''[[The Princess and the Frog]]''\n|games = ''[[The Princess and the Frog (video game)|The Princess and the Frog]]''\n|voice = [[Danielle Mon\u00e9 Truitt]]\n|personality = Caring, fun\n|appearance = Slender, dark skin, magenta lipstick, short dark brown hair, black eyes, magenta dress/shirt, a purple beanie with a flower accent, gold tear drop earrings\n|alignment = Good\n|home = [[New Orleans]]\n|friends = [[Tiana]], four unnamed friends\n|likes = Dancing\n|dislikes = Tiana's workaholic ways\n|fate = Eats at Tiana's new restaurant with her friends\n|quote = \"All [[Tiana|you]] ever do is work!\"|status=Active}}\n'''Georgia''' is a minor character in [[Walt Disney Animation Studios|Disney]]'s [[2009]] animated feature film, ''[[The Princess and the Frog]]''. She is a friend of [[Tiana]]'s and is seen eating at the restaurant at which Tiana previously worked.\n\n==Background==\nGeorgia is a very minor character so not much is known about her background. She is a friend of Tiana's, but [[Charlotte La Bouff|Charlotte]] remains as Tiana's closest friend. It is unknown how long she and her other friends have known Tiana or how they met. She does show concern for Tiana because she openly expresses her dislike on how much Tiana works and never takes a break.\n\n===Physical appearance===\nGeorgia is a young woman of African-American heritage. She has a dark brown skin complexion, short dark brown hair, black eyes and she is slender in frame. She wears a magenta-colored lipstick, a magenta dress/shirt, a purple beanie with a flower accent and gold tear drop earrings.\n\n==Appearences==\n===''[[The Princess and the Frog]]''===\nGeorgia has a brief role. She appears at the restaurant at which Tiana was previously employed as a waitress. She and Tiana's other four friends offer to take her out dancing. When Tiana refuses in order to make more time to work, she expresses her concern and distaste in that Tiana overwork herself. Tiana says she might join them next time, but it later turns out Tiana has actually never danced.
