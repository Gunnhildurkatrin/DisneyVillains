"{{Infobox_character\n|image = Diane-Darcy.png\n|films = ''[[Herbie Goes to Monte Carlo]]''\n|shows = ''[[Herbie, the Love Bug]]''\n|actress = Julie Sommars (film)<br>Andrea Howard (TV series)\n|alias = Diane\n|personality = Competitive, stubborn, shows her softer and friendly side later, opinionated, emotional\n|occupation = Racing driver\n|alignment = Good\n|goal = To win the Trans-France Race\n|friends = [[Giselle (The Love Bug)|Giselle]] (her car), [[Herbie]], [[Jim Douglas]], [[Wheely Applegate]]\n|likes = Racing, winning, proving women can race\n|dislikes = Being judged because she's a woman, men getting in her way\n|quote=\"I'm a race driver and I can win it on my own!\"}}\n'''Diane Darcy''' is a female race car driver who appears in ''[[Herbie Goes to Monte Carlo]]'' and the TV series ''[[Herbie, the Love Bug]]''.\n\n==Appearances==\n===''[[Herbie Goes to Monte Carlo]]''===\nDiane is the only female race car driver in the Trans-France Race and first meets [[Jim Douglas]] by accident when they bump into each other.\n\nDiane owns a [[1976]] Lancia Scorpion which, like [[Herbie]], is alive, however, she didn't know it at the time. When Herbie first sees her car, he falls in love and she gets annoyed every time he gets in the way while racing. She starts accusing Jim and his mechanic, [[Wheely Applegate]], of trying to keep her out of the race because she's a female driver.\n\nAs the film goes on, Diane learns to like Jim and finally believes her car is alive and encourages Herbie to win the race for his friends and loved one.\n\nIn the end, Diane starts dating Jim and names her car \"[[Giselle (The Love Bug)|Giselle]].\"\n\n===''[[Herbie, the Love Bug]]''===\nDiane returns in the third episode of the series. She discovers Jim is now dating another woman named Susan MacLane but it is clear they are just good friends.
