She is very similar to [[Mertle Edmonds]] from [[Lilo & Stitch: The Series|the original series]], [[Jessica (Stitch!)|Jessica]] from the third season of ''Stitch!'', and [[Meiying]] from ''[[Stitch & Ai]]''.\n\n==Role in the series==\nShe is young girl who lives on a fictional island off the shore of Okinawa in the Ryukyus called [[Izayoi Island]] in [[Japan]]. She is known as [[Yuna Kamihara|Yuna]]'s rival. She is self-centered, impatient, short-tempered, aggressive and in some cases, a bit snobby. Piko/Penny often takes great pride to mock and deride Yuna and [[Stitch]] in any case, often ending with Yuna and her fighting. This makes her remarkably similar to [[Mertle Edmonds]]. There's rare occasions where she and Yuna can get along though. Her father runs the island's pineapple plantations and her family runs the highest rated hotels and resorts on Izayoi, making her snobbery justifiable.\n\nThere's a habit with Piko/Penny where when she's angered, she'll often end her ranting with a comparison of something akin to pineapples. She is also a black-team karate leader. Piko/Penny may have been idealized after Hawaiian pop song Pineapple Princess. Piko/Penny dislikes [[Sasha (Stitch!)|Sae/Sasha]] but secretly does believe that she dresses better than her.
