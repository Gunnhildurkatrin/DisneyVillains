"{{Infobox character\n|image = Boo-Goo.png\n|shows = ''[[Stitch!]]''\n|games = ''[[Stitch Jam]]''\n|alignment = Neutral/Good\n|friends = [[Yuna Kamihara]], [[Stitch]], [[Jumba Jookiba]], [[Pleakley]]\n|enemies = [[Reuben]], [[Captain Gantu|Gantu]], [[Dr. H\u00e4msterviel]], [[Delia]]\n|likes = Helping [[Yuna Kamihara|Yuna]] and her friends\n|dislikes = Losing his memory\n|powers = Flight}}\n'''BooGoo''' is a mysterious creature from outer space in the ''[[Stitch!]]'' anime. Although a self-centered indulgence, he is a pet that is loved by everyone present on the island. He often accompanies [[Yuna Kamihara|Yuna]], [[Stitch]], [[Jumba Jookiba|Jumba]] and [[Pleakley]] on certain missions throughout the anime series.\n\n==Role in the series==\nIn one episode, Experiment 303 ([[Amnesio]]) zaps the memories of BooGoo, Stitch, Jumba, [[Captain Gantu|Gantu]], [[Reuben]] and [[Dr. H\u00e4msterviel|H\u00e4msterviel]]. Everyone's memory is shown to be stored in capsules under Amnesio's wings. When Amnesio is eventually captured, Gantu demands him to give up the memory-containing capsules. Near the end of the episode, it is shown that the capsule Gantu had\u00a0swallowed didn't contain his own personality\u00a0\u2013 it contained BooGoo's!
