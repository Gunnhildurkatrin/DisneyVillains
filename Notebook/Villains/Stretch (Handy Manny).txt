He can speak out accurate measurements, can count well, and use his tape as an extendable hand. He bonds with every tool, except Turner and Felipe. \u00a0\n==Appearance==\nStretch is a magenta tape measure with yellow tape. Due to the tip being too big, the tape resembles buck teeth.\n==Personality==\nStretch is very smart. Stretch is shown to be a perfectionist. He's always right, knows everything, has a mathematical mind, and a good memory. He also makes good remarks and loves to be held by Manny.
