"[[File:Ash.jpg|thumb]]'''Ashley \"Ash\" Tyler''' is the drummer of Iron Weasel. he is played by [[Stephen Full]].\n==Biography==\nAsh can always be depended upon for a good deed, a laugh, and Tug of War Tuesday. Ash is the band's amiable drummer, who is often depicted as being the dumbest of the three. He used to have a pet fish named Sushi, which got killed after Burger's shorted-out bass zapped it.\n==Personality==\nAsh is usually described as the dumbest of the lot. He is good natured, and seems to have a deep love and talent for music. This has been proven several times, as he is adepth in rock-n'-roll, classical, and jazz music. Ash also seems to be slightly selfrightous and always takes the blame for what other do. He is also very forgetfull easily forgetting and mixing up locations and directions.
