He was voiced by [[Wikipedia:Ron Gans|Ron Gans]].\n\n==Personality==\nHe is\u00a0a slow-witted, but good-natured orangutan who is the resident strongman and calliope player (in earlier episodes, he was accompanied by a small pink creature while playing the calliope). He makes friends easily and has other talents, like kite making. He sometimes doesn't know his own strength.\n\nQ.T. joined the circus following Lilli.\n\n{{CharacterStub}}\n\n==Trivia==\n*While possibly unintentional, Q.T.'s appearance is slightly similar to [[King Louie]] from the 1967 film ''[[The Jungle Book]]''.\n{{Dumbo}}\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:Disney characters]]\n[[Category:Dumbo's Circus characters]]\n[[Category:Males]]\n[[Category:Heroes]]\n[[Category:Singing characters]]\n[[Category:Circus performers]]\n[[Category:Servants]]\n[[Category:Anthropomorphic characters]]\n[[Category:Apes]]\n[[Category:Musicians]]\n[[Category:Disney Channel characters]]"
