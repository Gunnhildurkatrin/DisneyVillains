He is an anthropomorphic goose who lives in [[Duckburg]].\n\n==Background==\nVacation Van Honk, in concept, is a \"recurring extra\"; though he has little characterization, this permanent tourist and globe-trotter is persistently encountered by the Ducks wherever they go. Although Van Honk has made few appearances, he attended Scrooge's surprise birthday party, implying that he is a close friend of the family.\n==Appearances in the series==\nHe appeared in the episode \"[[Sir Gyro de Gearloose]]\", where he is one of the Duckburg residents complaining about Gyro's inventions malfunctioning.\n\nIn \"[[Sweet Duck of Youth]]\", Vacation was seen among Scrooge's family and friends at Scrooge's surprise birthday party.\n\nHe also appeared in \"[[Magica's Shadow War]]\" and \"[[Duckworth's Revolt]]\".\n==Other appearances==\nThough largely forgotten since the end of ''[[DuckTales]]''<span>'</span>s original run, Van Honk has made a few cameos in comics in his role of recurring bystander, such as in the 2004 story ''The Beagle Boys and the Last Pirate''.\n==Gallery==\n<gallery spacing=\"small\" position=\"center\" captionalign=\"center\">\nVan Honk 2.JPG\nVacationVanHonksComicCameo.jpg|Van Honk's cameo in ''The [[Beagle Boys]] and the Last Pirate'', drawn by Giorgio Di Vita.\n</gallery>\n[[Category:DuckTales characters]]\n[[Category:Geese]]\n[[Category:Anthropomorphic characters]]\n[[Category:Males]]\n[[Category:Donald Duck universe characters]]\n[[Category:Animated characters]]\n[[Category:TV Animation characters]]\n[[Category:Disney characters]]\n[[Category:The Disney Afternoon characters]]"
