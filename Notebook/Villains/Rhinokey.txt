"{{Infobox character\n|image = Rhinokey6.jpg\n|name = Rhinokey\n|shows = ''[[The Wuzzles]]''<br>[[DuckTales (2017 series)|''DuckTales'' (2017)]] (cameo)\n|voice = [[Alan Oppenheimer]]\n|personality = Fun-loving, happy-go-lucky, obnoxious\n|appearance = Tall and slender hybrid creature, half rhinoceros/half monkey, body of a monkey, pink fur, rhinoceros legs, rhinoceros snout with striped horn\n|occupation = Prankster\n|friends = [[Bumblelion]], [[Butterbear]], his best friend [[Moosel]], [[Eleroo]], [[Hoppopotamus]], [[Tycoon]]\n|enemies = [[Crocosaurus]], [[Brat]], [[Flizard]]\n|alignment = Good\n|affiliations = Wuzzles\n|likes = Playing pranks, telling jokes}}'''Rhinokey''' is one of the main characters of [[The Wuzzles]], [[Alan Oppenheimer]] voiced him.\n==General Description==\n===Physical Appearance===\nHalf rhinoceros and half monkey, Rhinokey is a Wuzzle who has a rhino-like snout with a horizontal-striped horn, pink fur, a long tail, and rhino-like legs. He is in a posture that is very much like that of a monkey's.\n===Personality===\nRhinokey is a fun-loving, happy-go-lucky prankster. He loves to play practical jokes. He can be obnoxious, but he loves his friends.\n\n==Appearances==\n===''[[DuckTales (2017 series)]]''===\nRhinokey makes a cameo in the episode \"[[The Lost Cargo of Kit Cloudkicker!]]\". This version is a non-sapient fusion of a rhino and a gorilla, implied to be the result of being fused by the Stone of What Was, and referred to as a \"Rhinocera\" by [[Della Duck]] and \"Gorill-roceros\" by [[Kit Cloudkicker]].
