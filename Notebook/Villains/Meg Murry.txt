When her father disappears, she uses the tesseract to travel to the planet Camazotz to rescue him. While she succeeds, her brother, [[Charles Wallace Murry|Charles Wallace]], is left behind on Camazotz, his mind overtaken by the sinister technorganic being known as [[It|It (or Red)]]. Meg manages to pull an act of true love to free Charles Wallace from It/Red's mind control, in the process also defeating It/Red.\nWhile Meg starts out having trouble socializing and distanced from her family, her adventure allows her to both be more sociable and reestablish her bonds with her family.\n\nMeg is just as smart as her parents, able to master complex technology and craft brilliant plans.\n\n==Gallery==\n<gallery position=\"center\" captionalign=\"center\">\nMeg_Murry.jpg\nA Wrinkle In Time - Meg and Calvin.jpg\nA-wrinkle-in-time-movie-storm-reid.jpg\nA-wrinkle-in-time-movie-oprah-winfrey-storm-reid.jpg\nA-wrinkle-in-time-movie-storm-reid-reese-witherspoon.jpg\nA_Wrinkle_In_Time_Character_Poster_04.jpg\nWp4801104.jpg\n</gallery>[[Category:Live-action characters]]\n[[Category:Disney characters]]\n[[Category:Heroines]]\n[[Category:Females]]\n[[Category:Teenagers]]\n[[Category:Siblings]]\n[[Category:Lovers]]\n[[Category:American characters]]\n[[Category:A Wrinkle in Time characters]]\n[[Category:Protagonists]]"
