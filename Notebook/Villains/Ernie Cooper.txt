He is Craig and Kira Cooper's son, K.C.'s younger brother and Judy's older brother.\n\n== Summary ==\nHe\u2018s mostly labeled as an outsider and a geek. He is also rather unlucky when it comes to romance, having only dated two girls before (Jolie) and (Akina). He is a computer genius in the Cooper family.\n\n== Abilities ==\n\n=== '''Computer Hacking''' ===\nErnie is a very skilled computer hacker and at hacking firewalls. He was able to hack into the closed-circuit camera system, hack into GPS systems and can get details about anyone.\n\n=== '''Engineering''' ===\nErnie is quite skilled at engineering, he was able to fix Judy when she was being controlled by a virus and helped Judy get her memory back in the Season 2 opener.\n\n=== '''Strength''' ===\nErnie has some strength, he was able to lift Marisa and ran away when they were attacked with a laser gun from Ursula, and was able to send a criminal flying to a wall by jump kicking him in \"Web of Lies\".\n\n=== '''Luck''' ===\nErnie is an extremely lucky guy as he single-handedly defeated a criminal on his own and stopped the coma of the president thanks to his luck and unintentionally ruining Richard Martin's shot.\n\n=== '''Chemistry''' ===\nErnie is very good with chemistry as he was able to synthesize a truth serum & love serum that were very effective.\n\n=== '''Items to Advantage''' ===\nHe seems to be very skilled in combat when it comes to using items.
