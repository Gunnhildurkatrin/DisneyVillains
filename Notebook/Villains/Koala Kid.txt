He was climbing the dam to see what was outside of the city, which of course was against the rules. He was rescued by [[Aladdin (character)|Aladdin]] when the cliffs gave way.\n\nThroughout the episode, he tried to prove to [[Queen Kimbla]] that Aladdin was a good-natured man.\n\n==Role in the episode==\nWhen Aladdin first happened upon him, the Koala Kid was climbing the dam. But the cliffs gave way beneath him, so Aladdin and [[Magic Carpet|Carpet]] decided to rescue him.
