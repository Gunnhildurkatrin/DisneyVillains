Her Fireside Girl uniform has a red collar with a dent in the middle and two small splits at the sides of her sleeves, with sneakers with a strap going all the way across. In some appearances, Holly has two small buck teeth. However, in other appearances, the teeth are not visible. In winter, she switches to an orange sweater with an off-white collar and red zigzags, and red boots.\n\n==Trivia==\n*Her first speaking role was the episode \"[[Raging Bully]]\", as she tells Candace
