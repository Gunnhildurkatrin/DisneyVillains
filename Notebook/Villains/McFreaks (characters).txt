McFist captured them for his McFreaks show. However, Randy breaks them out, and soon Randy returns them to Detention Island in the episode \"[[McFreaks]]\".\n\n==Background==\n===Barnabutt Jones===\nBarnabutt is a brown creature with the face of a mandrill, two legs, and four colorful butts surrounding his head. He is able to shoot a sonic pulse from his butts.\n\n===Rondo===\nRondo is a mix between a brown bear head (with the top of the head being bald) and a purple octopus body.\n\n===Nine-arm Snake===\nThe nine-arm snake is a blue-green snake with nine arms and orange-brown hair on his head. It is quite capable with machinery, hotwiring and driving [[Principal Slimovitz|Slimovitz]]'s car, as well as piloting (but not being able to land) McFist's hovercraft.\n\n===Parrot Shark===\nThe parrot shark is a mix between a red parrot head and a shark body.
