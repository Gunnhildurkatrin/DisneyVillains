"{{Infobox character\n|image = Tumblr_nriu4ttRbn1r3jmn6o1_1280.png\n|name = Shawebizad\n|shows = ''[[DuckTales]]''\n|inspiration = [[Wikipedia:Scheherazade|Scheherazade]]\n|personality = creative, sweet\n|appearance = Female duck dressed in harem outfit\n|occupation = Harem girl, storyteller\n|alignment = Good\n|friends = [[Scrooge McDuck]]\n|enemies = [[Flintheart Glomgold]]<br>[[Djinni]]\n|alias = Desert Flower\n|fate = Manages to escape the palace and returns to the Emir}}'''Shawebizad''' is a beautiful young story teller (and a reference to [[Wikipedia:Scheherazade|Scheherazade]]) who is being held captive by the Sultan as part of his harem in the ''[[DuckTales]]'' episode \"[[Master of the Djinni]]\".\n\nThe Sultan's nickname for her is 'Desert Flower', and he tends to favor her over the rest of the harem. He mistakes [[Scrooge McDuck|Scrooge]] and [[Flintheart Glomgold|Glomgold]] as spies trying to free her and orders them thrown to the crocodiles.
