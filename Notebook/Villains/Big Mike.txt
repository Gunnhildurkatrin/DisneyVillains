"\n[[File:Big_Mike.jpg|thumb|250px]]\n'''Big Mike'''<ref>http://www.imdb.com/character/ch0398733/?ref_=tt_cl_t8</ref> is a minor character on the show ''[[Kim Possible]]''. He was voiced by [[Brad Garrett]].<ref>http://www.behindthevoiceactors.com/tv-shows/Kim-Possible/Big-Mike/</ref><ref>http://www.imdb.com/name/nm0004951/</ref>\n\n==History==\nHe is a regular in detention at [[Middleton High School]]. He appears to get along well with his other detention mates, [[Vinnie]] and [[Junior (Kim Possible)|Junior]]\n\nBig Mike doesn't speak much. Until he helped [[Kim Possible (character)|Kim Possible]] with [[Shego]] he did little more than growl or speak in single words.\n\nBig Mike is a very sensitive and loyal person. He whimpered when it looked like Kim was going to get blown up and sat on Shego when she tried to stop [[Ron Stoppable]] from removing the nanotick.\n\nHe also appears to have befriended the cheerleaders as he was seen socializing with [[Tara]] and [[Hope]] during a school dance.\n\n==Appearances==\n*''[[Tick-Tick-Tick]]''\n*''[[Queen Bebe]]''\n\n==Trivia==\n*Most of Mike's appearances do not show above his shoulders, so his face was only seen twice.\n*Big Mike appears to be afraid of rodents, as he jumped onto his chair and screamed when \"escaped mutant vermin\" Rufus infiltrated detention to spring Kim.\n\n==References==\n<references/>\n{{Kim Possible}}\n[[Category:Kim Possible characters]]\n[[Category:Teenagers]]\n[[Category:Males]]\n[[Category:Disney characters]]\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:Neutral characters]]\n[[Category:Giants]]\n[[Category:Silent characters]]\n[[Category:Animated characters]]\n[[Category:TV Animation characters]]\n[[Category:Disney Channel characters]]"
