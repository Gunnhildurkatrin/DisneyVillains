Frank Baum}}\nThe '''Munchkins''' are people that reside in the [[Land of Oz]].\n==Appearances==\n\n===''[[Return to Oz]]''===\nThe Munchkins appear in the coronation scene along with many other Oz characters.\n\n===''[[The Muppets' Wizard of Oz]]''===\n[[File:Munchkins.jpg|thumb|185x185px|\"Munchkins\" in ''The Muppets' Wizard of Oz''|left]]\nIn the [[Muppet Studios|Muppets]]' version of the Wizard of Oz, the Munchkins are combined with the Field Mice. The Munchkin Field Mice were formerly oppressed by [[Evanora the Wicked Witch of the East|the Wicked Witch of the East]], but were freed from her tyranny by [[Dorothy Gale]] when her double-wide trailer crushes her to death. [http://muppet.wikia.com/wiki/Tattypoo Tattypoo] is the protector and friend of the Munchkin Field Mice, dropping in frequently for visits and rounds of cuddles. [[Glinda the Good Witch of the South|Glinda]] also drops in from time to time, though she seems to have a more distant relationship to them, displaying at best tolerance rather than affection.\n\n===''[[Oz the Great and Powerful]]''===\nIn ''Oz the Great and Powerful'', the Munchkins live in fear of the [[Evanora the Wicked Witch of the East|Wicked Witch of the East]] and the [[Theodora the Wicked Witch of the West|Wicked Witch of the West]], but later gain hope as [[Oscar Diggs]] drives them out of the [[Emerald City]].\n\n===''[[Once Upon a Time]]''===\n[[File:Once Upon a Time - 5x16 - Our Decay - All So Fond.png|thumb|250x250px]]\nThe Munchkins are first mentioned when [[Theodora the Wicked Witch of the West|Zelena]] see's [[Sneezy]] and mistakes him for one. They make their first appearance in the episode \"[[Our Decay]]\". Having informed a now adult [[Dorothy Gale]] of Zelena having taken over [[Land of Oz|Oz]], she arrives to stop her. Zelena, in retaliation, visits them to find out who informed Dorothy she was alive. A Munchkin named Boq speaks out which causes Zelena to use her powers to turn him into ash and she then threatens the others before [[Hades]] arrives. Revealing his identity to the Munchkins as the Lord of the Underworld cause them to flee.\n\n===''[[Phineas and Ferb]]''===\nIn the episode \"[[Wizard of Odd]]\", the Munchkins are parodied as the '''Patchkins''', portrayed by the [[Fireside Girls]].\n\n==Disney Parks==\n===''[[The Great Movie Ride]]''===\nThe Munchkins appear in the beginning of the Wizard of Oz scene during The Great Movie Ride in [[Disney's Hollywood Studios]] at [[Walt Disney World]] in Orlando, [[Florida]].\n\n{{Oz}}\n{{Once Upon a Time}}\n[[Category:Character groups]]\n[[Category:Singing characters]]\n[[Category:Live-action characters]]\n[[Category:Oz characters]]\n[[Category:Character stubs]]\n[[Category:Once Upon a Time characters]]\n[[Category:Males]]\n[[Category:Females]]\n[[Category:Return to Oz characters]]\n[[Category:Oz the Great and Powerful characters]]\n[[Category:Disney characters]]\n[[Category:The Muppets characters]]\n[[Category:Characters in Disney parks]]"
