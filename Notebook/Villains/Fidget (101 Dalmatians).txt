He is one of the average looking pups in the litter and is quite hard to distinguish among the others. His name comes from the way he itches and scratches himself, due to fleas. He and his siblings were kidnapped by [[Jasper and Horace]] for [[Cruella De Vil|Cruella]] and brought to [[Hell Hall|De Vil Manor]] to be made into a fur coat. With the help of his family, he escapes Cruella along with his siblings.\n\nFidget is very close to his sister, [[Jewel]], as seen in promotional artwork of the two playing together.\n\nAccording to the December [[1996]] magazine issue of ''[[Disney Adventures]]'', Fidget is a [[Wikipedia:Heterochromia iridum|heterochromiac]], meaning that one of his eyes is brown while the other is blue.\n\n===''[[101 Dalmatians: The Series]]''===\nFidget did not physically appear in the series; however, he has been mentioned on numerous occasions.\n*His name appears on a picnic basket in \"[[The Dogs of DeVil]]\".\n*His name also appears on a photo reel in \"[[Frisky Business]]\".\n*Cruella verbally names him in \"[[The Making of...]]\"\n\n==Gallery==\n<gallery captionalign=\"center\" spacing=\"small\" position=\"center\">\n\nFidget.png|Fidget in \"live-action\" film\n5835956070 968a3bbf48.jpg|Mattel Fidget plush toy\nFidget plush.jpg\nFisget.jpg\nFidget 3.jpg\nFidget 2.jpg\n\n</gallery>\n\n{{101 Dalmatians}}\n[[Category:Dogs]]\n[[Category:Males]]\n[[Category:Kids]]\n[[Category:Siblings]]\n[[Category:Pets]]\n[[Category:Disney characters]]\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:Live-action characters]]\n[[Category:101 Dalmatians characters]]\n[[Category:Characters introduced in remakes]]"
