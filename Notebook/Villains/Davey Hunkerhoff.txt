[[Riley Daring]] replaced a dorky older lifeguard with Davey and pretended to be in love with him in order to make her crush [[Johnny Hitswell]] jealous.\n\nHowever, Davey had fallen in love with Riley and was saddened to learn that she was just using him to make Johnny jealous and quit his job as lifeguard and the pool is locked up. All the kids including Johnny were mad at Riley for this and after some urging from [[Todd Daring|Todd]], Riley sets out to find Davey. She finds him serving as lifeguard to a paddling pool.
