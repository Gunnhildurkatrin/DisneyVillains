"{{Infobox_character\n|image = Dr JL Baterista.jpg\n|rides = [[Mystic Manor]]\n|model = Joe Lanzisero\n|occupation = Jungle Explorer (presumed)\n|affiliations = The [[Society of Explorers and Adventurers]]<br>Captain [[Mary Oceaneer]]<br>Lord [[Henry Mystic]]\n|friends = S.E.A. and its members}}'''Dr. J.L. Baterista''' is a character affiliated with [[Disney Parks]]' ''[[Society of Explorers and Adventurers]]''.\n\n==History==\n\n===Background===\nDr. J.L. Baterista was a member of the Society of Explorers and Adventurers in the late-nineteenth and early-twentieth century. In 1899, he appeared alongside [[Harrison Hightower III]], Lord [[Henry Mystic]], Captain [[Mary Oceaneer]] and [[Maestro D'Elfman]]. In 1906, he went on a river journey along the Congo.\n\n===Developmental History===\nDr. J.L. Baterista was created for the attraction [[Mystic Manor]] as a member of SEA. He was modelled after [[Joe Lanzisero]] who was the Senior Vice President of Walt Disney Imagineering when the ride was being developed. \" J.L.\" are made to represent Lanzisero's initials.\n\n==Appearances==\n\n===Mystic Manor===\nTwo portrait appearances of the doctor are visible in Mystic Manor. One is a profile portrait of him, while the other shows him at an 1899 S.E.A. meeting.\n\n===[[Disney Cruise Line]]===\nA hat in Mary Oceaneer's collection is slated as coming from Baterista's collection and was an apparent gift to Mary.\n\n===[[The Tropical Hideaway]]===\nAn oar commemorating Baterista's 1906 journey on the Congo is on display at the Tropical Hideaway.\n\n==Trivia==\n*The name \u2018baterista\u2019 means drummer in Portuguese.\n\n{{Society of Explorers and Adventurers}}\n[[Category:Theme park characters]]\n[[Category:Society of Explorers and Adventurers]]\n[[Category:Doctors]]\n[[Category:Males]]\n[[Category:The Haunted Mansion characters]]\n[[Category:Disney characters]]\n[[Category:Adults]]\n[[Category:Heroes]]"
