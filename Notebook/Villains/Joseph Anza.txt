"[[File:Char_32185.jpg|thumb]]\n'''Joseph Anza'''<ref>http://www.imdb.com/character/ch0395118/</ref> is a member of the Safety Patroller and the force's data gatherer, grounds patroller, and bodyguard. He typically appears as a background character in most episodes of ''[[Fillmore!]]'' He is voiced by [[wikipedia:Danny Tamberelli|Danny Tamberelli]].<ref>http://www.imdb.com/name/nm0848543/</ref><ref>http://www.behindthevoiceactors.com/tv-shows/Fillmore/Joseph-Anza/</ref>\n\n==History==\nLittle is known about his background, other than he's of Italian descent and is the muscle of his team, and organization. He is partnered with [[Karen Tehama]] and known for his bodyguarding training. He is Italian-American.
