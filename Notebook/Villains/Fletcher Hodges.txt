"{{Infobox_character|image=Fletcher Hodges.webp|inspiration=Fletcher Hodges Jr. (1905-2006)|affiliations=The Adventurers Club|rides=[[Adventurers Club]]<br>\n[[Trader Sam's]]|occupation=Curator of the Adventurers Club|paraphernalia=Skull cane}}\n\n'''Prof. Fletcher Hodges''' is a character from the [[Adventurers Club]] of [[Pleasure Island (Downtown Disney)|Pleasure Island]].\n\n== History ==\nIn the early 20th century, Fletcher Hodges was an esteemed but absent-minded professor of antiques and archaeology.  By around [[1932]], he became a member of a group called The Adventurers Club which made its headquarters on Pleasure Island in [[Lake Buena Vista]], [[Florida]].  Under club founder and [[Society of Explorers and Adventurers|SEA]] member [[Merriweather Adam Pleasure]], Hodges managed the club's collection of rare and supernatural antiques whim the professor claiming to have mounted every artifact in their collection.\n\n== Appearances ==\n\n=== [[Disneyland]] ===\n\n==== [[Trader Sam's|Trader Sam's Enchanted Tiki Bar]] ====\nIn this bar is a message sent from Fletcher to one Trader Sam via the Jungle Navigation Company.
