"[[File:Guardian_Dog.png|thumb|200px|Guardian Dog]]The '''Guardian Dog''' is a character involved in the play ''[http://clubpenguin.wikia.com/wiki/Secrets_of_the_Bamboo_Forest Secrets of the Bamboo Forest]\u00a0''in [[Club Penguin]]. He is a dog that guards the castle, and only lets [http://clubpenguin.wikia.com/wiki/Penguins penguins] who have the [http://clubpenguin.wikia.com/wiki/Feather_Pin Feather Pin] in. He is also [http://clubpenguin.wikia.com/wiki/Phoenix_Queen Phoenix Queen]'s guard.
