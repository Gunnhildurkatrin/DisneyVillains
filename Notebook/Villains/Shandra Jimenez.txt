"{{Infobox character\n|image = Shandra.png\n|shows = ''[[Gravity Falls]]''\n|voice = [[Kari Wahlgren]]\n|appearance = Tall, tan skin, brown hair, black eyes\n|alignment = Neutral, later good\n|home = [[Gravity Falls, Oregon]]\n|occupation = News reporter}}'''Shandra Jimenez''' is a supporting character in the TV animated series ''[[Gravity Falls]]''. She is the town's news reporter, whom [[Toby Determined]] has a huge crush on.\n\n==Background==\n===Physical appearance===\nShe is a tall, tan woman with reddish-brown hair. She wears a light blue shirt with a slightly darker blue vest and a black skirt that goes down to her knees. She has very high, very distinct cheekbones, a 'beauty mark' mole on her face, and wears dark pink lipstick. She usually carries a microphone in her hand.\n\n==Role in the series==\nShandra makes her debut in season 1 episode \"[[Headhunters]]\". She is present at [[Stan Pines|Stan Pine's]] grand reopening of the [[Mystery Shack]] wax museum and asks him if he will give them free pizza as promised.\n\nIt is later revealed that Toby Determined has a crush on her and owns a cardboard cutout of her, which he sometimes makes out with.\n\nIn \"[[The Hand That Rocks the Mabel]]\", she is mentioned by Toby when [[Li'l Gideon|Gideon Gleeful]] gave him Shandra's phone number as part of a deal to get back at [[Dipper Pines|Dipper]].\n\nIn \"[[Boyz Crazy]]\", Shandra is shown on TV reporting on Ergman Bratsman's arrest, describing how he was pulled over and arrested for not having a rear license plate.\n\nHer latest appearance is in the season finale, \"[[Gideon Rises]]\".
