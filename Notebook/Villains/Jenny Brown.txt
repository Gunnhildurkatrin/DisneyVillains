Jenny says she doesn't like persimmon. She likes pigeons and likes the idea of being [[Candace Flynn|Candace]]'s best friend.\n===Physical appearance===\nJenny has long brown hair decorated by a white daisy. Her usual clothing consists of a yellow turtleneck sweater, a red skirt decorated with wavy orange lines, yellow socks, sandals with orange straps and an orange Peace Sign necklace.\n==Appearances==\n===''[[Phineas and Ferb]]''===\nJenny is a somewhat\u00a0close friend of Candace as well as [[Stacy Hirano|Stacy]], having been seen hanging out with or conversing with them over the phone from time to time. She first appeared in the episode \"[[Lawn Gnome Beach Party of Terror]]\" when she took an interest in [[Phineas Flynn|Phineas]] and [[Ferb Fletcher|Ferb]]'s activities when they built an [[Backyard Beach|artificial beach]] in their backyard and came over to spend the day there with [[Stacy Hirano]]. She appears to own and care for a dog with a resemblance to a [[wikipedia:Komondor|Komondor]]. She also appears in other episodes helping or hanging with Candace.\n\n===''[[Phineas and Ferb the Movie: Across the 2nd Dimension]]''===\nJenny plays a role in the film, during the [[Heinz Doofenshmirtz (2nd Dimension)|second dimension Doofenshmirtz's]] invasion when she took control of the Candace Crusher to assist in defense against the attack.\n\n==Trivia==\n*She is named after series creator [[Jeff \"Swampy\" Marsh]]'s daughter, Jenny.\n*[[Dan Povenmire]] revealed that she is the sister of Django Brown.<ref name=\"Correspondence\">Marekos1996/Dan Povenmire Correspondence on the Phineas and Ferb wiki</ref>\n\n==Gallery==\n<gallery spacing=\"small\" position=\"center\" widths=\"150\" captionalign=\"center\">\nJenny.png|promotional artwork image of Jenny\nJennyInTears.png\nCandace Gets Busted (2).jpg\nJenny Monster Truck A2SD.JPG\nCandace, Stacy and Jenny singing.jpg\nThePhineasAndFerb-Busters.png\nCandace Gets Busted (4).jpg\nCandace Gets Busted (5).jpg\nDay of the Living Gelatin.jpg\nStacy Candace and Jenny You're Going Down.jpg\nJenny dog.jpg\nSummer Belongs to you (2).jpg\nSummer Belongs to you (5).jpg\nSummer Belongs to you (7).jpg\nSBTY Closing Cast Shot.jpg\nStacy and Jenny ask what he wants.JPG\nCandace's Party.JPG\nSwafggh.jpg\nBreath.jpg\nJenny and Stacy.png\nPhineas ferb jenny brown.gif\n</gallery>\n\n==References==\n<references/>\n{{Phineas and Ferb}}\n{{PF-Wiki}}\n[[es:Jenny (Phineas and Ferb)]]\n{{DEFAULTSORT:Brown, Jenny}}\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:Phineas and Ferb characters]]\n[[Category:Females]]\n[[Category:Teenagers]]\n[[Category:Singing characters]]\n[[Category:Disney characters]]\n[[Category:Siblings]]\n[[Category:Heroines]]\n[[Category:American characters]]\n[[Category:Lovers]]\n[[Category:TV Animation characters]]\n[[Category:Animated characters]]\n[[Category:Disney Channel characters]]"
