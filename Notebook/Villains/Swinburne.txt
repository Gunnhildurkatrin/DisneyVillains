He forced Miss Price, Professor Brown, [[Charlie Rawlins|Charlie]], [[Carrie Rawlins|Carrie]], and [[Paul Rawlins|Paul]] to come with him to see the Bookman by holding a knife on Professor Brown. He joined the Bookman on trying to get Paul's book, implying they would do so by violence.
