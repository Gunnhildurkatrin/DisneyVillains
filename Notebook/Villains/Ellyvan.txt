"{{Infobox_character\n|name = Ellyvan\n|image = Ellyvan.png\n|shows = ''[[Jungle Junction]]''\n|voice = [[Billy West]]\n|personality = Cheery, nice, friendly, caring, kind, funny\n|appearance = Large blue elephant with wheels\n|occupation = Delivery elephant\n|home = Jungle Junction\n|friends = [[Zooter]], [[Bungo]], Taxicrab, Miss Jolly, The Beetlebugs, Toadhog, Carla, Lance, Hippobus, Bobby, Dozer\n|powers = Sucks improbable quantities of liquids and sprays them far\n|alignment = Good}}'''Ellyvan''' is one of the main characters and the best friend of [[Zooter]] from the [[Playhouse Disney]] (now [[Disney Junior]]) show ''[[Jungle Junction]]''. He is voiced by [[Billy West]].\n\n==Biography==\nEllyvan carries the deliveries around the jungle. His name is a compound word of \"elephant\" and \"van.\" He is able to suck improbable quantities of liquids up his trunk and spray them distances of up to a half a mile. He lives in the base of a big hollow tree some distance out of town. Ellyvan is very naive, and throws himself into any situation, especially if there is food involved. Ellyvan is a cheery and optimistic character, and rarely becomes upset.\n\n==Appearance==\nEllyvan is a blue elephant with a US accent.
