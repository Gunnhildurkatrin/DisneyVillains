Afterward, she is seen on the train and then walks off to Goofy\u2019s car. And after that happens, Goofy seems to accidentally keep embarrassing Max by showing Mona Max\u2019s baby pictures. Mona is seen making popcorn with Goofy while Max takes a walk outside. When Max returns to the house, Goofy and Mona make popcorn, but Goofy puts too much in the popcorn machine.
