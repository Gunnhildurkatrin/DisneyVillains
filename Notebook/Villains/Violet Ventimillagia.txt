She has a big crush on\u00a0[[Fletcher Quimby]]. She is a recurring character and is known to have anger issues. She has unmatched strength, which gives her keen talent in sports such as football, baseball, basketball, ice-hockey, lacrosse and formerly ultimate fighting. Violet is mainly known for her quick-temper, dry-wit, and abrasive comments.\n\n==Personality==\nViolet has an aggressive, fearless, and blunt exterior. While she harbors her real feelings underneath, particularly her romantic feelings for\u00a0Fletcher.
