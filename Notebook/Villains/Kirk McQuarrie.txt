"{{Infobox character|image = Kirk.jpg|shows = [[The Weekenders]]|voice = Jason Marsden|alignment = Good|family = Lor}}'''Kirk McQuarrie''' (voiced by [[Jason Marsden]]) is a secondary character from [[The Walt Disney Company|Disney]]'s animated series ''[[The Weekenders]]''.
