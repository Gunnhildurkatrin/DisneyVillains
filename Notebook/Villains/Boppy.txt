"{{Infobox character\n|image = File:Doc-McStuffins03.jpeg\n|shows = ''[[Doc McStuffins]]''<br>''[[The Doc Files]]''\n|voice = [[James Arnold Taylor]]\n|personality = Kind, friendly, gentle, self-confident \n|alignment = Good\n|likes = Getting bopped\n|dislikes = thorns, being flat.\n}}'''Boppy''' is a dog-shaped punching bag from the [[Disney Junior]] series ''[[Doc McStuffins]]''. He is voiced by [[James Arnold Taylor]].\n==Role in the series==\nBoppy is generally self-confident and kind-hearted but he doesn't like being injured. In \"[[A Bad Case of the Pricklethorns]]\", Boppy is quickly taken in by [[Dottie McStuffins|Doc]] to get healed, and Doc finds a hole in him and puts a band-aid on him, then she gives him a quick check-up. But when Boppy goes back out, he gets another hole, and Doc quickly puts another band-aid on him. [[Stuffy]] tells her how Boppy got his second injury and Doc finds out that he got popped by a rose thorn and calls her diagnosis the 'Pricklethorns'.
