"{{Infobox character\n|image = Braver5.jpg\n|shows = ''[[Adventures of the Gummi Bears]]''\n|voice = [[Michael Rye]]\n|occupation = Knight\n|home = [[Dunwyn Castle]]\n|paraphernalia = Silver axe\n|alias = [[King Gregor]]\n}}\nThe '''Mysterious Knight''' is a character and perceived antagonist in the ''[[Adventures of the Gummi Bears]]'' episode \"[[Girl's Knight Out]]\".\n\n==History==\nThe Knight is seen protecting the Golden Apple. He is the major foe when the squires compete to protect the [[Princess Calla|princess]]. Little do any of them know that the Mysterious Knight is really [[King Gregor]] in disguise.\n\nAs it turns out, his most formidable opponent during this competition turns out to be Princess Calla herself, who enters in disguise to avoid being shackled with a protector.\n\nOnce Gregor learns the of whole situation, he is deeply impressed at how formidable his daughter has become, and learned to defend herself from those that seek to harm her.\n\n<gallery widths=190 spacing=small position=center captionalign=center>\nScreen Shot 2014-10-21 at 5.54.17 pm.png\nScreen Shot 2014-10-21 at 5.54.26 pm.png\n</gallery>\n\n{{Adventures of the Gummi Bears}}\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:Adventures of the Gummi Bears characters]]\n[[Category:Animated characters]]\n[[Category:TV Animation characters]]\n[[Category:Males]]\n[[Category:Disney characters]]\n[[Category:Knights]]\n[[Category:The Disney Afternoon characters]]\n[[Category:Alternate Forms]]\n[[Category:Villains]]\n[[Category:Anti-villains]]"
