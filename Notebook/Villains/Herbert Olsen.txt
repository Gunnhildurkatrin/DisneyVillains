"[[File:Grandba_olsen.png|thumb]]'''Herbert Olsen ''' is a character in the Disney [[Italy|Italian]] comic ''[[W.I.T.C.H.]]'' and [[Matt Olsen|Matt]]'s grandfather. He acts as a father figure to Matt. He is very kind but seems to not be very organized. He owns a pet shop and he gave Will a part-time job at his shop. In \"[[M is for Mercy]]\", he was attacked by Shagon. In the television series of ''[[W.I.T.C.H.
