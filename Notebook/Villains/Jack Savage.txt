He was supposed to be the main character in the film before [[Judy Hopps|Judy]] was chosen for the final version of the film.\n\n==Background==\n===Physical Appearance===\nJack was a rabbit with grey fur and black stripes. He had a spy suit, in a nod to espionage films such as ''James Bond''.\n\n===Personality===\nIt is assumed he was a serious character.\n\n===Development===\nDuring early development of the film, Jack was supposed to leave the city of [[Zootopia]] under the command of his boss where he would explore a world of dangerous animals. This led to Byron Howard's idea of films about rabbit spies. During development pitch, the idea for a modernized all-animal city in the first act, as the city was to be the film's setting.
