"{{Infobox character\n|name = Mummy Boy\n|image = Mummy Boy.jpg\n|films = ''[[The Nightmare Before Christmas]]''\n|inspiration = Egyptian mummy\n|appearance = Short mummy, white bandages wrap around the body, one eye\n|personality = Friendly, funny\n|alignment = Good\n|occupation = Halloween Town resident\n|friends = [[Winged Demon]], [[Corpse Family|Corpse Kid]]\n|enemies = [[Oogie Boogie]]}}'''Mummy Boy\u00a0'''is a minor character from ''[[The Nightmare Before Christmas]]''.\n\n==Background==\n\n===Physical Description===\nMummy Boy appears to be short with white bandages wrapped around his entire body, having a single eye on his forehead.\n\n===Personality===\nHe is a friendly mummy with a good sense of humor.\n\n==Appearances==\n\n===''[[The Nightmare Before Christmas]]''===\nThe Mummy Boy is a friend of the [[Winged Demon]] and [[Corpse Family|Corpse Kid]] who celebrates Halloween with the other citizens of Halloween Town. He can be seen while\u00a0[[Jack Skellington]] is talking about Christmas and also helps the other [[Halloween Town]] residents in making the haunted Christmas gifts during the song \"[[Making Christmas]]\". He is later seen with the other citizens of Halloween Town celebrating Jack's return after Jack rescues Santa from Oogie Boogie and helps him save Christmas.
