"{{NeedImage}}{{Infobox character\n|shows = ''[[Fillmore!]]''\n|voice = [[Michael Welch]]}}'''The Young Artist''' (real name unknown) is an X Middle School student and kind of artist that paints and sketches, to be more specific. He is voiced by [[Michael Welch]].\n==Physical Appearance==\nHe's a caucasian, blind boy with brown hair, wears a black cap, shades, a white, V-neck shirt, jeans, and white shoes.\n\n== History ==\nIn [[Field Trip of the Just]], when [[Cornelius Fillmore|Fillmore]] and [[Ingrid Third|Ingrid]] follow [[Eric Orben]] outside the school while pursuing him, he steals away the young artist's miniature horse, Jules, which is used for guidance and refers to it as his dog. In a running gag, the young artist corrects those who call Jules as such.
