She and the elephant ballerinas represent the dusk during the middle part when Hyacinth Hippo takes a nap in the [[Dance of the Hours]] segment.\n\n==Appearances==\n\n===''[[Fantasia]]''===\nElephanchine first appears along with her elephant troupe, while [[Hyacinth Hippo]] is sleeping. She and the other elephants dance and blow some bubbles along with her who notices a fish in one of them; after a while, a huge wind blows them away. Elephanchine and the other elephants appear in the finale when the [[Alligators]] chase the [[Hippos]], [[Ostriches]], and the Elephants themselves around and fall in love with each other.\n\n==Trivia==\n*Elephanchine's name\u00a0was never revealed in the movie.\n*Her name is most likely a parody of ballet choreographer George Balanchine.\n\n==Gallery==\n<gallery navigation=\"true\" position=\"center\">\nfantasia-disneyscreencaps_com-11499.jpg|link=http://disneyscreencaps.com/fantasia-1940/\nfantasia-disneyscreencaps_com-11505.jpg\nfantasia-disneyscreencaps_com-11540.jpg\nTumblr nklw1f8D9F1r3jmn6o1 1280.png\nfantasia-disneyscreencaps_com-12230.jpg\nfantasia-disneyscreencaps_com-12231.jpg\nfantasia-disneyscreencaps_com-12232.jpg\nfantasia-disneyscreencaps_com-12233.jpg\nVlcsnap-2015-07-18-10h50m25s003.png\nVlcsnap-2015-07-18-10h50m37s787.png\nVlcsnap-2015-07-18-10h50m56s207.png\nFantasia .jpg|Elephanchine in the Parks \n</gallery>\n\n==External Links==\n{{Fantasia}}\n[[Category:Disney characters]]\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:Elephants]]\n[[Category:Females]]\n[[Category:Fantasia characters]]\n[[Category:Fantasmic characters]]\n[[Category:Dancers]]\n[[Category:Silent characters]]\n[[Category:Characters in the Disney animated features canon]]\n[[Category:Wonderful World of Color]]\n[[Category:Mickey Mouse (TV series) characters]]"
