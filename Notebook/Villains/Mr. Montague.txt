He is the owner of \"Montague Hotel\", the rival hotel of the one where the film took place.\n\n==Role in the film==\n\nAt the beginning, it was mentioned by Hollis, the hotel manager that the \"Montague Hotel\" was bad for his business. Later, he was seen talking with Jenny behind the gate about telling her to steal an important document from the office; afterwards, he paid her. In the climax, after [[Jenny (Beverly Hills Chihuahua)|Jenny]]'s defeat, he was seen being dangled under a tree by [[Delgado]] with a rope.
