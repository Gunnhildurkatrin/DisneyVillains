Both are often seen together throughout the show, carrying out their duties as guards. The duo are recognized by [[Corona]]'s [[Captain of the Guards]] as his top men.<ref>According to \"[[Great Expotations]]\"</ref> As such, though they provide a fair amount of comedy to each episode, both guards are competent and efficient when carrying out their duties.\n==Background==\n===Physical Appearances===\nBoth Pete and Stan wear standard Corona Guard uniforms. Pete is shorter and skinnier than Stan by a few inches. He has light dark blue eyes and freckles on each cheek. Stan is a few inches taller than Pete with more body build figure. He has dark brown eyes and a handlebar mustache.\n\n==Trivia==\n*Besides the Captain of the Guards, [[Maximus]] also has authority over the duo.<ref>As seen in ''[[Tangled: Before Ever After]]''</ref>\n*Pete has a fear of mimes.<ref>Mentioned by Eugene in \"[[What the Hair?!]]\"</ref>\n*Stan is married, and was introduced to his wife by Corona's local candy man, [[Monty (Tangled)|Monty]].<ref>Mentioned in \"[[Rapunzel's Enemy]]\"</ref>\n*Stan may be illiterate, as he spelled \"sweet\" as \"sweat\" when drawing a map to Monty's shop for Rapunzel in \"[[Rapunzel's Enemy]]\".\n*Pete dislikes quiches.<ref>Mentioned by Stan in \"[[In Like Flynn]]\"</ref>\n*Both Pete and Stan's middle names are \"the\".\n*When they were 10 years younger, Pete had a mustache and Stan didn't in \"[[No Time Like the Past]]\".\n*Pete has a cat named Mr Fluffy Paws.<ref>Mentioned in \"[[The Alchemist Returns]]\"</ref>\n\n==Gallery==\n<gallery position=\"center\" widths=\"150\" spacing=\"small\" captionsize=\"small\" captionalign=\"center\">\nRapunzel's Enemy 14.jpg\nRapunzel's Enemy -
