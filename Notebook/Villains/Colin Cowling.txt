He got his big break\u2014so to speak\u2014when his Eye-In-The-Sky-In-Sports weekly recap show was noticed by producers of Racing Sports Network (RSN).''<ref name=\"sk\">[http://www.stitchkingdom.com/disney-planes-official-character-descriptions-casting-character-poses-hires-61766/ 'Planes' Official Character Descriptions, Casting and Character Poses (Hi-Res)]</ref>\n\n==Appearances==\n===''[[Planes]]''===\nThroughout the film, Colin acts as the co-commentator of the Wings Around the Globe Rally alongside Brent Mustangburger, mentioning about how [[Ripslinger]] is hoping to win his fourth title, and the first time a crop duster, [[Dusty Crophopper]], is competing in the Wings Around the Globe. During the final race, Colin announces another victory for Ripslinger being inevitable, though he later witnesses Dusty Crophopper in second place trying to approach the finish line as he finally overcomes his fear of heights and defeats Ripslinger to win his first title.\n\n==Video games==\n===''[[Planes: The Video Game]]''===\nColin is not seen, but is heard commentating some of the races that the Wings Around the Globe racers participate in.\n\n==Trivia==\n*Colin is an Aaron Fullogas.
