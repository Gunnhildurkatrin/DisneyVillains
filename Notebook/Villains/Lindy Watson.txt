She is the twin sister of [[Logan Watson]].\n\n\n\n==Summary==\nLindy is Logan's twin sister. When something goes wrong they both try to explain what happened, hence why the name of the series is ''I Didn't Do It.'' Lindy has evolved from a scrawny sarcastic kid to a very attractive girl, and boys are taking notice. Her best friend Jasmine helps her keep being grounded after her newfound popularity.<ref>[http://ididntdoitnews.blogspot.co.uk/2013/07/lindy-watson-character-introduction.html?m=1 Lindy Watson - Biography]</ref>\n\n==Personality==\nLindy grew up geeky, nerdy, an outcast and on the athletic side.
