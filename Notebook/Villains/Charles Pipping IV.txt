"{{Infobox character\n|name=Charles Pipping IV\n|image= Charles.jpg\n|pets=\n|voice=Dominic Wood\n||shows = ''[[Phineas and Ferb]]''}}'''Charles Pipping IV''' is a boy [[Candace Flynn|Candace]] meets in [[England]]. She develops a crush on him. Charles is oblivious to it, but is nice to Candace. He has a British accent that Candace adores. Interestingly, Charles bears a remarkable similarity to Candace's normal crush [[Jeremy Johnson]]. He has a good sense of humor.
