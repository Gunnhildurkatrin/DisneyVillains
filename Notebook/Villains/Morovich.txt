"{{Infobox character\n|image = Morovich.gif\n|name = Morovich\n|films = ''[[Condorman]]''\n|actor = Jean-Pierre Kalfon\n|occupation = Leader of the Prognovieh\n|goal = Capture Natalia and kill Woody\n|friends = Krokov\n|minions = Pursuit squadron\n|enemies = [[Woodrow Wilkins]], [[Natalia Rambova]], Harry\n|weapons = Sniper rifle, handgun\n|fate = Blows up after crashing into a cliff\n|personality = Ruthless, cold\n|appearance = Bark hair, glass left eye, dresses in black, black eye patch}}'''Morovich''' is one of the main antagonists in the film ''[[Condorman]]''.\n\n==Background==\nMorovich is the leader of the K.G.B. pursuit squadron, the Prognovieh who is assigned by Krokov to return [[Natalia Rambova]] to him as she is trying to defect to the U.S.A. He is known the be fearsome, ruthless and known to have a glass eye which he sometimes covers with an eye patch.\n\nHis first attempts to capture her fail, since she's being escorted by [[Woodrow Wilkins]] a comic book writer.\n\nHe succeeds in his third attempt and nearly kills Woody and Harry in the process. When Woody and Harry rescue Natalia he sends is squadron after them as they attempt to escape by boat.
