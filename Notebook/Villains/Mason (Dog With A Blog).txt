He is portrayed by Griffin Kunitz.\n\n==Personality==\nMason is a kind and nice boy who Chloe made to be his girlfriend. He is also very absent-minded with Chloe. His personality resembles that of Bennett. He doesn't really understand how relationships work, as he has playdates with other girls.\n\n==History==\nChloe makes friends with a boy named Mason from her school and then becomes his girlfriend.
