He was voiced by [[Brian Cummings]].\n\n\n==General Description==\n===Physical appearance===\nHalf bumblebee and half-lion, Bumblelion is mostly lion in appearance. He is a short, squat orange furred creature with a pink mane, fuzzy antennae, a lion's tail, small insect wings, and horizontal brown stripes up his tummy.\n===Personality===\nHe lives in a beehive, likes sports, is courageous, and has a crush on [[Butterbear]]. He is said to be the sort who \"... rush in where angels fear to tread\".
