Graft\n|likes = Baseball, boxing, sports, reading, having fun\n|alliance = Good\n}}'''Grandma Goofy''', also known as '''Granny''', is [[Goofy]]'s elderly yet energetic grandmother, who is a dedicated sports fan.\n== Appearances ==\n=== Comics ===\nGrandma Goofy made her first appearance in a [[1944]] ''Mickey Mouse'' Sunday page by Hubie Karp and Bill Wright. Her first speaking appearance was in [[1955]], in the daily ''Mickey Mouse'' newspaper strips which formed the 120-page counting comic''''' '''Dr. X''. \n[[File:Hr (2).png|left|thumb|220x220px|Grandma Goofy, as drawn by [[Floyd Gottfredson]] in ''Dr. X''.]]Goofy's grandma later made an appearance in ''[[Super Goof]]: The Granny Hang-Up''.<ref>https://coa.inducks.org/story.php?c=W+SG+++42-01</ref> Goofy's living room is adorned with pictures of his beloved grandmother. After her grandson calls her for help, she single-handedly defeats a bank robber using her umbrella.  \nGrandma Goofy had a prominent role in the ''Mickey Mouse'' comic <nowiki/>''Topolino e l'insolita indagine ''(\"Mickey and the Unusual Investigation\"),'' ''<nowiki/>in which [[Mickey Mouse |Mickey]] doesn't seem too pleased with her presence.\n\nWhen Goofy was young, he and [[Clarabelle Cow|Clarabelle]] once borrowed some of his Grandma's clothing to disguise themselves so that they could spy on their opponent's soccer team without being noticed, as mentioned in ''Paperino e Topolino in: la finale, finalmente'' (\"Donald and Mickey: The Final, Finally\"). In this story, Goofy also mentions that his grandma is a big fan of F.C. Duckburg, [[Duckburg]]'s local soccer team.\n\n=== ''[[Mickey Mouse (TV series)|Mickey Mouse]]'' ===\nGrandma Goofy made her first animated appearance (eventually being revealed to be Goofy in disguise) in \"[[Goofy's Grandma]]\", the twenty-fourth episode of ''[[Mickey Mouse (TV series)|Mickey Mouse]]'', where she was voiced by [[Bill Farmer]].\n\n=== ''[[The Wonderful World of Mickey Mouse]]'' ===\nIn \"[[The Big Good Wolf (The Wonderful World of Mickey Mouse)|The Big Good Wolf]]\", Grandma Goofy made a cameo on portrait in [[Goofy]]'s house.\n\n== Trivia ==\n*Although her first name is unknown in the original version, she's called '''Goofine''' in German.\n*Though unseen, Goofy mentions his '''Granmammy''' in the ''[[Goof Troop]]'' episode \"[[Tub Be or Not Tub Be]]\" as having a recipe for a saltwater taffy so sticky that once one has sunk their teeth into it, they \"won't get them out for days.\"\n\n==Gallery==\n<gallery>\nGrannyGoofy.png\nGrandma_goofy_(Mickey_Mouse).jpg|Grandma Goofy as she appeared in \"[[Goofy's Grandma]]\"\nGoofy_and_grandma.jpg\n2014-mickeyS2-06.jpg\nF5f038044e64cc27bcc83006182f8b3e0f7e2b5d.jpg\nGoofy's_grandma_concern.jpg\nGoofy's Grandma with Mickey and Donald.jpg\nNonnaPeppa.png\n6787406964850650785-IV.jpg|Goofy, disguised as his grandmother.\nSuper Goof 42 11.jpg\nSuper Goof 42 12.jpg\n</gallery>\n\n==References<nowiki/>==\n{{Reflist}}{{DEFAULTSORT:Goofy, Grandma}}\n\n{{Mickey Mouse (TV series)}}\n[[Category:Disney characters]]\n[[Category:Dogs]]\n[[Category:Females]]\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:Comic characters]]\n[[Category:Characters introduced in comics]]\n[[Category:Mickey Mouse universe characters]]\n[[Category:Adults]]\n[[Category:Grandparents]]\n[[Category:Animated characters]]\n[[Category:Elderly characters]]\n[[Category:Anthropomorphic characters]]\n[[Category:Mickey Mouse (TV series) characters]]\n[[Category:Spouses]]\n[[Category:Parents]]\n[[Category:TV Animation characters]]"
