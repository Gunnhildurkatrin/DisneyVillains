"{{Infobox character\n|name = Mary Lou Wentz\n|image = Mary lou.jpg\n|shows = ''[[Good Luck Charlie]]''\n|parents = [[Harry Wentz]] (husband)\n|children = [[Ivy Wentz]] (daughter)\n|alignment = Good\n|home = Denver, [[Colorado]]\n|friends = [[Amy Duncan]], [[Teddy Duncan]]\n|actor = Elliah English}}\n'''Mary Lou Wentz''' is [[Harry Wentz|Harry]]'s wife and [[Ivy Wentz|Ivy]]'s mother.\n\n==Personality==\nShe doesn't know how to text or use a cell phone. [[Amy Duncan]] becomes her friend and teaches her to do so. She enjoys knitting, reading knitting magazines, of which she stashes in her purse (\"The Curious Case of Mr. Dabney\"), playing jigsaw puzzles, singing and playing the organ (\"Gabe's 12-\u00bd Birthday\" and \"Name That Baby\")\n\nMary Lou was a boring, lethargic character that was addicted to knitting, prior to meeting Amy, who now refers to her as her \"best friend\", though Mary Lou believes that she is of higher social status than her. She is shown to be very protective of her daughter, Ivy, as she often restricts Ivy from going to parties. (\"Appy Days\")\n\nMary Lou is a very good singer and even won a singing contest before.
