She is a fox that loves nature and solving mysteries.\n\n==Background==\n\n===Story===\nOne day, on an outing, [[Mickey Mouse|Mickey]] and [[Duffy the Disney Bear|Duffy]] got separated following butterflies. \n\nA fox passed by, \"What's wrong?\"\n\n\"I can't find my way back\"\n\n\"Hmm. Sticky seed. Butterfly. Orchid. Got it. I can bring you back!\"\n\n\"Wow, you're amazing!\"\n\nExcited to be reunited with Mickey, Duffy said, \"She is really smart!\", and gave her an orchid. \"My name is LinaBell and I love solving mysteries.\"\n\nWhat mysteries will you solve with LinaBell?\n\n== [[Disney Parks, Experiences and Products|Disney Parks]] ==\nLinaBell made her debut at [[Shanghai Disneyland]] on September 29, 2021, being introduced on social media two weeks earlier on the 16th.
