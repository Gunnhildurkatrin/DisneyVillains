"[[File:George2.jpg|thumb|250px]]\n'''Principal Conrad Wexler''' is a character and antagonist in ''[[Even Stevens]]''. He is portrayed by George Anthony Bell.\n\n==History==\nHe is the fun-loving, caring principal of Lawrence Jr. High School in Sacramento, California from the show [[Even Stevens]]. He tries his hardest to be stern (especially with students like [[Louis Stevens]]...), but is generally a softie.
