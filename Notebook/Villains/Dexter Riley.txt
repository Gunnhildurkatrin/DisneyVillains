Every year, he has to help save the college from bankruptcy, through various means of scientific inventions or discoveries.\n\n==Appearances==\n===''[[The Computer Wore Tennis Shoes]]''===\nOne night, he gets struck by lightning while installing a replacement computer part and becomes a human-computer able to absorb vast amounts of information and make incredibly fast calculations.\n\n===''[[Now You See Him, Now You Don't]]''===\nDexter is working on a formula for invisibility, but can't crack it, until a lightning storm strikes the chemicals, creating a working invisibility serum.\n\n===''[[The Strongest Man in the World]]''===\nDexter experiments with a serum to increase the size of a scrawny cow that mixes with a super vitamin experiment of [[Richard Schuyler]]'s.
