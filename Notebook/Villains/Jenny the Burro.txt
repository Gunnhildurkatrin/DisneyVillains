"[[File:Burro.jpg|thumb|250px]]\n'''Jenny the Burro''' is a small burro which [[Donald Duck|Donald]] rides on in the [[1936]] short, ''[[Mickey's Polo Team]]'' and again to seduce [[Donna Duck]] ([[Daisy Duck|Daisy]] in her first appearance) only to later be ignominiously replaced by an automobile in the [[1937]] animated short, ''[[Don Donald]]''. She also appeared in the [[1942]] animated shorts, ''[[The Village Smithy]]'' and ''[[Donald's Gold Mine]]''.
