When Persephone and Sparky first meet in the backyard, the attraction is instantaneous and electrifying.\n==Physical appearance==\nPersephone is a perfectly groomed black poodle with an enormously large poof of hair on her head. When she got electified by Sparky, her hair turned frizzy and gained a white lightning-shaped streak.\n==Role in the film==\nPersephone and Sparky first met when his ball rolled into her backyard. As he tried to get it out, he noticed Persephone trotting around inside the backyard.
