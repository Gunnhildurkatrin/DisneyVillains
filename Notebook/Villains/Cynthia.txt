"[[File:Cynthia.jpg|thumb]]'''Cynthia''' is a background character in the [[Disney XD]]'s series ''[[Randy Cunningham: 9th Grade Ninja]]''.\n\n==Biography==\nShe is a student who attends [[Norrisville High]] School.\n\n==Trivia==\n*She is based on the producer of RC9GN, [[Jackie Buscarino]].\n*We learn her name in \"[[Randy Cunningham and the Sorcerer's Key]]\" when Flute Girl notices that Stevens gave her a pin.\n*Cynthia, [[Heidi Weinerman]], and [[Cass Simonson]] are talking with each other during the montage of Randy wasting [[Smoke Bombs|smoke bombs]]  in \"[[Silent Punch, Deadly Punch]]\".\n*Jackie Buscarino was also part of the crew of Disney Channel's hit series ''[[Gravity Falls]]''.\n*She had her first and so far only speaking role, talking about Howard, in \"[[Gossip Boy]]\", saying \"He saved us from eating chipmunk-changas!\".\n*She is a girl that [[Dickie]] checked and threw because she wasn't Tawny in \"[[Stank'd to the Future]]\".
