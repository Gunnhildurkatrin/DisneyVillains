"{{Infobox_character|image=Samantha Sterling Portrait.webp|rides=[[Adventurers Club]]<br>\n[[Trader Sam's]] (referenced)<br>\n[[Jock Lindsey's Hangar Bar]] (referenced)<br>\nThe Edison (referenced)|affiliations=Adventurers Club|friends=[[Trader Sam]]<br>\n[[Jock Lindsey]]|occupation=Explorer<br>\nSinger|inspiration=Amelia Earhart}}\n\n'''Samantha Sterling''' is a character from [[Pleasure Island (Downtown Disney)|Pleasure Island]].\n\n== History ==\n\n=== Background ===\nIn the early 20th century, Samantha Sterling was a world-renowned explorer and cabaret singer.  By [[1928]] she was enlisted in \"The Adventurers Club\", a group of explorers which was branched off from the ''[[Society of Explorers and Adventurers]]'' by SEA-member [[Merriweather Adam Pleasure]].  She would attend the annual [[December 31|New Year's Eve]] festivities in the club's headquarters on Pleasure Island in Lake Buena Vista and in 1928 was deemed victor of the, \"Balderdash Cup\" for having the best true-life adventure story.\n\nAt some point, Samantha became close with club-affiliate [[Trader Sam]] who she spent five days and ten nights with.  In [[1941]], Pleasure Island was hit by a massive hurricane and subsequently abandoned though the club persisted its operations.  She also became affiliated with S.E.A. aviator [[Jock Lindsey]] who she frequented the bar of in the 1950s.\n\n== [[Disney Parks]] ==\n\n=== [[Disneyland]] ===\n\n==== [[Trader Sam's|Trader Sam's Enchanted Tiki Bar]] ====\nFramed on the wall of this bar is a letter from Samantha to one Trader Sam, a character from the [[Jungle Cruise]].
