Throughout the chase, the herd stampedes across the wilderness to which the three hide behind kayaks as the herd leaves to which Kenai tells the two to never milk one.\n\n==Trivia==\n*The scene where Kenai, Sitka, and Denahi hide behind the kayaks to avoid getting trampled is reminiscent of the tragic stampede scene from ''[[The Lion King]]''.\n\n{{Brother Bear}}\n[[Category:Silent characters]]\n[[Category:Disney characters]]\n[[Category:Males]]\n[[Category:Females]]\n[[Category:Deer]]\n[[Category:Brother Bear characters]]\n[[Category:Animated characters]]\n[[Category:Characters in the Disney animated features canon]]"
