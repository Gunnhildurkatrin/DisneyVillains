She is also the girlfriend of [[Warren Peace]].\n\n==Role in the film==\nFreeze Girl makes a brief appearance throughout the movie. On the first day at Sky High, she is seen entering the school until she uses her cryokinesis powers to freeze two boys in an ice block in retaliation for one of them using his laser vision to zap her on the rear, thus, earning her the nickname (and possible future heroine name) \"Freeze Girl\" from spectators.\n\nAt the end of the movie, she is seen dancing and probably dating [[Warren Peace]], who doesn't have a dance partner, at the Homecoming dance when both discover having opposite powers: Fire and Ice and after [[Will Stronghold]], the heroes, and the sidekicks have defeated the villainous [[Gwen Grayson]], who hosted the Homecoming dance as a trap in the first place, and her henchmen.\n\n==Gallery==\n<gallery position=\"center\">\nsky-high-disneyscreencaps.com-1136.jpg\nsky-high-disneyscreencaps.com-1141.jpg\nsky-high-disneyscreencaps.com-9806.jpg\n</gallery>\n\n==Trivia==\n\n*Her real name is never revealed in the movie.\n*The name \"Freeze Girl\" could be considered as a possible future heroine name she will earn after graduating from Sky High.\n{{Sky High}}\n[[Category:Sky High characters]]\n[[Category:Superheroes]]\n[[Category:Live-action characters]]\n[[Category:Disney characters]]\n[[Category:Females]]\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:Teenagers]]\n[[Category:Lovers]]\n[[Category:Heroines]]\n[[Category:Students]]"
