Watson is [[Sherlock Holmes]]' friend and assistant, and is the first person narrator of all but four stories in the Sherlock Holmes canon. He is a minor character from [[Walt Disney Animation Studios|Disney]]'s [[1986]] animated feature film,\u00a0''[[The Great Mouse Detective]]''.\n\n==Role in the film==\nWatson appears with Sherlock as silhouette cameos in the film.
