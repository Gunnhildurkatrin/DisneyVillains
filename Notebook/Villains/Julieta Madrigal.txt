"{{Upcoming}}{{Infobox character|films=''[[Encanto]]''|voice=Angie Cepeda|home=Casa Madrigal, [[Encanto (location)|Encanto]]|powers=Makes healing food|status=Active|parents=[[Alma Madrigal]] (mother)<br>Pedro Madrigal \u2020 (father)|siblings=[[Pepa Madrigal]] (twin sister)<br>[[Bruno Madrigal]] (twin brother)|family=[[F\u00e9lix Madrigal]] (brother-in-law)<br>[[Dolores Madrigal]] (sororal niece)<br>[[Camilo Madrigal]] (sororal nephew)<br>[[Antonio Madrigal]] (sororal nephew)|children=[[Isabela Madrigal]] (daughter)<br>[[Luisa Madrigal]] (daughter)<br>[[Mirabel Madrigal]] (daughter)|image=Julieta Madrigal.jpg|partner=[[Agust\u00edn Madrigal]] (husband)}}\n\n'''Julieta Madrigal''' is a character of the upcoming [[Walt Disney Animation Studios]] feature film ''[[Encanto]]''.\n\n== Background ==\nJulieta is one of the three children [[Alma Madrigal|Alma]] and Pedro had, alongside her brother [[Bruno Madrigal|Bruno]] and her sister Pepa. Some time later, she married [[Agust\u00edn Madrigal|Agust\u00edn]] and they had three daughters: [[Isabela Madrigal|Isabela]], [[Luisa Madrigal|Luisa]] and [[Mirabel Madrigal|Mirabel]]. Her gift allows her to heal people's wounds. Julieta always supports her younger daughter, Mirabel, because she understands how hard it is for the young girl to be the only ordinary member of a magical family.\n\n=== Physical appearance ===\nJulieta is a middle-aged woman with curly black and gray hair which she wears in a bun and she has hazel eyes. She wears a long-sleeved baby blue dress with a tiffany blue cooking apron, light brown flat shoes and golden earrings. Her apron is tied to a ribbon and its pockets have flowers and green plants inside them.\n\n=== Powers and abilities ===\nJulieta can heal hurt people with her food recipes, when a hurt person eats something cooked by her, their wounds heal instantly.
