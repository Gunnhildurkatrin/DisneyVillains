They had their second performance in the episode \"[[Home Scream Home]]\".\n\n==Members==\n*'''[[Vampirina Hauntley]]''' - The vampire kid girl, who is the lead singer of the Ghoul Girls and spookylele player. She wears a purple band outfit and has a drawing of webs.\n*'''[[Poppy Peepleson]]''' - The human kid girl, who is the drummer and plays with the drumsticks. She wears black-and-pink band outfit.\n*'''[[Bridget (Vampirina)|Bridget]]''' - The nervous human kid girl, who is the bassist and keyboardist.
