"{{NeedImage}}\n'''James Jefferson''' is a human character who appeared in ''[[The Muppets Present\u2026 Great Moments in American History]]'' stage show at the [[Liberty Square]] part of the [[Magic Kingdom]] area in the [[Walt Disney World]] theme park. James Jefferson (also known as \"JJ\") is a Revolutionary history buff who will serve as the Town Crier of Liberty Square at the Magic Kingdom.\n\nA Disney Parks casting description described the role as follows posted in July 2016:\n:''male improvisational actor (must portray 20-30 years old) who sings, 5\u201910\u201d and taller, to be James Jefferson, \"JJ\", Town Crier of Liberty Square.
