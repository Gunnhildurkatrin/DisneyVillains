She is the mother of [[Mia Thermopolis]].\n\n==Background==\nIn her young adult years in college, she had met and fallen for a handsome man named Philippe Renaldi, whom she later discovered was, in fact, a prince from the country of Genovia. A few years, they married and had one child; a daughter whom they named Amelia, nicknamed Mia. At some point, due to Philippe's status as a prince and being forced to return to Genovia, the two had a falling out and divorced, although they still retained love for each other.\n\n== Appearances ==\n\n===''[[The Princess Diaries]]''===\nHelen calls her fifteen-year-old daughter from her tower room as she is late for school.\n\n===''[[The Princess Diaries 2: Royal Engagement]]'' ===\nFive years later, she is seen at her 21-year-old daughter's college graduation next to her husband and is pregnant with her son.
