She is 6 years old and is immensely kind. Her best friend is [[Pinch Raccoon]]. Jelly is an idea person and loves to help out her friends. She is usually the only one that suggests doing a \"[[Noodle Dance]]\" and is often the one to come up with ideas.\n\n==Personality==\nJelly is somewhat athletic and enjoys a variety of sports. She can also sometimes be girly, but usually leaves the really girly stuff, such as pretty lace or ribbons, to Pinch.
