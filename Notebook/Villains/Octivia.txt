"\n[[File:Octivia.jpg|thumb]]'''Octivia''' is a baby octopus who appears in the ''[[T.O.T.S.]]'' episode \"[[A Splashy Delivery]]\". She is delivered in a special crate full of water.\n\n==Appearance==\nShe is a pink octopus with a light blue headband, purple eyes, a purple bow on the upper right side of the head and she wears a teal collar with T.O.T.S. logo and a white diaper with teal lining and T.O.T.S. symbols.\n\n==Trivia==\n*Her name is often misspelled as Octavia.\n*Her voice actress is Charlie Townsend.\n\n{{T.O.T.S.}}\n[[Category:T.O.T.S. characters]]\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:Females]]\n[[Category:Infants]]\n[[Category:Disney Junior characters]]\n[[Category:Octopus]]\n[[Category:Cephalopods]]\n[[Category:Animated characters]]\n[[Category:TV Animation characters]]\n[[Category:Anthropomorphic characters]]\n[[Category:Disney characters]]"
