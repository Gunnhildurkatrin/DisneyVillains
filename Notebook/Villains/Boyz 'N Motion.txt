They make their first appearance in the episode \"[[Boyz 'N Commotion]]\".\n\n== Appearances ==\n\n=== ''[[That's So Raven]]'' ===\nThe band are fist mentioned in the episode \"[[A Fish Called Raven]]\".\n\n==== \"[[Boyz 'N Commotion]]\" ====\nThe Boyz arrive to [[San Francisco|town]], while touring, and book a reservation at the [[Chill Grill]], in hopes of having some form of privacy, much to the delight of [[Raven Baxter|Raven]]. Having bragged about in school however, attracts the attention of many students, who bombard their way into the restaurant. With Raven's assistance. they manage to hide in the kitchen away from the screaming fans, and she then manages to sneak them to her house. They are unable to leave,due to Raven having talked about the hotel they were residing in, and a delighted [[Tanya Baxter|Tanya]] welcomes them for dinner. When [[Cory Baxter|Cory]] hears that he can make money from items used by the band, he takes items they've discarded, such as chicken bones they've eaten from. Upon hearing their disappointment to having to return to rabid fans, Raven offers them a chance to live at their house temporarily, and the trio in return promise her a favour. While staying there, the boys become lazy and slobbish, to Cory's amusement, who takes items they've used. Raven, who has just returned from school following  confrontation from Bianca, asks the boys to perform at [[Bayside High School|Bayside]], but the band refuse, now wanting to retire after taking some advice Raven gave them previously, prompting Raven to leave disappointed. In the meantime, [[William]] informs Cory that a lock of the boys hair will make them a lot of money, and so the two invite JJ to their room, in the guise of giving him a haircut. While the three play video games, a fed-up Tanya snaps at the boys, prompting them to clean after themselves, and help Raven out. At Bayside High, Raven, along with [[Eddie Thomas|Eddie]] and [[Chelsea Daniels|Chelsea]], dress up as the boy band. When [[Bianca]] reveals to their classmates who they really are however, the real band show up, and perform for Raven.\n\n==== \"[[Be Prepared (That's So Raven)|Be Prepared]]\" ====\n\n=== [[Raven's Home|<u>''Raven's Home''</u>]] ===\nAlthough they don't appear they are mentioned by [[Raven Baxter|Raven]] in the episode \"[[The Bearer of Dad News]]\".
