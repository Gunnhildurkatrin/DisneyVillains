"{{Infobox character\n|name = Monkey\n|shows = ''[[Jake and the Never Land Pirates]]''\n|voice = [[Dee Bradley Baker]]\n|personality = Wild, humorous, mischievous, trouble making\n|home = [[Neverland]]\n|friends = [[Jake (Pirate)|Jake]], [[Izzy]], [[Cubby]], [[Skully]], [[Never Bird]], [[Marina]], [[Sandy (Jake and the Never Land Pirates)|Sandy]], [[Octopus (Peter Pan)|Octopus]], [[Lucille the Seal]]\n|enemies = [[Captain Hook]], [[Mr. Smee]], [[Sharky and Bones]]\n|likes = Playing the bongos, swinging through the trees, bananas\n|dislikes = Captain Hook\n|image = 133998709139455.jpeg|alignment = Good/Neutral}}'''The Monkey''' is a mischievous primate from the [[Disney Junior]] television series ''[[Jake and the Never Land Pirates]]''.\n\n==Role in the [[Jake and the Never Land Pirates|series]]==\nThe Monkey first appeared in the episode \"[[Jake's Jungle Groove]]\" where he stole [[Cubby]]'s bongos which the young pirates were using to help [[Jake (Pirate)|Jake]] develop dance moves. The bongos also had the ability to force [[Captain Hook]] to dance uncontrollably, prompting the villain to attack in an attempt to destroy the musical instrument. The episode ends with the Monkey deciding to return the bongos but join Jake and his crew in their dance party with [[Marina]], the [[Mermaids (Peter Pan)|mermaids]], and her friends.\n\nThe Monkey would reappear in \"[[Captain Hook's Parrot]]\". Here, [[Skully]] is kidnapped by Hook and the Monkey uses the banana peels to slow Hook and his crew down, giving Jake, [[Izzy]], and Cubby some time to catch up.\n\nThe Monkey was featured with the other recurring characters in \"[[Pirate Rock!]]\" that attended Jake, Izzy, and [[Cubby]]'s rock and roll concert held at [[Pirate Rock (Island)|Pirate Rock]]. The Monkey also attended Jake, Izzy, and Cubby's holiday celebration in the episode\"[[It's a Winter Never Land!]]\".\n\nThe Monkey reappeared in the episode \"[[Cubby's Mixed Up Map!]]\" with other look-like monkeys they swipe Cubby's map from Captain Hook and his crew. The Monkey makes a cameo appearance at the end of \"[[Hook's Playful Plant!]]\".\n\nThe Monkey reappeared in the episode \"[[Pirate-Sitting Pirates]]\". While enjoying a snack on the beach, Jake, Izzy, Cubby, and Skully's meal was interrupted by a mischievous monkey who steals Skully's crackers forcing the pirate parrot to give chase with Jake and his crew in pursuit.
