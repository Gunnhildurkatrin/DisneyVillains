She is [[Goldie (Goldie & Bear)|Goldie Locks]]' mother and businesswoman who travels the world collecting things for the people of her home [[Fairy Tale Forest (Goldie & Bear)|forest]]. She is handicapped so she moves around in a customized wheelchair.\u00a0She was first mentioned at the end of season one as scouting ahead looking for a new place for her family to live. She later sent a letter that she was in another forest and asked why her the family was not there.
