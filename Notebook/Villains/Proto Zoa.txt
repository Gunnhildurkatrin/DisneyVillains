At some point he became a famous and became the lead singer for Microbe. He wrote the song \u201cSuper Nova Girl\u201d which became a major hit. In 2049, Proto Zoa\u2019s band Microbe is the first Rock Band to have a Rock concert in Outer Space.\n\n=== Appearances ===\n\n=== [[Zenon: Girl of the 21st Century]] ===\nIn April of 2049 Proto Zoa\u2019s band Microbe is about to become the first Rock Band to have a Rock Concert in Outer Space. Right before he becomes the first Rock Star to have a Concert in Outer Space he holds a contest called \u201cDance with Me\u201d where he chooses one lucky fan from Wyndham Space Station to dance with him at his first Rock concert in Outer Space. Both [[Zenon Kar]] and [[Nebula Wade]] who are huge fans of Microbe enter the contest.
