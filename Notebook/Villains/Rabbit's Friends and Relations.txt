"{{Infobox character\n|image = Winniethepooh-02.jpg\n|name = Rabbit's Friends and Relations\n|films = ''[[Winnie the Pooh (film)|Winnie the Pooh]]'' (deleted)\n|alignment = Good}}'''Rabbit's Friends and Relations''' are deleted characters from ''[[Winnie the Pooh (film)|Winnie the Pooh]]''. They originally appeared in the original books by A.A. Milne.\n\nRabbit's Friends and Relations are also present in the [[Hundred Acre Wood]] in ''Pooh's Neighborhood'', a book in the ''My Very First Winnie the Pooh'' line. In this title, [[Winnie the Pooh]] passes by nine of Rabbit's friends and relations, all living in trees, while out for a walk in the Wood.\n\n{{CharacterStub}}\n{{Winnie the Pooh}}\n[[Category:Deleted characters]]\n[[Category:Winnie the Pooh characters]]\n[[Category:Rabbits]]\n[[Category:Character groups]]\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:Disney characters]]\n[[Category:Silent characters]]"
