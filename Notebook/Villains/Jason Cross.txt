"[[File:Ryne-Sanborn-de.jpg|thumb]]\n'''Jason Cross''' is a member of the East High School boys' varsity basketball team and one of [[Troy Bolton]], [[Chad Danforth]], and [[Zeke Baylor]]'s good friends in ''[[High School Musical]]''. He tends to ask questions or make statements that make him come off as a kiss-up or unintelligent. In [[High School Musical 2|''High School Musical 2'']], he works as a bus boy at Lava Springs country club. Although ''High School Musical'' and ''High School Musical 2'' hint at a possible relationship between Jason and [[Kelsi Nielsen]], he attends prom with Martha Cox in the third film. He is played in the High School Musical movies by Ryne Sanborn.
