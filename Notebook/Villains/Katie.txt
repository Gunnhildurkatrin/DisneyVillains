She is the blonde-haired member of [[Fireside Girls]] Troop 46231.\n\n==Background==\n===Physical appearance===\nKatie has blond hair in two low pigtails with red elastics, green eyes and is light-skinned. She wears a uniform with a red collar with large shoes and no socks, but in winter, she wears a red sweater with orange kerchief, yellow boots, has brown elastics in her hair, and wears an orange hat. She does not wear leggings in winter, unlike the other girls. \u00a0\n\n==Trivia==\n*She and [[Gretchen]] are the only Fireside Girls who appear in the [[Phineas and Ferb (video game)|''Phineas and Ferb'' Nintendo DS game]].\n*Her first speaking role was interrupting Isabella in \"[[Out to Launch (Phineas and Ferb)|Out to Launch]]\", just as she was about to ask Phineas to the dance.\n*She may be ambidextrous and can play the guitar, the violin, and the trumpet.
