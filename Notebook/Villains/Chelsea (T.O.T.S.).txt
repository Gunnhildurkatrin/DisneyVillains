"{{Infobox character|image=Chelsea 115521.jpg|name=Chelsea|shows=\"[[T.O.T.S.]]\"|likes=Running|status=Active|Gender=Female|Animal=Chinchilla}}\n\nChelsea is an energetic chinchilla from the ''[[T.O.T.S.]]'' episode \"[[Woodbird's Wish]]\".\n==Personality==\nShe is very fast and energetic. She had an exercise wheel specially built for her by Mr. Woodbird.\n\n==Appearance==\nChelsea is a baby chinchilla with gray fur surrounding her body, along with light gray fur going from her mouth to the underside of her body. She has cerulean blue eyes, a pink, heart shaped nose, and a tuft of hair on the top. Her ears are also pink.
