"[[File:RalphDWaB.jpg|thumb|250px]]'''Ralph''' is [[Stan Mordecai James|Stan]]'s guardian angel in ''[[Dog With a Blog]]''. He is portrayed by [[Steve Valentine]].\n\n==History==\nRalph and his employees were delivering a new couch to the James-Jennings family when they donated their old one to the Guardian Angels charity.\n\nAfter Stan wished he never been dog, Ralph revealed himself to a guardian angel and granted his wish. Stan was a cat, including Princess, and Freddy and Gracie. However, he also showed him how the family changes.\n\nAfter seeing the family falling apart without a dog, Stan wished to be one again, to which he granted.\n\n==Trivia==\n*Ralph is the third character to know Stan's secret and keep it quiet, followed by [[Nora James]] and [[Karl Fink]].\n**He's also the first person to be aware of Stan's secret from the start.\n*Ralph reveals himself to be a guardian angel, as he showed Stan about how the family would fall apart if he were a cat.\n*While working for a charity company, he also poses as an amateur magician when he's not working.\n*He used to have a dog of his own named Quat.\n*Ralph is just a nickname, but his real name is not understandable to mortals.
