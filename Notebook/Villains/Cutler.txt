"{{Infobox character\n|image = Cutler.png\n|name = Cutler\n|shows = ''[[Tron: Uprising]]''\n|voice = [[Lance Reddick]]\n|species = Program\n|alignment = Good, later bad|fate = Derezzed|personality = Original: friendly, cheeky, warm\nTruly: Scary, mean, vile, sinister, dishonest}}'''Cutler''' is a veteran on [[the Grid]] and an antagonist from the animated [[Disney XD]] series, ''[[Tron: Uprising]]''.\n\n==History==\n===[[The Renegade|The Renegade, Part 1]]===\nHe\u00a0and [[Beck]]\u00a0team up after being captured and taken to the light disc coliseum. They also join forces in a two man verses three light cycle fight which they win. He encourages Beck unintentionally, saying that the renegade is a hero and he isn't alone. As a \"reward\" for doing a good job, [[General Tesler]] lets them have a light disc battle to the death... against each other.\n\n===[[The Renegade|The Renegade, Part 2]]===\nCutler and Beck refuse to fight until Beck provokes Cutler. Soon Cutler forfeits much to Tessler's dismay.\u00a0sentences him to be derezzed at Argon Square. Before being taken away, he gives Beck his veteran arm band. Beck, as Tron,\u00a0boards the Interceptor that Cutler is being held in, but Pavel notices and tries to shoot them down. They fight [[Paige]]\u00a0at the docks but soon get away. They take a boat and are attacked by Pavel on the water. After a mysterious underwater boat breaks the bond between their two boats, they take off and Cutler leaves his saviour on a dock.
