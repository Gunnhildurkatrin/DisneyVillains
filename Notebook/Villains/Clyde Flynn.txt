Time for vittles!\"}}'''Clyde Flynn''' is a recurring character of [[Disney Television Animation|Disney]]'s television series ''[[Phineas and Ferb]]'', voiced by [[Barry Bostwick]]. He lives in [[Danville]]'s woods and hosts Camp Phineas and Ferb. He is not a very good bugler, but loves to sing and entertain the children. He often tells stories that he heard in his childhood like Bigfoot and Badbeard. He is not only a story teller, he is a color commentator as stated by Phineas for the roller rink commentary, but rather than adding \"color\" to the information, he actually said the names of different colors ('''Clyde:''' \"Red, green, blue!\" Phineas:\"Excellent colors, grandpa\"). He is the father of [[Linda Flynn-Fletcher|Linda Flynn]] and [[Tiana Webber]]. He's also the grandfather of[[ Phineas Flynn]] and [[Candace Flynn]], and step-grandfather of [[Ferb Fletcher]].\n\n==Trivia==\n*Clyde wears his watch on his right arm.\n*Clyde's actor (Barry Bostwick) has previously appeared in ''The Rocky Horror Picture Show'', which was, ironically, written by Richard O'Brien, who now voices [[Lawrence Fletcher]] in all of the character's appearances.\n*In one episode, he calls Ferb \"Turbo Ferbo.\"\n*He won the famous hot air balloon race in history.\n*He has a monotreme manners book.\n\n==Gallery==\n<gallery spacing=\"small\" captionalign=\"left\">\nClyde Flynn.jpg\n</gallery>\n\n{{Phineas and Ferb}}\n{{PF-Wiki}}\n[[Category:Characters|Flynn, Clyde]]\n[[Category:Disney characters|Flynn, Clyde]]\n[[Category:Males|Flynn, Clyde]]\n[[Category:Phineas and Ferb characters|Flynn, Clyde]]\n[[Category:Musicians|Flynn, Clyde]]\n[[Category:Grandparents]]\n[[Category:Adults]]\n[[Category:Parents]]\n[[Category:American characters]]\n[[Category:Elderly characters]]\n[[Category:Spouses]]\n[[Category:Singing characters]]\n[[Category:TV Animation characters]]\n[[Category:Animated characters]]\n[[Category:Disney Channel characters]]"
