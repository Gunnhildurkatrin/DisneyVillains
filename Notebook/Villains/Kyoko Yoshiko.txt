She is always by his side, and knows a lot about his operations. [[Kim Possible|Kim]] first met Yoshiko when [[Dr. Drakken]] stole a video game factory from Nakasumi in order to build a giant robot. Yoshiko and Nakasumi helped Kim the second time by providing transportation to [[England]] for a mission to find [[Ron Stoppable|Ron]]. Kim met Yoshiko the third time to foil Nakasumi's kidnapping.
