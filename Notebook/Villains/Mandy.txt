"{{Infobox character\n|image = Aliki_Mandy.jpg\n|voice = [[Aliki Theofilopoulos Grafft]]<ref>http://www.imdb.com/name/nm1507229/</ref>\n|shows = ''[[Phineas and Ferb]]''\n|nationality = [[United States|American]]\n|species = Human\n|occupation = Student (on vacation)\n|friends = [[Candace Flynn]] (briefly)\n|family = Mrs. Weaver (aunt)<br>[[Thaddeus and Thor (characters)|Thaddeus]] (younger brother)<br>[[Thaddeus and Thor (characters)|Thor]] (younger brother)\n|dislikes = Her brothers' antics\n|quote = \"[[Candace Flynn|Your]] brothers are annoying you, too?\"|alignment = Bad/Neutral|personality = Obnoxious, rude, uncaring}}\n'''Mandy'''<ref>http://www.imdb.com/character/ch0398977/</ref> is a minor antagonist of the animated television series ''[[Phineas and Ferb]]''. She is the older sister of [[Thaddeus and Thor (characters)|Thaddeus]] and [[Thaddeus and Thor (characters)|Thor]], and is driven crazy by the antics of her brothers, similar to [[Candace Flynn|Candace]] is by [[Phineas Flynn|Phineas]]' and [[Ferb Fletcher|Ferb]]'s activities in the background.\n\n==Personal life==\nDuring her visit to her aunt Mrs. Weaver's house, a next-door neighbor of the Flynn-Fletcher family, she overheard Candace describing Phineas and Ferb's antics, and it quickly escalated into a point of pride that her brothers were more obnoxious than Candace's. She acted as lookout and cheerleader for her brothers during their fort-building competition with Phineas and Ferb. In the end, after Phineas and Ferb beat her brothers in the fort building contest, she admits that Candace's brothers are more obnoxious than her own brothers (\"[[Thaddeus and Thor]]\").\n\nShe owns a \"Stop the Busting\" program (\"[[Perry the Actorpus]]\"), which helps people to stop trying to bust their siblings. Phineas and Ferb gave her a machine to help Candace stop having a busting obsession. She let the former slip which led to Candace destroying the machine with some sort of lead pipe.\n\n==Physical Appearance==\nMandy is as tall as Candace. She wears a light blue sleeveless t-shirt and navy blue jeans.
