He alongside his wife the White Queen and his daughter Lily the pawn take a journey through the land through Alice's adventures in the looking-glass world, unaware that Alice is watching them.\n\n==Background==\n===''[[Alice Through the Looking Glass]]''===\nThe White King was shown playing a game of chess with his family and asking his army soldiers to help [[Humpty Dumpty]] after [[Alice Kingsleigh|Alice]] knocked him over after he rolled over off the chessboard and he was still on the board with his family. [[Absolem]] (in his butterfly form) flies by Alice at the spot, explaining that the [[Tarrant Hightopp|Hatter]] is in the risk of dying, forcing Alice to return to Wonderland before the Hatter dies to which Alice manages to save him from dying in time by finding the evidence of Tarrant's missing family.\n\n===''[[Once Upon a Time in Wonderland]]''===\n[[Will Scarlet]] is taking the place of the White King after he was freed from the genie lamp and he kissed Anastasia the Red Queen and he broke the spell that Jafar placed on her after making her fall in love with him. That was after the lovers came to Wonderland until Anastasia's mother wouldn't let her date him because he was a thief. They got married soon after they went to Alice's wedding and Alice stated in her book that they lived happily ever after alongside the [[Red Queen]] who is now taking place of the [[White Queen]]. His background story is that the Queen of Hearts ripped out his heart and took it away from him when he became a Knave of Hearts to her, he was forced to serve her.
