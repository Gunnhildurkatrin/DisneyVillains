"{{dablink|the character Violet from [[Lloyd in Space]]|other characters|[[Violet]]}}[[File:Char_36818.jpg|thumb]]\n'''Violet''' (voiced by [[Ashley Johnson]])<ref>http://www.behindthevoiceactors.com/tv-shows/Lloyd-in-Space/Violet/</ref> is a supporting character of [[Disney's One Saturday Morning]]'s ''[[Lloyd in Space]]''.\n\n==Biography==\nShe is a student and friend of [[Brittany Boviak]] and [[Megan Uno]] who had a crush on [[Lloyd Nebulon]]. She is a student in Lloyd's class at Luna Vista Middle School who appears recurringly throughout the series with her most notable speaking role being in the episode in [[The Big Sleepover (Lloyd in Space)|The Big Sleepover]].
