He is one of [[Cricket Green|Cricket]] and [[Tilly Green]]'s friends.\n\n\n==Background==\n===Personality===\nWeezie is a cool guy through and through. Much like [[Benny (Big City Greens)|Benny]] and [[Kiki (Big City Greens)|Kiki]], he enjoys the many entertaining things that Cricket brings both figuratively and literally. He always has a look of relaxation on his face and speaks in short sparse phrases. However as seen in \"[[Cyberbullies]]\" he can become downtrodden though he still speaks in short cool phrases that causes surrounding sympathy to those who hear him.\n===Appearance===\nWeezie is a blue skinned boy with short shaved down hair similar to African-Americans. He has noticeable lips ended with puffy cheeks.
