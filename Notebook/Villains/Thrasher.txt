Tell anyone I exist and [[Lanny|you]] won't exist.''\n}}'''Thrasher''' is a reindeer character featured in ''[[Prep & Landing]]''.\n\n==Character bio from the official website==\n:Rumored by many to be only an urban myth, the highly secretive Thrasher the Reindeer is purportedly the cousin of [[Dasher]] the Reindeer. Thrasher is responsible for transporting Prep & Landing elves to their mission sites. He prides himself on his unparalleled stealth and speed, supposedly shaving his antlers smooth for better aerodynamics and painting their underside jet black to go undetected by radar.\n\n:'''Alleged Likes:''' Thrasher likes candy canes. And his cool shades.\n\n:'''Supposed Dislikes:''' Thrasher dislikes almost everything except candy canes. And his cool shades.\n\n:'''Rumored Random Fact:''' Word has it that Thrasher plays cards with [[Wayne]] every Thursday night. At Thrasher\u2019s insistence, the location is undisclosed.\n\n:Thrasher has a tattoo of a holly leaf on his front leg. Only Blitzen knows the real circumstances involved behind it.\n\n==Appearances==\n===''[[Prep & Landing]]''===\nThrasher makes a brief appearance when introduced to [[Lanny]] as the reindeer that will be transporting him, [[Wayne]] and several other Prep and Landing elves to their sites.\n\nAfter meeting him, Lanny expresses his believe that he thought that Thrasher was a myth, to which the reindeer says that he is, before warning Lanny not to tell others of his existence or else he (Lanny) won't exist.\n\n===''[[Prep & Landing: Naughty vs. Nice|Naughty vs. Nice]]''===\nThrasher is revealed to be friends with Wayne's brother [[Noel]], one of the few elves that has his respect.\n\n==Printed media==\n===''Mansion Impossible''===\nThrasher appears on the first page of the crossover short story delivering Wayne and Lanny to the Avengers Mansion.\n\n==External links==\n*[[w:c:prepandlanding:{{PAGENAME}}|Prep & Landing Wiki: {{PAGENAME}}]]\n\n{{Prep & Landing}}\n[[Category:Prep & Landing characters]]\n[[Category:Deer]]\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:Disney characters]]\n[[Category:Males]]\n[[Category:Characters who fly]]\n[[Category:Animated characters]]\n[[Category:TV Animation characters]]"
