Is he okay?\"<br>\"Sorry about that!\"\n}}The '''Mammoths''' are minor characters that appear in ''[[Brother Bear]]'' and [[Brother Bear 2|its sequel]]. They are the relatives of modern elephants and lived from 10,000 B.C. to 4000 B.C.\n\n==Appearances==\n===''[[Brother Bear]]''===\nA mammoth is first seen being ridden by [[Kenai]] while [[Sitka]] and [[Denahi]] are fishing, and the mammoth suddenly knocks Kenai off its back. Later, some mammoth spirits can be seen before Kenai is transformed into a bear, and during the song \"[[On My Way]]\", a herd of mammoths are seen being ridden by Kenai, [[Koda]], [[Rutt and Tuke]].\n\n===''[[Brother Bear 2]]''===\nIn opening of ''Brother Bear 2'', during the song \"[[Welcome to This Day]]\", a mammoth couple can be seen walking through the wilderness together. Later, during the song \"[[Feels Like Home]]\", a herd of mammoths are seen being ridden by Kenai, Koda, and [[Nita]] on their way to Hokani Falls.\n\n==Gallery==\n<gallery widths=\"150\" position=\"center\" spacing=\"small\">\nBrother Bear Screenshot 1163.jpg\nBrother Bear 2 Screenshot 1164.jpg\nBrother Bear 2 Screenshot 1165.jpg\n</gallery>\n\n==Trivia==\n*They are based on real-life {{WikipediaLink|woolly mammoth}}s that lived in [[North America]], Europe, and [[Asia]] and existed from 400,000 to 3,000 years ago during the {{wikipediaLink|Pleistocene}} Epoch to the {{WikipediaLink|Holocene}} Epoch.\n*During [[Outtakes#Brother Bear|Koda's Outtakes]], one mammoth talks in two scenes: when he accidentally flings Koda backwards and when he unintentionally steps on a gerbil.
