"[[File:3x15TheGood05.jpg|thumb|250px]]\n'''Warren Nichols''' (portrayed by Richard Chiu) is the older brother of [[Stevie Nichols]]. He appears in \"The Good, The Bad and the Alex.\" Stevie says that she misses her brother, Warren, and [[Alex Russo|Alex]] decides to track Warren down. She finds him outside the magical power-draining room. He states that Stevie is evil and that she ran away when she lost the Wizard competition. He tends to faint.
