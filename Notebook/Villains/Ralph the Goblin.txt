"[[File:ralph.gif|thumb|250px]]'''Ralph The Goblin'''<ref>http://www.imdb.com/character/ch0388173/</ref><ref>http://www.jeffgoode.com/tv/americandragon/characters/ralph.htm</ref> (voiced by [[John DiMaggio]]) is a fictional character from [[Disney]]'s animated series ''[[American Dragon: Jake Long]]''. He is a goblin seen in \"[[Shapeshifter]]\". He never make a reappearance afterwards (not even in Season 2).\n\n==History==\nHe used to be working for [[the Huntsclan]] by capturing the \"Orb Of Malarfuse\". He is now [[Fu Dog|Fu Dog's]] sidekick. He doesn't have a lizard tail like other goblins, but he still has a keen sense of smell.
