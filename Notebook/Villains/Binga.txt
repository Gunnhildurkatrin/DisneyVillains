She eats bugs faster than Bunga and smells more than him. Bunga even thinks that she's smellier than him.\n\n===Physical appearance===\nBinga is a purple honey badger with blue eyes. Her hair is white and kind of looks like Bunga's. She's very kind and stinky.\n\n==Role in the series==\n===''[[Little Old Ginterbong]]''===\nAfter [[Bunga]]'s chewing annoys [[Fuli]], [[Baliyo]] explains that Bunga's chewing is soft for a honey badger to which he introduces him to Binga. Both honey badgers compete in different games to see who is the better honey badger to which Binga constantly wins. Later when [[Mama Binturong (character)|Mama Binturong]]'s true identity is revealed, Bunga introduces her to [[Ono]] and the [[Lion Guard]]. She and Bunga later use their stench abilities to defeat Mama Binturong once again, foiling her plan.\n\n===''[[Long Live the Queen]]''===\nAlong with the other animals of the [[Tree of Life (The Lion Guard)|Tree of Life]], Binga is sad when Queen [[Janna]] completes her Circle of Life. She later attends the queen's funeral at the Willows. Later, Binga comes to [[Rani (The Lion Guard)|Rani]]'s coronation when she becomes the queen of the Tree of Life.\n\n===''[[Return to the Pride Lands]]''===\nBinga stands right next to Bunga at [[Kion]]'s coronation during Kion and Rani's wedding.\n\n==Relationships==\n\n===Friends===\n====[[Bunga]]====\n\nBunga and Binga are good friends. When Bunga first meets Binga, they have a bug-eating contest. Binga and Bunga have many honey badgers challenges and Bunga always looses. They just don't know which one is better. Bunga soon introduces his friends to her. Bunga and Binga are also seen together at Kion's coronation. It's possible that Bunga and Binga might have deep feelings for each other.\n\n====[[Rani (The Lion Guard)|Rani]]====\nBinga and Rani are good friends. Binga comes to Rani's coronation and her wedding.\n\n====[[Baliyo]]====\n\nBaliyo and Binga are good friends. Baliyo introduces Bunga to Binga.\n\n====[[Kion]]====\n\nBinga and Kion are good friends. Binga cheers for Kion at his coronation.\n\n====[[Janna]]====\n\nBinga and Janna are good friends. Binga was sad when Janna completed her journey through the Circle of Life.\n\n===Acquaintances===\n====[[Fuli]], [[Beshte]], [[Ono]], and [[Anga]]====\n\nBinga is on good terms with Bunga's friends. They're all amazed that Binga can beat Bunga in everything.\n\n====[[Nirmala]] and [[Surak]]====\n\nBinga, Nirmala and Surak are on good terms.\n\n====[[Makini]]====\n\nBinga and Makini are on good terms.\n\n===Enemies===\n====[[Makucha's Army]]====\nBinga hates Makcuha's Army since they tried to attack her.\n\n==Trivia==\n*Binga is the second known honey badger to appear in the franchise.\n\n==Gallery==\n<gallery position=\"center\" widths=\"185\">\nThe Lion Guard Little Old Ginterbong WatchTLG snapshot 0.03.00.630 1080p.png\nThe Lion Guard Long Live the Queen WatchTLG snapshot 0.05.17.529 1080p.png\nThe Lion Guard Long Live the Queen WatchTLG snapshot 0.05.24.218 1080p.png\nThe Lion Guard Long Live the Queen WatchTLG snapshot 0.09.37.255 1080p.png\nReturn-to-the-Pride-Lands (835).png\nKion Rani Last Scene.jpg\n</gallery>\n\n{{The Lion King}}\n[[Category:Disney characters]]\n[[Category:Animated characters]]\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:Disney Junior characters]]\n[[Category:TV Animation characters]]\n[[Category:The Lion Guard characters]]\n[[Category:Asian characters]]\n[[Category:Females]]\n[[Category:Singing characters]]\n[[Category:Anthropomorphic characters]]\n[[Category:Badgers]]"
