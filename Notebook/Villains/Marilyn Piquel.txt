Bobcat]]\n|likes = Toons, art, story writing|fate = Moves To Washington D.C. With Lucky}}'''Marilyn Piquel''' is [[Lucky Piquel]]'s child genius prodigy daughter. She is an aspiring artist as well as script/story writing and has deep connection to toons such as [[Bonkers D. Bobcat|Bonkers]], with her favorite toon being TV star [[Skunky Skunk]]. She is more than capable of taking care of herself and aids her father in a number of his cases often being the words of wisdom or source of knowledge to Lucky. Although looking like her mom [[Dilandra Piquel]] (\"Dil\"/\"Dyl\"), aside from her big round glasses and freckled face; she also shares her father's hair color.
