"{{Infobox character\n|name = Snooty Flamingos\n|image = Icerafantasia20002195.jpg\n|films = ''[[Fantasia 2000]]''\n|animator = [[Eric Goldberg]]\n|enemies = [[Yo Yo Flamingo]]\n|rides = ''[[Parade of the Stars]]''\n|shows = ''[[House of Mouse]]''<br>''[[Once Upon a Time in Wonderland]]''\n|alignment = Neutral\n|personality = Snooty, pompous, orderly, cranky, rude, grumpy\n|appearance = Slender flamingos, dark-pink feathers, indigo legs, feet, and beaks\n|friends = Each other, [[Yo Yo Flamingo]] (formerly)\n|likes = Swimming practice\n|dislikes = Yo-yos\n|fate = Tied up by Yo Yo (Originally, after shoving the Yo-Yo Flamingo in the water, they walked off taking his yo-yos and played with them)}}The '''Snooty Flamingos''' are the main antagonists from the ''[[Fantasia 2000]]'' short [[The Carnival of the Animals]]. The Flamingos are trying to get rid of [[Yo Yo Flamingo]]'s yo-yo toy.\n\n==Appearances==\n===''[[Fantasia 2000]]''===\nThe Snooty Flamingos wake up in the morning and start their swimming practice. The other Flamingos realize [[Yo Yo Flamingo|Yo Yo]] is playing with his toy and force him to swim with them. While swimming, Yo Yo plays with his toy again and the other Flamingos realize and a chase follows. Yo Yo manages to fool the Snooty Flamingos and traps them in a log. The Snooty Flamingos free themselves and push Yo Yo to drown him underwater. They then throw his yo-yo into a tree and shake feet, believing they have won. They end up being tied up, as it is revealed Yo Yo has hundreds of yo-yos as he rises from the water.\n\n===''[[House of Mouse]]''===\nThe Snooty Flamingos made occasional cameo appearances in two ''[[House of Mouse]]'' episodes: \"[[Not So Goofy]]\" and \"[[Donald Wants to Fly]]\", where they were apart of [[Magic Carpet|Carpet's]] performance.\n\n===Disney Parks===\nThe Snooty Flamingos,
