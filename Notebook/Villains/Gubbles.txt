"{{Infobox character||image = Gubblesfish.jpg\n|name = Gubbles\n|shows = ''[[Mickey Mouse (TV series)|Mickey Mouse]]<br>[[The Wonderful World of Mickey Mouse]]''\n|voice = [[Dee Bradley Baker]]\n|alliance = \n|fullname=Gubbles\n|family=[[Mickey Mouse]] (owner)}}\n\n'''Gubbles''' is [[Mickey Mouse|Mickey's]] pet fish that debuts in the ''[[Mickey Mouse (TV series)|Mickey Mouse]]'' episode \"[[Gasp!]]\".\n\n==Appearances==\n===''[[Mickey Mouse (TV series)|Mickey Mouse]]''===\nIn the short, it is shown that Gubbles has the personality and traits of a dog, including barking, fetching, growling, and eating dog biscuits. He has been taught tricks by Mickey, which allowed him to escape from a cat's stomach.\n\nGubbles also makes an appearance in the short, \"[[No]]\". Since Mickey can't bring himself to say no, the cat takes Gubbles away (though the fish manages to get away). Toward the end of the episode, it's revealed that Gubbles has found a girl.\n\nGubbles also makes an appearance in the short \"[[Flushed!]]\", where Mickey mistakes him sleeping as him going belly up, which made him accidentally flush Gubbles and go into the sewers to rescue him.\n\n===''[[The Wonderful World of Mickey Mouse]]''===\nGubbles returned in the episode \"[[School of Fish]]\", where he attends his first day at school while Mickey attempts to help him, usually making embarrassing results.\n\n==Gallery==\n<gallery>\nImage-5349 51F385C8.jpg\nImage-7D34 51F385F6.jpg\nMickey Mouse S01 E07 HDTV x264 W4 F s.jpg\nD621699de976e64ab17ce66169f09959.jpg\nGHbzJ.jpg\nAMhEzjNU.jpg\n3ndaS9b.jpg\nMickey Mouse No Swimming.jpg\nVlcsnap-2016-03-03-18h54m24s223.png\nVlcsnap-2016-03-03-18h54m27s099.png\nVlcsnap-2016-03-03-18h54m34s633.png\nVlcsnap-2016-03-03-18h54m43s795.png\nVlcsnap-2016-03-03-18h55m14s409.png\nVlcsnap-2016-03-03-18h55m24s167.png\nVlcsnap-2016-03-03-18h55m42s306.png\nVlcsnap-2016-03-03-18h55m47s697.png\nVlcsnap-2016-03-03-18h55m58s356.png\n</gallery>\n\n==References==\n{{Reflist}}\n\n{{Mickey Mouse (TV series)}}\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:Disney characters]]\n[[Category:Fish]]\n[[Category:Pets]]\n[[Category:Mickey Mouse universe characters]]\n[[Category:Silent characters]]\n[[Category:TV Animation characters]]\n[[Category:Animated characters]]\n[[Category:Mickey Mouse (TV series) characters]]\n[[Category:Disney Channel characters]]\n[[Category:Males]]"
