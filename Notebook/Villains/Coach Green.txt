He also makes the students climb a rope while being hit with dodgeballs. He speaks with a British accent and wears glasses.\n\nHe is also the Coach of Norrisville's Jet Ski team, the \"Wave Slayers\".\n\nHe is possibly one of the most dangerous and insane teachers in Norrisville High. Despite that, he has a jolly attitude.\n\n==Personality==\nCoach Green is possibly one of the most dangerous and insane teachers in Norrisville High, wanting his kids to do things like play hopscotch with a flamethrower involved in \"The Tale of the Golden Doctor's Note\" and do blindfolded archery in \"[[Sword Quest]]\". He believes in learning by doing, so he sends his students on a three-legged race to the most  dangerous places on the high school campus in \"[[Randy Cunningham and the Sorcerer's Key]]\". Despite his wackiness, he has a jolly attitude, always enthusiastic about what he's doing no matter how extreme it really is.\n\n==Appearance==\n===Everyday===\nCoach Green is tall and lanky with golden-brown hair and hazel eyes. He usually wears glasses and a white sports jacket with yellow stripes and blue sides over a black shirt. He also has very short yellow shorts, long white socks with red stripes, and blue tennis shoes with yellow stripes.\n\n==Trivia==\n*His appearance is a reference to his voice actor John Oliver. \n\n==Gallery==\n<gallery position=\"center\" spacing=\"small\" captionalign=\"center\">\nThe Tale of the Golden Doctor's Note - Julian, Randy and Howard.png\nCoach Green in TOTGDN 1.png\nCoach Green in TOTGDN 2.png\nCoach Green in TOTGDN 3.png\nCoach Green in TOTGDN 4.png\nCoach Green in TOTGDN 5.png\nThe Tale of the Golden Doctor's Note - Randy and Howard 01.jpg\nCoach Green in TOTGDN 6.png\nCoach Green in TOTGDN 7.png\nCoach Green in TOTGDN 8.png\nButtermaker 11.png\nWave Slayers - Howard and Randy.jpg\nRandy and Howard in Randy Cunningham and the Sorcerer's Key 4.jpg\nCoach Green Full Body.jpg\nCoach Green Swimwear.jpg\n</gallery>\n\n{{DEFAULTSORT:Green}}\n{{RC Wiki}}\n{{Randy Cunningham}}\n[[Category:Disney characters]]\n[[Category:Adults]]\n[[Category:Randy Cunningham: 9th Grade Ninja characters]]\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:Males]]\n[[Category:Character stubs]]\n[[Category:Schoolteachers]]\n[[Category:Coaches]]\n[[Category:Animated characters]]\n[[Category:TV Animation characters]]\n[[Category:Disney XD characters]]"
