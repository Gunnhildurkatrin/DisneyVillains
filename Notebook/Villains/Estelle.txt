She is voiced by [[Debi Derryberry]].<ref>http://www.behindthevoiceactors.com/tv-shows/Fillmore/Estelle/</ref><ref>http://www.imdb.com/name/nm0220635/?ref_=ttfc_fc_cl_t10</ref>\n\n== History ==\nIn \"[[Ingrid Third, Public Enemy Number One]]\", after [[Cornelius Fillmore|Fillmore]] and [[Ingrid Third|Ingrid]] discovered an orange Dancey Lads pin button and a bag of what was supposedly flour that [[Brad Parnassus]] had been using, they went to Estelle's house to find out if she happened to know anything about them. She told them that she'd been missing a pin button that belonged to her, as well as a black sweater, and wanted the pin button back. But Fillmore and Ingrid had to keep it for evidence. Then, they asked her about the empty bag of flour.
