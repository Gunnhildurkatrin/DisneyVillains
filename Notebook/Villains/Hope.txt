"{{Infobox character\n|shows = ''[[Kim Possible]]''\n|name = Hope\n|image = Hope 01.png\n|voice = [[Grey DeLisle]]\n|animator = [[Bob Schooley]]<br>[[Mark McCorkle]]\n|personality = Stylish, kind, athletic, teenage-like\n|appearance = Dark skin, black hair\n|home = [[Middleton]]\n|occupation = Student at [[Middleton High School]]<br>Cheerleader\n|friends = [[Kim Possible]], [[Bonnie Rockwaller]], [[Tara]]\n|fate = Graduates from Middleton High School}}'''Hope''' is a [[Middleton High School]] student and [[Middleton High School Cheerleaders|cheerleader]] from ''[[Kim Possible]]''.\u00a0She was voiced by [[Grey DeLisle]].\n\n==Appearance==\nHope is a dusky olivine-skinned girl with black eyes and raven-black hair. When not in her cheer uniform, she wears a pink one-piece mini with black Chinese ''ku-li'' slippers.\n\n==Abilities==\nAs an acrobatic cheerleader, Hope has much of\u00a0the same athleticism which Kim is known for. Furthermore, she may have an interest and ability in Track and Field.\n\n==Relationships==\nHope is mostly seen in the company of [[Tara]] and with\u00a0[[Bonnie Rockwaller]]. She went to the prom with the Track and Field captain, whose name was not mentioned.\n\n==Trivia==\n*Her name was revealed in \"[[Steal Wheels]]\".\n*For those who have trouble telling her apart from [[Marcella]], Hope has no mole by her lip.\n*Hope only had two solo speaking parts.
