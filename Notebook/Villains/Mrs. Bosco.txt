"[[File:Mrs._bOSC.jpg|thumb]]'''Mrs. Bosco''' is [[Dorinda Thomas]]' foster mother. She is okay with most of the stuff Dorinda does, even let her travel to Spain. Mrs. Bosco chats with Chanel briefly, and then tells Dorinda to come get the door. Chanel and the others are shocked that Dorinda is a foster child.\n\n==The Cheetah Girls 2==\nWhen the Cheetahs try to go to Spain, Dorinda mentioned that Mrs. Bosco doesn't mind that Dorinda is going to Spain.\n\n==Physical Appearance==\nMrs. Bosco is a black female.
