Even though Giselle is a stranger to her at first, she speaks to her without being shy. She, however, is shy at the beginning of the movie.\n\n===Physical appearance===\nShe is small and slender with brown hair and eyes, fair skin and rosy cheeks.\n\n=== Personality ===\nShe is [[Robert Philip|Robert Philip's]] imaginative 6-year-old daughter. She befriends Giselle and is not sure how she feels about the initial thought of her dad marrying [[Nancy Tremaine|Nancy]].\n\n==History==\nMorgan Philip likes the woman ([[Nancy Tremaine]]) that [[Robert Philip|her father]] is dating, but she does not consider her as the mother material type. When [[Giselle]]\u00a0comes to the real world, Morgan immediately befriends her. Later, she starts to show tearful expressions when Giselle leaves.
