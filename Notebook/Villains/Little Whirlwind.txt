"[[File:Littlewhirlwind.jpg|thumb|250px]]\n'''Little Whirlwind''' is a baby tornado who [[Mickey Mouse|Mickey]] tangles with while trying to clean up [[Minnie Mouse|Minnie]]'s yard in the [[1941]] animated short, ''[[The Little Whirlwind]]''. After tormenting Mickey for a while, it calls for [[Mama Whirlwind|its mother]] when Mickey begins actively chasing it - with it following behind as its mother wreaks havoc. After Mickey is caught by its mother and dumped in Minnie's fountain, it leaves with its mother.\n[[Category:Males]]\n[[Category:Mickey Mouse universe characters]]\n[[Category:Animated characters]]\n[[Category:Disney characters]]\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:Kids]]\n[[Category:Silent characters]]"
