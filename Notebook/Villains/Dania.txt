He is nervous to talk to her and [[Terkina|Terk]] tells him that he'll be fine and makes him go to her. Tantor asks Dania on a date and she agrees.\n\nAt night, Dania meets Tantor's friends, except for [[Tarzan (character)|Tarzan]]. She then meets Terk but finds Terk's antics to be annoying and obnoxious. The next day, Tantor and Dania are about to go on another date, but Dania tells Tantor to not invite Terk. When Terk finds out that Dania dislikes her and she wants to be alone with Tantor, she tries to break up their relationship but fails. Tantor and Terk have an argument and they break up.\n\nWhen Tantor finds out about Terk getting captured by [[German Poacher|poachers]], he is about to rescue her, making Dania annoyed. After Terk and Tantor reunite, Dania gives Tantor a second chance to leave Terk and go out with her.
