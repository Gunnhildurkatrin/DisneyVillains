"{{Infobox_character\n|name = Patch\n|image = Jake-Pirate-Pup.png\n|shows = ''[[Jake and the Never Land Pirates]]''\n|voice = [[Dee Bradley Baker]]\n|personality = Cute, silly, funny, playful\n|occupation = Jake's pet puppy\n|alignment = Good\n|home = [[Pirate Island]]\n|friends = [[Jake (Pirate)|Jake]], [[Izzy]], [[Cubby]], [[Skully]], [[Mr. Smee]], [[Sharky and Bones]]\n|enemies = [[Captain Hook]]\n|likes = Playing, dog cookies\n|dislikes = Captain Hook\n|weapons = Fangs, speed, cleverness}}'''Patch''' is [[Jake (Pirate)|Jake]], [[Izzy]], and [[Cubby]]'s pet puppy from television series ''[[Jake and the Never Land Pirates]]''.\n\n==Role in the series==\nPatch first appeared in the episode \"[[The Pirate Pup!]]\" where the rambunctious pup sneaks aboard the [[The Jolly Roger|Jolly Roger]] and runs off with one of [[Captain Hook]]'s boots, causing Hook and his crew to chase Patch all across [[Neverland]] in attempt to retrieve his boot back.\n\nPatch returns in the episode \"[[The Golden Smee!]]\" where the rambunctious puppy discovers a golden statue that resembles [[Mr. Smee]] while exploring with Jake and his crew on Never Land. Later in the episode, Patch aided Jake and Hook's crew to locate Mr. Smee who had taken the curse statue of the Smee's to [[Belch Mountain]].\n\nPatch returns in the episode \"[[Little Red Riding Hook!]],\" digging up many treasures with Jake and his crew when he hears his canine friend calling he rush into the [[Never Land Jungle]], forgetting to take the treasures with him, so Izzy decides to deliver the basket of treasures to Patch before Captain Hook tries to steal it. Later, Patch and pirate pup friends confront Hook and Smee as they tried to escape with the basket but during the chase slip down a cliff only to rescued by [[Izzy]] and her [[Pixie Dust|pixie dust]]. With everyone safe and Patch's friends chasing Hook and Smee away, Izzy returned the basket of treasures back to Patch.\n\n==Episode Appearances==\n*\"[[The Pirate Pup!]]\" (first appearance)\n*\"[[The Golden Smee!]]\"\n*\"[[Little Red Riding Hook!]]\"\n\n==Trivia==\n*He shares his name with [[Patch]] from the ''[[One Hundred and One Dalmatians|101 Dalmatians]]'' franchise.\n\n==Gallery==\n<gallery spacing=\"small\" widths=\"150\" position=\"center\" captionalign=\"center\">\nBfd19dad2a82.jpg|Patch with Cubby, Skully, Jake, and Izzy.\npacthpup.jpg|Patch With Hook's boot.\n640px-Jakepup.png|Patch with Jake.\nPatch the Pirate Pup01.jpg\nPatch the Pirate Pup02.jpg\nPatch the Pirate Pup03.jpg\nPatch the Pirate Pup04.jpg\nPirate-pack-izzy-and-patch.jpg|Patch and Izzy figures.\nPatch the Pirate Pup05.jpg\nPatch the Pirate Pup06.jpg\nPatch the Pirate Pup07.jpg\nPatch the Pirate Pup08.png\nPatch the Pirate Pup09.png\nPatch the Pirate Pup10.png\nPatch the Pirate Pup11.jpg\nPatch the Pirate Pup12.png\nPatch the Pirate Pup13.png\nPatch the Pirate Pup14.png\nPatch the Pirate Pup15.png\nPatch the Pirate Pup16.png\nPatch the Pirate Pup17.png\nPatchJake&Izzy.jpg\nHook&amp;crew-The Pirate Pup!.jpg\nSmeeHookPatch-The Pirate Pup!.jpg\nPatch&amp;Smee-The Pirate Pup!.jpg\nPatch&amp;Smee-The Pirate Pup.png\nThe Pirate Pup Picture Viewer01.jpg\nThe Pirate Pup Picture Viewer03.jpg\nSmee&Patch-The Pirate Pup03.jpg\nPatchSmeeSharky&Bones-The Pirate Pup.jpg\n</gallery>\n\n{{Jake and the Neverland Pirates}}\n[[Category:Jake and the Never Land Pirates characters]]\n[[Category:Dogs]]\n[[Category:Males]]\n[[Category:Pets]]\n[[Category:Silent characters]]\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:Disney characters]]\n[[Category:Disney Junior characters]]\n[[Category:TV Animation characters]]\n[[Category:Animated characters]]"
