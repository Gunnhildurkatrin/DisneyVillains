As a member of the Lion Guard, he was devoted to protecting the Pride Lands and the [[Circle of Life]], so he refused the order of his leader, Scar, to aid him in usurping [[Mufasa]].\n\n===Abilities===\n*'''Animal Speed:''' The Fastest is the fastest member in Scar's Lion Guard, as he can run really fast and endure long distances without any problems.\n\n*'''Animal Stamina:''' The Fastest has the highest stamina in Scar's Lion Guard, as he is she able to travel very long distances.\n\n== Role the series ==\n===''[[The Lion Guard: Return of the Roar]]''===\nHis only appearance was in a painting at the [[Lair of the Lion Guard|Lion Guard's lair]].\n\n===''[[Eye of the Beholder (The Lion Guard)|Eye of the Beholder]]''===\nAs in his previous appearance, he only appears as a painting during the scene when [[Rafiki]] checks on [[Ono]].\n\n===''[[Never Roar Again]]''===\nHis only appearance was in a painting at the Lion Guard's lair.\n\n===''[[The Rise of Scar|The Lion Guard: The Rise of Scar]]''===\nLike in the first season, he only appears in a non-physical form in the Lion Guard's lair during the part where [[Makini]] takes her Bakora staff,\n\n===''[[Cave of Secrets]]''===\nAs in previous appearances, only appearance was in a painting at the Lion Guard's lair.\n\n===''[[Battle for the Pride Lands]]''===\nThe only time the Fastest member appears in a physical form was during Scar's flashback in the musical number \"[[When I Led the Guard]]\".
