"{{Infobox_character|image = Sammy the Raccoon.png|rides = [[Country Bear Jamboree]]<br>[[Country Bear Christmas Special]]|occupation = Hat|home = Grizzly Hall|friends = [[Henry]]|voice = Bill Cole}}'''Sammy''' is [[Henry|Henry's]] living raccoon hat in the Disney theme park attraction [[Country Bear Jamboree]]. He acts like a coonskin cap for Henry and sings harmony with him for [[The Ballad of Davy Crockett]].\n\nIn the [[Country Bear Vacation Hoedown]], he is replaced by [[Randy the Skunk|a skunk named Randy]].\n==Other appearances==\nAround 2018, years after the Country Bear Theater was removed from Disneyland's Critter Country, Sammy was put onto a mural for recycling bins near Splash Mountain.\n\n[https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=844WrBxlEKE A video] in the Walt Disney World Goat Friends YouTube series is centered around [[Billy the Disney Goat|Billy]] the [[Big Thunder Mountain Railroad]] goat giving him an existential crisis when suggesting that as a hat on a hat, he should start wearing a hat with a raccoon on it and endlessly stacking more raccoons in hats on top of that for infinity.
