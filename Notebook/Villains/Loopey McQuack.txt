She, [[Birdie McQuack|her mother]], and [[Ripcord McQuack|her father]] make up a team of stunt-pilots called the ''Flying McQuacks''. It appears they make their living traveling to different air shows, preforming stunts. She makes her first appearance in ''[[DuckTales]]'' when the ''The Flying McQuacks ''are coming to [[Duckburg]] for an air show, where Launchpad is flying an experimental jet.
