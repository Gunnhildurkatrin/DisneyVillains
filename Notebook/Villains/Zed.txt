He sports a chili-bowl style haircut.\n\n===Personality===\nZed seems to be easy to intimidate.\n\n==Role in the series==\nHe works with [[Beck]]\u00a0and [[Mara]]\u00a0at [[Able]]'s Garage in [[Argon City]]. With Mara's help, he tracks Perl down to the docks where her gang, led by Gage, has taken the light cycle. After getting the baton back, Mara and Zed ride away on it, with the gang in pursuit.
