"{{Infobox character\n|name = Lefty\n|image = Normal 05robinsons193.jpg\n|films = ''[[Meet the Robinsons]]''<br>''[[Wreck-It Ralph (film)|Wreck-It Ralph]]'' (cameo)\n|personality = Mysterious\n|appearance = Large purple octopus with one red eye\n|alignment = Good\n|affiliations = The Robinsons\n|voice = [[Nathan Greno]]\n|friends = [[Lewis Robinson|Lewis]]\n|dislikes = Lewis thinking that he's a monster\n|enemies = [[Tiny the T-Rex]] (formerly)}}'''Lefty''' is a minor character from [[Walt Disney Animation Studios|Disney]]'s [[2007]] animated feature film ''[[Meet the Robinsons]]'', and he also makes a cameo appearance in [[Wreck-It Ralph (film)|''Wreck-It Ralph'']]. He is a one-eyed purple octopus who lives without water, and works as the Robinson butler.\n==Appearances==\n===''[[Meet the Robinsons]]''===\nLefty first appears when Lewis accidentally rings the doorbell, later When [[Bud Robinson]] shows [[Lewis Robinson]] around in the future, they come across Lefty again, who opens the door. Lewis thinks that he's a monster until Bud clears things up. Bud then asks Lefty where to get to the garage, so Lefty suggests asking [[Franny Robinson]].\n\nLater on, as the Robinson's were having dinner, Lefty unknowingly allows [[DOR-15|Doris]] into the house.
