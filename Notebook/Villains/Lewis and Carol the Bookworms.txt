"[[File:X240-rhl.jpg|thumb|360x360px]]\n'''Lewis and Carol the Bookworms''' are major, supporting characters in ''[[Sing Me a Story with Belle]]''. They inhabit [[Belle]]'s bookshop. Lewis is the yellow worm, and Carol is the green one.\n\nLewis is voiced by [[Jim Cummings]] (also the voice of Big Book in the same program), and Carol is voiced by the late [[Christine Cavanaugh]].\n\n==Trivia==\n*Both worms were named after the famous British author Lewis Carol, who wrote the original ''[[Alice in Wonderland]]'' book.\n{{Beauty and the Beast}}\n[[Category:Character stubs]]\n[[Category:Animated characters]]\n[[Category:Beauty and the Beast characters]]\n[[Category:Males]]\n[[Category:Females]]\n[[Category:TV Animation characters]]\n[[Category:Insects]]\n[[Category:Character pairs]]\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:Disney characters]]"
