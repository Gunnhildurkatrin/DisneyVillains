"{{Infobox character\n|image = Harold Hatchback.jpg\n|name = Harold Hatchback\n|shows = [[Goof Troop]]\n|voice = Patrick Duffy\n|occupation = Car dealer\n|alignment = Good \n|home = [[Spoonerville]]\n|family = Coop Hatchback (son)\n|enemies = [[Pete]]\n|likes = Putting celebrities in his commercials }}\n'''Harold Hatchback''' is one of [[Pete|Pete's]] rival car dealers and the father of [[Coop Hatchback]] from [[Disney Television Animation|Disney's]] 1992-1993 animated series, ''[[Goof Troop]].''\n\n\n\n\n==Background==\nHe, along with [[Pete]], tries to get a celebrity to appear in one of his commercials. He appears in only one episode.\n\n===Personality===\nHarold appears to have good business skills, as he is quite successful as a car dealer. He also is very competitive, as he tries to include celebrities in his advertisments when he can.\n\n===Physical appearance===\nHarold is an averagely built anthropomorphic dog with red fur. He wears a striped green tank top, blue shorts with light blue stripes down the sides, and yellow flip-flops. When doing commercials, he wears a red tuxedo with a black bow-tie.\n\n===Role in the series===\nHarold's only appearance is in the episode \"[[Buddy Building]]\". He tries to out-do [[Pete]] by finding celebrities to appear in his car commercials. Meanwhile, his son Coop Hatchback bonds with Pete's son, [[P.J.]], and [[Max Goof|Max]].\n\n==Trivia==\n*Harold uses [[Biff Fuddled|Biff Fuddled's]] animation model.\n*Harold's voice actor, Patrick Duffy, has a son who provided the voice for Harold's son.\n\n{{Goof Troop}}\n[[Category:Goof Troop characters]]\n[[Category:Males]]\n[[Category:Adults]]\n[[Category:Businesspeople]]\n[[Category:Disney characters]]\n[[Category:The Disney Afternoon characters]]\n[[Category:Dogs]]\n[[Category:Heroes]]\n[[Category:Animated characters]]\n[[Category:American characters]]\n[[Category:TV Animation characters]]\n[[Category:Mickey Mouse universe characters]]"
