"{{Infobox character\n|name = Officer Ester\n|films = ''[[Bolt (film)|Bolt]]''<br>''[[Big Hero 6]]'' (cameo)\n|voice = Kelly Hoover\n|alias = Ester\n|occupation = Security Guard\n|enemies = [[Mittens]], [[Bolt (character)|Bolt]], [[Rhino]]\n|image = EsterBoltACO.jpg\n|possessions = Flashlight<br>Pepper Spray\n|friends = [[Officer Lloyd]], [[Officer Martin]]}}'''Officer Ester''' is an animal control officer and is a minor antagonist in [[Walt Disney Animation Studios|Disney]]'s [[2008]] animated feature film, ''[[Bolt (film)|Bolt]]''. She is voiced by Kelly Hoover.\n\n==Role in the film==\nOfficer Ester was working at desk when Lloyd turned in for the night. Not knowing [[Bolt (character)|Bolt]] and [[Rhino]] are sneaking in to free [[Mittens]], she assumes the doors sliding open are Lloyd, and threatens to use her pepper spray on him. When she returns to find her truck blown up, she sprayed Martin when he came out, scaring her, thinking that he and Lloyd are the cause of it.\n\n==Trivia==\n*Ester made a ironic comment that she \"feels like she's working with toddlers\", knowing that she suffers from [[Wikipedia:Dwarfism|dwarfism]].\n*Ester makes a cameo in ''[[Big Hero 6]]'' as a portrait on [[Sergeant Gerson]]'s desk.\n\n{{CharacterStub}}\n{{Bolt}}\n[[Category:Females]]\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:Bolt characters]]\n[[Category:Animated characters]]\n[[Category:Disney characters]]\n[[Category:Characters in the Disney animated features canon]]\n[[Category:Neutral characters]]\n[[Category:Adults]]"
