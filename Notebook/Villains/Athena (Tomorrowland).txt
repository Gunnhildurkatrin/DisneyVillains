Casey has courage and determination and hope and that is what Tomorrowland needs\".\n\n==Role in the film==\nAthena is first seen at the [[1964 New York World's Fair]], where a young [[Frank Walker]] shows off his invention: an experimental Jet Pack, to [[David Nix]]. Nix denies Frank admittance to the invention contest he's judging. Athena, seeing the potential in Frank, gives him a[[1964 World's Fair Vintage Pin| Pin]], and tells him to follow herself and Nix.\n\nShe is next seen on [[It's a Small World]], with Nix and the rest of his guests. She is later seen welcoming Frank to [[Tomorrowland (location)| Tomorrowland]].\n\nMany years later, she gives a pin to [[Casey Newton]], whom she believes is the key to saving the world.\n\nShe is destroyed when Nix tries to shoot Frank, but she sacrifices herself to save him.
