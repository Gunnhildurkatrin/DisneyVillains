She is one of [[Jenny Szalinski|Jenny]]'s best friends who attends the party thrown by the kids behind their parents' backs. Although it's unclear, she has a crush on [[Vince (Honey, We Shrunk Ourselves)|Vince]] and was seen dancing with him at the party.
