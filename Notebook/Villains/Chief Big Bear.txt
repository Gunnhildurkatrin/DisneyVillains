"{{Infobox character\n|name = Chief Big Bear\n|image = the-lone-ranger-518.jpg\n|films = ''[[The Lone Ranger]]''\n|actor = Saginaw Grant\n|fullname = Chief Big Bear\n|occupation = Leader of the Comanche tribe\n|affiliations = His tribe\n|friends = [[John Reid]], [[Tonto]]\n|enemies = [[Captain Jay Fuller]]\n|dislikes = Dan's betrayal\n|status = Deceased\n|quote = \"Tonto told you these things?\"}}'''Chief Big Bear''' is the leader of the Comanches and a minor character from the [[2013]] Disney film adaptation of ''[[The Lone Ranger]]''. He is portrayed by Saginaw Grant.\n\n==Role in the film==\nAs [[John Reid]] and [[Tonto]] arrive at the Comanche Border area, the two end up in the Comanche tribe's village where John is taken to a tepee where Chief Big Bear is at. While talking to him, Big Bear gazes upon John wearing the mask, explaining to him about Tonto's flashback where Tonto as a child rescued two men from the desert and who asks him to know where silver was found to which suddenly, Tonto's village is burned down, noticing his tribe members killed to which he calls the evil spirits \"windigo\". After he finishes telling the story of progress to John, he explains to him that he claims his brother [[Dan Reid|Dan]] betrayed the community to which John informs that's not true, because Dan was murdered, and decides to make a deal with him that he can continue keeping Dan's promise in keeping the peace between the Comanche and the White Men.
