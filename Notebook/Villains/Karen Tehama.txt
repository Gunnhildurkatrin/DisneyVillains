Karen normally appears as a background character in most episodes of ''[[Fillmore!]]'' and she can be bribed to give lessons on forensics. She is voiced by [[Lauren Tom]].<ref>http://www.imdb.com/name/nm0866300/</ref><ref>http://www.behindthevoiceactors.com/tv-shows/Fillmore/Karen-Tehama/</ref>\n\n== Appearance ==\nKaren is an light-skinned Japanese-American teenage girl with black eyes. Her hair is styled in pigtails, are naturally black and dyed with pink streaks, but held by pink hairbows. She wears a pink short-sleeved shirt with a white cat design, orange sash, blue jeans with red cuffs, white socks and black sneakers.\n\n==History==\nKaren is mostly a background character so never a lot is known about her history other than the fact that she has a sharp interest in forensic science and she is Japanese-American. Karen has appeared in most episode and can generally depict the nature of a scene by the aftermath and the components of various crimes with her trusty powder, magnifying glass and microscope.\n\nIn one episode it is revealed that she can be bribed with a certain candy bar for lessons.
