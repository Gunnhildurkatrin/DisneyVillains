The youngest Madrigal, his gift allows him to communicate with animals.\n\n==Background==\nAntonio is [[Mirabel Madrigal|Mirabel]]'s youngest cousin who looks up to her, considering her a big sister. He is the youngest child of [[Pepa Madrigal|Pepa]] and [[F\u00e9lix Madrigal|F\u00e9lix]] and the younger brother of [[Dolores Madrigal|Dolores]] and [[Camilo Madrigal|Camilo]]. What makes Antonio unique amongst the Madrigal family is when he is able to talk to animals, to which amongst them are capybaras, toucans, hummingbirds, coatis, a tapir, and a jaguar, which assist their owner. In spite of that, he is capable of keeping animals in safe condition such as whenever Antonio's pet jaguar attempts to eat rats to which he tells the jaguar to avoid eating them, as they can help him.\n\n===Physical appearance===\nAntonio is a 5-year-old boy with dark skin, curly black hair and dark brown eyes. He wears a yellow and light orange stripped shirt with an orange long-sleeved vest over it, along with a red scarf. He has brown jeans and dark beige and orange shoes.\n\n===Powers and abilities===\nAntonio has the power to communicate with animals.\n\n==Appearances==\n==Gallery==\n<gallery position=\"center\" captionalign=\"center\" spacing=\"small\" widths=\"150\">\nEncanto-PromoPicture-04.jpg|\nEncanto family.jpg|\nEncanto-family-tree-4789609-949x1200.jpg|\nEncanto poster 2.JPG|\nEncanto british poster.jpg|\nEncanto Antonio Poster.jpg|\nLEGO Encanto - Antonio's Magical Door set.jpg|\nLEGO Encanto - The Madrigal House set.jpg|\nFunkoPOP-1149-AntonioMadrigal.png|[[Funko]] [[Funko/Disney/Pixar|POP!]] Antonio Madrigal\n</gallery>\n\n==Trivia==\n*In real life, it is illegal to keep exotic animals such as capybaras, jaguars, toucans, and other types of exotic animals like the ones that Antonio has as pets, as it is considered illegal trade.\n\n==References==\n{{reflist}}\n\n==External links==\n{{Encanto}}\n{{Disney Emoji Blitz}}\n[[pt-br:Antonio Madrigal]]\n[[Category:Encanto characters]]\n[[Category:Hispanic characters]]\n[[Category:Animated characters]]\n[[Category:Males]]\n[[Category:Characters in the Disney animated features canon]]\n[[Category:Kids]]\n[[Category:Heroes]]\n[[Category:Magic Users]]\n[[Category:Disney characters]]\n[[Category:Siblings]]\n[[Category:Nephews]]\n[[Category:Cousins]]"
