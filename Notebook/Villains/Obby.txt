"{{Infobox character\n|image = Atlantis-milos-return-disneyscreencaps.com-432.jpg\n|films = ''[[Atlantis: Milo's Return]]''\n|voice = [[Frank Welker]]\n|home = [[Atlantis (location)|Atlantis]]\n|family = Other lavadogs\n|owner = [[Milo Thatch]]<br>[[Kida Nedakh]]\n|likes = Rocks, sleeping in fire, having fun with his masters and comrades\n|enemies = [[Krakken (Atlantis: Milo's Return)|Krakken]]\n|dislikes = His masters and comrades in danger\n|powers = Speed, barks, growls\n|status = Active}}'''Obby'''\u00a0is a lovable obidalbedsugs (a.k.a. lavadog) and faithful companion of\u00a0[[Milo Thatch]] and [[Kida Nedakh]]. He appears in the direct-to-video sequel ''[[Atlantis: Milo's Return]]''.\n\n==Appearances==\n===''[[Atlantis: Milo's Return]]''===\nObby is first seen in [[Atlantis (location)|Atlantis]] with [[Kida Nedakh|Kida]] (now Queen, and married to [[Milo Thatch|Milo]]). After Kida\u00a0restores her city's former glory using\u00a0the [[Heart of Atlantis]], she and Obby\u00a0travel over to see Milo. Obby then runs over to greet Milo by jumping on top of\u00a0him and licking his face. Later, Obby accompanies Milo, Kida, [[Audrey Ramirez|Audrey]], [[Vincenzo Santorini|Vinnie]], [[Gaetan Moli\u00e9re|Mole]], and [[Joshua Sweet|Joshua]]\u00a0on three exciting and dangerous quests. He often helps out when the team is in trouble, such as trying to stop a huge leak in their sub pod\u00a0by chomping at the flowing water, gnawing through ropes when Milo and his\u00a0friends are tied up, and saving them from a vicious wolf by biting on the wolf's tail.
