After being captured by the Colonel and his men, Antoinette frees the ducks. When Antoinette tries to wed Donald, Donald tries to escape; only to get himself caught by two of the Colonel's specialists: [[Horst and Klebb]], who then tried to pull him apart. Antoinette saves Donald by jumping and buttslam landing flattened off-screen on them and kisses Donald after he thanks her for saving him.
