She has a twin sister, Anginette \"Angie\" Walker, who does not appear in the films. She has a warm personality, and is strong-willed and speaks her mind. Her interests include horror movies, animals, and one day becoming a doctor. She is also interested in fashion, history, and acting. She is portrayed by [[Kiely Williams]].\n\n\n\n==Background==\nAqua and her twin moved with their father to New York from Texas, and had a southern accent in the first film. She has three pets: two guinea pigs and a dog.
