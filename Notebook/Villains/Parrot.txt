"{{Infobox character \n|image = Parrot.jpg\n|name = Parrot\n|shows=''[[The Wonderful World of Mickey Mouse]]''\n|shorts = ''[[Steamboat Willie]]''<br>''[[The Barn Dance]]''<br>''[[The Gorilla Mystery]]''\n|games = [[Mickey Mania: The Timeless Adventures of Mickey Mouse]]\n|voice=[[Walt Disney]] (original)<br>[[Bill Farmer]] ([[The Wonderful World of Mickey Mouse]])\n|animator = [[Wilfred Jackson]]\n|friends = [[Mickey Mouse]] (sometimes), [[Minnie Mouse]], [[Daisy Duck]], [[Clarabelle Cow]]\n|enemies = [[Mickey Mouse]], [[Tuppence]]\n|likes = Annoying Mickey, music, dancing\n|dislikes = Getting thrown into water\n|quote = \u201cI hope ya don\u2019t feel hurt, big boy!\u201d\n}}\n\nThe '''Parrot''' is a character that first appeared in ''[[Steamboat Willie]]'' on [[November 18]], [[1928]]. Originally one of the livestock aboard [[Pete]]\u2019s [[Steamboat|steamboat]], the Parrot finds amusement in cracking wise at [[Mickey Mouse]]\u2019s expense.\n\n==Appearances==\n===''[[Steamboat Willie]]''===\nThe parrot appears mocking Mickey, after falling down the stairs on a bar of soap, and begins to laugh, which provokes Mickey to throw the bucket of water on the bird. It squawks for help as Mickey walks away.\nThe Parrot is last seen flying into the window, beginning to mock and laugh at him again, provoking Mickey to throw a half-peeled potato at him, sending the parrot splashing into the river. Triumphant, Mickey begins to laugh and brings the picture to a close.\n===''[[The Barn Dance]]''===\nThe parrot appears briefly as Minnie's pet, looking unamused at her suitors Pete and Mickey. In the colored version of the short, is revealed his feathers are green.\n\n===''[[The Wonderful World of Mickey Mouse]]''===\nThe Parrot appears in the episode \u201c[[An Ordinary Date]]\u201d, as a singing telegram from Minnie to Mickey. Before the Parrot can deliver his message, however, he crosses paths with one of the [[Tuppence]] birds with whom the Parrot holds a grudge. The Parrot then engages in a fight with the Tuppence birds, ruining Minnie\u2019s and Mickey's surprise.\n\n==Video games==\n===''[[Mickey Mania: The Timeless Adventures of Mickey Mouse]]''===\nThe parrot appears as a regular enemy in the Steamboat Willie world.
