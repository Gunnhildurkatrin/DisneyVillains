"\nThe Overtakers are a group of Disney villains that conspire to conquer the Disney Parks in the ''[[Kingdom Keepers]]''\u00a0novels. The main and biggest group is lead by [[Chernabog]] though there are at least two other independent groups lead by [[Jafar]] and [[Shan Yu]] respectively.\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:Villains]]\n[[Category:Kingdom Keepers characters]]"
