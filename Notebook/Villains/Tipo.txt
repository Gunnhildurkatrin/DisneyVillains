He is the oldest son of [[Pacha]] and [[Chicha]].\n==Appearances==\n===''[[The Emperor's New Groove]]''===\nHe is the son of [[Pacha]]. While his dad is taking [[Kuzco]] to the palace, he has a vision where his dad went over a waterfall. His mother calms him down. After [[Chaca|his sister]] tells them about her dream, the two siblings get into an argument over which dream could actually happen. This scene implies that they are psychic.\n\n===''[[Kronk's New Groove]]''===\nTIpo appears in this movie as one of the campers at Camp Chippamunka. Toward the end of the memory, he follows [[Kronk]]'s advice which was, \"Do whatever it takes to win.\" He put itching powder in [[Ms. Birdwell]]'s troop's hand chalk. When Birdwell finds out and scolds Tipo, Kronk defends him but also alienates his true love.
