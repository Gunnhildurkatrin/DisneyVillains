"[[File:Finch.jpg|thumb|250px]]'''Finch''' (portrayed by [[Jace Norman]]) is one of [[Luke Ross|Luke]]'s best friends in the episode \"[[Are You Cooler than a 5th Grader?]]\". He has blond hair and likes to play baseball, and also has a nervous rash. He likes to make fun of [[Ravi Ross|Ravi]] when he meets him at school, not knowing that Luke and Ravi are brothers. He and Luke are enemies with the school bully, Dale.
