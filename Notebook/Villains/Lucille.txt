She is voiced by\u00a0[[Renee Olstead]].<ref>http://www.behindthevoiceactors.com/tv-shows/Fillmore/Lucille/</ref>\n\n\n\n==Background==\n===Personality===\nSince Lucille has a responsibility to the Praline Committee, the missing pralines cause her to have a great concern or be in distress. She's also dissatisfied with their absence. \n\n===Physical Appearance===\nShe is a teen caucasian girl with long, blonde hair and pale blue, beady eyes. Her apparel includes a powder blue cowboy (or cowgirl) hat and aquamarine mini dress.\n\n==South of Friendship, North of Honor==\nLucille appears in\u00a0<ref>South of Friendship, North of Honour</ref>. She's upset after finding the vault where the pralines were kept empty and they're now gone, and she tells [[Wayne Liggett|Wayne]] about it. The\u00a0Tennessee Safety Patrol\u00a0are brought in to search inside the vault. As she had been keeping a watchful eye on it, she says that she didn't see anybody who could've taken them from there. By nearly the episode's end, after the pralines are found, she awards Wayne with a medal at a ceremony honoring him for solving the case and getting the pralines returned.\n\n== Episode Appearance ==\n* South of Friendship, North of Honor\n\n==References==\n<references/>\n{{Fillmore!}}\n[[Category:Fillmore characters]]\n[[Category:Disney characters]]\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:Females]]\n[[Category:Teenagers]]\n[[Category:Heroines]]\n[[Category:American characters]]\n[[Category:TV Animation characters]]\n[[Category:Animated characters]]\n[[Category:Students]]"
