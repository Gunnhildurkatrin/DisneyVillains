Sniff, sniff \u2018I have an idea!\u2019 What new things will you try?|CookieAnn's backstory.}}\n'''CookieAnn''' (\u30af\u30c3\u30ad\u30fc\u30fb\u30a2\u30f3) (formerly just '''Cookie''' (\u30af\u30c3\u30ad\u30fc) is a dog that appears at [[Hong Kong Disneyland]]. She is part of the ''Duffy and Friends'' line.<ref name=\"Twitter\">{{Cite web|format=Tweet|website=Twitter|url=https://twitter.com/LeDisneyMoi/status/1011170419779530752|title=\u203c\ufe0f BREAKING NEWS \u203c\ufe0f \ud83d\udc96\ud83d\udc9b Please welcome the newest member of the #Duffy family, \"#Cookie, A Disney friend of Duffy\"! Officially arriving on July 3, 2018 at #HongKongDisneyland \ud83d\udc9b\ud83d\udc96|date=June 25, 2018}}</ref>\n\n''Cookie'' was renamed as ''CookieAnn'' on October 9, 2019, with the new name being introduced in a video where Cookie wanted to have a double name like her friends [[ShellieMay the Disney Bear|ShellieMay]] and [[StellaLou]].<ref>https://www.youtube.com/watch?time_continue=27&v=p3BbXX0GzOw</ref> She made her debut at [[Tokyo DisneySea]] on December 26, 2019, at [[Aulani]] on January 20, 2020 and at [[Shanghai Disneyland]] on January 26, 2020.\n\n==Background==\n===Story===\n====[[Hong Kong Disneyland]]====\nAs Duffy was about to eat his cotton candy, he saw a mountain of waffles heading toward him! \"Ahhh!\" The waffles and his cotton candy flew through the air.\n\nCookie, the girl, caught his treat. ''Sniff, sniff'' \"I have an idea! Try a cotton candy waffle sandwich!\"\n\nDuffy wasn't sure. \"Is it...good?\" \"My nose knows!\" \"Wow, this is yummy!\" Cookie was happy to share a new recipe with a new friend.\n\n====Tokyo DisneySea====\nDuffy first met CookieAnn when he was enjoying some delicious cotton candy. He accidentally bumped into her as she was carrying a huge stack of waffles, and all their treats flew into the air! Luckily, CookieAnn caught them, and turned the ingredients into a cotton candy waffle sandwich. The accident turned out to be a recipe for friendship!\n\n==[[Walt Disney Parks and Resorts|Disney Parks]]==\nCookieAnn debuted at Hong Kong Disneyland on [[July 3]], [[2018]].<ref name=\"Twitter\"/> She can be found outside [[Main Street Cinema (Hong Kong Disneyland)|Main Street Cinema]] as a walk-around character to greet guests.
