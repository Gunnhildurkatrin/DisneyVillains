"{{Infobox character|image = Rodriguez.jpg}}'''Brooke Nichols''' is a character from the American\u00a0''[[As the Bell Rings]]''\u00a0miniseries. Brooke is the smartest out of the gang. She is portrayed by\u00a0Gabriella Rodriguez.\n\n==Character Story==\n[[File:Brooke.png|thumb|left]]Brooke is the smartest main character out of the seven that have appeared on the show. In the first episode of season 2, it is portrayed that Lexi is just as smart as her. She often disagrees with Tiffany as Tiffany is possibly the most flaky girl in school. Her talent is dancing and being funny. She is best friends with Charlotte, Tiffany and later with Lexi. She also has a crush on Toejam. She is usually called a nerd and a geek by the school bully, until Toejam sticks up for her, proving that he likes her and wants what's best for her.
