"{{Infobox_character\n|image = LennyTheCab.jpg\n|name = Lenny the Cab\n|rides = [[Roger Rabbit's Car Toon Spin]]\n|voice = [[Brad Abrell]]\n|inspiration = Benny the Cab from [[Who Framed Roger Rabbit]]\n|occupation = Cab driver (and car) for the Toontown Cab Co.\n|affiliations = Toontown Cab Co.\n|home = Toon Town\n|family = [[Benny the Cab]] (twin-cousin)\n|friends = [[Roger Rabbit]]\n|enemies = [[Toon Patrol]]\n}}\n'''Lenny the Cab''' is [[Benny the Cab|Benny the Cab's]] twin cousin who works for the [[Toontown (Who Framed Roger Rabbit)|Toontown]] Cab Company.\n\n==Description==\n\n=== Background ===\nLenny is the Cab is a cartoon taxi-car who is the identical-twin cousin of Benny the Cab.  While Benny appears to be somewhat freelance, Lenny is an employee of the Toontown Cab Company.\n\n== Appearances ==\n\n=== [[Roger Rabbit's Car Toon Spin]] ===\nLenny serves as the ride vehicle for the Roger Rabbit's Car Toon Spin attractions at [[Disneyland]] and [[Tokyo Disneyland]].  Lenny was employed to take humans on a tour of Toon Town only for the tour to be intercepted by the [[Toon Patrol]], attempting kidnap [[Jessica Rabbit]] and dousing the roads with the [[Dip]].\n\nBenny then teams up with [[Roger Rabbit|Roger]] to try and pursue the Weasels through toon-town, bringing the guests along until the day is finally saved.\n\n\n==Trivia==\n*Lenny being a \"twin cousin\" is made to reflect the nonsensical realities of toons from the Roger Rabbit franchise.\u00a0{{Who Framed Roger Rabbit}}\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:Disney characters]]\n[[Category:Theme park characters]]\n[[Category:Who Framed Roger Rabbit characters]]\n[[Category:Vehicles]]\n[[Category:Transportation]]\n[[Category:Anthropomorphic characters]]\n[[Category:Objects]]\n[[Category:Cousins]]"
