"'''Paul Tate'''\u00a0is an unseen character who is mentioned in\u00a0''[[Honey, I Shrunk the Kids]]''.\n[[Amy Szalinski|Amy]] brings him up once in a while, such as whether he invited him to a dance party, and that at 5:00, she would meet him at a mall. It was mentioned that he and some girl (presumably one named Barbara) had broken up because he disagreed when she said she was God. Later, in a phone call between Amy and [[Diane Szalinski|Diane]], the latter asks the former if Paul asked her to the dance. And finally, when the kids are shrunk and the phone rings, Amy threatens to kill [[Nick Szalinski|Nick]] if it's Paul.\n\n{{Honey, I Shrunk the Kids}}\n[[Category:Honey, I Shrunk the Kids characters]]\n[[Category:Unseen characters]]\n[[Category:Males]]\n[[Category:Teenagers]]\n[[Category:Disney characters]]"
