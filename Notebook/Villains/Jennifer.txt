"[[File:Jennifer_(Avalon_High).jpg|thumb]]'''Jennifer''' is a character of [[Disney Channel Original Movie|Disney Channel's Original Movie]] ''[[Avalon High]]''. She is portrayed by Molly C. Quinn.\n==History==\nShe is a redheaded cheerleader who corresponds to Queen Guinevere in the legend. She originally dated Arthur William \"Will\" Wagner while cheating on him with Lance, but they broke up when Marco revealed their secret while Will, Allie, Marco, Jennifer, and Lance were out sailing.\n==Personality==\nJennifer is described as one of those \"perky little blondes who seem to know instinctively what color eye-shadow looks best on them.\" Although she is a bit of the stereotypical cheerleader, she is also a very nice person, and is very difficult not to like.\n[[Category:Avalon High characters]]\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:Disney characters]]\n[[Category:Females]]\n[[Category:Teenagers]]\n[[Category:Lovers]]\n[[Category:American characters]]\n[[Category:Live-action characters]]\n[[Category:Cheerleaders]]\n[[Category:Heroines]]\n[[Category:Tritagonists]]\n[[Category:Disney Channel characters]]"
