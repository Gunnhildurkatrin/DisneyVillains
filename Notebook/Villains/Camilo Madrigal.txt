"{{Upcoming}}\n{{Infobox character\n|image = Camilo Madrigal.jpg\n|films = [[Encanto]]\n|voice = Rhenzy Feliz\n|home = Casa Madrigal, [[Encanto (location)|Encanto]]\n|powers = Shape-shifting\n|parents = [[Pepa Madrigal]] (mother)<br>[[F\u00e9lix Madrigal]] (father)\n|siblings = [[Dolores Madrigal]] (older sister)<br>[[Antonio Madrigal]] (younger brother)\n|family = Pedro Madrigal \u2020 (maternal grandfather)<br>[[Alma Madrigal]] (maternal grandmother)<br>[[Bruno Madrigal]] (uncle)<br>[[Julieta Madrigal]] (aunt)<br>[[Agust\u00edn Madrigal]] (uncle)<br>[[Isabela Madrigal]] (cousin)<br>[[Luisa Madrigal]] (cousin)<br>[[Mirabel Madrigal]] (cousin)\n|status = Active\n}}\n'''Camilo Madrigal''' is a character of the upcoming [[Walt Disney Animation Studios]] feature film ''[[Encanto]]''.\n\n==Background==\nCamilo is [[Mirabel Madrigal|Mirabel]]'s second youngest cousin, he is the middle child of [[Pepa Madrigal|Pepa]] and [[F\u00e9lix Madrigal|F\u00e9lix]], younger brother of [[Dolores Madrigal|Dolores]] and older brother of [[Antonio Madrigal|Antonio]]. Camilo is described as someone who \"doesn't quite know who he is yet\", he sometimes uses his shape-shifting gift to mock his relatives.\n\n===Physical appearance===\nCamilo is a young Colombian teenager boy, with a brown afro and brown eyes. He wears a white long-sleeved shirt with a yellow poncho with darker streaks, black pants and brown sandals.\n\n===Powers and abilities===\nCamilo has the power of shape-shifting; he can assume the form of any living human he knows.
