"{{Infobox character\n|name = Becky Thatcher\n|image = 740full-tom-and-huck-screenshot.jpg\n|films = ''[[Tom and Huck]]''\n|actress = [[Rachael Leigh Cook]]\n|fullname = Rebecca Thatcher\n|alignment = Good\n|friends = [[Tom Sawyer]]\n|likes = Tom Sawyer|rides = [[Tom Sawyer Island]]|inspiration = Becky Thatcher from the works of [[Mark Twain]]}}'''Becky Thatcher''' is a literary character from the works of [[Mark Twain]].\n\n\n\n== Background ==\n\n=== Development History ===\nBecky is a literary character created by Sam \"Mark Twain\" Clemens who first appeared in his 1876 love, ''The Adventures of Tom Sawyer''. She is the girlhood crush of [[Tom Sawyer]] and a considerably uptight and stuck-up yet mischievous girl. Tom's story largely revolves around his on-again/off-again puppy-love romance with her as she learns the virtue of his cunning.\n\n== Appearances ==\n\n=== Film ===\n\n==== [[Tom and Huck]] ====\nBecky is the tritagonist of this film based on ''The Adventures of Tom Sawyer'', reprising her role from the book.\n\n=== [[Disney Parks]] ===\nBecky made her first Disney appearances at the Disney theme-parks, before having received any appearances in other media.\n\n==== [[Main Street Cinema]] ====\nA posting from the [[Disneyland Casting Agency]] reads, \"''[[Mark Twain Riverboat|Riverboat]] Pilots: Cast sought for classic Mark Twain costume adventure, '''B. Thatcher''' Productions''\".\n\n==== [[Tom Sawyer Island]] ====\nBecky is references throughout the island. Notably there is a fence with white paint on it reading, \"Tom X Becky\".\n\n==== [[Theme Park Characters|Meet 'n' Greets]]\u00a0====\nIn the early days of [[Disneyland]], Becky was among the Mark Twain characters who would appear portrayed by cast-members.\n\n== Gallery ==\n<gallery>\nWalt Disney Tom Sawyer Island.jpg|[[Walt Disney]] with cast-members playing Tom and Becky on Tom Sawyer Island\n</gallery>\n\n{{Tom Sawyer}}\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:Disney characters]]\n[[Category:Live-action characters]]\n[[Category:Tom and Huck characters]]\n[[Category:Females]]\n[[Category:Kids]]\n[[Category:Tritagonists]]\n[[Category:Heroines]]\n[[Category:Theme park characters]]\n[[Category:Characters in Disney parks]]"
