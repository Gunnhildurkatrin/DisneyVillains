"[[File:Ocean.png|thumb]]'''Ocean''' is a recurring character in ''[[Liv & Maddie|Liv and Maddie]]''. She is portrayed by [[Cozi Zuehlsdorff]].\n\n==Personality==\nOcean is a positive girl who likes [[Liv Rooney|Liv]] without knowing she was on a TV show. She usually refers to people by their full name. She seems to love learning and school. She doesn't have much friends, but also seems to care for the environment, and she is kind and really wants to help.\n\n==Biography==\n===Background===\nOcean lives on a sunflower farm with no TV, therefore she didn't know who Liv was.\n===History===\nIn \"[[Steal-a-Rooney]]\", [[Maddie Rooney|Maddie]] puts Ocean in Liv's direction to be her friend. In Kang-A-Rooney she and Liv scare Maddie.\n\n==Physical Appearance==\nOcean is a girl with brown hair and brown eyes and usually wears her hair in braids.\n\n==Strengths and Weaknesses==\n===Strength===\n''To be added in.''\n===Weaknesses===\n\nOcean's weakness is making friends. \u00a0 \u00a0 \u00a0 \u00a0 \u00a0 \u00a0 \u00a0 \u00a0 \u00a0 \u00a0 \u00a0 \u00a0 \u00a0 \u00a0\u00a0\n\n==Relationships==\n===Friends===\n====[[Liv Rooney]]====\n\nLiv is Ocean's friend.
