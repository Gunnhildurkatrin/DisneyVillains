Peg-Leg Peg is a pirate ghost who first appeared in the episode \"[[Pirate Ghost Story]]\".\n\n==Background==\nNot much of Peg-Leg Peg's passing is revealed. But during her life, she sailed the vast Never Sea with her beloved [[Captain Treasure Tooth]]. At sometime she passed away and ascended into Pirate Paradise, still waiting for Treasure Tooth.\n==Appearances==\n===''[[Jake and the Never Land Pirates]]''===\nPeg-Leg Peg is briefly seen as a painting aboard Treasure Tooth's ship as he informs [[Jake (Pirate)|Jake]] and his crew his quest to find his golden figurehead so he can be reunited with Peg in Pirate Paradise. Peg is not seen until the end of the episode reuniting with Treasure Tooth aboard the Spirit of the Seas after discovering his figurehead once more.
