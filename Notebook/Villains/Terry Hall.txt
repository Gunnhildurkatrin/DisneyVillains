"{{Infobox_character|image=Terry_Hall.jpg|films=''[[The Mighty Ducks]]''|shows=|actor=Jussie Smollett|affiliations=Mighty Ducks (formerly)|status=Alive|parents=Mr. Hall|siblings=[[Jesse Hall]]}}\n'''Terry Hall''' is a hockey player from [[Minneapolis]], [[Minnesota]]. His first and only appearance was in ''[[The Mighty Ducks]]''. He played for the [[District 5 Ducks]], along with his older brother [[Jesse Hall]]. Terry plays at the forward position and wears the number 1.
