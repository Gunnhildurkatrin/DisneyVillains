Cassie usually never breaks the rules, but she has one exception: Bitty, her pet glowfur \u2013 which she is not allowed to have at school!''\n===Personality===\nCassie is extremely imaginative, super smart, loyal, highly intelligent, extremely logical, shy, friendly, artistic, and very intuitive. On the other hand, she can be distrustful, jealous, moody, bragging, hypersensitive, and brooding.\n\n== Appearance ==\nCassie\u2019s theme color is light pink. She is a teenage girl with pale skin, light pink eyes, lips, and hair tied up into two mini buns. Her outfit is a light pink dress with beige brown sneakers. Her accessories are two star clips that hold her buns together and her star-charmed glasses.\n\nOn Earth, Cassie\u2019s appearance changes to look like a normal human. Her hair color changes to light blonde. Her skin loses its sparkle and her outfit changes to look more human.
