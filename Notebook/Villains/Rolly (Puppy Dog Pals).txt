He is constantly joking and making the best of moments, and loves a good joke. He is not as smart as his brother Bingo, you could say, but makes up for it with his hilarious personality. He is very kind to others. He is playful, funny, clumsy, easily distracted, and overall a great pup. \n\n=== Physical Appearance ===\nRolly is a pug with a beige skin tone-ish color. You could call it tumbleweed. He has darker/brown spots at his ears and muzzle, and blue eyes.
