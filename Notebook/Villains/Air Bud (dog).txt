He plays a different sport in each film (basketball, football, soccer, baseball, and volleyball, respectively). In ''Air Bud: World Pup'', he becomes the father of five puppies: [[Budderball]], [[B-Dawg]], [[Buddha]], [[MudBud]], and [[RoseBud]], though none of them are given actual names until the first ''[[Air Buddies]]'' movie. He only appeared in the first two films of those series.\n\n==''Air Bud'' films==\nThe first two films in the franchise were released in theaters, while later entries were direct-to-DVD releases.\n\n*''[[Air Bud]]'' ([[1997]]) - Josh meets a golden retriever and finds out that he can play basketball.\n*''[[Air Bud: Golden Receiver]]'' ([[1998]]) - Josh's golden retriever learns to play football.\n*''[[Air Bud: World Pup]]'' ([[2000]]) - Josh and Emma discover that Buddy's ball-playing skills apply to soccer.\n*''[[Air Bud: Seventh Inning Fetch]]'' ([[2002]]) - Buddy makes the baseball team and becomes a star player.\n*''[[Air Bud: Spikes Back]]'' ([[2003]]) - Buddy tackles volleyball.\n\n==''Air Buddies'' films==\nAir Bud has a less important role in ''Air Buddies'' and ''Snow Buddies''. The films were all straight-to-DVD (later Blu-ray) releases, except for the first film that remains a DVD only release.\n\n*''[[Air Buddies]]'' ([[2006]]) - He and his mate Molly were dognapped by [[Grim and Denning]] for [[Selkirk Tander|Selkirk]]'s greedy purposes. It's up to their puppies (and other animals) to come to the rescue.\n*''[[Snow Buddies]]'' ([[2008]]) - His final appearance in which he and Molly go to [[Alaska]] to find their puppies.\n*''[[Santa Buddies]]'' ([[2009]]) - Although he doesn't appear in this film, he is briefly mentioned (though not by name) by B-Dawg at the beginning.\n\n==Other films==\nIn ''[[Ralph Breaks the Internet]]'', Air Bud is mentioned by the [[First Order Stormtroopers|Stormtroopers]] when they lost track of [[Vanellope von Schweetz|Vanellope]], thinking she was hiding in the ''Air Bud'' pavilion.\n\n==Gallery==\n<gallery position=\"center\" spacing=\"small\">\nair-bud-disneyscreencaps.com-108.jpg\nair-bud-disneyscreencaps.com-1892.jpg\nair-bud-disneyscreencaps.com-2569.jpg\nair-bud-disneyscreencaps.com-6319.jpg\nair-bud-disneyscreencaps.com-6586.jpg\nair-bud-disneyscreencaps.com-7194.jpg\nair-bud-disneyscreencaps.com-7207.jpg\nair-bud-disneyscreencaps.com-9388.jpg\nair-bud-disneyscreencaps.com-9456.jpg\nair-bud-disneyscreencaps.com-9475.jpg\nair-bud-disneyscreencaps.com-10263.jpg\nair-bud-disneyscreencaps.com-11117.jpg\n</gallery>\n\n{{Air Bud}}\n[[Category:Disney characters]]\n[[Category:Dogs]]\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:Athletes]]\n[[Category:Protagonists]]\n[[Category:Air Bud]]\n[[Category:Lovers]]\n[[Category:Parents]]\n[[Category:Live-action characters]]\n[[Category:Males]]\n[[Category:Air Bud characters]]\n[[Category:Spouses]]\n[[Category:Anthropomorphic characters]]\n[[Category:Pets]]\n[[Category:Characters who break the Fourth Wall]]"
