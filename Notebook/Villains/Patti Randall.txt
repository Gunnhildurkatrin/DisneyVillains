(1965)|That Darn Cat!]]'' and [[That Darn Cat (1997)|the remake]].\n\n==Role in the films==\n===1965 film===\nIn the original film, Patti lives with her sister, [[Ingrid Randall|Ingrid]], while their parents are away and is dating a surfer boy who does nothing but surf and eat strange sandwiches. She loves her cat, [[D.C.]], and becomes part of a kidnapping investigation when she discovers him wearing a wristwatch with a message that says \"Help!\" around his neck. She gets FBI agent [[Zeke Kelso]] to investigate and leads to misadventures with her darn cat.\n\n===Remake===\nIn the 1997 remake, Patti is portrayed as a typical gothic teenager (portrayed by [[Christina Ricci]]) who dresses in nothing but black and her only friend is her cat, D.C. Unlike her 1965 counterpart, she is a lonely teenage girl who lives with her parents who are bright and cheerful much to her dismay.\n\nShe becomes part of a kidnapping investigation when she discovers D.C. wearing a wristwatch with a message that says \"Help.\" She hires Agent Zeke Kelso (portrayed by [[Doug E. Doug]]) to investigate.\n\nIn the end, she changes her ways, starts dressing in brighter colors, makes new friends, and shows more respect for the town she lives in.\n\n==Gallery==\n<gallery position=\"center\" captionalign=\"center\" widths=\"150\" spacing=\"small\">\nD.C. with Petti.jpg|Hayley Mills as Patti in the 1965 film\nChristina Ricci as Patti 1.jpg|Christina Ricci as Patti in the 1997 film\n1649.jpg|Patti and D.C. in the ice cream shop\nUntitled Patti Randall, and Her sister.png|Patti and Ingrid\nTumblr nc4kwgkAPP1qhcrb0o1 1280.jpg\nTumblr nb96fbLSUh1qiceiuo1 1280.jpg\nTumblr_ncwkylivtV1qhcrb0o1_1280.jpg\n070301030725_l7kh2fbomn.jpg\nTumblr nl2iezAESN1qiceiuo1 1280.jpg\nTdc03.jpg\nTdc11.jpg\n</gallery>\n\n{{That Darn Cat!}}{{DEFAULTSORT:Randall, Patti}}\n[[Category:Disney characters]]\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:Heroines]]\n[[Category:Protagonists]]\n[[Category:That Darn Cat! characters]]\n[[Category:Females]]\n[[Category:Teenagers]]\n[[Category:Lovers]]\n[[Category:Live-action characters]]\n[[Category:American characters]]\n[[Category:Siblings]]\n[[Category:Detectives]]"
