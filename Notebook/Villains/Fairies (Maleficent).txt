They are all known to be friendly creatures and warmly welcome Aurora when Maleficent brings her to the forest.\n\n*'''Wallerbog''' - The Wallerbog's all tiny elf like creatures with small trunks. They are known to be quite playful and enjoy mud fights.\n*'''Mushroom Fairy''' - Elderly elf looking fairies covered in mushrooms.\n*'''Water Fairy''' - Tiny blue mermaid like fairies that glow and fly and live in the waters of their forest home.\n*'''Leaf fairy''' -
