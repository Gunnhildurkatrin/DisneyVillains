By 2015, the channel had accumulated more than four million subscribers and more than 700 million total views. Her most popular videos include her cinnamon challenge video, which has accumulated more than 53 million views, and \"My Push up Bra will help me get my man\" video, with more than 25 million views. She gained wide notice when her blog about meeting [[Elijah Wood]] was mentioned by the actor during a 2011 interview on Jimmy Kimmel Live!.
