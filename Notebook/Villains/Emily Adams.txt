"[[File:Emily_Adams.jpg|thumb]]\n'''Emily Adams''' is a minor character of [[Disney Channel Original Series]], ''[[Dog With a Blog]]''. She is portrayed by [[Kathryn Newton]].\n\n==History==\nShe is the [[The Hawk|Hawk]]'s niece and [[Tyler James|Tyler]]'s ex-girlfriend.\n\n==Personality==\nShe is adventurous and has personality similar to Tyler. She is very laid-back and isn't afraid to break the rules.\n\n==Physical Appearance==\nEmily usually has long, straight blonde hair. Her hair has noticeably changed since the events of \"Tyler Gets a Grillfriend\", where she had wavy, medium length hair. She has brown eyes and fair skin.\n\n==Episode Appearances==\n*Tyler Gets a Grillfriend\n*Lost In Stanslation\n*Love Ty-Angle\n*I Want My Nikki Back, Nikki Back, Nikki Back\n\n==Trivia==\n*The Hawk is her uncle.\n*She is skilled at BMX riding and was nicknamed \"The Beast\" because of it.\n*She and Tyler broke up in Love Ty-Angle.\n*[[Nikki Ortiz]] was jealous of her when she and Tyler were dating.\n*In ''Love Ty-Angle'', she and Tyler favor strawberry-banana smoothies.\n\n\n{{DEFAULTSORT:Adams, Emily}}\n{{Dog With a Blog}}\n[[Category:Dog with a Blog characters]]\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:Disney characters]]\n[[Category:Females]]\n[[Category:Lovers]]\n[[Category:American characters]]\n[[Category:Teenagers]]\n[[Category:Live-action characters]]\n[[Category:Students]]\n[[Category:Nieces]]\n[[Category:Athletes]]\n[[Category:Disney Channel characters]]"
