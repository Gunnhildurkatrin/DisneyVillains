She \"knows everyone's dirt\" via enhanced hearing.\n\n===Physical appearance===\nDolores is a young Colombian woman with curly brown hair and brown eyes. She wears a white and yellow shirt with golden-colored trimming on it and a necklace with a red ribbon and a golden gem with a ruby on the middle of it. She wears an embroidered red long skirt with yellow streaks, red sandals and heart-shaped golden earrings.
