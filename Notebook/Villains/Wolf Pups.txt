"{{Infobox character\n|image = Jungle-book-disneyscreencaps.com-237.jpg\n|films = ''[[The Jungle Book]]''<br>[[The Jungle Book (2016 film)|''The Jungle Book'' (2016)]]\n|personality = Playful, friendly, lovable\n|appearance = Small, slender gray wolf pups, yellow eyes\n|occupation = Mowgli's adoptive siblings\n|alignment = Good\n|affiliations = Wolf Pack\n|goal = To play with Mowgli\n|home = [[Jungle (Jungle Book)|Jungle of India]]\n|family = [[Rama]] (father)<br>[[Raksha]] (mother)<br>[[Mowgli]] (adopted brother)\n|friends = Their \"foster brother\" [[Mowgli]], their mother [[Raksha]], their father [[Rama]], [[Akela]], the Wolf Council, [[Bagheera]], [[Baloo]]\n|enemies = [[Shere Khan]]\n|likes = Fun, playing\n|dislikes = Shere Khan\n|status=Active}}The '''Wolf Pups''' are [[Rama]] and [[Raksha]]'s children, [[Mowgli]]'s adoptive siblings, and minor characters in Disney's [[1967]] film the ''[[The Jungle Book|Jungle Book]]''.\n\n==Appearances==\n===''[[The Jungle Book]]''===\nThey\u00a0are first seen when [[Bagheera]] brings an infant [[Mowgli]] to their cave. The man-cub's cries alert [[Raksha]] and [[Rama]], the wolf pups' parents. They notice Mowgli and, knowing the man-cub will die if not cared for, take him inside the cave. Many years later, Mowgli is almost 10 years old, The wolf pups are seen ten\u00a0years later as young adults with pups of their own, and Mowgli has grown up as well. Mowgli howls to his adult wolf siblings, who jump on top of the man-cub and lick his face. They are not seen throughout the rest of the film after that.\n\n===[[The Jungle Book (2016 film)|''The Jungle Book'' (2016)]]===\nThe wolf pups make major appearances in the live-action remake of ''The Jungle Book''.\n\n==Trivia==\n*The scene where three young wolves look across a rock and the scene where four more jump off the rock are [[List of recycled animation in Disney movies|recycled animation]] from ''[[One Hundred and One Dalmatians]]'', where the [[Dalmatian Puppies|Dalmatian puppies]] are watching [[Thunderbolt]] on television.\n**Also, the scene where Mowgli is licked by the young adult wolves is recycled animation from the ''[[The Sword in the Stone|Sword in the Stone]]'', where [[Arthur Pendragon|Arthur]] is licked by the castle dogs Tiger and
