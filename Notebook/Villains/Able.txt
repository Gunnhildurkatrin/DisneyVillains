He owns a successful [[Light Cycles|light cycle]]\u00a0garage, called Able's Garage,\u00a0in [[Argon City]]. His employees include [[Beck]], [[Zed]], [[Mara]], and [[Link (Tron: Uprising)|Link]]. He acts as a grandfather figure to Beck. He is also one of the few programs who know that [[Tron (character)|Tron]] is still alive. He seems to be good friends with him, and supports the renegade.
