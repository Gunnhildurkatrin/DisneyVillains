"{{Infobox_character\n|image = Profile_-_Erica_the_Cruise_Director.jpg\n|name = Erica\n|shows = ''[[Mickey Mouse (TV series)|Mickey Mouse]]''\n|voice = Illya Owens\n|personality = Excitable, happy, ignorant, torturous\n|appearance = Red-haired dog wearing a cruise director uniform\n|occupation = Cruise director\n|alignment = Neutral\n|goal = To make [[Mickey Mouse]] and [[Minnie Mouse]] enjoy the VIP experience\n|minions = Bodyguards\n|likes = People having fun\n|dislikes = People not having fun\n|quote = \"Hi, I'm Erica, and I'll be your cruise director!\"}}'''Erica''' is the cruise director who appeared in the ''[[Mickey Mouse (TV series)|Mickey Mouse]]'' episode \"[[Shipped Out]]\" as the main antagonist. Mickey and Minnie decide to take a vacation on a luxury cruise ship, and Erica wants them to relax. But after hearing that they have V.I.P. experience, she decides to torture them for fun.\n==Personality==\nErica is very excitable. At first she seems like a normal, calm cruise director that wants her guests to relax. But when Mickey and Minnie mention the \"VIP Experience\", she gets really excited and goes nuts trying to get them to enjoy the ship.\n\n==Role in ''[[Mickey Mouse (TV series)|Mickey Mouse]]''==\nErica is the cruise director for the ship Mickey and Minnie go on. She tells them to relax and to let the ship's staff do everything for them. But after hearing that Mickey and Minnie have the VIP experience, she gets all excited, and presses a button, sending the two flying into all sorts of activities, to which they're confused and scared. Eventually, the two hide from her and she appears in their hiding spot, sending them gliding into the sky. She then sends up fireworks, accidentally knocking the two down off the ship onto a private island.
