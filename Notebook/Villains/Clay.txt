"{{Infobox character\n|image = Playhouse-Clay-Chiodo-Bros.png\n|name = Clay\n|animator = Chiodo Bros Productions\n|actress = [[Debi Derryberry]]\n|personality = Curious\n|appearance = Yellow (clay)\n|alignment = Good\n|goal = To meet new friends\n|home = Clay's playroom\n|likes = Singing, making artwork, playing dress-up}}\n'''Clay''' is a stop-motion character created by [https://web.archive.org/web/20200202171909/http://www.chiodobros.com/clay.html Chiodo Bros Productions.] He was voiced by [[Debi Derryberry]]. Clay appeared as a mascot in interstitials that were aired on [[Playhouse Disney]] from [[2002]] to [[2005]].\n\n==History==\nChiodo Bros Productions, a special effects group, created this character in the early 2000s.\n\nClay interstitials started airing in the United States in September [[2002]]. Playhouse Disney was in the process of phasing out its older interstitial material, and Clay's shorts were used to introduce shows on the block.\n\nUntil 2020, the Chiodo Bros website had a homepage for Clay that featured some of his shorts.
