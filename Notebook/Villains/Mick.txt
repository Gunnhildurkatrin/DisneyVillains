He is a football player on Norrisville High Football Team. He was part of Bash's band (Bash & the Bros) in the episode \"[[So U Think U Can Stank]]\". He also appeared in \"[[The Tale of the Golden Doctor's Note]]\" and bit a volleyball twice, which was an issue that bothered Bash. He also appeared in \"[[Secret Stache]]\" as a bouncer for the Mustache Society's secret club party.\n\n==Personality==\nMick rarely ever speaks, and acts as the strong, silent type.
