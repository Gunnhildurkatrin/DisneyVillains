She, [[Ripcord McQuack|her husband]], and [[Loopey McQuack|daughter]] make up a team of stunt-pilots called the \"Flying McQuacks\". It appears they make their living traveling to different air shows, preforming stunts. She made her first (and so far only) appearance in ''[[DuckTales]]'' when the \"The Flying McQuacks\" are coming to [[Duckburg]] for an air show, where Launchpad is flying an experimental jet.\n\n==Appearances==\nSo far, Birdie's only appearance was in the ''[[DuckTales]]'' episode \"[[Top Duck]]\", where she and the rest of the McQuack family save [[Scrooge McDuck]]'s money bin from the [[Beagle Boys]].\n\nLaunchpad briefly mentioned his mother in ''[[DuckTales Remastered]]''.When he accidentally drops his fuel regulator, he says: \"Uh, my mom always said I was a butterfingers!\"\n\n==Gallery==\n<gallery widths=\"180\" spacing=\"small\" position=\"center\">\nLaunchpad and his family.jpg\nDuckTales_LaunchpadMcQuack-Family-3.jpg\nTopDuck05.jpg\nTopDuck02.jpg\nTopDuck01.jpg\n</gallery>\n\n{{DEFAULTSORT:McQuack, Birdie}}\n\n{{DuckTales}}\n[[Category:DuckTales characters]]\n[[Category:Disney characters]]\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:Animated characters]]\n[[Category:Ducks]]\n[[Category:Females]]\n[[Category:Adults]]\n[[Category:Pilots]]\n[[Category:TV Animation characters]]\n[[Category:Donald Duck universe characters]]\n[[Category:The Disney Afternoon characters]]\n[[Category:Lovers]]\n[[Category:Heroines]]\n[[Category:Parents]]\n[[Category:Spouses]]"
