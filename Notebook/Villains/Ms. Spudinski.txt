She only makes a single appearance in the episode \"[[Bite Father, Bite Son]]\" from Season 2.\n\n==History==\nMs. Spudinski is [[Arthur \"Spud\" P. Spudinski]]'s unnamed mother, who runs a family restaurant called the [[Familio Festevedro]] in Little Italy.\n\n==Physical Appearance==\nMs. Spudinski is a plump woman at the average height of an adult. She has long black hair that she often ties on top of her head and wears a pair of red glasses. When working at her restaurant, Ms. Spudinski wears an olive green dress.\n\nShe also wears bracelets around her arm and two loop earrings.\n\n==Episode Appearances==\n*\"[[Bite Father, Bite Son]]\" (first appearance)  \n\n==Personality==\nMs. Spudinski is a very kind person who deeply cares for her son.
