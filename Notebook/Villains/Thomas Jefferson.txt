As one of the most influential figures in American history, he has been featured in many pop culture, especially in the [[1953]] [[Walt Disney Animation Studios|Disney]] animated short, ''[[Ben and Me]]'', which he was voiced by [[Hans Conried]]. He plays as one of the main antagonists in the hit musical [[Hamilton]]. He was  also mentioned in ''[[The Swap]]''.\n\n==Appearances==\n===''[[Schoolhouse Rock!]]''===\nThomas Jefferson appeared in the ''Schoolhouse Rock!'' songs \"[[Fireworks]]\" and \"[[Elbow Room]]\".\n\n===''[[The Muppets]]''===\nJefferson appeared as one of the four heads on [[Mount Rushmore]] in ''The Muppet Show: Sex and Violence'' (performed by [[Jerry Nelson]]), a National Wildlife Federation PSA ad, and in ''[[Muppets Tonight]]''.
