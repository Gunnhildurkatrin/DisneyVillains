His spells are used by\u00a0[[Miss Eglantine Price]]\u00a0throughout the film.\n\n==Background==\nAstoroth is the author of\u00a0''[[The Spells of Astoroth]]''\u00a0and namesake of\u00a0[[The Star of Astoroth|the Star of Astoroth]]. He enchanted animals to make them act more human. They rebelled, ganged up on Astoroth, and killed him.
