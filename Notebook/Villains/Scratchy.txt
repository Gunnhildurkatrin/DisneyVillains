He is a brown mongrel with a tan belly, dark brown ears, and a slight loss of fur on his left arm and tail.\n\n===Personality===\nHe doesn't talk or do much other than scratch himself each time he has fleas and help his friends out with something. He eventually leaves [[The Junkyard|the junkyard]] with the other Junkyard Dogs\u00a0minus [[Buster (Lady and the Tramp)|Buster]], and gets adopted by a family along with [[Sparky (Lady and the Tramp)|Sparky]].\n\n==Trivia==\n*Scratchy doesn't talk, thus making him the only silent Junkyard Dog.\n\n==Gallery==\n<gallery spacing=\"small\" position=\"center\" captionalign=\"center\">\nLadytramp2-disneyscreencaps.com-2325.jpg\nScratchy_Full.jpg\nLadytramp2-disneyscreencaps.com-2586.jpg\n346036 1261575615523 full.jpg|Scratchy howling with the other Junkyard Dogs\nLady-tramp-2-disneyscreencaps.com-7487.jpg|Scratchy and the other junkyard dogs with their new owners\n</gallery>\n\n{{Lady and the Tramp}}\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:Disney characters]]\n[[Category:Dogs]]\n[[Category:Lady and the Tramp characters]]\n[[Category:Males]]\n[[Category:Adults]]\n[[Category:Heroes]]\n[[Category:Silent characters]]\n[[Category:Animated characters]]\n[[Category:Characters who break the Fourth Wall]]"
