"[[File:Jen.png|thumb|250px]]'''Jennette Brocolleti''' is a minor character and antagonist from ''[[Wizards of Waverly Place]]''\u00a0who only appeared in the episode \"[[Alex Does Good]]\". She is portrayed by Kathryn Foley.\n\n==History==\nIn the episode, Max asks her out on a date and tried to impress her, but things didn't go well when Max turned into a clown during the date after trying to turn Justin into one, and made Jennette feel disturbed and repulsed by him.
