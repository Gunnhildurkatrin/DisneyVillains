"[[File:Rubydonahue.png|thumb|250px]]'''Ruby Donahue''' is a famous actress and singer within the ''[[Wizards of Waverly Place]]'' world. [[Alex Russo|Alex]] mentions she is a big fan of her, and even owns her CD. She is portrayed by Malese Jow.\n\n==History==\nRuby Donahue grew up on a ranch in Montana, and eventually start her own charity. After achieving fame, she created her own price-conscious barrette line. Ruby has also experimented with music, which Alex describes as \"sensitive but mildly angry rock\" songs.\n\nIn ''Night of the Halloween Sorority Party Disaster 2'', Ruby plays Bambi, the sensitive one.\n\n==Personality==\nAs seen in \"Movies\", Ruby appears to be friendly, but not very bright.
