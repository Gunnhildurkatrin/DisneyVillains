"{{Infobox character\n|image = S1e15 Mr. Poolcheck.png\n|imagesize = 210x240\n|shows = ''[[Gravity Falls]]''\n|voice = Michael Rianda\n|personality = Serious, emotionally unstable\n|appearance = Tall, muscular\n|alignment = Neutral\n|minions = [[Dipper Pines]] (briefly)<br>[[Wendy Corduroy]] (briefly)\n|enemies = [[Dipper Pines]], [[Wendy Corduroy]], [[Soos Ramirez|Soos]], [[Bill Cipher]]\n|likes = Serious lifeguards, safety in the pool\n|dislikes = Bad lifeguards, wreck pool supplies\n|powers = Strength\n|quote = \"Welcome to the deep end, son.\"}}\n'''Mr. Poolcheck''' is a character and antagonist in\u00a0''[[Gravity Falls]]''. He is the boss of the [[Gravity Falls, Oregon|Gravity Falls]] pool.\n\n==Role in the series==\nHe is first seen in\u00a0[[Dipper Pines|Dipper]]'s\u00a0story during \"[[Bottomless Pit!]]\", sitting at the\u00a0Skull Fracture\u00a0bar. When\u00a0Tats\u00a0calls for an arms against Dipper (for being a prank caller), he joins in the chase along with the rest of the bar's occupants.\n\nPoolcheck makes a bigger role in \"[[The Deep End]]\", he is\u00a0[[Wendy Corduroy|Wendy]]'s\u00a0boss at the Gravity Falls Pool and later agrees to hire on Dipper. At first he takes a liking to Dipper, but comes to realize both Wendy and Dipper do not take the job of lifeguard seriously enough. Near the conclusion of the episode, he fires both Wendy (offscreen) and Dipper.
