"{{Infobox character\n|image = 133443961.jpg\n|name = Ice Ogre\n|shows = ''[[Jake and the Never Land Pirates]]''\n|appearance = A large blue beast \n|powers = Brute strength\n|personality = Intimidating, brutish, grumpy\n|occupation = Guardian of the path of the Blue Ice Diamond\n|alignment = Neutral\n|home = Great Ice Bridge in [[Neverland]]\n|friends = [[Jake (Pirate)|Jake]], [[Izzy]], [[Cubby]], [[Skully]], [[Mr. Smee]], [[Sharky and Bones]]  \n|enemies = [[Captain Hook]] \n|likes = Izzy's ice skating performance \n|dislikes = Being disturbed from his slumber, trespassers, Captain Hook \n|fate = Enjoys Izzy's performance and waves farewell before returning back under the bridge\n|voice = [[Dee Bradley Baker]]}}The '''Ice Ogre''' is a large blue beast that lives under the Great Ice Bridge guarding the path of the path to legendary blue Ice Diamond\u00a0in\u00a0''[[Jake and the Never Land Pirates]]'',\u00a0and is said to eat trespassers. It is voiced by [[Dee Bradley Baker]].\n==Role in the series==\nThe Ice Ogre first appeared in the episode \"[[Hook on Ice!]]\" [[Jake (Pirate)|Jake]]'s crew teams up with [[Captain Hook]]'s to find a legendary snow treasure. When both pirate crews make it to the Great Ice Bridge,\u00a0[[Cubby]] tries to warn Hook that this is the home of the Ice Ogre, but Hook doesn't take the young pirate's words seriously. Then the beast emerges from under the bridge, about to attack the captain. But Hook is rescued by Jake, while [[Izzy]] distracts the monster with her ice skating performance.After locating the ice treasure Hook refused to share it with Jake and his crew only for Izzy to remind him that he would never have found the treasure if they hadn't rescue him from the ogre.\n\n==Episode Appearances==\n*\"[[Hook on Ice!]]\" (first appearance)\n\n==Gallery==\n<gallery>\nOgre.jpg\nIce Ogre-Hook on Ice01.png\n</gallery>\n{{Jake and the Neverland Pirates}}\n[[Category:Jake and the Never Land Pirates characters]]\n[[Category:Disney characters]]\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:Disney Junior characters]]\n[[Category:Neutral characters]]\n[[Category:TV Animation characters]]\n[[Category:Monsters]]\n[[Category:Silent characters]]\n[[Category:Males]]\n[[Category:Animated characters]]"
