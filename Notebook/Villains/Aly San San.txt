Buh-bye.\"}}'''Aly San San''' is a\u00a0droid from ''[[Star Tours: The Adventures Continue]]''.\n\n==History==\n\n=== Background ===\nShe is the spokesbot of the [[Star Tours (company)|Star Tours Travel Company]] \u00a0sometime between\u00a019 BBY\u00a0and\u00a00 BBY. She was fluent in many languages and often did instructional and safety videos for the Star Tours company. By the time of the battle of [[Endor]], Aly San San was replaced with a human spokesperson.\n\n=== Development History ===\nAly San San is designed after a waitress droid from ''Star Wars: Attack of the Clones''. Her name was chosen by [[George Lucas]], and she was named after the voice actress [[Allison Janney]].\n\n== Appearances ==\n\n=== Star Tours: The Adventures Continue ===\nAly San San can be seen various monitors throughout the queue, as well as instructing passengers on how to board the [[StarSpeeder 1000]] within the load-area.\n\n=== [[Oga's Cantina]] ===\nAly San San is mentioned by [[RX-24]] (going by R-3X) to a number of cantina patrons while he was working as a DJ on Batuu.
