"{{Infobox character\n|image = Giblet the Clown.jpg\n|name = Giblet the Clown \n|shows = [[Goof Troop]] \n|voice = [[Frank Welker]] \n|occupation = Clown\n|alignment = Neutral\n|affiliations = [[Ringmaster (Goof Troop)|The Circus Ringmaster]] \n|home = [[Spoonerville]]\n|likes = Selling balloons\n|dislikes = Children }}\n'''Giblet the Clown''' is the secondary antagonist of the episode, \"[[Three Ring Bind]]\", as well as a minor character, from [[Disney Television Animation|Disney's]] 1992-1993 animated series, ''[[Goof Troop]].'' \n==Background==\nHe only appears in three episodes, usually selling balloons. In some episodes, he is depicted as a villain, while in others, he helps the protagonists. Despite being a clown, he is usually frowned up.\n\n===Personality===\nGiblet is a grumpy yet helpful person. While he was originally evil, he has been shown to be thoughtful towards others. \n\n===Physical appearance===\nGiblet is an obese man. As he is always in his clown costume, it is unknown what he looks like underneath, as well as his species. He wears white clown makeup with red lips; two large orange tufts of hair; a red clown nose; a yellow frilled collar; a green clown suit with a pink patch on the left elbow; brown, over-sized clown shoes with yellow pom-poms on the tops; and a large pink-and-purple striped tie with matching party hat.\n\n===Role in the series===\nIn \"[[Hot Air]]\", Giblet sells balloons at the Spoonerville Aviation Fair, where he meets [[Pistol Pete|Pistol]]. \n\nIn \"[[Three Ring Bind]]\", he is the [[Ringmaster (Goof Troop)|Circus Ringmaster's]] accomplice, luring [[Max Goof|Max]] and [[P.J.]] away from the circus. \n\nIn \"[[Buddy Building]]\", he helps out Max, as well as Ronald Streudelnossher, while at Lake Zester selling balloons.\n\n==Trivia==\n*Although he is a clown, he is shown to dislike children, as he treats them very poorly, going as far as to pop the balloons he sells them.\n\n{{Goof Troop}}\n[[Category:Goof Troop characters]]\n[[Category:Males]]\n[[Category:Adults]] \n[[Category:Clowns]]\n[[Category:Disney characters]]\n[[Category:The Disney Afternoon characters]]\n[[Category:Neutral characters]]\n[[Category:Animated characters]]\n[[Category:American characters]]\n[[Category:TV Animation characters]]\n[[Category:Mickey Mouse universe characters]]\n[[Category:Anthropomorphic characters]]"
