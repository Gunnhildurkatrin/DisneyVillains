"{{CharacterStub}}\n{{NeedImage}}\n\n'''Stanislav''' is a recurring character in ''[[101 Dalmatian Street]]''. He is\u00a0[[Snowball (101 Dalmatian Street)|Snowball]]'s human.\n\n== Appearance ==\nHe is a buff man who is bald. He has a gold necklace, a pink tank top and coat with black and pink with white sleeves.\n\n== Personality ==\nDespite his burly appearance he has a soft spot for Snowball. His soft side is also shown in \"Diamond Dogs\", where he gives one of the collars he is selling to Dorothy for free. He is often seen on his phone, or trying to retrieve Snowball through a retractable leash. Based on numerous episodes, he appears to be the main merchant of Camden, selling things in the Camden Market, as well as running the local corner shop.
