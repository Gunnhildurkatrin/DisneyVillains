He's a 12 year old kid who's a reformed juvenile delinquent. His parents abandoned him and he was forced to steal things to survive, which got him arrested and sent to juvie. He was also in and out of foster homes. He was sent to Camp Kikiwaka to help him stay out of trouble and to help him reform.
