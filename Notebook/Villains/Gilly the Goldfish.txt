The small pirate team is not the only one to enjoy Gilly and her tricks: [[Captain Hook]] swipes the fish away from the sea pups for his own fish tank, causing [[Jake (Pirate)|Jake]] and his crew to pursue the greedy captain to recover Cubby's beloved pet.\n\nGilly returns in the special ''[[Jake and the Never Land Pirates: Jake Saves Bucky]]'', but unlike her first appearance where she was in a fishbowl, Gilly was freely swimming through the Never Sea.
