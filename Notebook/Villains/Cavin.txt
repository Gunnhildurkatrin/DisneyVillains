He was voiced by multiple people over the course of the series.\n\n==Background==\nCavin is presented as a page to [[Sir Tuxford]] of [[Dunwyn Castle]]. His only known family is his grandfather, [[Sir Gawain]].\n\nCavin is one of the few people to know of the [[Gummi Bears]]' existence, and becomes an ally to them. He is shown to be best friends with [[Cubbi Gummi|Cubbi]], as they both share the dream of becoming a knight one day.\n\nHe quickly becomes friends with [[Princess Calla]], with each harboring mutual feelings for each other.\n\n==Appearances==\nIn the [[A New Beginning|premiere episode]], Cavin wears a [[Gummi Bear Medallion]] given to him by his grandfather. He ends up stumbling into [[Gummi Glen|the home of the Gummi Bears]] by accident, and convinces them to assist him in the defense of Dunwyn from [[Duke Igthorn]].\n\nCavin leaves his Gummi Bear Medallion with [[Zummi Gummi|Zummi]], which allows the Gummi Bears to unlock the [[Great Book of Gummi]] and discover much of their lost heritage.\n\nCavin becomes a close ally to the Gummi Bears, while keeping their existence a secret from other humans.\n\n==Gallery==\n{{Gallerylink}}\n\n==Trivia==\n*During the developmental process, the character was briefly named \"Calvin\"; the name appears on early storyboards.\n*Cavin is one of a relatively small group of humans who find out about the existence of Gummi Bears during the course of the series.
