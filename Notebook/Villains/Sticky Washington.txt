He is an overly cautious boy who has the ability to remember everything that he reads and expounds a vast array of knowledge and skills.\n\n==Background==\n\n===Personality===\nSticky is often very anxious. It is implied that he has low self-esteem and is the least confrontational of the kids. However, he is very smart and does display some common sense and decent social skills. He is shown to be more of a follower and is willing to stand behind Reynie and Kate, who are more up-front than him. \n\nHe has discovered that he is pretty brave under pressure, and extremely loyal to his chosen family. \n\n===Physical Appearance===\nSticky is of African-American descent and is always wearing his round spectacles, even in bed. He is usually wearing a pull over sweater over an undershirt and long pants with nice shoes.\n\n===Abilities===\nSticky possesses an eidetic memory that allows him to memorize everything he reads. He loves to fill his head with various pieces of knowledge that proves to be useful in one form or another. \n\nHe is fluent in Morse Code.  \n\n==Trivia==\n*Each of the four children feature a dominant color of some kind.
