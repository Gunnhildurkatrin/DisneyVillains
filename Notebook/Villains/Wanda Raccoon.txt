"{{Infobox character\n|name = Wanda Raccoon\n|image = Wanda Raccoon.png\n|shows = ''[[PB&J Otter]]''\n|voice = Corinne Orr\n|personality = Very caring, motherly, lovely, strict (if necessary)\n|appearance = Blue-gray raccoon, teal headband, red socks, brown sneakers\n|alignment = Good\n|home = Lake Hoohaw\n|family = [[Walter Raccoon]] (husband)<br>[[Pinch Raccoon]] (daughter)<br>[[Scootch Raccoon]] (son)<br>Ricky Raccoon (nephew)\n|friends = [[Peanut Otter]]<br>[[Baby Butter Otter]]<br>[[Jelly Otter]]<br>[[Flick Duck]]}}\n\n'''Wanda Raccoon''' is a character from ''[[PB&J Otter]]''. She is married to [[Walter Raccoon]] and is the mother of [[Pinch Raccoon|Pinch]] and [[Scootch Raccoon]]. She is the post raccoon of Lake Hoohaw and it is her job to send letters. Unlike her husband Walter, Wanda works during the day, which means she does not always get to see much of her husband. Despite being the post raccoon and caregiver, she still has time for her family. When she is at home, she is seen wearing a dark blue apron and when she works, she is seen driving her mail boat and has a pochettes around her.
