"[[File:Bowling for Soup.jpg|thumb|250px]]\n\n'''Bowling for Soup''' (often typeset as '''\u00a1Bowling for Soup!''' and abbreviated as '''BFS''') is an American rock band originally formed in Wichita Falls, [[Texas]], in [[1994]]. The band consists of [[Jaret Reddick]] (lead vocals, guitar), Chris Burney (guitar, backing vocals), Erik Chandler (bass, backing vocals, acoustic guitar), and Gary Wiseman (drums, percussion, backing vocals).\n\n==Disney Media==\n===Television===\nThe band performed the ''[[Phineas and Ferb]]''\u00a0theme song\u00a0\"[[Today is Gonna Be a Great Day]]\" as well as \"[[Winter Vacation]]\", the theme from [[Phineas and Ferb Christmas Vacation!|the holiday special]]. They also have animated cameos in\u00a0some\u00a0episodes\u00a0of ''Phineas and Ferb''.\n\n===Movies===\nThe band also performed the song \"The Greatest Day\" for ''[[Max Keeble's Big Move]]'' as well as \"I Melt With You\" for ''[[Sky High]]''.\u00a0A cover version of [[Britney Spears]]' \"...Baby One More Time\" is performed in the [[2003]] ''[[Freaky Friday (2003)|Freaky Friday]]'' remake.\n\n===Other===\nThe band also did a cover of \"[[The Bare Necessities]]\"\u00a0for ''[[Disneymania 3]]''.\n\n{{Phineas and Ferb}}\n[[Category:Bands]]\n[[Category:Phineas and Ferb characters]]\n[[Category:Phineas and Ferb]]\n[[Category:People]]\n[[Category:Males]]\n[[Category:Singers]]\n[[Category:Musicians]]\n[[Category:Max Keeble's Big Move]]\n[[Category:Sky High]]\n[[Category:Characters based on real people]]\n[[Category:Animated characters]]\n[[Category:Character groups]]\n[[Category:Heroes]]\n[[Category:Singing characters]]\n[[Category:The Jungle Book]]\n[[Category:TV Animation characters]]\n[[Category:Freaky Friday]]\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:Disney characters]]\n[[Category:Rock bands]]"
