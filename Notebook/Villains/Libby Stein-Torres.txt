She is a quirky, yet nervous young girl who is [[Molly McGee]]'s best friend.\n\n==Background==\n\n===Personality===\nLibby tends to be shy and sometimes a downer and has trouble speaking for herself. Despite this, she is very friendly and will be eager enough to help Molly with whatever she wants, even if it negatively affects her.\n\n===Physical appearance===\nLibby is a tall, skinny, tan skinned girl with brown eyes and wood brown hair that is long and slightly messy.\n\nHer clothes consist of a teal green sweater and black jeans that are ripped at the knee. She wears big black boots with straps on them.\n\n==Trivia==\n*As her name implies, she is of Jewish-Mexican heritage.\n*A running gag has her constantly getting physically harmed in some way; an unusual trait for female characters in animation.\n\n==Gallery==\n<gallery position=\"center\" captionalign=\"center\" spacing=\"small\" widths=\"150\">\nNot So Honest Abe (7).jpg\nMazel Tov, Libby (3).jpg\nMazel Tov, Libby (5).jpg\nTG&MM Mazel Tov!
