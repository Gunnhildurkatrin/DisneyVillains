Her first and only speaking appearance is in \"[[Bromance]]\". She is voiced by Fergie.\n\n==Background==\nApril is a girl that works at Hamburger On A Stick who initially dislikes Brad, but she really enjoys [[Kick Buttowski (character)|Kick]] and only agrees to date Brad when Kick promises to be there. Kick shows up to help Brad make her like him back. However, after Brad angers Kick too many times, he ends up on his own. When April catches Brad and Kick fighting, she loses interest in him and tells him off.
