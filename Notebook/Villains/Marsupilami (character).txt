He lives in the jungle with [[Maurice (Marsupilami)|Maurice]]. The character was originally created by Andr\u00e9 Franquin; the cartoon character is greatly different from Franquin's original version.\n\n==Appearances==\n\n=== ''[[Raw Toonage]]/[[Marsupilami (TV series)|Marsupilami]]'' ===\nMarsupilami made his first animated debut on Disney's Raw Toonage. There, He does not only say \"HOUBA!\" He can actually talk. And has a gorilla sidekick named Maurice.\n\n===''[[Bonkers]]''===\nHe appeared with [[Darkwing Duck (character)|Darkwing Duck]] in the ''[[Bonkers]]'' episode, \"[[The 29th Page]]\".\n\n===''Marsupilami'' (Franquin's version)===\nThe original version of ''Marsupilami'' lives in South America with his wife and 3 children. In this version, he only says \"Houba, houba\", but he can mimic people like a parrot.\n\nInstead of Norman, Marsupilami\u2019s human foe is the hunter Brenham Backalive.\n\n===''Mon ami Marsupilami''===\nHe appeared in ''Mon ami Marsupilami''. He is based on the Franquin version as he has a wife and three children.
