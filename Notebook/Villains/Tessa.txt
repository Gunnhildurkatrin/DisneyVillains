"{{Infobox character\n|250px\n|image = Profile - Tessa.PNG\n|name = Tessa\n|shows = ''[[Star Darlings]]''\n|voice = [[Eden Riegel]]\n|inspiration = Applejack\n|personality = Hard-working, charming, affectionate, trustworthy, dependable, loyal, sometimes stubborn\n|appearance = Emerald green hair, blue-green irises, blue-green dress\n|occupation = Third-year student at Starling Academy\n|alignment = Good\n|goal = To take care of everyone, especially all the friends at Starling Academy\n|home = Solar Springs\n|family = [[Gemma]] (younger sister)\n|friends = Gemma, [[Adora]]\n|enemies = [[Vivica]]\n|likes = Cooking for her friends, trying new foods, creature comforts, flowers\n|dislikes = Being rushed, change\n|powers = Positive Wish Energy\n|possessions = Vertessema, Gossamer, Brooch}}\n'''Tessa''' is one of the twelve Star Darling students at Starling Academy and also one of the supporting characters and protagonists in Disney's ''[[Star Darlings]]''.\n\n==Background==\n\n=== Official Description ===\n''From the time she began to sparkle, Tessa showed a love for gourmet meals! Tessa loves to take care of her sister, [[Gemma]], and her friends. She's the nurturer of the group and encourages her friends every step of the way.''\n\n===Personality===\nTessa is described as being loyal to those she loves and has a deep connection with Starland's magical creatures. But she can be quite stubborn when it comes to settling arguments - she can be rather stubborn and is always convinced she's right. She doesn't deal well with change and is prone to exaggeration. She can be easily sidetracked.\n\n==Appearance==\nTessa\u2019s theme color is emerald green. She is a teenage girl with dark skin, green eyes, lips, and curly green hair with steaks of blue. Her outfit consists of a green dress with flower designs under a short blue vest, green gloves, and leggings. Her accessory is her star-charmed brooch.\n\nOn Earth, Tessa\u2019s appearance changes to look like a normal human. Her hair color changes to black with only a streak of green. Her skin loses its sparkle and her outfit changes to look more human. The only item that remains the same is her star-charmed brooch. \n\n==Gallery==\n<gallery>\nTessa profile.jpeg\n</gallery>\n==Trivia==\nTessa is named after [[wikipedia:Taurus (astrology)|Taurus]] (\u2649), the second of the twelve astrological signs of the zodiac, which means the bull.\n\n{{Star Darlings}}\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:Disney characters]]\n[[Category:Animated characters]]\n[[Category:TV Animation characters]]\n[[Category:Teenagers]]\n[[Category:Females]]\n[[Category:Students]]\n[[Category:Protagonists]]\n[[Category:Star Darlings characters]]\n[[Category:Magic Users]]\n[[Category:Chefs]]\n[[Category:Siblings]]"
