In her debut appearance \"[[Two to Tangu]]\", she meets [[Sofia the First (character)|Sofia]] and [[Princess Amber|Amber]] in the kingdom of [[Tangu]], and offers them some cryptic advice to find Tangu Peek and their [[Magic Carpet (Sofia the First)|Magic Carpet]].\nShe appeared again in \"[[Finding Clover]]\" where Sofia asked if they'd met before but Madam Ubetcha dismissed this question and showed Sofia and her friends, [[Minimus]], [[Crackle]], [[Robin (Sofia the First)|Robin]] and [[Mia (Sofia the First)|Mia]], how to find their friend [[Clover]].
