He is a hockey player from Minneapolis, [[Minnesota]]. \n\n<br />\n\n== Biography ==\nPeter was a member of the [[District 5 Ducks]] and played as a defenseman as number 24. He is a player with an attitude and induces conflict; he leads a walkout on Coach Bombay when he overhears a sarcastic comment about the team out of context. Peter is friends with [[Jesse Hall]] and thinks he's a hotshot in his leather jacket. He also has a thing for overhearing things and misinterpreting them. He is portrayed by J.D. Daniels.\n<br />\n\n== Trivia ==\n\n* Peter could be considered the biggest troublemaker of the ducks in the first film.\n* He does not appear in the sequels nor is he mentioned, like Tammy, Terry, Tommy and Karp.\n\n\n==Quotes==\n*Gordon Bombay: \"SHUT UP!
