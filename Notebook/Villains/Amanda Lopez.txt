She is a Hispanic student in [[Milo Murphy|Milo's]] class who is a perfectionist.\n==Background==\n===Personality and Traits===\nAmanda is obsessed with order and perfection, and sometimes worries when Milo is about as things can definitely go wrong when he is around. As the series progresses however, she has shown to have become more calm towards Murphy's law, but still remains insecure. Amanda has also started to grow on Milo, developing a possible crush on him. Her hair is dark violet and shoulder-length with a dark magenta headband. She wears pink pearl earrings and has dark purple lips.
