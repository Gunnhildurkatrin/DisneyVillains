Her dragon form resembles a large aqua blue dragon. She wears a yellow tiara and wrist plates. Her mane is the same yellow as her tiara and she has long black hair. Her underbelly is a slightly lighter blue compared to her scales. Her wings are similar to most dragons, having an arm-and-fingers bone structure.
