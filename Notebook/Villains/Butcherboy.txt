"[[File:The BFG giants 1.png|thumb|277x277px]]\nThe '''\"Butcher Boy\"''' is one of the nine child-eating giants that live in giant country. Butcher Boy's clothing is made out of a circus tent, hence the red and yellow stripes on his clothes. He is the \"baby\" of the tribe, being quite immature and known to collect cars which he keeps in a sack. He is quite chubby and has short dark hair.\n\nHe, along with the\u00a0[[Fleshlumpeater]],\u00a0[[Bloodbottler]], [[Meatdripper]], [[Gizzardgulper]], [[Maidmasher]],\u00a0[[Manhugger]], [[Childchewer]], and [[Bonecruncher]],\u00a0enjoy snatching and gobbling up little children at night while\u00a0[[The BFG (character)|The BFG]]\u00a0delivers dreams to the children.\n[[Category:The BFG characters]]\n[[Category:Monsters]]\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:Live-action characters]]\n[[Category:Villains]]\n[[Category:Live-action villains]]\n[[Category:Giants]]\n[[Category:Immortal]]\n[[Category:Legendary creatures]]\n[[Category:Disney characters]]"
