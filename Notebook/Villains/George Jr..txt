the [[George of the Jungle (character)|the titular character]] and [[Ursula Stanhope]] and heir apparent to the jungle throne.\n\nHe was a few months old in the events of the first film and five years old in the sequel.\n\n==Role in the films==\n===''[[George of the Jungle]]''===\nJunior is born toward the end of the film and he is revealed to have inherited George's clumsiness of bumping into things when he accidentally bumps his chin on an overspreading branch in the treehouse.\n\nGeorge and Ursula then show him to the land (a preference of ''[[The Lion King]]'').\n\n=== ''[[George of the Jungle 2]]'' ===\nFive years have passed and Junior is being taught by George his duties as heir apparent to the jungle throne.\n\nHowever he prefers vine surfing to George's vine swinging.\n\nOn his birthday, George gives Junior a spear which he shows great appreciation for. However his grandmother, [[Beatrice Stanhope]], has come for a visit and showers him with loads of expensive gifts. She is secretly planning to break George and Ursula up and teams up with George's enemy, [[Lyle Van de Groot]], to destroy Ape Mountain so he can have her as his wife.\n\nUrsula is later hypnotized into believing Lyle is her husband and is forced to go to [[San Francisco]].\n\nGeorge however manages to free Ursula from the hypnotism, defeat Lyle and Beatrice and save Ape Mountain.\n\nHe is then seeing George and Ursula getting remarried and George promising him that he will try to spend more time with him.\n\nHe is finally seen with George heading for the treehouse for lunch with the latter crashing into the side of it.\n\n{{George of the Jungle}}\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:George of the Jungle characters]]\n[[Category:Princes]]\n[[Category:Kids]]\n[[Category:Live-action characters]]\n[[Category:Males]]\n[[Category:Heroes]]\n[[Category:Disney characters]]"
