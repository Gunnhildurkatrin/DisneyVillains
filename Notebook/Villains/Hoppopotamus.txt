She is called '''Hoppo''' by her friends. She is a Wuzzle who is a hippopotamus with a rabbit's ears, buck teeth and a fluffy tail. She has blue fur with a purple tummy, and she loves to sing and act.\n===Personality===\nShe is a pushy demanding diva, but she can be sweet. However, when toughness is called for (particularly in dealing with the regular villain, [[Crocosaurus|Crock]]), she is the toughest Wuzzle of all.
