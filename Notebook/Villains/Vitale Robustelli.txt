"{{Infobox_character|rides = [[Soarin'|Soaring:Fantastic Flight]]|occupation = President of the [[Society of Explorers and Adventurers]]|affiliations = The S.E.A.<br>\n[[Jason Chandler]]<br>\n[[Lord Henry Mystic]]<br>\n[[Camellia Falco]]|image = Vitale Robustelli.jpg}}'''Vitale Robustelli''' is a character from [[Soarin']] affiliated with the [[Society of Explorers and Adventurers]].\n\n== History ==\n\n=== Background ===\nVitale Robustelli was the president of the Society of Explorers and Adventurers in the early 19th-century.  In 1851 he officially brought in the first female S.E.A. member in the form of [[Camellia Falco]].  At an unknown point of time he was removed from his position as president and was succeeded by former club-secretary, [[Jason Chandler]].\n\n== Appearances ==\n\n=== Soaring: Fantastic Flight ===\nVitale appears in portrait form, bringing Camellia Falco into SEA.  The portrait also features her pet falcon Aletta and [[Lord Henry Mystic]] visible in the crowd.  He is identified by a plaque as being Vitale Robustelli and the president of SEA at the time.{{Society of Explorers and Adventurers}}\n[[Category:S.E.A. characters]]\n[[Category:Soarin' characters]]\n[[Category:Theme park characters]]\n[[Category:Italian characters]]\n[[Category:Disney characters]]\n[[Category:Males]]\n[[Category:Adults]]"
