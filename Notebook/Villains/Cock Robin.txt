He is a caricature based on the late singer and actor Bing Crosby, and he was voiced by Melvin J. Gibby (singing) and Purv Pullen (whistling).\n== Role in the film==\nOne night, Cock Robin decides to perch on a branch and sing. He arouses the curiosity of [[Jenny Wren]]. Later he was shot with an arrow. At the trial Cupid explains he shot him, but he is not dead.
