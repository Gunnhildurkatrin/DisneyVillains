She is voiced by [[Tara Strong]].<ref>http://www.behindthevoiceactors.com/tv-shows/American-Dragon-Jake-Long/Veronica/</ref><ref>http://www.imdb.com/name/nm0152839/?ref_=tt_cl_t10</ref>\n\n==History==\nShe is a female Arachne, a half-human/half-spider. She works as a merchant at a stand in the [[Magus Bazaar]] selling magical items.<ref name=\"breath\">[[Dragon Breath]]</ref>\u00a0Upon\u00a0[[Fu Dog|Fu]] and [[Jake Long|Jake]]'s\u00a0first encounter, Jake was taken by surprise to discover her arachnid lower half. Fu was trying to help Jake find a date for a school dance and tried to get him to ask her, as he thought Jake might be into girls with legs. However, Jake had a little problem with Veronica's \"eight hairy spider legs\".\n\nVeronica was one of Fu's many \"lady\" friends, and apparently one of his still-active love interests. She made another appearance as an entry in the Miss Magical World Beauty Pageant to help provide Jake and company cover for their mission.<ref name=\"eye\">[[Eye of the Beholder]]</ref>\n\nVeronica was also seen in three other episodes,<ref>[[The Halloween Bash]]</ref><ref>[[Keeping Shop]]</ref><ref name=\"hkn\">[[Hong Kong Nights]]</ref>\u00a0where she phoned Fu with a warning that the Biker Trolls were back to wreak havoc. While she makes several appearances in the show, she generally did not play vital roles except for her part in the Miss Magical World Beauty Pageant as Jake's way onto [[Eli Excelsior Pandarus|Eli Pandarus]]' island, and in helping rescue [[Luong Lao Shi|Lao Shi]] from the [[The Dark Dragon|Dark Dragon]].<ref name=\"hkn\">[[Hong Kong Nights]]</ref>\n\n==Episode Appearances==\n===Season One===\n*\"[[Dragon Breath]]\" (first appearance)\n*\"[[Eye of the Beholder (American Dragon: Jake Long)|Eye of the Beholder]]\"\n*\"[[Hong Kong Nights]]\"\n*\"[[The Halloween Bash]]\"\n*\"[[Keeping Shop]]\" (final appearance)\n\n==Trivia==\n*Veronica is one of Fu Dog's many sources of Intel from which he gets information from.\n*One time, Veronica is seen using spider webs and spinning silk.<ref name=\"hkn\">[[Hong Kong Nights]]</ref>\n*She never appeared in Season 2.\n*Veronica's appearance is a bit similar to '''Velma Green the Spider Queen from''' from ''Wrath of the Spider Queen''.\n\n==Gallery==\n{{Gallerylink}}\n\n==References==\n<references/>\n{{American Dragon: Jake Long}}\n[[Category:American Dragon: Jake Long characters]]\n[[Category:Females]]\n[[Category:Lovers]]\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:Disney characters]]\n[[Category:Adults]]\n[[Category:Heroines]]\n[[Category:Spiders]]\n[[Category:Hybrids]]\n[[Category:TV Animation characters]]\n[[Category:Animated characters]]\n[[Category:Disney Channel characters]]"
