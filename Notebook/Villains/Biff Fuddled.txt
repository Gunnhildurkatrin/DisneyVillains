"{{Infobox character|name = Biff Fuddled|shows = ''[[Goof Troop]]''|alignment = Good/Neutral|image = [[File:Char_49816.jpg|thumb|250px]]|occupation = Television Host|personality = Friendly, smiley, dramatic|voice = [[Rob Paulsen]]}}'''Biff Fuddled''' is a TV personality for [[Spoonerville]]'s local TV station KBOB T.V., hosting such shows as ''Odd Facts, Strange Stuff, and Things Too Weird to Fake'' and ''The World's Most Painful Home Videos''. He also reported the news once and hosted the Mrs. Spoonerville Society's semi-biannual Househelper Contest. He appeared in the episodes \"[[Close Encounters of the Weird Mime]]\", \"[[Slightly Dinghy]]\", \"[[Wrecks, Lies, & Videotape]]\", and \"[[Mrs. Spoonerville]]\".
