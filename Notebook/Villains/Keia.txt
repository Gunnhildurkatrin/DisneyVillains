She lives next door to [[Bingo (Puppy Dog Pals)|Bingo]] and [[Rolly (Puppy Dog Pals)|Rolly]] and is Chloe's pet. Keia is a Pomeranian Mix puppy and the third member of the Puppy Dog Pals group. She is energetic and the youngest.\n\n==Background==\n===Personality===\nKeia is a very excited puppy, she usually gets excited easily and talks a lot. She is energetic, and like Rolly: silly. She loves to play Puppy Pounce with her friends and help everyone. Keia is hyper and energetic and is always ready for an adventure to save Chloe or her friends. Along with that, she is artistic and creative.  \n\n===Physical Appearance===\nKeia is a Shiba Inu puppy who has a creamy fur with white markings along her body, along with brown red-ish eyes. Her casual outfit is a blue puppy sweater and a pink collar with a golden star shaped tag. And usually, a cute, happy smile.  \n\n==Trivia==\n*Her voice actress actually confirmed that Keia does not have a breed, but she actually is an pommeranian samoyed mix\n*Merchandise of her was released before her debut\n*According to her voice actress, Keia's name was originally going to be \"Tikka\".
