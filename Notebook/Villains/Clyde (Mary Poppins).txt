"{{Infobox character\n|image = Clyde the Horse.jpg\n|name = Clyde\n|films = ''[[Mary Poppins Returns]]''\n|voice = [[Mark Addy]]\n|personality = \n|occupation =\n|alignment = Good \n|home = Royal Doulton Bowl\n|friends = [[Shamus]], [[Mary Poppins (character)|Mary Poppins]], [[Jack (Mary Poppins)|Jack]], [[John Banks]], [[Annabel Banks]], [[Georgie Banks]]\n|enemies = Wolf, Badger, Weasel\n|quote =\"Well of course she can talk.\"}}'''Clyde''' is a minor character in the film ''[[Mary Poppins Returns]]''. He resides in [[John Banks|John]], [[Annabel Banks|Annabel]], and [[Georgie Banks]]' late mother Kate's Royal Doulton Bowl bowl and travels through it with his master, [[Shamus]].\n\n==Role in the film==\nWhen a fight over the bowl accidentally causes it to crack, Clyde and Shamus tell John, Annabel, and Georgie that that caused their carriage wheel to break. [[Mary Poppins (character)|Mary Poppins]] takes them and [[Jack (Mary Poppins)|Jack]] inside the bowl to help fix the wheel. When John, Annabel, and Georgie ask for a ride because they feel the trip is too short, Clyde and Shamus give them one to the [[The Royal Doulton Music Hall|Royal Dalton Music Hall]].\n\nLater, when Georgie gets kidnapped by the Wolf and his accomplices, the Badger and Weasel, Clyde and Shamus help John and Annabel get onto the wagon carrying him.
