They appear in the episode \"[[Out of Scale (Rescue Rangers episode)|Out of Scale]]\".\n\n== History ==\nThey are the two henchmen who work for the crime lord [[Ratso Ratzkiwatzki|Ignatz \"Ratso\" Ratzkiwatzki]]. The two took [[Professor Norton Nimnul]] hostage and got their hands on his [[Gigantico Raygun]]. They used the ray gun to shrink the park's statue first, bringing it to Ratso's daughter [[Buffy Ratzkiwatzki|Buffy]], who enjoyed doll houses. Moose and Rocco then captured [[Chip and Dale]], whom they thought were squirrels.
