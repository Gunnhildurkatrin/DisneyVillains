She is first seen as a cheerleader at a basketball game at which </span>[[Oscar Proud|Oscar]]<span style=\"font-family:sans-serif;\">, Felix, and Bobby are working as Ice Cream men. She is the first of many women on the series who Oscar attempts to have an affair with. She seduces Oscar by rubbing his chin and inviting him to come to a party after the game. Oscar accepts the invitation right away, but doesn't attend because he needs to help </span>[[Trudy Proud|Trudy]]<span style=\"font-family:sans-serif;\">.
