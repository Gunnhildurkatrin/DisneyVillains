"{{NeedImage}}\n{{Infobox_character|fullname=Ariana Rose|home=Auradon|family=Audrey \n\nAurora \n\nPhilip}}\n\n'''Ariana Rose''' is a minor character in the Descendants franchise. She is the cousin of [[Audrey (Descendants)|Audrey]] and appeared in [[Escape from the Isle of the Lost: A Descendants Novel]]. \n\n== Personality ==\nShe behaves as a snobby and self absorbed girl who shows dislike towards Audrey. She was rude with [[Evie]] and told her it was naive that she said that Audrey does not need a prince to be happy. She didn't seem to be friends with [[Mal]] either and went right passed her without talking. \n\n== Printed Media ==\n\n=== [[Escape from the Isle of the Lost: A Descendants Novel]] ===\nShe is first seen in chapter thirty-eight. She had to get her dress from Evie because the the [[Flora, Fauna, and Merryweather|Three Good Fairies]] were making Audrey's gown at the time.
