He is portrayed by Tim Piggott Smith.\n\n== Appearances ==\n=== ''[[Alice in Wonderland (2010 film)|Alice in Wonderland]]'' ===\nHe is shown to be a very rich man in the movie. At the start of the movie he is seen in a meeting with Alice's dad Charles Kingsleigh, who explains to Lord Ascot and a couple of other men about an expedition to China. The meeting is interrupted by a very young Alice who woke up from a bizarre dream.\n\nFast forward 13 years, Alice attends a party held at Lord Ascot's mansion. this party is a surprise proposal from Lord Ascot's son Hamish, which everyone but Alice knew about. Alice declines the proposal and, after a discussion with Lord Ascot, goes on an expedition to China.\n=== ''[[Alice Through the Looking Glass]]'' ===\nPrior to the events of ''[[Alice Through the Looking Glass]]'', during Alice's three year trip to [[China]], Lord Ascot died from unknown causes.\n\n{{Alice in Wonderland}}\n{{DEFAULTSORT:Ascot, Lord}}\n[[Category:Tim Burton's Alice in Wonderland characters]]\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:Disney characters]]\n[[Category:Males]]\n[[Category:Live-action characters]]\n[[Category:Lords]]\n[[Category:Nobility]]\n[[Category:Deceased characters]]\n[[Category:English characters]]\n[[Category:Reformed characters]]"
