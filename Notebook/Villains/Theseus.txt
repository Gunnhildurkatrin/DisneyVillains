He was voiced by Eric Stoltz.\n\n==Role==\nAfter the Minotaur escapes from the Labyrinth, he helps Hercules face it. Theseus has a double identity, his second self being the superhero \"Grim Avenger\", whose costume resembles that of DC Comics' character [[wikipedia:Doctor Fate|Doctor Fate]], though his personality and back story are more inspired by [[wikipedia:Batman|Batman]]. As the Grim Avenger, Theseus is constantly narrating his every move aloud.
