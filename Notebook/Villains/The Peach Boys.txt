"{{Infobox character\n|name = The Peach Boys\n|image = B6842c9abd7328f5cb2de7d22a5f0793.jpg\n|rides = [[Food Rocks]]\n|inspiration = [[The Beach Boys]]}}'''The Peach Boys''' are characters that performed in the former [[Food Rocks]] attraction at [[Epcot]].\n\nThey are parody of [[The Beach Boys]].\n\nThe group is comprised of four diminutive humanoids, each with a different fruit for a head: an orange, a pear, a peach, and an apple.\n\nThe orange-headed member played a keyboard, the pear-headed member played a tambourine, the peach-headed member played a guitar, and the apple-headed member played a bass guitar.\n\n{{CharacterStub}}\n{{DEFAULTSORT:Peach Boys}}\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:Character groups]]\n[[Category:Singing characters]]\n[[Category:Theme park characters]]\n[[Category:Foods]]\n[[Category:Food Rocks characters]]\n[[Category:Disney characters]]\n[[Category:Males]]\n[[Category:Bands]]\n[[Category:Musicians]]"
