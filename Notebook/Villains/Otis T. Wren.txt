Wren''' is a character from the ''[[Adventurers Club]]''.\n\n== History ==\n\n=== Background ===\nOtis T. Wren was an adventurer and ichthyologist in the early half of the 20th century.  He became a member of the Adventurers Club, a splinter-group of the [[Society of Explorers and Adventurers]] which was created by one [[Merriweather Adam Pleasure]].  By [[1937]], Otis would serve as the resident club-treasurer of the Adventurers Club.  Otis also befriended club-affiliate [[Trader Sam]] whom he went on a river expedition with.\n\nIn [[1941]], he left the club's headquarters on [[Pleasure Island (Downtown Disney)|Pleasure Island]] in [[Lake Buena Vista]] due to it having been devastated by Hurricane Charlotte.  Circa [[1948]], Otis was among several Adventurers Club members who befriended aviator [[Jock Lindsey]].  Otis assisted Lindsey in opening his hangar bar in Disney Springs, [[Florida]] in the year [[1955]].\n\n== Appearances ==\n\n=== [[Disneyland]] ===\n\n==== [[Trader Sam's|Trader Sam's Enchanted Tiki Bar]] ====\nThere is a photograph from Wren in this bar which shows him on a raft being driven by Trader Sam.  On the photograph is a message from Otis which reads, \"''Sam, thanks for keeping me ahead of the rest''\".\n\n=== [[Walt Disney World Resort|Walt Disney World]] ===\n\n==== Adventurers Club ====\nOtis was one of the major characters featured in this defunct attraction of Pleasure Island.\n\n==== [[Jock Lindsey's Hangar Bar]] ====\nThere are several photographs of Otis in this bar, showing him drinking at Jock Lindsey's bar and having assisted in its ribbon-cutting.\n\n== Gallery ==\n<gallery>\nFile:Adventurers Club Cast.jpg\n</gallery>{{Society of Explorers and Adventurers}}\n[[Category:Theme park characters]]\n[[Category:Males]]\n[[Category:Pleasure Island]]\n[[Category:S.E.A. characters]]\n[[Category:Disney characters]]"
