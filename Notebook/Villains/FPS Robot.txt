"\n{{Infobox Character\n|image = [[File:FPSBotInfobox.webp|thumb]]\n|films = ''[[Wreck-It Ralph]]''\n|occupation = \n|likes = Proper gameplay\n|dislikes = Ralph messing with it\n|possessions = \"Hero's Duty\" gun}}\nThe '''FPS Robot''' is a character in ''[[Wreck-It Ralph (film)|Wreck-It Ralph]]''. It appears in the arcade game, ''[[Hero's Duty]]''.\n\n==Background==\n===Personality===\nIt seems to take its role seriously. It also gets upset with Ralph.\n\n===Physical appearance===\nIt has silver skin, wheels for feet, and a screen head.\n\n==Appearances==\n===''[[Wreck-It Ralph (film)|Wreck-It Ralph]]''===\nAs the [[Moppet Girl]] prepares to play \"[[Hero's Duty]]\", the FPS Robot appears during gameplay as the girl begins her duty playing the arcade game. During gameplay, [[Wreck-It Ralph (character)|Ralph]] constantly interrupts by going into the robot's screen as the girl is tasked to fight against the [[Cy-Bugs]] in order to progress through gameplay. The girl's gameplay, however, was unsuccessful when one of the Cy-Bugs flies toward the robot, causing her to lose the game.
