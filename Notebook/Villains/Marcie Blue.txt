"[[File:Marcie_0001.jpg|thumb]]'''Marcie Blue'''\u00a0is the mother of\u00a0[[Ty Blue]]\u00a0and\u00a0[[Rocky Blue]], and wife of Dr.\u00a0[[Curtis Blue]]. She first appeared in the episode \"Heat It Up\". She is portrayed by\u00a0Carla Renata.\n\n==Personality==\nIn \"Heat It Up\", Rocky, Ty and Mrs. Blue moved in with\u00a0CeCe\u00a0and her family. She had a minor fight with\u00a0Georgia Jones, because of Mrs. Blue's remarks about how she has cleaned up more hair on their floor then in her two hair salons that she owns. It was revealed that her husband is always on business trips; he returned in\u00a0\"Doctor It Up\" and hid the fact that she bought a new car while he was away. Her deceased mother is named Rose and it is revealed in \"Wild It Up\" that Rocky missed her funeral due to her need for having perfect attendance at school.
