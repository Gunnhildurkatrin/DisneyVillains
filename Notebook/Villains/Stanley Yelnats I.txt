He is the son of [[Elya Yelnats]].\n==History==\n\nStanley made a fortune in the stock market. Unfortunately, he was robbed and left stranded by [[Katherine Barlow|Kissin' Kate Barlow]]. Kate stole his fortune and even his hat, which she would wear in some of the robberies she committed. He wandered in the desert with no food and water for sixteen days and eventually made his way to [[God's Thumb]], where he found water.
