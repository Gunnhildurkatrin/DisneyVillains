She is the [[Olympian Gods|Olympian Goddess]] of Family and Hearth.\n\n==Background==\nThe goddess of family and the hearth is seen as a cheery housewife type. She is often seen cooking or marveling at her own confections, most of which end up being ruined in a comical fashion. She is the sister of [[Hera]], [[Demeter]], [[Poseidon]], [[Hades]] and [[Zeus (Hercules)|Zeus]]. Hestia is known as the peacemaker of the Olympian family. She appears very rarely in the series but has a big role in \"The Tiff On Olympus\"\u00a0 She is the daughter of [[Rhea]] and [[Kronos]].\n\n===Powers and Abilities===\nAs a Goddess, Hestia Possesses:\n*Immortality\n*Superstrength\n*Teleportation\n*Metamorphosis (Shapeshifting)\n*Vast magical abilities\n\nOther\n*Great Cook\n*Orange/Dark Yellow Aura\n\n==Role in the [[Hercules (TV series)|series]]==\nIn \"[[Hercules and the Tiff on Olympus]]\", Hestia appeared cooking for the gods but her food were mostly destroyed in comical fashion.\n\n==Trivia==\n*Betty White, who plays Hestia, previously played Sue Ann Nivens, The \"Happy Homemaker\" on the Mary Tyler Moore Show, a charcter similar to Hestia.\n\n==Gallery==\n<gallery position=\"center\">\nHestiaherc.jpg\nHestiaherc2.jpg\nHercules and the Tiff on Olympus (48).jpg\n</gallery>\n\n{{Hercules}}\n[[Category:Greek Mythology]]\n[[Category:Hercules characters]]\n[[Category:Olympian Gods]]\n[[Category:Greek characters]]\n[[Category:Characters in the Disney animated features canon]]\n[[Category:Disney characters]]\n[[Category:Animated characters]]\n[[Category:TV Animation characters]]\n[[Category:Goddesses]]\n[[Category:Siblings]]\n[[Category:Aunts]]\n[[Category:Heroines]]\n[[Category:Chefs]]\n[[Category:Immortal]]\n[[Category:Shapeshifters]]"
