She is a giant sea serpent who lives in the Cave of Neptune at the bottom of the Never Sea with her baby [[Slink]].\n\n==Role in the Series==\nWhile [[Jake (Pirate)|Jake]]\u00a0and his crew were exploring the wonders of the Never Sea with Stormy they reach the Cave of Neptune when they hear Slink's Mother roar mistaken it for [[Slink]] on their first meeting. Not wanting to invoke the rage of Slink's mother, they decide to return to [[Pirate Island (Jake and the Neverland Pirates)|Pirate Island]] unknown to them that the baby sea serpent has followed them back to the island and later gets captured by [[Sharky and Bones]] as the main attraction of [[Captain Hook]]'s petting zoo.
