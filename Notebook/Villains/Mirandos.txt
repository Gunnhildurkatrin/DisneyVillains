"{{Infobox character\n|image = Mirandos.jpg\n|shows = ''[[Miles from Tomorrowland]]''\n|voice = Ivy Bishop\n|inspiration = Miranda from ''The Tempest''\n|fullname = Mirandos Ariellian\n|occupation = [[Cosmic Explorers|Cosmic Explorer]] (formerly)<br>Member of [[Mission Force One]]\n|home = [[Tempestoro]] (formerly)<br>[[Zenith]]\n|parents = Prosperos Ariellian (father)\n|pets = [[Bowtie]] (Robo-Penguin)\n|likes = Computers, Quantum ball\n|dislikes = Outsiders (formerly)\n|possessions = Calibandroid<br>Solidizer Gloves\n|affiliations = Tomorrowland Transit Authority}}'''Mirandos Ariellian''' is a character, who appears in the [[Disney Junior]] animated series, ''[[Miles from Tomorrowland]]''. She is a young intelligent Ariellian from the planet [[Tempestoro]]. Later in the series, she becomes a member of [[Mission Force One]].\n\n==Background==\n===Personality and Traits===\nMuch like the rest of her people, Mirandos likes computers, and like her best friend Loretta is a genius at computer coding with the use of her Calibandroid (similar to Loretta's [[Bracelex]]). Common for her species, Mirandos speaks with a computerized-like voice.\n\n==Trivia==\n*Mirandos is modelled and named after Miranda from Shakespeare's ''The Tempest''.\n**Mirandos' species is also named after the character [[Wikipedia:Ariel (The Tempest)|Ariel]] from Shakespeare's play.\n*Ivy Bishop, who provides the voice of Mirandos is the sister of Fiona Bishop, who provides the voice of [[Loretta Callisto]].\n*Although Mirandos made her debut appearance in \"[[The Quantum Cup]]\", her first meeting with the Callistos was in \"[[The Discovery Expedition]]\".\n*As of \"[[Unfair Getaway]]\", she is now an official Cosmic Explorer.\n*It is shown that she has a close friendship with Loretta.\n*In \"[[Operation Groovestar]]\", she sings for the first time.\n\n==Gallery==\n<gallery position=\"center\" captionalign=\"center\" widths=\"150\" spacing=\"small\">\nThe Discovery Expedition 2.jpg\nGalactech - March of the Robo-Penguins 1.jpg\nGalactech - March of the Robo-Penguins 2.jpg\nThe First Day of Galactic School 3.jpg\nBuilding Day 1.jpg\nConnect and Protect 4.png\nThe Great Space Train Robbery-Mission Pets One 1.jpg\nVillain After Villain 1.jpg\nMFO Hoverbikes.png\nMiles, Loretta, Haruna and Mirandos in Gi Guit.jpg\nMFO - Loretta, Mirandos, and Miles.png\nThe Space Station Situation 11.jpg\nAggro Jam 1.png\nThe Nemesystems Takeover 1.png\nThe Nemesystems Takeover 2.png\nMission Force One.png\nMission Force One poster.jpg\n</gallery>\n\n{{CharacterStub}}\n\n{{Miles from Tomorrowland}}\n[[Category:Disney characters]]\n[[Category:Animated characters]]\n[[Category:TV Animation characters]]\n[[Category:Females]]\n[[Category:Miles from Tomorrowland characters]]\n[[Category:Aliens]]\n[[Category:Kids]]\n[[Category:Scouts]]\n[[Category:Heroines]]\n[[Category:Disney Junior characters]]\n[[Category:Pilots]]\n[[Category:Singing characters]]\n[[Category:Engineers]]"
