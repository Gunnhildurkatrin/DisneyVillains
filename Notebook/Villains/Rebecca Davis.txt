"[[File:Rddogwithablod.jpg|thumb]]'''Rebecca Davis''' is a character in [[Disney Channel Original Series|Disney Channel's Original Series]], ''[[Dog With a Blog]]''. She is portrayed by Kimmy Caruso.\n\n==Biography==\nTyler continued to spy on her as she was one of Bennett's new patients. After overhearing Bennett talking to the vet about the parrot, Tyler thinks that the suggestions the doctor gave were really for Rebecca. At school, he started shoving grapes into her mouth and blowing into her face, which made her flee. At the end of the episode, Tyler apologizes and she says that he might be a nice guy to take a chance with, but she wished there could be some sort of sign to tell her, when Lucy comes down and says \"Tyler's a jerk!\", this made her to believe it and leaves.\n\n==Personality==\nWhile many believe she is stuck-up for not talking very much, the truth is she is very shy around other people.\n\n==Episode Appearance==\n===Season One Episodes===\n*\"[[The Parrot Trap]]\"\n\n==Trivia==\n*She is the second known patients of Bennett James.
