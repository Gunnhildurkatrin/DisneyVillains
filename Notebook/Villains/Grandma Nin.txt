"{{Infobox character\n|image = Grandma Nin.png\n|name = Grandma Nin\n|shows = ''[[The Ghost and Molly McGee]]''\n|voice = [[Sumalee Montano]]\n|fullname = Nin Quimby\n|alias = Grandma\n|home = [[Los Angeles]], [[California]]\n|children = [[Sharon McGee]] (daughter)\n|family = [[Pete McGee]] (son-in-law)<br>[[Molly McGee]] (granddaughter)<br>[[Darryl McGee]] (grandson)\n|likes = Her family, [[Scratch (The Ghost and Molly McGee)|Scratch]], making traditional Thai snacks\n|dislikes = Her daughter giving up her dream|status=Active}}\n'''Grandma Nin Quimby''' is a recurring character in the [[Disney Channel]] animated series ''[[The Ghost and Molly McGee]]''.\n\n==Background==\n===Personality===\nGrandma Nin is shown to be a very feisty, yet wise and caring woman. Despite this, she is also very stubborn and not afraid to show her true emotions or opinions on various subject matters. Her relationship with Sharon, her daughter was strained. Initially, it was thought to be because she did not fulfill what Nin wanted of her, when in actuality, Nin wanted her daughter to follow her dreams, showing that she does care and believe in her.\n\n===Physical appearance===\nGrandma Nin is shorter than Sharon and Pete, but still taller than Molly and Darryl. She has tan brown skin and grey, neatly flowing short hair. She wears big glasses with brown frames and pink lenses, a white shirt, pink coat, tan pants, white socks and dark pink slip-ons. Additionally, she wears a necklace medallion with a red gem in it. \n\n==Trivia==\n*Grandma Nin has the same voice actress as her daughter.\n*Her living in Los Angeles, California is most likely a reference to the Thai protagonist [[Anne Boonchuy]] in ''[[Amphibia]]''.\n\n==External links==\n*[[w:c:theghostandmollymcgee:Nin Quimby|Grandma Nin]] at the [[w:c:theghostandmollymcgee|The Ghost and Molly McGee Wiki]]\n{{CharacterStub}}\n{{The Ghost and Molly McGee}}\n{{DEFAULTSORT:Nin, Grandma}}\n[[Category:Animated characters]]\n[[Category:Parents]]\n[[Category:The Ghost and Molly McGee characters]]\n[[Category:Females]]\n[[Category:Adults]]\n[[Category:Elderly characters]]\n[[Category:TV Animation characters]]\n[[Category:Disney characters]]\n[[Category:Disney Channel characters]]\n[[Category:Asian characters]]\n[[Category:Grandparents]]"
