One of his greatest desires was to have his very own private ski slope. He requested [[Higher For Hire]] to deliver a giant iceberg to his kingdom and drop it off near the palace.\n\nNevarhas Bin-Broak also has his own harem, which is shown briefly with him during a cameo appearance in the episode \"[[The Golden Sprocket of Friendship]]\".\n\n==Gallery==\n<gallery position=\"center\">\ngoldsprocketfriend - nhbb.png|Neverhas Bin-Broak's cameo in \"The Golden Sprocket of Friendship\"\n</gallery>\n\n{{TaleSpin}}\n{{DEFAULTSORT:Bin-Broak, Nevarhas}}\n[[Category:TaleSpin characters]]\n[[Category:Middle Eastern characters]]\n[[Category:Royalty]]\n[[Category:Nobility]]\n[[Category:Hyenas]]\n[[Category:TV Animation characters]]\n[[Category:Anthropomorphic characters]]\n[[Category:Males]]\n[[Category:The Disney Afternoon characters]]\n[[Category:Animated characters]]\n[[Category:Princes]]\n[[Category:Disney characters]]\n[[Category:Adults]]"
