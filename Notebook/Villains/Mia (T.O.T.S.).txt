She is the adoptive daughter of [[Captain Beakman]]. She first appeared in \"[[The Purrfect Little Helper]]\".\n\n==Background==\n===Physical appearance===\nMia is a kitten with orange fur and purple eyes. She has a purple bow and collar with the T.O.T.S. symbol on it, and wears a white diaper with purple trim and a pattern of T.O.T.S. symbols.\n\n===Personality===\nMia is a baby kitten that may be small, but despite that, she is strong (both physically and emotionally), brave, and is always willing to help others when they need it. She can also be very sweet and very playful.\n\n==Trivia==\n*In \"[[Lost Lovey]]\", she likes Freddy a lot.\n*In \"[[Lend Me Your Paw]]\", she looks up to both [[Pip (T.O.T.S.)|Pip]] and [[Freddy (T.O.T.S.)|Freddy]].\n*She owns Freddy\u2019s stuffed monkey named Mr. Muffins. She used to own a stuffed bunny named Lady Foo Foo, until it ended up in Genie's crate and fell into a waterfall.\n*She tried to make friends with Linus the lion cub, but his roar was so loud that it scared her she most likely ran back to her mother Captain Beakman after this scene.\n*She was best friends with Chloe until she had to go to her forever family, but Pip and Freddy taught her to use the Fly Pad to visit her whenever she wanted.\n*In the episode \"[[Puppy Problems]]\", she is friends with Lucky and they act like brother and sister.\n*In the episode \"[[Toy Trouble]]\", she gives her old toys away with the help of Pip and Freddy.\n*In the episode \"Mia Goes To School\", Mia starts pre-school with many different TOTS babies since she's big enough, unlike Lucky. \n\n==Gallery==\n<gallery widths=\"150\">\nMia with a white yarn ball.jpeg\nMia hugs Mr Muffins.jpeg\nMia holding Mr Muffins.jpeg\nMia says happy birthday to Pip.jpeg\nMia plays with Freddy\u2019s feathers on his forehead.jpeg\nMia and Lady Foo Foo are introduced by Mr Muffins.jpeg\nMia in her mother\u2019s wings.jpeg\nMia, how\u2019d you like to meet Linus?.jpeg\nKC gives Mia a little broom.jpeg\nBye bye mommy.jpeg\nMia and Freddy with their loveys.jpeg\nMia in KC\u2019s arms.jpeg\nMia cries.jpeg\nLinus roars at Mia.jpeg\nMia sees Mr Muffins.jpeg\nLend Me Your Paw.png\nBest Friends Wherever.png|\"No go!
