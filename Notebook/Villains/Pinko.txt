Otherwise, he wears a white shirt with a vest and grey pants, plus a red necktie.\n\n==Character==\nPinko is very loyal to his duty and obligations. he was commissioned to capture the elusive thief Fantomallard and this task has become a life mission. Despite his commendable efforts, he is clumsy, bumbling and lacks a deductive reasoning that may help him solve cases, and for this reason he is always defeated by the gentleman thief.\n\nHe has a relationship of peaceful coexistence with his wife Adelaide.
