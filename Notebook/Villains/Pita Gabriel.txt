"{{Infobox character\n|name = Pita Gabriel\n|image = Frocks10.jpg\n|rides = [[Food Rocks]]\n|inspiration = Peter Gabriel|voice = [[Jess Harnell]]}}'''Pita Gabriel''' is a character in the attraction [[Food Rocks]] at [[Epcot]].\n\n== Appearances ==\n\n=== [[Food Rocks]] ===\nOne of the many musicians participating in the Benefit Concert for Good Nutrition, Pita Gabriel performs \"[[High Fiber]]\", a parody of Gabriel's Sledgehammer.\n\nHe later appears for the closing number of \"[[Choose Before You Chew]]\".\n\n== Trivia ==\n* While Peter Gabriel was willing to record his own voice for the attraction, hearing Jess Harnell's demo tracks impressed him enough to convince him that it wasn't necessary, a sentiment also shared by [[Sting]] when hearing Harnell's impression of him for [[The Refrigerator Police]].<ref>https://twitter.com/TammyTuckey/status/1287487601323147264</ref>\n* Pita Gabriel was one of several Food Rocks animatronics to have their remains sold at auction after the attraction's closure, with only his head remaining as of a 2017 Van Eaton Galleries auction.\n\n== Gallery ==\n<gallery>\nPita Gabriel's Head.jpg|Pita Gabriel's head in 2017\nPita Gabriel Poster.jpg|Queue poster for Pita Gabriel\n</gallery>\n== References ==\n<references />\n\n{{DEFAULTSORT:Gabriel, Peter}}\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:Singing characters]]\n[[Category:Theme park characters]]\n[[Category:Foods]]\n[[Category:Disney characters]]\n[[Category:Food Rocks characters]]\n[[Category:Males]]"
