She is the [[Chip 'n Dale Rescue Rangers|Rescue Rangers]]' client in the final two episodes, \"[[A Chorus Crime]]\" and \"[[They Shoot Dogs, Don't They]]\".\nThe first time she was their client was when her tap shoes were stolen by [[Professor Norton Nimnul]]. The scientist was using them to make a group of penguins tap dance a chorus.\n\nThe second was when she was shooting a commercial in Taxidermia.
