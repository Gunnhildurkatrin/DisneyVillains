He followed [[Anne Boonchuy|Anne]] and her surrogate frog family on their road trip to [[Newtopia]]. He eventually reached them in [[Wartwood Swamp|Wartwood]] where he was befriended by [[Polly Plantar|Polly]] who gave the robot the name Frobo.
