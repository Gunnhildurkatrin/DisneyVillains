"[[File:Dog Lobster.jpg|thumb|250px]]The '''Dog-Lobster''' is\u00a0a creature featured on the animated television show ''[[Kim Possible]]''.\n\n==History==\nDog-lobsters are mutant cuddle buddies, that are mixed between dogs and lobsters. It may seem cute, but also dangerous. This creature has the body and head of a dog, six lobster legs, lobster claws, and a lobster tail.\n\n[[Steven Barkin|Mr. Barkin]] and [[Ron Stoppable|Ron]] saw what appeared to be a dog coming out of the trees, but when it came out of the trees it is revealed that the dog is a Dog-Lobster; a [[Rabbit-Rhino]] scared the Dog-Lobster, until [[Amy Hall|DNAmy]] arrived with her own Dog-Lobster and was ashamed that her Rabbit-Rhinoceros ran away.\n\n{{Kim Possible}}\n[[Category:Creatures]]\n[[Category:Hybrids]]\n[[Category:Kim Possible characters]]\n[[Category:Dogs]]\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:Animated characters]]\n[[Category:TV Animation characters]]\n[[Category:Disney characters]]\n[[Category:Crustaceans]]"
