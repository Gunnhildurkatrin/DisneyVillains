For example, in the episode [[Just One of the Princes|\"Just One of the Princes\"]], when Robin gave Sofia advice by saying \"An early bird catches the worm\", Mia takes it literally and asks what Sofia needs a worm for.\n\nEven so, she can have a witty comeback to say, often toward [[Clover]].\n\n===Physical Appearance===\nLike her friend [[Robin (Sofia the First)|Robin]], Mia also has an orange beak, but instead she has blue and white feathers.\n\n==Gallery==\n===Screenshots===\n<gallery position=\"center\" captionalign=\"center\" widths=\"150\" spacing=\"small\">\nSofia.jpg\nSofia02.jpg\nSofia-the-first02.png\nSofiathefirsts1011080p.png\nSofia-minimus07.png\nSofia-Finding Clover.jpg\nRobin&Mia.jpg\nSofia with Clover Robin and Mia.jpg\nGizmo-Gwen-29.png\nEverything Is Gonna Be Great.png\nFR Clover, Robin, Mia & Whatnaught.png\nSofia's animal friends watching the graduation.png\nBird Sofia and her bird friends.png\nFR Robin & Mia load Whatnaught's catapult.png\nFR Sofia's animal friends overjoyed that she's safe.png\nJan-06-0003_23-19-02.gif|\"Clover!
