"{{Infobox_character\n|image = Spink (2).jpg\n|name = Spink\n|films = ''[[Cinderella (1950 film)|Cinderella]]'' (deleted)\n|personality = Loyal\n|appearance = Short man with bulbous nose, red hair tied in a ponytail, red waistcoat with gold epaulets, blue trousers, white stockings, black shoes\n|occupation = [[Prince Charming]]'s valet\n|alignment = Good\n|goal = To help [[Prince Charming]] find the [[Cinderella (character)|girl]] he fell in love with (succeeded)\n|home = The [[King's Castle]]\n|friends = His boss [[Prince Charming]], [[The King (Cinderella)|The King]], [[Cinderella (character)|Cinderella]], Clarissa, [[Jaq and Gus]], [[Mice|other mice]]\n|enemies = [[Lady Tremaine]], [[Drizella Tremaine|Drizella]], [[Anastasia Tremaine|Anastasia]], [[Lucifer]]\n}}Spink\u00a0was a deleted character that was present in an early draft for [[Walt Disney Animation Studios|Disney]]'s [[1950]] animated feature film, [[Cinderella (1950 film)|''Cinderella'']].\n\n\n\n==Role in the film==\nSpink was [[Prince Charming]]'s valet. He made his first appearance hunting with his master. Later, after receiving news of the ball from the [[The King (Cinderella)|King]], he went out to deliver the invitations with the royal heralds. On the night of the ball, Spink fell in love with [[Cinderella (character)|Cinderella]]'s pet turtle, Clarissa, whom the [[Fairy Godmother]] had turned human so that she could accompany her mistress. Upon seeing that [[Cinderella (character)|Cinderella]] was leaving, Spink chased her down the palace steps, only for him to trip and fall into a reflecting pool.\n\nThe next day, Spink went from house to house, searching for the owner of the [[Glass Slipper|glass slipper]]. When he reached the [[Cinderella's Ch\u00e2teau|chateau]], [[Drizella Tremaine|Drizella]] nearly succeeded in cramming the slipper onto her foot. However, the [[Mice|mice]] quickly stole it from her.
