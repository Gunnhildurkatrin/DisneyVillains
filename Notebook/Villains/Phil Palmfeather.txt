"{{Infobox_character|image = Phil_Palmfeather.png|shows = ''[[Mighty Ducks (TV series)|Mighty Ducks]]''|voice = [[Jim Belushi]]|personality = friendly, arrogant, greedy, dimwitted, egocentric, nervous|appearance = fat brown-haired man, with blue suit and purple tie|likes= Making money|occupation= The Ducks manager}}'''Phil Palmfeather'''\u00a0is a character in the ''[[Mighty Ducks (TV series)|Mighty Ducks]]'' TV series. He is the Ducks' manager when they are an ice hockey team. He'd rather have the team do sometimes rather dangerous or stupid promotional gimmicks and autograph signings with fans than having them save the world, although he does acknowledge that they do good.
