Ron is the son of two superheroes, but does not have any powers himself. Despite this, he feels a great sense of pride in merely driving the \"superheroes of tomorrow\" to school.\n\nHe helps to save Sky High by preventing [[Royal Pain]]'s henchman from escaping with the superhero babies, claiming he is the only one authorized to transport superheroes.\u00a0He is the rare case of a person born to two supers who inherits no powers. However, he later falls into toxic waste and gains superpowers.
