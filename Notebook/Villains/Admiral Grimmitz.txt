He is the Navy commander on the aircraft carrier that [[Donald Duck|Donald]] is an enlisted sailor on. He always gets annoyed at Donald's accident-proned stunts, and likes to play with military equipment that makes things \"go ka-blooey.\"\n\nHe was voiced by [[Peter Cullen]], and the voice was based on that of John Wayne (much like how he did so with Optimus Prime).
