"{{Infobox_character\n|image = Whitefang-01.jpg\n|films = ''[[White Fang]]''<br>''[[White Fang 2: Myth of the White Wolf]]'' (cameo)\n|actor = [[Ethan Hawke]]\n|fullname = Jack Conroy|occupation = White Fang's kind master\n|home = San Francisco<br>Alaskan wilderness\n|pets = [[White Fang (character)|White Fang]]\n|friends = [[Henry Casey]], [[Alex Larson]], [[Clarence Skunker Thurston]], [[Belinda Casey]], Heather, [[Grey Beaver]]\n|enemies = [[Beauty Smith]], [[Luke and Tinker]]\n|likes = Gold, White Fang\n|dislikes = Being robbed\n|status = Active|inspiration = Weedon Scott from Jack London's novel|family = Scott Conroy \u2020 (father)<br>Alice Conroy \u2020 (mother)}}'''Jack Conroy''' is one of the main characters in Disney's ''[[White Fang]] ''and a minor character in [[White Fang 2: Myth of the White Wolf|its sequel]]. He is based on Weedon Scott from Jack London's novel.\n\n== Appearances ==\n\n=== ''[[White Fang]]'' ===\nJack Conroy arrives in Alaska from San Francisco to look for his deceased father's mining claim. Conroy meets a musher named Alex Larson and Clarence \"Skunker\" Thurston, Conroy's father's buddy, who reluctantly agrees to guide Jack to his father's claim in the Yukon. When Jack encounters White Fang who has been cruelly treated by his owner, Beauty Smith, he rescues the canine from the jaws of death during a fight with a fierce bulldog named Cherokee. Aided by Alex, Jack nurses White Fang back to health, and the animal becomes a close companion to the men.\n\n=== ''[[White Fang 2: Myth of the White Wolf]]'' ===\nWhen Jack Conroy goes to San Francisco, he leaves White Fang with his friend, Henry Casey.
