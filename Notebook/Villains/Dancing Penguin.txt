"{{Infobox_character||image = Dancing Penguin.jpg\n|name = Dancing Penguin\n|games = ''Club Penguin''\n|appearance = Brown<br>Wears glasses\n|occupation = Secret Mission Subplot or Side-Mission\n|goal = To dance\n|alignment = Good/Neutral\n|friends = [[Dot the Disguise Gal]], two unnamed penguins, Keeper of the Boiler Room (possibly)}}'''Dancing Penguin''' is a cheerful [http://clubpenguin.wikia.com/wiki/Brown brown] [http://clubpenguin.wikia.com/wiki/Penguin penguin]. He likes to dance (hence his name), owns a portable telescope, and likes riding on the Ski Lift. He appears in most [http://clubpenguin.wikia.com/wiki/Secret_Missions Secret Missions] and he gives players a thank-you card at the end of Mission 3, as an optional reward. The main reason in why he usually appears in Secret Missions is usually as a \"side-mission\" or \"subplot\", like having to fix his telescope or unjam the [http://clubpenguin.wikia.com/wiki/Ski_Lift Ski Lift].
