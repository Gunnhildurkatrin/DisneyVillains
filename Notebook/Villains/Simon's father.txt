"{{Infobox character\n|image = Simon's father.png\n|name  = Simon's father\n|shows = [[Timon & Pumbaa]]\n|voice = [[Jim Cummings]]\n|home = The Dingling Brothers' Circus\n|likes = Naps\n|dislikes = Being disturbed from his naps \n}}\n'''Simon's father''' is a character from the ''[[Timon & Pumbaa]]'' TV series who makes his first and only appearance in the episode \"[[Big Top Breakfast]]\". He is voiced by [[Jim Cummings]]. \n\n==Background==\n===Physical Description===\nSimon's father is a large monkey with dark gray fur on his body, and beige skin on his face, ears, toes, and stomach. He has black eyes. He also has black, bushy eyebrows and wears blue pants with matching suspenders, a green fez, and a purple tie with green spots.\n\n===Personality===\nSimon's father is experienced. Not much else is known about him, as he is mostly seen napping. However, he tells [[Simon]] about his future, indicating his wisdom. He is also supportive, helping Simon when he messed up his lines. As he does stand-up comedy, it can also be inferred that he is humorous.\n\n==Appearances==\n===\"[[Big Top Breakfast]]\"===\nSimon's father takes a nap while his son practices his act. While Simon worries that he isn't any good, his father tells him that he'll be in the spotlight one day. He then goes back to sleep, unaware that his son, along with a crate of circus things, had fallen out of the Dingling Brothers' Circus aircraft.\n\nLater, Simon manages to get back to the aircraft, where he reunites with his father. However, his father simply reiterates what he stated earlier about his future, and tells his son to go back to sleep.\n\n==Trivia==\n*Unlike his son, Simon's father does not appear to have a tail.\n\n==Gallery==\n<gallery position=\"center\">\nSimon's father 1.png\nSimon's father 2.png|Simon's father sleeping, while his son performs\nSimon's father 3.png|Simon's father telling him about his future\n</gallery>\n\n{{The Lion King}}\n[[Category:Disney characters]]\n[[Category:Males]]\n[[Category:Timon & Pumbaa characters]]\n[[Category:Monkeys]]\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:Animated characters]]\n[[Category:TV Animation characters]]\n[[Category:The Disney Afternoon characters]]\n[[Category:Parents]]"
