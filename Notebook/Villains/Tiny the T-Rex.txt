"{{Infobox character\n|image = Tin.jpg\n|name = Tiny\n|status = Active\n|films = ''[[Meet the Robinsons]]'' <br> ''[[Wreck-It Ralph (film)|Wreck-It Ralph]]'' (cameo)\n|rides = ''[[Wonderful World of Animation]]''\n|voice = Joe Mateo\n|personality = Dangerous, aggressive, persistent (while under Bowler Hat Guy's control)<br />Sweet, docile, puppy-like (while not under Bowler Hat Guy's control)\n|appearance = Slender brown Tyrannosaurus Rex with red markings on his back and tail\n|occupation = Minion of [[Bowler Hat Guy]] (formerly)<br>The Robinsons' family pet\n|alignment = Bad (under the influence of [[Mini-Doris]]), later good\n|home = [[Anderson Observatory]]\n|likes = Being part of the Robinsons family\n|dislikes = His big head and little arms (while under Mini-Doris's control)\n|fate = Is freed from mind control and becomes the Robinsons' pet\n|quote = \"I have a big head, and little arms...I'm just not sure how well this plan was thought through.\"<br>\"Master?\"\n}}'''Tiny''' is a [[Tyrannosaurus Rex]] that appeared in ''[[Meet the Robinsons]]''. He was briefly under the control of [[Michael Yagoobian|Bowler Hat Guy]]'s [[Mini-Doris]], but later became a pet to the Robinsons. He is voiced by Joe Mateo.\n\n==Appearances==\n===''[[Meet the Robinsons]]''===\nTiny is a Tyrannosaurus Rex that was brought under the control of the [[Mini-Doris]] that was remotely controlled by [[Michael Yagoobian|Bowler Hat Guy]], who got the idea when seeing the Mini-Doris hovering over a dinosaur topiary on the grounds of [[Anderson Observatory]]. Wanting to keep it a surprise, he didn't inform Doris, who was planning on cutting the chandelier to get rid of [[Wilbur Robinson|Wilbur]], and was enraged by the blatantly obvious attack as opposed to her subtle actions. After a fight with the family and Bowler Hat Guy again being told by a controlled minion that his plans weren't thought through, the Tyrannosaurus is freed from Mini-Doris' control when Wilbur shoots it off his head with [[Gaston Framagucci|Gaston]]'s Meatball Canon and turns out to be a rather docile specimen and is shown to be acting like a puppy. Even after [[Doris]]' actions are undone by the climax of the film and he should have been sent back as a result, Tiny still appears to see [[Lewis Robinson|Lewis]] back off to the past, now a pet of the Robinson household.\n\n===''[[Wreck-It Ralph]]''===\nTiny makes a cameo appearance in the [[2012]] animated film ''[[Wreck-It Ralph]]'' as a video game dinosaur in [[Game Central Station]], heavily resembling [[w:c:primalrage:Sauron|Sauron]] from ''[[w:c:primalrage:Primal Rage|Primal Rage]]''. The colors on this Tiny are lighter and more cartoony than seen in ''Meet the Robinsons''.\n\n== Trivia ==\n* Tiny was featured on a promotion for ''[[The Good Dinosaur]]''. \n* Tiny is essentially the opposite of Frankie. While Frankie is an amphibian given sentience and sapience, Tiny is a time-displaced extinct\u00a0reptile who has a dog-like mental state. While under Bowler Hat Guy's control,\u00a0Frankie's weakness is his frail body in comparison to the humans and\u00a0Tiny's weakness is his huge, clumsy body.\n\n==Gallery==\n<gallery
