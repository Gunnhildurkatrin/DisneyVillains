He is asleep for most of the meeting but is woken up by [[Mother Nature]] to vote on a name change for the Tooth Fairy, on which he votes no, apologizes, then goes back to sleep.\n\n===''[[The Santa Clause 3: The Escape Clause]]''===\nThe Sandman's role is expanded a bit in the sequel. Sandman attends an emergency meeting of the Council, where [[Jack Frost]] is accused of trying to upstage Santa, sleeping through most of it.\n\nLater, he accompanies Santa to pick up his in-laws, Bud and Sylvia Newman, posing as Santa's business associate, Sandy. After they get into an argument, he throws sleeping dust on them because he couldn't listen to them arguing, then he goes to sleep in a chair after Santa tries to get him to help him get Bud and Sylvia out to the car.\n\nLater, he is summoned to the [[North Pole]] by [[Charlie Calvin|Charlie]] to finish making all of the toys in time for Christmas.
