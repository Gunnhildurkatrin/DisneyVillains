While he is of a stern and intimidating visage, he takes his responsibilities toward his students seriously and genuinely cares for their success within and beyond Hexside.\n\n===Physical appearance===\nPrincipal Bump is an elderly man with light beige skin, long black hair and brown eyes without his palisman. He wears Frewin, his demon-like palisman as a hat that covers up half of his face to help him see due to his blindness, and traditional academic dress consisting of a long black gown with a gold yoke with black cape, gold trim at the sleeves and a blue stole.\n\n==Trivia==\n\n*\"Hieronymus\" comes from the painter Hieronymus Bosch whose art style set the aesthetic for ''The Owl House''.\n\n==Gallery==\n<gallery position=\"center\" spacing=\"small\" widths=\"150\" captionalign=\"center\">\nI Was A Teenage Abomination 51.png\nI Was A Teenage Abomination 52.png\nI Was a Teenage Abomination 29.png\nI Was A Teenage Abomination 57.png\nCovention (16).png\nSomething Ventured, Someone Framed (18).png\nSomething Ventured, Someone Framed (30).png\nSomething Ventured, Someone Framed (41).png\nSomething Ventured, Someone Framed (53).png\nSomething Ventured, Someone Framed (55).png\nThe First Day (4).png\nThe First Day (7).png\nThe First Day (20).png\nThe First Day (22).png\nThe First Day (23).png\nThe First Day (31).png\nThe First Day (37).png\nThe First Day (85).png\nThe First Day (93).png\nFile:Hunting Palismen (17).png\nFile:Hunting Palismen (18).png\nFile:Hunting Palismen (33).png\n</gallery>\n\n==External links==\n\n*[[w:c:theowlhouse:Principal Bump|Principal Bump]] on [[w:c:theowlhouse|The Owl House Wiki]]\n\n{{CharacterStub}}\n{{The Owl House}}\n[[Category:The Owl House characters]]\n[[Category:Males]]\n[[Category:Witches]]\n[[Category:Principals]]\n[[Category:Magic Users]]\n[[Category:Elderly characters]]\n[[Category:Adults]]\n[[Category:Disney characters]]\n[[Category:Animated characters]]\n[[Category:TV Animation characters]]\n[[Category:Neutral characters]]\n[[Category:Immortal]]\n[[Category:Disney Channel characters]]\n[[Category:Characters with disabilities]]"
