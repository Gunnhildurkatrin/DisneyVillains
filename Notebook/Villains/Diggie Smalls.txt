"[[File:DiggieClearer.png|thumb]]'''Diggie Smalls''' was a recurring character in the\u00a0[[Disney Channel]]\u00a0original series\u00a0[[Liv & Maddie|''Liv and&nbsp;Maddie'']]. He is captain of the boys' basketball team and close friends with\u00a0Maddie. He was Maddie's boyfriend in seasons 1, 2, 3 and 4. It is later revealed he is [[Artie Smalls|Artie's]] brother.\n\nDiggie is portrayed by actor/singer\u00a0[[Ryan McCartan]].\n\n==Personality==\nDiggie is not your typical jock. He\u2019s driven, and loves basketball, but he has a soft side that he isn\u2019t afraid to expose. He\u2019s funny and quirky, and he has a certain charming nature to him that we see when he gets nervous and playful around Maddie. He\u2019s loyal and a great friend, not only to Maddie but to her brother [[Joey Rooney|Joey]] and to all of Maddie\u2019s friends as well!<ref>[http://celebrityhautespot.wordpress.com/2013/08/30/exclusive-interview-with-ryan-mccartan-from-liv-maddie-ryanmccartan/  Character Description]</ref>\n\n==Biography==\n===Background===\nDiggie is a sophomore at [[Ridgewood High]] and is a basketball player at the school's basketball team.\n\n===History===\n''To be added''.\n\n==Appearance==\nDiggie usually wears casual clothes throughout the series. He is a brunette with brown eyes.\n\n==Strengths and Weaknesses==\n===Strengths===\n*Diggie is a talented basketball player.\n===Weaknesses===\n*Diggie is bad at homophones and sometimes confuses others when he mistakes them.\n\n==Relationships==\n===Friends===\n====[[Maddie Rooney]]====\n\n'''(Close friend; Girlfriend)'''\n\nDiggie and Maddie have a friendly relationship. They're good friends and appear to be very alike. Diggie has made an attempt to ask Maddie out, but her miserably failing to flirt/respond back. When\u00a0[[Liv Rooney|Liv]]\u00a0pretends to be Maddie and asks Diggie to the dance, he rejects, only because he knew it was Liv pretending to be Maddie. He claims that he\u00a0''knows Maddie'', that even though her sister has the same face, he knew that it wasn't her. He explained how Liv-pretending to be Maddie-didn't do the\u00a0''cute thing\u00a0''she did with her charm bracelet when she was nervous. He and Maddie got back together in Choose-A-Rooney and they are still together when the series ends.\u00a0\n\n====[[Liv Rooney]]====\n\n'''(Close Friends)'''\n\nWhen Liv first meets Diggie, she's a little confused of how he talked like a sport's announcer, but then he explained why.
