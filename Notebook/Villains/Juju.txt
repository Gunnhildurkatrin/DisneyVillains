"{{Infobox character\n|image = Juju (Princess and the Frog).jpg\n|films = ''[[The Princess and the Frog]]''\n|games = ''[[The Princess and the Frog (video game)|The Princess and the Frog]]<br/>[[Disney Magic Kingdoms]]'' (cameo)\n|animator = [[Andreas Deja]]<br/>Andreas Wessel-Therhorn\n|occupation = Mama Odie's assistant\n|home = [[Mama Odie's Tree]]\n|likes = Mama Odie, gumbo\n|dislikes=Being turned into different animals\n|status= Active\n|owner = [[Mama Odie]]}}\n'''Juju''' is a minor character in [[Walt Disney Animation Studios|Disney]]'s [[2009]] film\u00a0''[[The Princess and the Frog]]''. He is [[Mama Odie]]'s pet snake.\n\n==Background==\nVery loyal and highly affectionate, Juju is Mama Odie's closest companion, acting as her eyes throughout their hidden home. And although Juju acts as Mama Odie's personal little assistant, he is also treated as her very own child, much like the stereotype that the elderly treat their pets as their own children.\n\n==Appearances==\n===''[[The Princess and the Frog]]''===\n[[File:Princess-and-the-frog-disneyscreencaps com-7287.jpg|thumb|left|250px|Juju in ''The Princess and the Frog''.]]\nJuju first appears in the film right after [[Mama Odie]] rescues [[Tiana]] and her friends from a pack of [[Shadow Demons|shadow demons]]. Juju is called by Mama Odie and shows his affection for her. It is shown she treats him like a baby. Juju is also shown during the scene to assist Mama Odie as her cane and guide through the tree due to her blindness. During the song \"[[Dig a Little Deeper]]\", Juju is turned into a dog, a pig, a cow, and a goat\u00a0before returning to his original form. Juju appears at Tiana and [[Prince Naveen|Naveen]]'s marriage and celebrates alongside Mama Odie and the bayou animals.\n\n==Gallery==\n{{Gallerylink}}\n\n==Trivia==\n*It is unknown what kind of snake Juju is.
