He is later seen hearing the now shrunken kids whistling and tries to go and help them, but is chased away by Spike, the Thompsons' cat. He also accidentally messes up one of [[Wayne Szalinski|Wayne]]'s attempts to find them. He is later seen at the end, finding and bringing them inside along the way, overcoming his fear of Spike. He manages to save [[Nick Szalinski|Nick]] from being eaten from Wayne by biting Wayne's ankle. When they are returned to normal size, he celebrates Thanksgiving with them and is seen eating an enlarged bone.\n\n===''[[Honey, I Shrunk the Kids: The TV Show]]''===\nQuark regularly appeared on the show. Originally, he was portrayed as a border collie. However, in the episode \"[[Honey, it's Quarkzilla]]\", he mutates into a giant creature, after having eaten some mutant tomatoes created by Wayne. After being returned to normal, he becomes a completely different breed of dog.\n\n==[[Disney Parks, Experiences and Products|Disney Parks]]==\nQuark appears in ''[[Honey, I Shrunk the Audience]]'' with his biggest part being the finale where he is accidentally blown up by Wayne's [[Szalinzki's Shrinking Machine 2.0|new shrinking machine]] and sneezes on the guests.
