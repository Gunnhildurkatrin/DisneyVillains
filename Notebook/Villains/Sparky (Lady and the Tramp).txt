He is an [[wikipedia:Irish Wolfhound|Irish Wolfhound]] with light gray on his mustache and eyebrows.\n\n=== Personality===\nSparky used to know the [[Tramp]], or so he says. He doesn't do much else, other than tell [[Scamp]] tales about his father. They weren't true, of course, but Sparky seems really into story-telling.
