"{{Infobox character\n|image = KingAugustus.jpg\n|shows = ''[[The Little Mermaid (TV series)|The Little Mermaid]]''\n|voice = Keene Curtis\n|friends = [[Thor (The Little Mermaid)|Thor]], [[King Triton]]\n|alignment = Good}}\n\n'''King Augustus''' is a minor character who appeared in the animated television series ''[[The Little Mermaid (TV series)|The Little Mermaid]]''.\n\n== Role in the series ==\nKing Augustus is ruler of Olympia, a neighboring kingdom of [[Atlantica]]. Olympia is known for its strong warriors, though the kingdom had actually never seen a full war. When King Augustus's son, [[Thor (The Little Mermaid)|Prince Thor]], returned to the palace after having been mocked by [[Ariel]] and her friends, King Augustus declared war on Atlantica. It wasn't until [[Urchin]] was injured that Augustus ended the battle, willing to discuss peace with [[King Triton]].\n\n{{The Little Mermaid}}\n{{DEFAULTSORT:Augustus, King}}\n[[Category:The Little Mermaid characters]]\n[[Category:Disney characters]]\n[[Category:Males]]\n[[Category:Kings]]\n[[Category:Parents]]\n[[Category:Hybrids]]\n[[Category:Adults]]\n[[Category:Animated characters]]\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:TV Animation characters]]\n[[Category:Elderly characters]]\n[[Category:Mermen]]\n[[Category:Anti-heroes]]\n[[Category:The Disney Afternoon characters]]"
