"{{Infobox Character\n|image = Fisherman Bear.png\n|films = ''[[Bedknobs and Broomsticks]]''\n|voice = [[Dallas McKennon|Dal McKennon]]\n|home = [[The Isle of Naboombu]]\n}}\n'''Fisherman Bear''' is a minor character in [[The Walt Disney Company|Disney]]'s [[1971]] musical hybrid film ''[[Bedknobs and Broomsticks]]''.\n\n==Role in the film==\nHe is one of the animals at [[the Isle of Naboombu]]. While he is fishing, he ends up catching the bed where the protagonists travel. Because humans cannot be on the island, he tries to throw them back into the sea. But [[Paul Rawlins|Paul]] convinces him to guide them to [[King Leonidas|the King]].\n\n{{Bedknobs and Broomsticks}}\n\n[[Category:Disney characters]]\n[[Category:Males]]\n[[Category:Bedknobs and Broomsticks characters]]\n[[Category:Bears]]\n[[Category:Neutral characters]]\n[[Category:Animated characters in live-action films]]\n[[Category:Anthropomorphic characters]]\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:Animated characters]]\n[[Category:Adults]]"
