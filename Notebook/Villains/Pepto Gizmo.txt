He helps the chief to lead the tribe. He appears in the ''[[Chip 'n Dale Rescue Rangers|Rescue Rangers]]'' episode \"[[Kiwi's Big Adventure]]\". [[Jim Cummings]] voiced him.\n\nThroughout the episode, Pepto is trying to return the kiwis to the sky using magic and the [[Ranger Plane]]. With the other members of the tribe, he pursues the Rescue Rangers in order to take away the Ranger Plane, which the Kiwi tribe consider a flying deity.
