"{{Infobox character\n|image = Isadore Eyesore.png\n|name = Isadore Eyesore\n|shows = [[Goof Troop]]\n|voice = [[Frank Welker]] \n|occupation = Optometrist\n|powers = Optometrical skills }}\n'''Isadore Eyesore''' is an eye doctor who aids [[Goofy|Goofy's]] historical counterpart in getting glasses in the Goof History episode \"[[Gunfight at the Okie-Doke Corral]]\" from [[Disney Television Animation|Disney's]] animated series, [[Goof Troop]].\n\n==Background==\nHe tried to help [[Goofy|Mopalong Goofy]] when he noticed he was having problems seeing. However, Mopalong declined at first, but eventually accepted his offer.\n\n===Personality===\nIsadore is a kind person who cares about others' well-being. He is also patient, not pushing [[Goofy|Mopalong]] into getting glasses when he intially refused.\n\n===Physical appearance===\nIsadore is a short, overweight anthropomorphic dog with brown hair. He wears a green dress coat, a white collared shirt, a light brown vest, green dress pants, dark brown shoes, a black bowler hat with a green band, spectacles, and a black tie.\n\n===Role in the series===\nHe only appears in the episode, \"[[Gunfight at the Okie-Doke Corral]]\". He attempts to give [[Goofy|Mopalong Goofy]] glasses when he notices he's having eye problems, but Mopalong stubbornly refuses until later in the episode, when he decides to give glasses a try.\n\n==Trivia==\n*Isadore's last name \"Eyesore\" is a word used to describe an ugly building, but is probably used to reference \"sore eyes\", since he is an optometrist.\n\n{{Goof Troop}}\n[[Category:Heroes]]\n[[Category:Goof Troop characters]]\n[[Category:TV Animation characters]]\n[[Category:Disney characters]]\n[[Category:Animated characters]]\n[[Category:Males]]\n[[Category:Adults]]\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:Dogs]]\n[[Category:The Disney Afternoon characters]]\n[[Category:Mickey Mouse universe characters]]\n[[Category:Doctors]] \n[[Category:Businesspeople]]\n[[Category:Historical characters]]\n[[Category:American characters]]"
