Her name comes from how the spots around her neck resemble a jeweled necklace. She and her siblings were kidnapped by [[Jasper and Horace]] for [[Cruella De Vil]] and\u00a0brought to [[Hell Hall|De Vil Manor]] to be made into a fur coat. With the help of her family, she escapes Cruella along with her siblings.
