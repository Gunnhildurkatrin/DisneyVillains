"{{Infobox character\n|image = ARTF 22394R.0.jpg\n|name = Artemis Fowl II\n|films = ''[[Artemis Fowl]]''\n|actor = Ferdia Shaw\n|inspiration = Artemis Fowl II from the book series\n|alignment = Good\n|home = Fowl Manor, [[Ireland]]\n|parents = [[Artemis Fowl I]] (father)<br>Angeline Fowl \u2020 (mother)\n|friends = [[Holly Short]], [[Domovoi Butler]], Juliet Butler, [[Mulch Diggums]]\n|enemies = Holly Short (formerly), [[Opal Koboi]]\n}}'''Artemis Fowl II''' is the main protagonist of the [[2020]] film ''[[Artemis Fowl]]'' who lives in a giant mansion known as Fowl Manor. He is the son of [[Artemis Fowl I]], who regularly traveled for work and sent him postcards for his fairy collection. After his father is kidnapped, Artemis hatches a daring plan to rescue his father, but discovers that his father was actually part of something much bigger than he ever imagined.\n\n==Appearance==\nIn the film, Artemis enjoyed studying lessons of fairy folk, but believed that they were just legends. One day, his father disappeared while traveling on the ''Fowl Star ''and he began seeing news reports claiming that his father was a thief wanted for robberies around the world. Artemis was shocked and determined to find his father.. He then discovered a secret message from his father which part of him had always believed.
