When Kevin seems close to winning the tug-of-war, the dogs mount up to have Drooler win, with Drooler himself shaking his slobber onto Kevin. When Kevin introduces the dogs to [[Chloe Simon|Chloe]], he claims that whilst they been able to help most of the dogs who arrive at Second Chance, Drooler hasn't really changed much. He is later seen with ''The Gang'' as they try to rescue the puppies.
