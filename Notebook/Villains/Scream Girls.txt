"{{Group Infobox\n|image = Scream Girls Vampirina.png\n|shows = ''[[Vampirina]]''\n|inspiration =Various Girl Bands\n|alias = \n|members = FrankenStacy (lead vocalist/guitarist)<br>Creepy Caroline (rhythm guitarist)<br>Ghastly Gayle (drummer)\n|occupation = Monster Girl Band\n|home = Transylvania, [[Romania]]}}\nThe\u00a0'''Scream Girls''' are supporting characters and a monster girl rock band who appear in the [[Disney Junior]] animated series, ''[[Vampirina]]''. They are [[Vampirina Hauntley|Vee's]] favorite band from Transylvania. The band members are FrankenStacy, (lead vocalist and guitarist), Creepy Caroline, (rhythm guitarist), and Ghastly Gayle (drummer).\n\n==Members==\n*'''FrankenStacy''' (Kailey Snider) - The lead singer and guitarist. Much like Frankenstein, her body is held together with stitches and has bolts on both sides of her neck. She has long black hair with a lightning bolt streak tied in a ponytail.\n**'''Nerve:\u00a0''' \"When I get nervous, my arms fall off. Awkward.\"\n*'''Creepy Caroline''' ([[Raini Rodriguez]]) - The rhythm guitarist, who wears a pumpkin themed dress. She is possibly a gorgon, mainly because she has snakes for hair, but does not appear to have the ability to turn people to stone by looking into her eyes.\n**'''Nerve:\u00a0''' \"When I get nervous, my hair gets all slithery.\"\n*'''Ghastly Gayle''' (Gabriela Milo) - The drummer who plays the drums with bones.
