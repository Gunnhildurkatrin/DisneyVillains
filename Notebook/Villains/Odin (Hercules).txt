He appears in the ''[[Hercules: The Animated Series]]'' episode \"[[Hercules and the Twilight of the Gods]]\".\n==Appearance==\nOdin is a blue muscular giant with long white hair and beard. He wears a black eyepatch over his right eye and purple robe over his right shoulder. He also wears a two-horned helmet on his head.\n==Powers and Abilities==\nAs a god, Odin possesses the natural powers and abilities of a Norse\u00a0deity such as immortality, omnipresence, vast strength, and the potential to do magic such as the ability to shape-shift, to teleport and to manifest objects.
