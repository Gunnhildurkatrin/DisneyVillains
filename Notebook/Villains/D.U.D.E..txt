"{{NeedImage}}'''D.U.D.E.''' is a rapping computer. He appears in the Mickey's Starland (predecessor to [[Mickey's Toontown Fair]], which is now dysfunction.) in a show called [[Mickey's Starland Show]], in which is a surprise birthday for [[Mickey Mouse|Mickey]]. It is hosted by him and his \"deejay prot\u00e9g\u00e9\", C.J. In order to turn him on, the audience must yell \"Hey D.U.D.E., what's happening?\"\n\nFor every variant of Mickey's Starland Show, his voice and a few of his lines change.\n==Quotes==\nMy name is D.U.D.E., I'm number one\n\nWe're gonna have all kinds of fun\n\nI'm the one who runs the show\n\nI'm the master-rapper computer control.\n\nI brought you friends from near and far;\n\nYou'll meet 'em now, each one's a star.\n\nEverybody clap, clap, clap!
