"\n[[File:Ephialtes.png|thumb]]\n'''King Ephialtes''' is a character in the TV series ''[[Hercules: The Animated Series|Hercules]]''. He is a tree giant who is the King of the Dryads and the father of [[Syrinx]]. He disapproves from the relationship of [[Syrinx]] with the satyr [[Philoctetes|Phil]]. He appeared in the episode ''[[Hercules and the Muse of Dance]]''.
