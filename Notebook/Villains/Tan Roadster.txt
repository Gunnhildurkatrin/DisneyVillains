"{{Infobox character\n|image = Tan Roadster.png\n|name = Tan Roadster\n|shows = [[Goof Troop]]\n|voice = Joe Piscopo \n|occupation = Car dealer\n|alignment = Neutral, later good \n|home = [[Spoonerville]]\n|enemies = [[Pete]] (formerly)\n|likes = Teasing Pete<br>\n[[Peg]] \n|powers = Physically fit }}\n'''Tan Roadster''' is the athletic car-selling rival of [[Pete]] from [[Disney Television Animation|Disney's]] 1992-1993 animated series, ''[[Goof Troop]].''\n==Background==\nHe challenges [[Pete]], whom he has known for years, to a skiing race. He makes jokes about Pete being overweight. He is in love with Pete's wife, [[Peg]], and tries to get her to marry him. Tan and Pete fight over Peg during a skiing competition.\n\n===Personality===\nTan likes to pick on [[Pete]] about his weight. He also appears to enjoy athletics, as he is a skilled skier and is very healthy. He is interested in strong-willed women, which is why he is attracted to [[Peg]], even going as far as to compete against her own husband for her love, as seen in the episode \"[[Gymnauseum]]\".\n\n===Physical appearance===\nTan is a bulky anthropomorphic dog with orange fur. He wears a yellow jacket over an orange snowsuit with dark orange trim, and orange bicep straps. He also wears dark orange boots.\n\n===Role in the series===\nTan's only appearance is in the episode \"[[Gymnauseum]]\". He offers to go up against [[Pete]] in a skiing race in exchange for [[Peg]]. Peg convinces Pete to join because he needs the exercise. \n\n==Trivia==\n*Tan's name and interest, skiing, may be based off of the expression \"skiers tan,\" used to describe a person's face when it is covered in ski goggle tan lines after prolonged skiing.\n\n{{Goof Troop}}\n[[Category:Goof Troop characters]]\n[[Category:Males]]\n[[Category:Adults]]\n[[Category:Businesspeople]]\n[[Category:Disney characters]]\n[[Category:The Disney Afternoon characters]]\n[[Category:Dogs]]\n[[Category:Neutral characters]]\n[[Category:Heroes]]\n[[Category:Animated characters]]\n[[Category:American characters]]\n[[Category:TV Animation characters]]"
