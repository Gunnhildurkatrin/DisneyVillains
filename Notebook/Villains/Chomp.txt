As [[Kevin Shepherd|Kevin]]\u00a0is introducing\u00a0the Dogs of Second Chance to [[Chloe Simon|Chloe]], he claims Chomp\u00a0chewed his way through three postmen before Kevin\u00a0found him and\u00a0thanks to Second Chance, wouldn't hurt a flea.\u00a0He joins ''The Gang ''as they help rescue the puppies from [[Cruella De Vil]]. When they arrive in Paris, Chomp is able to communicate with the Paris dogs, implying that he is able to speak French, to Kevin's surprise.
