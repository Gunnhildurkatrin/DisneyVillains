"\n[[File:Alan Platt.jpg|thumb|250px]]'''Alan Platt''' is a minor character from the television show ''[[Kim Possible]]''. He was briefly seen in the episode \"[[Downhill]]\".\n\n==History==\nAlan is a student at [[Middleton High School]]. He is first seen in line to get on the school bus to the Mount Middleton Ski Trip. He is sad and pitied because his parents are chaperones on the trip.\n\n[[Kim Possible (character)|Kim]]'s [[Dr. Ann Possible|mom]] then shows up, to everyone's surprise, telling them that the Platts came down with flu at the last minute. Kim's parents will be the new chaperones for the trip.\n\nAlan is then seen smiling and high-fiving another student after he finds out that his parents are sick.\n\n==Gallery==\n<gallery spacing=\"small\">\nAlan Platt 2.jpg\nAlan-Platt.png\n</gallery>\n\n{{CharacterStub}}\n{{Kim Possible}}\n\n{{DEFAULTSORT:Platt, Alan}}\n[[Category:Kim Possible characters]]\n[[Category:Disney characters]]\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:Males]]\n[[Category:Teenagers]]\n[[Category:Neutral characters]]\n[[Category:Italian characters]]\n[[Category:Animated characters]]\n[[Category:TV Animation characters]]\n[[Category:Students]]\n[[Category:Disney Channel characters]]"
