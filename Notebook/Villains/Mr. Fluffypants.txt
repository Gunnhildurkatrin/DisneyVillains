"[[File:Heinz_meets_Fluffypants.jpg|thumb|250px|Mr. Fluffypants held by Doofenshmirtz]]\n'''Mr. Fluffypants''' is a character in the [[Disney Channel]] animated series ''[[Phineas and Ferb]]''. He appeared in the episode ''[[Spa Day]].''\u00a0He was found by [[Heinz Doofenshmirtz|Doofenshmirtz]] while checking the mail and he took him in, naming him Mr. Fluffypants. Because he's so fluffy, he looks like he's wearing pants and he reminds him of his Uncle Fluffy Pants. While Mr. Fluffypants is messing with Doofernshmirtz's inators, [[Perry the Platypus]] believes that Doofenshmirtz is behind the disturbances, but Perry discovers the truth when Mr. Fluffypants messes with the inator that made Phineas and Ferb's spa disappear. In the end, Mr. Fluffypants had an original owner,\u00a0who reveals the feline's real name is Mr. Cat.
