"[[File:Musketeer's_Flag.jpg|thumb|250px]]'''Musketeers''' are a group of soldiers featured in the [[1993]] film ''[[The Three Musketeers]]\u00a0''and in the\u00a0[[2004]]\u00a0film\u00a0''[[Mickey, Donald, Goofy: The Three Musketeers]].''\n\n==History==\nThey were created in 1622 when King Louis XIII furnished a company of light cavalry with muskets. The Musketeers fought in battle both on foot (infantry) and on horseback (cavalry).
