"\n[[File:Tessa_and_Vanessa.jpg|thumb|250px]]'''Tessa and Vanessa James'''<ref>http://www.imdb.com/character/ch0398790/</ref> (voiced by [[Cree Summer]])<ref>http://www.behindthevoiceactors.com/tv-shows/Disneys-Pepper-Ann/Tessa-James/</ref><ref>http://www.behindthevoiceactors.com/tv-shows/Disneys-Pepper-Ann/Vanessa-James/</ref><ref>http://www.imdb.com/name/nm0838588/</ref> are recurring characters of [[Pepper Ann|''Pepper Ann'']].\n==History==\nThey are a pair of African-American twin girls who are students at [[Hazelnut Middle School]] along with the main characters [[Pepper Ann Pearson|Pepper Ann]], [[Nicky Little]] and [[Milo Kamalani]]. They are known as the gossipy twins as they tend to gossip a lot.\n\nIn \"[[The Finale]]\", it is established that in the series' version of [[2013]], they get married to another pair of twins and together have twin children each, implying that being twins is a strong genetic trait.\n\n== Physical Appearance ==\nTessa has her hair in cornrows, She wears gold jewelry, a orange basketball jersey, blue jeans and white sneakers.\n\nVanessa has long, kinky blonde hair.
