"[[File:Ruby.jpg|thumb|Lauren Frost as Ruby in ''[[The Even Stevens Movie]]''.]]\n'''Ruby Mendel''' is a supporting character from the ''[[Even Stevens]]'' television show and subsequent feature film, and is played by Lauren Frost. Ruby is [[Ren Stevens]]' best friend. She loves to gossip, usually about school crushes and who's dating who within her school.\n\nWhile under the spell of a lucky penny (Episode 2x01 - \"[[StarStruck]]\"), Ruby developed a crush on [[Louis Stevens]], and later in the episode, on the boy band BBMak.
