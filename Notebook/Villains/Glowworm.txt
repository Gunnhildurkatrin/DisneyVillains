She is like a grandmother figure to James. She hangs in a lantern and lights up the inside of the peach with her tail and has bad hearing. She accompanies the others on their journey to [[New York City]]. She and the others are separated from James during an attack from the [[Rhino (James and the Giant Peach)|Rhino]], but they later reunite with James, who has transformed back into his normal form, and she aids in the capture of [[Aunt Spiker]] and [[Aunt Sponge]].
