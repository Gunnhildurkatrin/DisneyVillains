"{{Infobox character\n|image = Ryker.png\n|voice = [[Kevin Michael Richardson]]\n|films = ''[[Planes]]<br>[[Planes: Fire & Rescue]]''\n|appearance = Yellow airport crash tender with blue lines on his sides, yellow rims blue eyes, a black grille and a white shape with the Transportation Management Safety Team logo\n|occupation = Transportation Management Safety Team Veteran<br>Airport Crash Tender\n|model = Oshkosh Striker fire truck\n|friends = [[Mayday]], [[Dusty Crophopper]], [[Skipper Riley]], [[Chug]], [[Dottie (Planes)|Dottie]], [[Sparky (Planes)|Sparky]]\n|enemies = [[Ripslinger]], [[Ned and Zed]]\n|likes = Keeping things intact, making sure airports are safe for operation\n|affiliations = Transportation Management Safety Team\n|alignment = Neutral/Good\n|personality = Fast-talking, strict}}'''Ryker'''\u00a0is a character in the [[2013]] [[Disneytoon Studios]] animated feature film, ''[[Planes]]'' and its [[2014]] [[Planes: Fire & Rescue|sequel]].\n\n\n\n\n==Background==\n===Official Description===\nRyker, a 17-year veteran of the Transportation Management Safety Team (TMST), not only lives by the book when it comes to airport accident investigations, he wrote the book. Nothing slips by this air safety investigator, so don\u2019t even try.<ref name=\"DIMeetChars\">[http://blogs.disney.com/insider/2014/03/10/meet-the-characters-from-planes-fire-rescue/ Meet the Characters from Planes: Fire & Rescue]</ref>\n\n==Appearances==\n===''[[Planes]]''===\nAt the end of ''Planes'', he is seen in the crowd celebrating [[Dusty Crophopper]]'s victory.\n\n===''[[Planes: Fire & Rescue]]''===\nIn ''Planes: Fire & Rescue'', after a fire was put out at [[Chug]] and [[Dottie (Planes)|Dottie]]'s Fill n' Fly when [[Dusty Crophopper|Dusty]] accidentally clipped the tower while testing his limits with his gearbox damaged, Ryker arrives in [[Propwash Junction]] the next day along with his pitty Kurtz, accusing [[Mayday]] of the accident until Dusty explains what he did. Ryker then figures out that the town has no plans for emergencies, and decides to close the airport down until Mayday hires a second firefighter.\n\nAt the end, he re-opens the airport after Dusty comes back to Propwash Junction with the news of being certified by [[Blade Ranger]] as a firefighter.\n\n==Trivia==\n*He is fashioned after a Striker airport rescue and fire fighting truck.\n*His name is taken from his model, an Oshkosh Striker.\n\n==Gallery==\n<gallery position=\"center\" widths=\"180\" captionalign=\"center\">\nPlanes-fire-and-rescue-RGB-ryker.jpg\nTumblr_n4112o61EK1re0hgho6_1280.jpg\nPlanes-Fire-&-Rescue-34.png\n</gallery>\n\n==References==\n{{Reflist}}\n{{Planes}}\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:Disney characters]]\n[[Category:Planes characters]]\n[[Category:Animated characters]]\n[[Category:Males]]\n[[Category:Vehicles]]\n[[Category:Neutral characters]]\n[[Category:Anthropomorphic characters]]\n[[Category:Objects]]"
