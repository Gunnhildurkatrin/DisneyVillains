"'''{{Infobox_character\n|image = The_General.jpg\n|shorts = ''[[The Vanishing Private]]''\n|voice = [[John McLeish]]}}\n'''The General''' is a character from the short film, ''[[The Vanishing Private]]''.\n\n==Role in the film==\nHe drives up to see [[Pete]]'s antics, including jumping around a tree while singing \"Here We Go Round The Mulberry Bush\" and throwing flowers.\u00a0This\u00a0convinces the General that he is acting crazy, especially after Pete asks the General \"Did you see a little guy that you can't see?\", and the invisible [[Donald Duck|Donald]] puts a cactus down Pete's pants, making Pete jump around like a madman.\u00a0\n\nThe General wonders what is up with Pete.\u00a0He sees that Pete is at Wit's end scaring The General out of his wits when Pete runs towards him. The frightened General hides behind a nearby power pole. The General tries to reassure Pete and stop his continuous blowing up, but when Donald pokes Pete in the rear with the General's sword, the startled sergeant jumps into the air and onto the General releasing all of the grenades that land on Pete and the General, injuring them in the explosion.
