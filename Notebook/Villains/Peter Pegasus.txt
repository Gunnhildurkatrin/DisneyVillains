Some of these proposed pieces were \"Invitation to the Dance\" and \"Flight of the Bumblebee\".<ref>[http://cartoonresearch.com/index.php/animation-anecdotes-216/ \"Animation Anecdotes #216\". ''Cartoon Research'']</ref>\n\nAlthough he was referred to as Peter Pegasus during the film's production,<ref>{{Cite web|website=D23|url=https://d23.com/15-fascinating-facts-about-fantasia/|title=Fifteen Fascinating Facts About ''Fantasia''|author=Fanning, Jim|publisher=Disney}}</ref> the character was originally not heavily promoted with this name, and is occasionally referred to as either \"Baby Pegasus\" or \"Pegasus\" in certain materials and merchandise.<ref>[https://dlove.jp/mezzomiki/wp-content/uploads/2015/12/DSC_6831.jpg Disney Tsum Tsum Fantasia Collection]</ref><ref>[https://www.entertainmentearth.com/product/disney-the-world-of-miss-mindy-fantasia-pegasus-statue/en6001167 \"Disney The World of Miss Mindy Fantasia Pegasus Statue\".
