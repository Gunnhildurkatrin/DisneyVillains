''\n\n==History==\nHe is Jake's cousin on his mother's side, and is also a [http://adjl.wikia.com/wiki/Dragon-in-training dragon-in-training]. He appeared in one episode in Season 2 \"[[Feeding Frenzy]].\" He never made a reappearance afterwards.\n\n==Dragon Anatomy==\nHe has brown stripes over the back of his body and his tail. His mane is a darker green than his scales and he has stiff black hair. His underbelly is a slightly lighter green compared to her scales. His wings are similar to most dragons, having an arm-and-fingers bone structure, except his wings are angular rather than curved. The wing's skeleton is also less visible compared to [[Jake Long|Jake]]'s wings. The outside of his wings are a darker green than his scales. The inside of his wings are the same color as the outside of his wings, but the tip of the inside of his wings match his scales.\n\n==Powers and Abilities==\nGregory possesses all of the standard powers of a dragon, however, as he has only gained his dragon form recently, he is very weak in all of its uses.\n\n==Trivia==\n*Despite being younger than his cousin Jake, he still keeps treating him like a baby.\n\n==Gallery==\n<gallery spacing=\"small\" widths=\"200\" position=\"center\">\nGreggy.jpg\nGregoryDragon.png|Gregory's Dragon Form\nGregory_and_Jake.png|Gregory with his cousin.\nScreenshot-1G.png|Gregory trying to fly to compete with Jake.\nScreenshot-2G.png|Gregory trying to fly.\nFeeding Frenzy (2).jpg\nScreenshot-4G.png|Gregory suffocating in a water bubble.
