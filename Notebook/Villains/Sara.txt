She is the cousin of [[Jenny Brown|Jenny]] who comes to Candace's party (or as Candace calls it, her \"intimate get-together\"). She meets Candace for the first time, but she knows or has heard of her brothers. Sara's residence is unknown as Jenny said that she came to pass the weekend at Jenny's house (\"[[Candace Gets Busted]]\").\n==Physical Appearance==\nSara is a teenage girl. She has blonde hair and wears a pink sleeveless top and purple pants. Although she is Jenny's cousin, they look completely different.   \n\n==Trivia==\n*She, [[Django Brown]], and [[Beppo Brown]] are the only known members of Jenny's family and she is her only known cousin.\n*She looks similar to [[Nicolette]].\n*Sara tends to get overexcited.
