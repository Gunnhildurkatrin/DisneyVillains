"[[File:Frederick.jpg|thumb]]'''Frederick Buttersleeves'''<ref>http://www.jeffgoode.com/tv/americandragon/characters/frederick.htm</ref> is a minor character in the\u00a0animated television series ''[[American Dragon: Jake Long]]''.\n\n==History==\nFred is a student in [[Jake Long|Jake]]'s class at [[Millard Fillmore Middle School]], and is typically an outcast from the \"cool\" groups.
