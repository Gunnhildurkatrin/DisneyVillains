"[[File:Shark_people.jpg|thumb|207px]]'''Shark People'''<ref>http://www.jeffgoode.com/tv/americandragon/critters/sharkpeople.htm</ref> are fictional creatures featured in the television show ''[[American Dragon: Jake Long]]''.\n==History==\nThey are magical creatures which make their home in the oceans, but are roughly bipedal and able to breathe air for a period, as well as walk on solid ground. They are a cross between a shark and a human. \u00a0 \u00a0 \u00a0 \u00a0 \u00a0 \u00a0 \u00a0 \u00a0\u00a0\n\n[[Tibur\u00f3n]] was the leader of the Shark People to rule the seas by using Neptune's Trident while riding on a Sea Serpent. Tibur\u00f3n's army of Shark People looked like makos, great whites, hammerheads, sand tigers, Megalodons, and other shark species. After the [[Sea Serpent]] found his weakness, which was steam, Tibur\u00f3n fell into the ocean with his Sea Serpent, but they might have survived.\n\nA Shark Woman was seen when she was dating [[Jake Long|Jake]]'s [[Luong Lao Shi|gramps]] and she bites the sofa with a huge chomp and still smiles while chewing<ref name=\"fishy\">[[Something Fishy This Way Comes]]</ref>.
