Nina is a fun-loving 15-year-old neighbor of the Imagination Movers and likes helping them solve their problems. She has the opposite personality from her uncle, Knit Knots.\n==Physical appearance==\nNina is slender with long black hair and fair skin. She also wears different pink and red outfits with red shoes.
