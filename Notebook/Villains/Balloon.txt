":''Not to be confused with [[Balloony]], a recurring \"character\" in [[Phineas and Ferb]].''\n{{Infobox character\n|image = Balloon.jpg\n|films = ''[[Winnie the Pooh (film)|Winnie the Pooh]]''\n|personality = Quiet, helpful\n|appearance = A big, red balloon on a string\n|occupation = [[Christopher Robin]]'s toy\n|goal = To find [[Christopher Robin]] and win the honey\n|home = [[Hundred Acre Wood]]<br>[[Christopher Robin]]'s room\n|family = Other balloons in the [[Winnie the Pooh (franchise)|''Winnie the Pooh'' franchise]] (possibly)\n|friends = [[Winnie the Pooh]], [[Piglet]], [[Eeyore]], [[Kanga]], [[Roo]], [[Rabbit]], [[Tigger]], [[Christopher Robin]]\n|enemies = [[The Backson]]\n|likes = Honey\n|dislikes = [[Christopher Robin]] lost\n|powers = Flying, floating, gliding\n|fate = Finds [[Christopher Robin]] and wins some honey}}\nThe '''Balloon''', also known as '''B'loon''', is a \"character\" in the ''[[Winnie the Pooh (franchise)|Winnie the Pooh]]'' franchise. He is an inanimate object, but everyone treats him like a real creature.\n\n==Appearances==\n===''[[Winnie the Pooh (film)|Winnie the Pooh]]''===\nThe Balloon is first shown in [[Christopher Robin]]'s [[Christopher Robin's Room|room]] tied to a note. He is later shown in the [[Zooey Deschanel]] version of ''Winnie the Pooh'' tied to a tricycle. The Balloon is the one that finds Christopher Robin, and it wins a pot of honey as a reward.\n==Trivia==\n*Balloon is male because [[Rabbit]] says, \"He seems to be wearing red today.\"\n*Balloon may have appeared in ''[[The Many Adventures of Winnie the Pooh]]'' as the balloon [[Winnie the Pooh|Pooh]] used to capture honey from the [[bees]], but this is unlikely since this balloon is blue.\n*Balloon was possibly seen in ''[[The New Adventures of Winnie the Pooh]]'' as the balloon Christopher Robin let Pooh borrow in \"[[Balloonatics]]\".\n*Balloon was likely inspired by the [[1956]] French film\u00a0''{{WikipediaLink|The Red Balloon}}'', about a boy with a sentient red balloon for a companion.\n\n{{Winnie the Pooh}}\n[[Category:Objects]]\n[[Category:Winnie the Pooh characters]]\n[[Category:Characters who fly]]\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:Disney characters]]\n[[Category:Characters in the Disney animated features canon]]\n[[Category:Silent characters]]\n[[Category:Animated characters]]"
