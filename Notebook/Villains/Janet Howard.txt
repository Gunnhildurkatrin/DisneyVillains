"{{Infobox character\n|image = Flight-of-the-navigator-disneyscreencaps_com-575.jpg\n|name = Janet Howard\n|films = ''[[Flight of the Navigator]]''\n|actress = Iris Acker\n|alignment = Good\n|appearance =\n|home = Fort Lauderdale, [[Florida]]\n|partner = [[Larry Howard]] (husband)\n|quote = \"Think you have the wrong house, dear.\"\n}}'''Janet Howard''' is [[Larry Howard|Larry]]'s wife in ''[[Flight of the Navigator]]''.\n\n==Role in the film==\nOn the night of [[July 4]], [[1986]], Janet answers a knock on her front door and finds a [[David Freeman|12-year-old boy]] she has never seen before on the doorstep.\n\nThe boy asks where [[Helen Freeman|his mom]] was, but Janet tells him he must have the wrong house and asks if he's lost. He suddenly rushes past her and runs through the house, shouting desperately for his parents, and a concerned Janet follows him. He then runs upstairs and bursts into the study where Larry is listening to music, before reemerging, followed by Larry, and bursting into tears.
