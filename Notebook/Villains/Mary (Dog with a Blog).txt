"{{DISPLAYTITLE:Mary (''Dog with a Blog'')}}\n[[File:MaryDWaB.jpg|thumb|250px]]\n'''Mary''' is a minor character in ''[[Dog with a Blog]]''. She's portrayed by LeAire George.\n\n==Character History==\nMary was one of Bennett's patients. Mary was brought in for her phobia of dogs. Bennett helped her overcome her fear and in 2 weeks she becomes very fond of Stan. However, she became obsessive, she once took him away when one was looking, but brought him back.\n\nStan tried his best efforts to make be afraid of dogs again but it didn't work, but she is afraid of clowns.\n\n==Trivia==\n*She's the third known patient of [[Bennett James]].
