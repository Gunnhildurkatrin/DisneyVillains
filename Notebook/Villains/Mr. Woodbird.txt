He enjoys inventing things that can make things easier for the fliers or K.C. in the nursery. He loves making loveys for the babies the most.\n\nHe enjoys eating sandwiches while he works. He has a habit of adding long numbers to his inventions even if they're the first one of the kind. He sometimes gives someone his inventions even if he hasn't tested them out himself.\u00a0 He also loves babies a lot like everyone at T.O.T.S.
