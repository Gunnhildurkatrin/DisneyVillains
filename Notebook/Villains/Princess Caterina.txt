She also admits to not being into sports.\n\n==Role in the Series==\nIn her debut appearance, Caterina arrives in the Kingdom of Avalor with her family. Cat becomes fast friends with Elena's sister Princess Isabel, who she goes on a tour with. In town, they come across the\u00a0''Voladores''. Isabel confides in her how she's always wanted to see how the\u00a0''Voladores'' spinning tower works. The next day, a bored Cat sneaks away with an equally bored Isabel to the\u00a0''Voladores'' tower. They ride to the top where they get stuck.
