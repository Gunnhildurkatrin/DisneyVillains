"{{Infobox character\n|image = Freedo.png\n|shows = ''[[Sofia the First]]'' \n|voice = [[Mick Wingert]]\n|fullname = \n|alias = \n|personality = Hyperactive, playful\n|appearance = \n|occupation = James' pet baboon\n|home = [[Enchancia]]\n|friends = [[Prince James]], [[Princess Amber]], [[Sofia the First (character)|Sofia]], [[Princess Vivian]], [[Clover]], [[Crackle]], [[Praline]]\n|enemies = \n|likes = Games, balls, having fun\n|dislikes = Not having fun\n|alignment = Good}}'''Freedo''' is [[Prince James]]' pet baboon who appears in the [[Disney Junior]] series ''[[Sofia the First]]''.\n\n==Background==\nFreedo made his debut appearance in the episode \"[[Blue Ribbon Bunny]]\" where James enters him in a pet contest.\n\nIn the episode \"[[Scrambled Pets]]\" he is shown to be playful and enjoys a game of fetch. However, sometimes his playful running around can cause problems and bother the other pets, especially [[Praline]]. When James brought him to school he got mixed up in a sorcery class accident turning him into a peacock baboon. enjoyed being mixed up and began to run around the school and when [[Princess Amber|Amber]] made him bigger he rampaged through the school gym playing with the balls. [[Sofia the First (character)|Sofia]], James, [[Princess Vivian|Vivian]], and Amber were able to catch him and change him back.\n\nHe is seen at [[Clover]]'s party in \"[[Clover Time]],\" showing off his trick of cracking nuts against his head.\n\n==Gallery==\n<gallery captionalign=\"center\" widths=\"180\">\nScrambled-Pets-40.png\nScrambled-Pets-44.png\nScrambled-Pets-36.png|Freedo big\nScrambled-Pets-35.png\nScrambled-Pets-31.png\nScrambled-Pets-6.png\nBlue Ribbon Bunny crowd.jpg\nZandar&James02.png\n</gallery>\n\n{{Sofia the First}}\n[[pt-br:Freedo]]\n[[Category:Sofia the First characters]]\n[[Category:Pets]]\n[[Category:Males]]\n[[Category:Disney Junior characters]]\n[[Category:Animated characters]]\n[[Category:Monkeys]]\n[[Category:Peacocks]]\n[[Category:Disney characters]]\n[[Category:TV Animation characters]]\n[[Category:Heroes]]\n[[Category:Characters Under Spells]]\n[[Category:Giants]]"
