He is a renowned teen pop star that most girls woo over. He makes a brief appearance on TV<ref name=\"tooth\">[[The Legend of Dragon Tooth]]</ref>, and is officially seen in person later on<ref name=\"shape\">[[Shapeshifter]]</ref>. He never makes another appearance throughout the series.\n==Physical Appearance==\nJaren is a young boy with short blond hair that he styles with hair gel. He wears a blue A-shirt with a red arrow pointing upwards over a regular orange shirt, a gray pair of khaki pants, and blue shoes. He is seen once in his underwear, which is neon green with purple polka dots, he also wears knee-length socks with two orange stripes at the end of them. His upper body may have a bit of a muscular build, as seen when he was in his underwear.\n\n==Personality==\nIn his very short appearance more than halfway through<ref name=\"shape\">[[Shapeshifter]]</ref>, Jaren appears to be quite an egotistical young man as he turns into a wreck after he realizes that his pants and hair gel were stolen from him and cries out in agony while exhibiting himself in his underwear on the streets. Because of this dilemma, it is mentioned that he may not be able to summon enough courage to take the stage as scheduled.\n\n==Hip Hop Career==\nJaren is a teen pop star that many teenage girls obsess over, including [[Rose]] and her friend [[Courtney]]. One of his biggest hit singles is called ''Girls, Don't Be Trippin'''. He planned to have a concert at the Radio City Music Hall in New York City<ref name=\"shape\">[[Shapeshifter]]</ref>, although it is unknown if he actually did his how after his pants and hair gel were stolen.\n\nJaren also participated in the 3rd Annual Hip-Hop Video Awards with his hit single ''Baby, Baby, Baby, Baby, Baby, Baby'', although this is only mentioned and never actually seen<ref name=\"tooth\">[[The Legend of Dragon Tooth]]</ref>.\n\n==Quotes==\n*''(recording of him singing)'' \"Girls, don't be trippin'; my mind, you're always flippin'; with all your talk of relationshippin'...\"\n*''(in his underpants after his pants and Tub-o-Hairgel were stolen)'' \"Why, yo?
