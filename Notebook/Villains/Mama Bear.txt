She is an adult bear who is [[Papa Bear]]'s wife and [[Bear (Goldie & Bear)|Bear]]'s mother. She is a stay at home mom and have a very cheerful personality. When not at home, she is the Troll Tamer, keeping the [[Trolls (Goldie and Bear)|troll]] under the local bridge from eating anyone.
