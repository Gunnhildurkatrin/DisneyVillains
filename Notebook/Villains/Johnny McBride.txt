He was a classmate in [[Penny Proud|Penny]]'s algebra class, who was secretly using graffiti on the school's walls to show his love for her. On the night he was caught, he confessed to the principal about the graffiti, and wound up suspended. Penny felt sorry for him and reluctantly became his girlfriend. He comes to the Proud family's house and Oscar doesn't mind letting him in, as Penny's boyfriend.
