"[[File:Shinobu.jpg|thumb]]'''Shinobu Takeda'''\u00a0is part of the supporting cast in the Eight Saga of the ''[[W.I.T.C.H.]]'' comic book series.\n\n==Background==\nShinobu is the younger daughter of Foreman Takeda and his wife, Mia. She is [[Mariko Takeda|Mariko]]'s younger sister. Because of her father's obsession with Mariko she became invisible to his eyes. She discovered her father's secret and decided to enter the Fast World and follow the W.I.T.C.H. girls. Shinobu was the one who found [[William Collins|William]] in the Fast Realm.
