"{{Infobox character\n|image = Dr. Watson.png\n|name = Doctor Watson \n|shows = Goof Troop \n|voice = [[Frank Welker]] \n|inspiration = [[John Watson|Dr. John Watson]]\n|occupation = Doctor<br>Writer\n|home = London, England \n|likes = Helping Sherlock Goof\n|dislikes = Professor Inferiority's schemes\n|powers = Lassoing skills<br>Writing skills<br>Medical knowledge\n|paraphernalia = Typewriter<br>Rope \n|status = Active}}\n'''Dr. Watson''' is the friend of [[Goofy|Goofy's]] historical counterpart in the Goof History episode \"[[Sherlock Goof]]\" from [[Disney Television Animation|Disney's]] animated series, [[Goof Troop]].\n\n==Background==\nHe followed [[Goofy|Sherlock Goof]] as he attempted to thwart [[Pete|Professor Inferiority's]] plot. After rescuing Sherlock from floating down some rapids, he received his permission to publish the documentation of his adventures.\n\n===Personality===\nDr. Watson is a nice person who, being a doctor, enjoys helping people. He is also brave and resourceful, using a rope to save [[Goofy|Sherlock Goof]] in his time of need.\n\n===Physical appearance===\nDr. Watson is a small, overweight anthropomorphic dog with light brown hair. He wears a green jacket, a white dress shirt, yellow pants, brown shoes, a dark yellow newsboy cap, a blue bowtie, a black belt, and white gloves.\n\n===Role in the series===\nHis only appearance is in the episode, \"[[Sherlock Goof]]\". His most notable actions are at the end of the episode when he helps [[Goofy|Sherlock Goof]] escape the danger of some rapids.\n\n==Trivia==\n*Dr. Watson is a parody of [[John Watson]], a character in the original Sherlock Holmes stories, who was also featured in silhouette form in Disney's film, \"[[The Great Mouse Detective]]\".\n\n{{Goof Troop}}\n[[Category:Heroes]]\n[[Category:Goof Troop characters]]\n[[Category:TV Animation characters]]\n[[Category:Disney characters]]\n[[Category:Animated characters]]\n[[Category:Males]]\n[[Category:Adults]]\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:Dogs]]\n[[Category:The Disney Afternoon characters]]\n[[Category:Mickey Mouse universe characters]]\n[[Category:Doctors]]\n[[Category:Writers]]\n[[Category:Historical characters]]"
