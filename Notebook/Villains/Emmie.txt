She is mostly giggly and playful, but she can get a little worried if her toys aren't working right. Emmie is also sisterly to Alma, mostly in the episode \"Blame It On The Rain\", when Alma accidentally leaves [[Moo-Moo]] outside in the rain and is upset about it, Doc gently tells her that she will dry Moo-Moo in the clinic.\n\n===Physical appearance===\nEmmie is a slender girl\u00a0with brown hair, fair skin, freckles and\u00a0blue eyes. She also wears two yellow hairclips,\u00a0a blue T-shirt, jeans with a green belt and\u00a0light blue mary janes. When sleeping she wears orange pjs with a cat on it. When near water she wears a two piece bathing suit.
