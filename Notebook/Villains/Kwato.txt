"{{Infobox_character\n|image = Kwato_(1).png\n|shows = ''[[The Lion Guard]]''\n|voice = [[Lyons Mathias]]\n|alignment = Good\n|affiliations = [[Pridelanders]]\n|home = [[Pride Lands]]\n|friends = [[Lion Guard]], [[Kambuni]], [[Mtoto]], [[Gumba]], [[Thurston]]|enemies = [[Janja]], [[Cheezi]], [[Chungu]], [[Makuu]]}}'''Kwato'''\u00a0is a character that appears in\u00a0''[[The Lion Guard]]''.\n\nHe resides in the\u00a0[[Pride Lands]].\n\n== Personality ==\nKwato is shown to be playful and fun-loving just like his friends. He admires the\u00a0Lion Guard\u00a0and enjoys pretending to be a member. He is shown to be considerate of his friends, and is quick to defend them, as well as himself, fiercely against foes.\n\n== Role in the Series ==\nIn \"[[Follow That Hippo!]]\" Kwato and his friends \u00a0are pretending to be hyenas when they scare the animals in the Pride Lands. The Lion Guard comes to talk to them. Kion says that they shouldn't be hyenas since Janaja's Clan is in the Pride Lands somewhere. Mtoto asks if the real Lion Guard can play with them. Kion says that it sounds like fun but they need to do some training. Later while playing they get chased by hyenas. Mtoto's friends warn Beshte about Mtoto being in danger and Beshte goes to save him. After hearing how Beshte saved Mtoto Kion gives all of the kids the Mark of the Guard.\n\nIn \"[[Never Roar Again]]\" [[Makuu]] scares the little zebra out of the flood plains. \n\n== Relationships ==\n\n=== Friends ===\n[[Mtoto]], [[Gumba]], [[Kambuni]] and [[Shauku]]\n\nKwato\u00a0is good friends with them.
