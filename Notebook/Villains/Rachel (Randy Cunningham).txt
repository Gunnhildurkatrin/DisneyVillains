"{{Infobox character\n|image = Ninjafan - Rachel.jpg\n|shows = ''[[Randy Cunningham: 9th Grade Ninja]]''\n|voice = [[Laura Marano]]\n|name = Rachel\n|designer = [[Jhonen Vasquez]]\n|alias = Claire, Annette (by Randy)\n|occupation = Student at [[Norrisville High]]<br>Clarinet player in the school marching band\n|home = [[Norrisville]]\n|likes = Cute things, the Norrisville Ninja\n|dislikes = Being unappreciated\n|status = Active\n|possessions = Guitar<br>Clarinet}}\n\n'''Rachel''' is a minor character from the [[Disney XD]] TV show ''[[Randy Cunningham: 9th Grade Ninja]]''. She is a student at [[Norrisville High]] and a clarinet player in the Norrisville High Marching Band. She is voiced by [[Laura Marano]].\n\n==Personality==\nRachel is cheerful, merry, and friendly. She loves things that are cute and enjoys showing kindness to other people. Whenever people need help, she graciously gives it to them. She always views things in a positive light, often being happy for others' successes. However, in more serious situations, she has proven to be more down-to-earth and sensible. She is also dedicated to music, as seen when she made a song to the Norrisville Ninja and is also a member of the school's marching band.\n\n==Appearance==\nRachel has grayish dark brown hair and green eyes. She wears a blue and gold marching band uniform.
