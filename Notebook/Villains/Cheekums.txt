He makes his first appearance in \"[[Little Dipper (Gravity Falls)|Little Dipper]]\".\n\n==History==\nCheekums appears when Li'l Gideon releases Mabel and Dipper Pines out of a jar and puts them in his bedroom, Li'l Gideon tells Cheekums to guard him, however, he does not. Mabel tries to ride him, but doesn't, and Mabel calls him a Big Ol' Dummy. Dipper then pokes him with an ear waxer.\n\n==Appearance==\nCheekums is a light brown hamster with a pink nose and brown freckles, he also has brown inside of his ears, whiskers, and he has two front teeth (buck teeth) and has gray claws.\n\n==Personality==\nCheekums is described as a \"Big Ol' Dummy\" and is very unresponsive and doesn't react to any commands.\n\n{{Gravity Falls}}\n[[Category:Pets]]\n[[Category:Gravity Falls characters]]\n[[Category:Neutral characters]]\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:Disney characters]]\n[[Category:Males]]\n[[Category:Animated characters]]\n[[Category:TV Animation characters]]\n[[Category:Hamsters]]\n[[Category:Silent characters]]\n[[Category:Disney XD characters]]"
