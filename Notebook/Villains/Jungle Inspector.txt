He is voiced by [[Tony Jay]].\n\nThe inspector is a no-nonsense marabou stork whose duty is keep things organized. One the day he came to visit [[Zazu's Tree]], he showed to the hornbill that he was off by one animal in the number of jungle animals. Afterwards, the inspector kicked [[Zazu]] out of the tree and gives the job to [[Gopher (The Lion King)|Gopher]]. But he found out the hard way that Gopher wasn't as talented as Zazu was.
