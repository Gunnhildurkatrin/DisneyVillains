"[[File:Missemotion.jpg|thumb|250px]]\n'''Miss Emotion''' is the half of a woman's brain rules by her uncontrollable passions in the [[1943]] animated short, ''[[Reason and Emotion]]''. She complains to Miss Reason over the fact that she can't have any fun and that the guy the woman met was cute. Miss Reason tries to keep her under control, saying that they are a lady and should act as such. Miss Emotion then says that she is hungry and they steer the woman into a diner.
