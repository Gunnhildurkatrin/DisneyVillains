"{{Infobox character\n|image = Mandy.png\n|name = Mandy\n|shows = ''[[Sofia the First]]''\n|voice = Madeleine Curry\n|personality = \n|appearance = \n|occupation = \n|alignment = Good \n|friends = [[Princess Amber]], [[Sofia the First (character)|Sofia]]\n|enemies = \n|likes = Camping\n|quote = }}'''Mandy''' is a character who appears in the ''[[Sofia the First]]'' episode, \"[[Camp Wilderwood]]\". She is one of the fellow campers at Camp Wilderwood, who becomes fast friends with Sofia and eventually Amber.\n\n==Role in the Series==\nShe first appears when she walks up to [[Princess Amber|Amber]], who is disappointed when Mandy asks \"And you are?\" Sofia comes over and introduces herself and Amber. Sofia and Mandy become fast friends and spend much of their time at Camp Wilderwood in each other's company. When [[Marty and Maple]]\u00a0announce that the Inter-camp Flag Race has begun, Sofia, who noticed how left out Amber felt, insists that they be buddies for the Race. Amber and Mandy finally become friends and when they see that Sofia doesn't have a buddy they invite her to join their team. During the race, the girls' boat run aground. They get saved by Sofia's brother Prince James and his buddy [[Wendell Fidget]]. Working together, the five of them win the Race for Camp Wilderwood.\n\n== Trivia ==\n* Mandy has the same hairstyle as [[Princess Isabel]] from [[Elena of Avalor|''Elena of Avalor'']].\n\n==Gallery==\n<gallery position=\"center\" captionalign=\"center\">\nCamp Wilderwood 6.png\n</gallery>\n\n{{Sofia the First}}\n{{DISPLAYTITLE:Mandy}}\n[[pt-br:Mandy (Princesinha Sofia)]]\n[[Category:Sofia the First characters]]\n[[Category:Females]]\n[[Category:Kids]]\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:Animated characters]]\n[[Category:TV Animation characters]]\n[[Category:Disney Junior characters]]\n[[Category:Disney characters]]"
