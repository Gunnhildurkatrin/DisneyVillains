"{{Infobox character\n|name = Buzz-Buzz the Bee\n|image = Buzz_Buzz_Bee.jpg\n|films = \n|shows =  ''[[Chip 'n Dale Rescue Rangers]]'' (cameo)<br>''[[Mickey Mouse Clubhouse]]<br>[[Mickey and the Roadster Racers]]<br>[[Legend of the Three Caballeros]]''\n|shorts = ''[[Honey Harvester]]<br>[[Inferior Decorator]]<br>[[Bee on Guard]]''\n|alliance = }}\n\n'''Buzz-Buzz the Bee''' is a bumble bee that is featured in Disney comics, television shows and films. He is from [[Duckburg]] and has an unnamed wife.\n\n==History==\nHis first appearance was in the film [[Inferior Decorator]] (Aug. 27, 1948). His first Comic appearance was in Walt Disney's Comics and Stories\" #92 in \"Inferior Decorator\" (May 1948) and later he was also in \"WDC&S\" #99 in \"[[Honey Harvester]]\" (Dec. 1948). Buzz-Buzz appeared in seven cartoons and was retroactively named, though in some cartoons he was called Spike, Claudius and Hector, Buzz-Buzz became the preferred Studio name. Usually a rival for [[Donald Duck]], Buzz-Buzz also appeared in at least one [[Pluto]] cartoon.\n\nIn ''[[Mickey Mouse Clubhouse]]'', Buzz-Buzz is a recurring animal companion.\n\nIn [[Legend of the Three Caballeros]], Buzz-Buzz (or at least a look-alike) appears in a brief cameo when Humphrey the Bear goes after a large amount of honey in New Quackmore.\n\n==Gallery==\n<gallery position=\"center\" captionalign=\"center\" widths=\"185\">\n1158-2-32.jpg|Buzz-Buzz with [[Minnie Mouse|Minnie]] and [[Donald Duck|Donald]] in ''[[Mickey Mouse Clubhouse]]''.\n1957-donald-award-09.jpg\nTumblr nto9uoXUd91qhcrb0o1 1280.jpg\n</gallery>\n\n{{Mickey Mouse & Friends}}\n{{Mickey Mouse Clubhouse}}\n[[Category:Characters who fly]]\n[[Category:Disney characters]]\n[[Category:Comic characters]]\n[[Category:Mickey Mouse Clubhouse characters]]\n[[Category:Males]]\n[[Category:Adults]]\n[[Category:Animated characters]]\n[[Category:TV Animation characters]]\n[[Category:Mickey Mouse universe characters]]\n[[Category:Donald Duck universe characters]]\n[[Category:Spouses]]\n[[Category:Bees]]\n[[Category:Anti-heroes]]\n[[Category:Heroes]]\n[[Category:Lovers]]\n[[Category:Neutral characters]]\n[[Category:Anthropomorphic characters]]\n[[Category:Chip 'n Dale Rescue Rangers characters]]\n[[Category:The Disney Afternoon characters]]\n[[Category:Disney Junior characters]]\n[[Category:Silent characters]]\n[[Category:Mickey and the Roadster Racers characters]]"
