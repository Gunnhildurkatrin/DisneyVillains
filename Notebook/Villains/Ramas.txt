"{{Species Infobox\n|image = Ramas.jpg\n|shows = ''[[Adventures of the Gummi Bears]]''\n|appearance = Llamas with the horns of rams }}\n'''Ramas''' are a cross between a ram and a llama. They appear in the ''[[Adventures of the Gummi Bears]]'' episode \"[[True Gritty]]\".\n\n==Background==\nRamas are the same size as llamas, but will ram you with their horns if you turn your back on them.\n\nFound in the wild around the Northern Mountains, they are used by the Barbic Bears of [[Ursalia]] to carry great amounts.\n\n[[Cubbi Gummi|Cubbi]] learns all about them from [[Gritty]] when the two go on a rama roundup in search of water for Ursalia.\n\n{{Stub}}\n\n{{Adventures of the Gummi Bears}}\n[[Category:Adventures of the Gummi Bears characters]]\n[[Category:Hybrids]]\n[[Category:Creatures]]\n[[Category:The Disney Afternoon characters]]\n[[Category:Goats]]\n[[Category:Neutral characters]]\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:Disney characters]]\n[[Category:Animated characters]]\n[[Category:TV Animation characters]]"
