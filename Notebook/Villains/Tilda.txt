She then appears when Ichabod makes her along with two other girls sing. During the party organized by [[Baltus Van Tassel]],\u00a0[[Brom Bones]] notices Tilda has no dance partner\u00a0and decided to use her as a way to swap her with\u00a0[[Katrina Van Tassel|Katrina]].
