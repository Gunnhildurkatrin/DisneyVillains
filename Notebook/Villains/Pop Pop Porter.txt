"{{Infobox character\n|name = Pop Pop Porter\n|image = Pop Pop Porter Close Up.jpg\n|shows = ''[[Kim Possible]]''\n|voice = [[Stephen Root]]}}'''Pop Pop Porter''' is a minor character from the animated television show ''[[Kim Possible]]''. He is voiced by [[Stephen Root]]. He is short and overweight. He wears a black business suit and has gray hair.\n\n==History==\nPop Pop is one of the five richest people in the world. He revolutionized the heat-and-eat snack industry, with his [[Pop Pop's Mini Corn Dogs]]. These are mini corn dogs which are Never fried but Flash-frozen. \u00a0\n\n[[Se\u00f1or Senior, Senior]] regularly has him and the three other richest people in the world (including [[Martin Smarty]]) over to play cards.\n\n==Appearances==\n*\"[[Animal Attraction]]\"\n*\"[[All the News]]\"\n*\"[[The Big Job]]\"\n\n==Trivia==\n*He shares the same surname with [[Vivian Francis Porter]], although no relationship was ever stated or implied.\n\n==Gallery==\n<gallery spacing=\"medium\" position=\"center\">\nPop Pop Porter Full.jpg\npop_____.jpg\npop pop porter.jpg\nPop_Pop_Porter_.jpg\nPop_Pop's_Blimp2.jpg\n</gallery>\n\n{{Kim Possible}}\n[[Category:Kim Possible characters]]\n[[Category:Males]]\n[[Category:Adults]]\n[[Category:Businesspeople]]\n[[Category:American characters]]\n[[Category:Disney characters]]\n[[Category:Chefs]]\n[[Category:Neutral characters]]\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:Animated characters]]\n[[Category:TV Animation characters]]\n[[Category:Disney Channel characters]]"
