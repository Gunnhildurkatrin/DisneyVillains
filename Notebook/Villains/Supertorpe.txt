"'''Supertorpe''' is the main character of the TV show of the same name, played by Candela Vetrano.\n\nSupertorpe is a very similar character to [[Darkwing Duck]]. Like Darkwing, she is a superheroine whose origins are shrouded in mystery. Like Darkwing, he is clumsy, ditzy and arrogant, but can be quite effective when she takes a situation seriously, and just like Darkwing, she has a noble heart and a sincere desire to protect the innocent.\n\nHer real name is Poli Truper. Her superheroine name was coined by the media due to her clumsy nature (\"torpe\" is the Spanish word for \"clumsy\"), and after resigned herself to it, she adopted the name herself.\n\nSupertrope is 16 years old,and has to balance the challenges of the life of a superheroine with the usual challenges that teenagers face.\n\nSupertorpe can fly, she has super-strength, super-speed, invulnerability, the ability to shrink to the size of an ant and the ability to grow to 40 ft. tall.[[File:Supertorpe.jpg|thumb]]\n[[Category:Disney characters]]\n[[Category:Live-action characters]]\n[[Category:Heroines]]\n[[Category:Superheroes]]\n[[Category:Teenagers]]\n[[Category:Characters who fly]]\n[[Category:Giants]]\n[[Category:Hispanic characters]]"
