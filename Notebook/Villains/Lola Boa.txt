She speaks with a heavy [[Spain|Spanish]] accent. While she often has great ideas Brandy believes her ideas aren't too bright, and Brandy secretly believes she is gross because she swallows rodents whole while they are still alive. However, Lola remains one of Brandy's best friends throughout the show.\n==Physical description==\nLola is known to look like a pink and purple striped snake with big red lips, wearing huge gold hooped earrings even though she has no ears. Her eyelashes are also very long.\n\n==Personality==\nLola is very kind and welcoming. She helps where she can, and always knows what is best. She is also sensitive of not having arms or legs.\n\n==Relationships==\n===Brandy Harrington===\n{{Main|Brandy Harrington}}\n\nBrandy Harrington is Lola's best friend.
