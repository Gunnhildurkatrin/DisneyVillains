He also makes a cameo appearance with recycled animation from the live-action/animated film ''[[So Dear to My Heart]]''.\n\n==Role in the film==\nBoreas is a two headed-cloud deity sent by [[Zeus (Fantasia)|Zeus]]. His task is to get rid of [[Bacchus (Fantasia)|Bacchus]], [[Jacchus]], and his honorers by blowing them away.
