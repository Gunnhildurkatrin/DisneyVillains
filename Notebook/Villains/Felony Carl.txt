He is an ex-con who gave up his criminal life after being released from prison. Despite his rough voice and intimidating appearance, Felony Carl appears to be a very calm person who is comfortable with his masculinity, meaning that he does not mind having or using things that would be considered \"girly\".\nFelony Carl is Globby's boyfriend and roommate.
