"[[File:Char_30382.jpg|thumb|250px]]\n'''Craig Bean'''<ref>http://www.imdb.com/character/ch0199549/</ref> (voiced by [[Jeff Bennett]])<ref>http://www.imdb.com/name/nm0071818/</ref><ref>http://www.behindthevoiceactors.com/tv-shows/Disneys-Pepper-Ann/Craig-Bean/</ref> is a character in the TV series ''[[Pepper Ann]]''.\n\n==History==\nHe is an 8th grader at [[Hazelnut Middle School]] who first appears in the season one episode \"[[Romeo and Juliet]]\" as a guy on who [[Pepper Ann Pearson|Pepper Ann]] has a crush. In the episode, he gets the part of '''Romeo''' in the school's play, which makes every girl in the school want to play the role of '''Juliet'''. He knows how to play the electric guitar and he is also the 'Cool Guy' of the show.\n\nIn \"[[The Finale]]\", it is established that in the series' version of [[2013]], that Craig stayed in Hazelnut to become a gym teacher and has been helpful with the community. He also adopts a dog and names her M' Girl, which Pepper Ann misinterprets as meaning that he already has a girlfriend. When she realizes the truth, she asks him out and he accepts.\n\nHe has braces, brown hair, and his eyes have never been seen due to the fact he always wears a pair of dark sunglasses.\n\n==Trivia==\n*His Birthday is November 27th, [[1983]].\n*In \"[[A No Hair Day]]\", he is revealed to have a mild form of [[wikipedia:Cutis verticis gyrata|cutis verticis gyrata]], in other words, a wrinkled head.\n\n==Gallery==\n<gallery spacing=\"small\" position=\"center\">\nCraig.jpg\n</gallery>\n\n==References==\n<references/>\n{{CharacterStub}}\n{{Pepper Ann}}\n{{DEFAULTSORT:Bean, Craig}}\n[[Category:Pepper Ann characters]]\n[[Category:Disney characters]]\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:Males]]\n[[Category:Lovers]]\n[[Category:Teenagers]]\n[[Category:Animated characters]]\n[[Category:Students]]\n[[Category:TV Animation characters]]"
