"Villains\u00a0which lack the qualities of a traditional villain due to despite being immoral, they have heroic, noble or at-least non-evil motives, goals or ideologies which oppose those of the central characters. Often in Disney media, these villains will reform or temporarily work with a hero for a shared purpose.\n\nNot to be confused with the antithesis of this classification, the [[:Category:Anti-heroes]], which are supporting characters or protagonists who lack certain traditional hero qualities, despite being on the side of good.\n\nThey are also not to be confused with tragic-villains or reformed villains. Although they can overlap, reformed or tragic villains are not necessarily anti-villainous.\n[[Category:Villains]]\n[[Category:Neutral characters]]\n[[Category:Characters]]"
