He is a child psychologist for a living and thought of adding a dog to the family, which came along [[Stan Mordecai James|Stan]]. He is not a very strict parent, but can sometimes surprise you in a lot of ways\u2014which is not a good thing.\n\n==Personality==\nBennett is the father of the Jennings/James family. He is a big-time child psychologist who has written several books, however, he is kind of a goof ball. He always thinks he knows the best way to handle everything, and comes up with some pretty unusual ways to deal with family issues. He always thinks of [[Chloe James|Chloe]] as a princess, himself as a king, and Ellen as a queen.\n\n==Character History==\n===Season 1===\nIn\u00a0\"[[Stan of the House]]\", Bennett got the kids a dog because they were not bonding very well. He later chewed the kids out for not taking care of Stan properly after he ran away.\n\nIn\u00a0\"[[The Fast and the Furriest]]\", Bennett brought [[Tyler James|Tyler]] home with his learners permit.
