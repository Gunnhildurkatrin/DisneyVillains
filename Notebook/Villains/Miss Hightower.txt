"[[File:Char 39323.jpg|thumb|250px]]'''Miss Hightower'''<ref>http://www.imdb.com/character/ch0416185/</ref> (voiced by Patricia Belcher)<ref>http://www.imdb.com/name/nm0006970/?ref_=ttfc_fc_cl_t27</ref><ref>http://www.behindthevoiceactors.com/movies/The-Proud-Family-Movie/Ms-Hightower/</ref> is a recurring character from\u00a0[[Disney Channel]]'s ''[[The Proud Family]]''.\n\n==History==\nShe is the principal of Willie T. Ribbs Middle School. She usually speaks in a meek, quiet voice, but then switches to a loud, authoritative voice when the kids won't listen to her, hence her name. When she yells, its usually insults about dim possibilities for the kids' futures.\n\n==Appearance==\nMiss Hightower is a short middle aged woman with brownish auburn hair. She wears glasses with round lenses. She wears a brown blouse with yellow flowers and a orange skirt.
