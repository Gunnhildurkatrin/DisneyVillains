"{{Infobox_character\n|image = Screen Shot 2015-05-09 at 6.49.58 PM.png\n|voice = Paris Van Dyke\n|films = ''[[Inside Out]]''\n|home = [[Minnesota]]\n|dislikes = Riley moving away|occupation=Middle school student<br>Hockey team member\n|name = Meg}}\n\n'''Meg''' is a minor character in the [[2015]] [[Walt Disney Pictures|Disney]]\u2022[[Pixar]] animated feature film, ''[[Inside Out]]''. She is [[Riley Andersen]]'s former old best friend from [[Minnesota]].\n\n==Role in the film==\nWhen Riley and her parents move to [[San Francisco]], they part their ways in a tearful goodbye. A few days later, they start video chatting and Meg reveals she has found a brand new friend she made who she continuously talks endlessly about, unknowingly angering Riley. Riley becomes fed up and hangs up on her ending her friendship with her for good.
