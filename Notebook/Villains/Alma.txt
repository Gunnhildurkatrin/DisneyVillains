He loves that.\"}}'''Alma''' is a minor character from the Disney Junior series ''[[Doc McStuffins]]''. Alma is Emmie's younger sister and is close friends with Donny. She loves her toys almost as much as playing with her sister.\n\n==Appearance==\nAlma is small and slender with short red hair, green eyes and fair skin with freckles.\u00a0She also\u00a0wears a blue jean dress with gray polka dots on it, a light blue T-shirt and purple mary janes.\n\n==Personality==\nAlma is sweet, cute and friendly, with a very nice smile. But when her toys don't work, Alma can get a little upset or worried about it, especially in the episode ''Blame It On The Rain'', when Alma accidentally leaves her stuffed cow, Moo-Moo, out in the rain, she feels upset about it\u00a0and starts to cry.\u00a0Doc and Moo-Moo\u00a0later see Alma crying on the picnic table seat, telling her other toys, her fairy and frog, about the memories she has had with Moo-Moo before leaving her out in the rain. She is happy again when Doc brings Moo-Moo, who is all dry again, to her.\n\n==Trivia==\n*Alma may look similar to her sister, Emmie, because of her hair style.\n*Alma may be the same age as Donny, Will and\u00a0Luca, like they are 4 years old.\n\n==Gallery==\n<gallery>\n407px-Alma.png\n640px-The_Kids_and_Bubble_Monkey.png\n</gallery>\n\n{{Doc McStuffins}}\n[[Category:Disney Junior characters]]\n[[Category:Females]]\n[[Category:Kids]]\n[[Category:Siblings]]\n[[Category:Doc McStuffins characters]]\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:Disney characters]]\n[[Category:Animated characters]]\n[[Category:TV Animation characters]]"
