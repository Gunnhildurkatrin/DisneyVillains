She wears red armor.\n== Role in the series ==\n=== [[Tangled: The Series]]===\n====Season 3====\nShe appears in \"[[Beginnings]]\". She is queen of Ingvarr, as her kingdom participates in other kingdoms, including [[Corona]]. She notices [[Cassandra|Cassandra's]] frustration towards Rapunzel for her optimism.
