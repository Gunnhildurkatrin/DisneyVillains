He was an all-conference boat racer in college when an injury ended his professional racing aspirations. Instead of becoming a police boat like his father, he went to [[New York City|New York]] to pursue acting. Now he is [[United States|America]]'s favorite leading boat!''<ref name=\"meettheplanes\">Disney (2014). ''Meet the Planes''. Disney Press. ISBN 978-1423175674</ref>\n\n===Physical appearance===\nReynolds is a white boat who has brown stripes painted on him, a black shape over his mouth that represents a mustache, brown eyes, and a see-through hat.\n\n==Appearances==\n===''[[Planes: Fire & Rescue]]''===\nBoat Reynolds only makes one appearance in the film, where he comes into the Grand Fusel Lodge at the time a party is being held for the grand reopening.
