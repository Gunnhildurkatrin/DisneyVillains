It appears as a flying dolphin with a small yellow bow tie, and two muscular humanoid arms. After spinning around, it grows another dolphin-like head and two more muscular humanoid arms. On each palm of its hands are dolphin faces, which shoot rainbow-colored rays from their mouths.
