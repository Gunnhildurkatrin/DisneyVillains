Bobcat]]'s main love interest and co-star when he was a cartoon star. \n\n==Personality==\nFawn is very sweet and passionate as well as friendly and likable. She cares a lot about those close to her, especially Bonkers.\n\n==History==\nFawn is [[Bonkers D. Bobcat|Bonkers]]' main love interest and co-star when he was a cartoon star. Bonkers is willing to do just about anything to please and impress her. Fortunately for Bonkers, Fawn clearly reciprocates his love for her, as she has proven it on numerous occasions by kissing him on the cheek, and sometimes on the lips.
