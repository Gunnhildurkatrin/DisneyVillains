"[[File:Lizzie McGuire 222 Dear Lizzie 037 0001.jpg|thumb|250px]]''All info was provided [[w:c:lizziemcguire:Veruca Albano|Veruca Albano at Lizzie McGuire Wiki]]''\n\n'''Veruca Albano''' was a nerdy girl who appeared once in Season 1 and more often in Season 2 when [[Lizzie McGuire (character)|Lizzie]] enters the eighth grade. In the episode \"Dear Lizzie,\" when asking Lizzie for advice on dealing with a bully named Cheryl, she signed her email as Fed Up. Lizzie's advice backfires and Cheryl assaults Veruca. An angry Veruca confronts Lizzie. Lizzie apologizes.
