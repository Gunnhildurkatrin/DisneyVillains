She made her debut in the nursery rhyme-based ''[[Who Killed Cock Robin?]].'' She also appeared in ''[[Toby Tortoise Returns]]'' and made cameos in ''[[Mickey's Polo Team]]'' and ''[[Who Framed Roger Rabbit]]''. She is very flirtatious with other boys, but her one true boyfriend is [[Cock Robin]]. She was voiced by Martha Wentworth.\n\n==Appearances==\n===[[Who Killed Cock Robin?]]===\nIn her debut, a robin named [[Cock Robin]] begins to sing and strum his guitar. Jenny walks outside her birdhouse and listens to him and falls in love. When he was shot with an arrow, she was the sole witness. Later she shows up to the trial and sings about [[Cock Robin]] in her song \"''Who Killed Cock Robin''?\" and tells what she saw. Later when Cupid explains he shot [[Cock Robin]] because he fell in love and he is not dead. Later Jenny wakes him up and they kiss while Jenny uses her parasol to cover them up.\n\n===[[Toby Tortoise Returns]]===\nWhen [[Toby Tortoise]] is punched out of the ring by [[Max Hare]], Jenny rushes to him and helps him wake up.
