She is the strapping middle-child of [[Julieta Madrigal]] and [[Agust\u00edn Madrigal]], who had been gifted with super strength.\n\n==Background==\nThe middle daughter, Luisa, is the rock of the Madrigals. Graced with super-strength, she is the one who is always there to lend a helping hand. She\u2019s the one that Mirabel relies on, she\u2019s the one in the family that\u2019s carrying all the burdens and never complaining. She\u2019s tough but also can be really funny and vulnerable.\n\n===Physical appearance===\nLuisa's body has a strong build. She has tan skin hazel eyes and black hair that's tied up in a bun held by a red ribbon. she wears a dress with the top half being white and short-sleeved, with a red ribbon at the top. and the bottom half being indigo, with dark purple and pink stripes at the bottom. she also has felt bracelets with the left being red and denim and the right being red denim and yellow. and she wears dark blue shoes with two dark blue ankle straps.\n\n===Powers and abilities===\nShe is the strongest in the family being able to lift things at ease.\n\n==Trivia==\n*Early in production, the character's name was Lydia.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://thedisinsider.com/2020/12/09/exclusive-new-details-for-walt-disney-animation-studios-encanto/|title=Exclusive: New Details for Walt Disney Animation Studios\u2019 \u2018Encanto\u2019|website=The Disinsider|last=Sharpe|first=Josh|date=December 9, 2020|access-date=July 8, 2021}}</ref>\n\n==Gallery==\n<gallery position=\"center\" captionalign=\"center\" widths=\"185\">\nEncanto-Family is Everything.png\nThe Ultimate Disney Encanto Sticker Book.png\nEncanto family.jpg\nEncanto-family-tree-4789609-949x1200.jpg\nFile:Encanto poster 2.JPG\n</gallery>\n\n==Reference==\n{{Reflist}}\n{{stub}}\n\n{{Encanto}}\n[[Category:Females]]\n[[Category:Hispanic characters]]\n[[Category:Siblings]]\n[[Category:Upcoming characters]]\n[[Category:Disney characters]]\n[[Category:Animated characters]]\n[[Category:Characters in the Disney animated features canon]]\n[[Category:Encanto characters]]\n[[Category:Character stubs]]\n[[Category:Teenagers]]"
