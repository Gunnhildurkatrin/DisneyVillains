He sometimes tricks others (usually [[Q.T.]]) to do his own work, but sometimes his tricks backfire on him. In the side show, he has a ventriloquist dummy called Sly with him.\n[[File:Sebastian_with_Sly.jpg|thumb|left|Sebastian with Sly]]\nSebastian was with Dumbo and Lionel when they first started out.
