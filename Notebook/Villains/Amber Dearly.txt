"{{Infobox character\n|image = AmberDearly.png\n|shows = ''[[101 Dalmatians: The Series]]''\n|voice = [[Francesca Marie Smith]]\n|likes = Performing, playing with the pups\n|dislikes = Ivy De Vil competing with her\n|status = Active\n|family = [[Anita Radcliffe|Anita]] (aunt)<br>[[Roger Radcliffe|Roger]] (uncle)}}\n'''Amber Dearly''' is a minor character from ''[[101 Dalmatians: The Series]]'' who appeared in the episode \"[[Beauty Pageant Pandemonium]]\". She is [[Roger Radcliffe|Roger Dearly]] and [[Anita Radcliffe|Anita]]'s niece.\n\n==Background==\n===Personality===\nShe is sweet, caring and kind to the Dalmatians on the farm. She is also very talented, training the pups to jump through a hoop being one of her talents.\n\n===Physical appearance===\nAmber is a young girl around eight or nine years of age. She has fair skin, blue-green eyes, long strawberry-blonde hair wrapped in a high ponytail with a turquoise hair tie and wears a turquoise shirt with a yellow flower on it, a violet skirt, turquoise socks and violet flats.\n\n==Trivia==\n*It is unknown which side of the Dearly couple Amber comes from.
