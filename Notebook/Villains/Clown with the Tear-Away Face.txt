He is a resident of [[Halloween Town]] who, like the other residents, sought to celebrate Christmas. \n\n\n==Appearance==\nHe is an obese clown with purple-ish skin, dark purple lips, a red nose and sharp teeth. He normally wears a cream-colored suit with purple spots and green and purple striped sleeves. He also wears a purple and green\u00a0propeller cap, a black belt with a purple and green star buckle and brown shoes. He also has a rubber chicken and a green yoyo on his belt and is always show riding a unicycle.\n\nAs his name suggests, he can tear his face clean off, revealing nothing but darkness.\n\n==Personality==\nHe appears to act quite jolly and demented and enjoys taking part in Halloween Town's events.\n\n==Appearances==\n===''[[The Nightmare Before Christmas]]''===\nThe Clown with the Tear-Away Face is a fat clown who rides a unicycle and can tear off his face to reveal complete darkness and disappear into a puff of smoke. When his face is on, he speaks in a high voice. But when his face is off, he speaks in a deep, booming voice.\n==Video Game Appearances==\n===''[[The Nightmare Before Christmas: The Pumpkin King]]''===\n[[File:Clown_Sprite.gif|left]]The Clown with the Tear-Away Face appears in the game and rewards Jack with the [[Pumpkin Bomb]], the final weapon of the game.\n===''[[The Nightmare Before Christmas: Oogie's Revenge]]''===\nHe also appears in Tim Burton's ''The Nightmare Before Christmas: Oogie's Revenge'' where Jack finds him the sewers upon his return. The Clown then unlocks the gate so that Jack could proceed to the graveyard. In the residential areas, the Clown's unicycle was out of control causing Jack to use one of Dr. Finklestein's inventions to stop the unicycle.
