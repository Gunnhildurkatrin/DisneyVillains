"[[File:A&AKimmy.png|thumb]]\n'''Kimmy''' is a recruiting character in Season 2 of ''[[Austin & Ally]],'' who has appeared in \"Freaky Friends & Fan Fiction\" as well as \"Sports & Sprains\". Her last appearance is in \"Sports and Sprains\". She is Portrayed by Cassidy Ann Shaffer.\n\n==Physical Appearance==\nKimmy has a blonde hair that she wears in two pigtails.
