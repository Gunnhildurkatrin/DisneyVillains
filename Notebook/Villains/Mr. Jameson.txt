"{{Infobox character\n|image = Mrjameson.jpg\n|name = Mr. Jameson\n|voice = [[Bryan Cranston]]\n|shows = ''[[Lilo & Stitch: The Series]]'' \n|personality = Serious, strict, firm, kind, fatherly, good-hearted\n|occupation = Businessman\n|home = [[Hawaii]]\n|alliance = \n|goal = To run businesses around Kauai, including the [[Birds of Paradise Hotel|hotel]]\n|family = [[Keoni Jameson]] (son)\n|likes = Running the [[Birds of Paradise Hotel]]\n|dislikes = Uninvited guests\n|friends = [[Lilo Pelekai]], [[Nani Pelekai]], [[David Kawena]], [[Stitch]], [[Jumba Jookiba]], [[Pleakley]]\n|enemies = [[Captain Gantu]] (formerly), [[Dr. H\u00e4msterviel]]\n|powers = Running businesses and companies\n|weapons = \n|fate = Remains in [[Hawaii]] as a businessman.\n|alignment = Good}}\n'''Mr. Jameson''' is a character in\u00a0''[[Lilo & Stitch: The Series]]''. He is the father of [[Keoni Jameson|Keoni]] and, on several occasions, [[Nani Pelekai|Nani]]'s employer. He owns several businesses around Kauai, including the [[Birds of Paradise Hotel]].\n\n==Background==\n===Physical appearance===\nLike his son [[Keoni Jameson|Keoni]], Mr. Jameson has tan skin, but has brown combed hair instead of red, and is usually seen wearing a pink shirt. He also has a lean and muscular build.\n\n===Personality===\nFor the most part, Mr. Jameson is a jovial man who takes the work of his employees to an earnest degree. He is usually seen with a smile on his face and in a bright, happy atmosphere. However, he can be strict when it comes to having unwelcome visitors at his [[Birds of Paradise Hotel|hotel]]'s pool, which is only reserved for guests. Overall, he is nice and compliments people on their hard work. Despite being a businessman, he is very friendly but always keeps his nose on any potential money-making businesses. He also seems to be a fairly good father, as he and Keoni get along just fine.\n\n==Role in the series==\nMr. Jameson appears in a few episodes, including \"[[Cannonball (episode)|Cannonball]]\", \"[[Nosy (episode)|Nosy]]\", \"[[Melty (episode)|Melty]]\", \"[[Babyfier (episode)|Babyfier]]\", and \"[[Drowsy (episode)|Drowsy]]\". He is mentioned in certain episodes by [[Nani Pelekai|Nani]] when she begins working under Mr. Jameson as a [[Rental Hut|rental person]].\n\nIn \"Cannonball\", he kicked Nani and [[David Kawena|David]] and out of his [[Birds of Paradise Hotel|hotel]]'s pool because it was only allowed for guests.\n\nIn \"Melty\", he fired Nani because he couldn't afford her after [[Lilo Pelekai|Lilo]] and [[Stitch]]'s antics caused one of his expensive hotels to be destroyed.\n\nIn \"Babyfier\", he was a victim of [[Babyfier]]'s de-aging powder, but was changed back to normal along with the rest of the babyfied adults.\u00a0\n\n==Trivia==\n*In the intro sequence of [[Lilo & Stitch: The Series|the series]], Mr. Jameson appears in two squares in the hula dance; however, his image and movements are not mirrored to one another.\n\n==Gallery==\n<gallery position=\"center\">\nLilo_&_Stitch_The_Series_intro_-_characters_hula_dancing.png\n</gallery>\n\n{{Lilo & Stitch}}\n{{DEFAULTSORT:Jameson}}\n[[Category:Disney characters]]\n[[Category:Lilo & Stitch characters]]\n[[Category:Males]]\n[[Category:Adults]]\n[[Category:Parents]]\n[[Category:Businesspeople]]\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:Animated characters]]\n[[Category:TV Animation characters]]\n[[Category:Disney Channel characters]]"
