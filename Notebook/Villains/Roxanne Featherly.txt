"{{Infobox character\n|image = Roxanne Featherly - DuckTales 2017.jpg\n|shows = [[DuckTales (2017 series)|''DuckTales'' (2017)]]\n|voice = [[Kari Wahlgren]]\n|inspiration = Webra Walters\n|personality = Professional, talkative, ambitious\n|appearance = Slender duck, green feathers, yellow bill, thick platinum blonde hair, purple suit, black shoes\n|occupation = Reporter\n|alignment = Neutral\n|home = [[Duckburg]]\n|quote = |friends = [[Scrooge McDuck]] [[Huey, Dewey, and Louie]], [[Webby Vanderquack]]}}'''Roxanne Featherly''' is a character in the [[2017]] series ''[[DuckTales (2017 series)|DuckTales]]''. She is a green-feathered duck and a news reporter in [[Duckburg]].\n\n==Role in the series==\nRoxanne first appears in the pilot episode, \"[[Woo-oo!]]\", attending a press conference where [[Flintheart Glomgold]] is citing to have found the Jewel of [[Atlantis]]. However, Roxanne and the others quickly turn their attention to [[Scrooge McDuck]] when he arrives with the real jewel. Roxanne then reports on Scrooge and family's return to adventuring and how they will be \"solving mysteries and rewriting history.\"\n\nIn \"[[JAW$!]]\", Roxanne interviews Scrooge on the collateral damage his adventures have on Duckburg. Although Roxanne is initially friendly and promises the interview to be civil, she then bombards Scrooge with antagonistic questions and accusations, and brings in Flintheart as a video guest. Scrooge loses his patience and lectures Roxanne on his integrity that it ruins his interview. Roxanne then tails Scrooge to [[the Money Bin]] following the destruction of a money shark.
