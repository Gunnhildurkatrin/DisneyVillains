"{{Infobox character\n|image = Randaay.jpg\n|shows = ''[[Kickin' It]]''\n|actor = Evan Hoefer\n|alignment = Good\n|likes = Pulling pranks, skateboarding}}'''Randy Plotski''' is a character from ''[[Kickin' It]]''. He likes skateboarding and pulling pranks.\n\nHe got detention for turning on the fire alarm. He is friends with Jack, Kim, Eddie,and Jerry. He then became friends with Milton in detention in \"The Great Escape\". He also became friends with Rudy after he showed his skills with the skatebord in \"Badge of Honor\". In \"Skate Rat\" it shows that he might have had a crush on Kim, since he said he wanted to marry Kim, but later on he does get upset with Kim and says he hates her.
