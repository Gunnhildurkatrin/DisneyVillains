"{{Infobox_character|image=Summer Gale.jpg|name=Summer Gale|voice=[[Hallie Todd]]|fullname=Summer Gale|occupation=Middleton News Reporter|alignment=Bad|shows=''[[Kim Possible]]''}} \n'''Summer Gale''' is a minor villain from the animated television show ''[[Kim Possible]]'', and a media news weather reporter in [[Middleton]]. \n\n== Appearances ==\n\n=== ''[[Kim Possible]]'' ===\nHer major concern is getting more airtime. To accomplish this, she went so far as to purchase or lease a used Weather Machine from Wacky Wally's Weather Machines to create a surprise overnight snowstorm over the Tri-City Area.<ref name=\"ep217b\">[[Day of the Snowmen]]</ref>. She used the tainted water from [[Lake Wannaweep]] with the unexpected side-effect of snowmen made from the storm's snow coming to malevolent life and attacking the populace. \n==Physical Appearance==\nSummer Gale has blonde hair and blue eyes. She wears a purple and lavender winter coat along with pink high heels.\n==Personality==\nSummer Gale is shown to be rather disregarding of the chaos that she caused.
