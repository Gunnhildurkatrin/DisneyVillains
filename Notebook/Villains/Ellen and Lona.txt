They appear in ''[[Lilo & Stitch: The Series]]''.\n\n==Role in the series==\nNani, Ellen and Lona know each other from junior high, though Nani didn't keep in touch them. Lona owns a cosmetics company while Ellen yachts around the world. They arrive in Kauai on vacation where they reunite with Nani. They assume she's Vice President of the [[Birds of Paradise Hotel]] and tell her about their careers. Ellen invites Nani and her family for dinner on her yacht. Nani surprised they become so successful and worried about inviting the group to the party. They meet the [[Lilo Pelekai|Lilo]], [[Jumba Jookiba|Jumba]] and [[Pleakley]] when arrive to the party and Lilo\u00a0tries to lie for her sister. For a while, everything goes well until [[Stitch]] shows up with Experiment 210 ([[Retro]]) and he devolves Lilo, as well as Jumba, Pleakley, and even Ellen and Lona.
