He goes by the Western name of \"Nick\". He appears in the episode \"[[Heritage]]\".\n\nEducated at the University at Harvard, Natsilane was skeptical about the legends and beliefs of his people, particularly the old tales of [[Raven (Gargoyles)|Raven]] lurking on a volcano top and periodically laying waste to the island.\n\n==Trivia==\n*Natsilane is based on the culture hero of Native American mythology, who is most famous for having created the first killer whale (orca) out of a block of wood.\n\n==Gallery==\n<gallery position=\"center\">\nHeritage 516.png\nHeritage 529.png\nHeritage 841.png\nHeritage 902.png|Natsilane prepares to face off against Raven.\n</gallery>\n\n==External Links==\n[http://www.gargwiki.net/Natsilane Natsilane] at GargWiki.\n\n{{CharacterStub}}\n{{Gargoyles}}\n[[Category:Gargoyles characters]]\n[[Category:Heroes]]\n[[Category:Males]]\n[[Category:TV Animation characters]]\n[[Category:The Disney Afternoon characters]]\n[[Category:Magic Users]]\n[[Category:Scientists]]\n[[Category:Animated characters]]\n[[Category:Disney characters]]\n[[Category:Adults]]\n[[Category:Native American characters]]"
