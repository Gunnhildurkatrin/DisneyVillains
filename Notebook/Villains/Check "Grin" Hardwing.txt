Unlike the stereotype of strong people, Grin is wise and does not let his anger cloud his judgment. He rarely uses weapons in battle, relying on his wisdom and strength instead. He has a strong sense of honor and can always be counted on for support. He is a good friend of [[Nosedive Flashblade|Nosedive]]. During Grin's younger years, he had a horrible temper. He found enlightenment with the training from the grand hockey master, [[Tai Quack Do]].
