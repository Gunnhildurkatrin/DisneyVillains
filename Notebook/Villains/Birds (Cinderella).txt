"{{Group Infobox\n|image = Iceracinderella0203.jpg\n|name = Birds\n|films = ''[[Cinderella (1950 film)|Cinderella]]<br>[[Cinderella II: Dreams Come True]]<br>[[Cinderella III: A Twist in Time]]<br>[[Mickey's Magical Christmas: Snowed in at the House of Mouse]]''<br>[[Cinderella (2015 film)|''Cinderella'' (2015)]]<br>''[[Ralph Breaks the Internet]]''\n|shows = ''[[House of Mouse]]''\n|rides = [[Cinderella's Fairy Tale Hall]]<br>[[Cinderella's Golden Carousel]]\n|voice = Marion Darlington<br>[[Clarence Nash]]\n|home = [[Cinderella's Ch\u00e2teau]] (formerly)<br>[[King's Castle]]\n|fate = Start living in the castle\n|affiliation = Good\n}}\nThe '''Birds''' are\u00a0[[Cinderella (character)|Cinderella]]'s feathered friends in [[Walt Disney Animation Studios|Disney]]'s [[1950]]\u00a0animated film, ''[[Cinderella (1950 film)|Cinderella]]''.\n\n==Physical appearance==\nThe birds have blue feathers with yellow beaks. Both wear brown shoes on their feet. The mother bird wears a green shawl, and the other bird wears a magenta hat and vest.\n\n==Appearances==\n===''[[Cinderella (1950 film)|Cinderella]]''===\nAt the start of the film, these bluebirds first appear\u00a0when waking Cinderella up, bathing her, and getting her dressed for the day. They also help to fix a [[Cinderella's Dress|ball gown]] for Cinderella with help from the [[mice]]. Near the end of the film, when [[Lucifer]] has trapped [[Jaq and Gus|Gus]] and a key to Cinderella's room (stolen from [[Lady Tremaine]] with [[Jaq and Gus|Jaq]]) in a bowl, the birds attack Lucifer by dropping cups and plates on him, but the cat shoos them off. Then Cinderella instructs them to get [[Bruno (Cinderella)|Bruno]].\u00a0They fly down to the shed to wake up the dog. The birds help Bruno into the chateau so\u00a0the dog can chase and frighten Lucifer away.\n\n===''[[Cinderella II: Dreams Come True]]''===\nThe birds help the\u00a0[[Fairy Godmother]] and the mice to create a book of stories for Cinderella. They also have a role in the short \"An Uncommon Romance\", in which, under Cinderella's instructions, they steal [[Anastasia Tremaine|Anastasia]]'s hat and get her to chase them in an attempt to bring her together with the [[Baker]].\n\n===''[[Cinderella III: A Twist in Time]]''===\nOnly two\u00a0of the bluebirds\u00a0appear in the film, where they assist Jaq and Gus on helping Cinderella's quest to\u00a0regain [[Prince Charming]]'s memories of her. They are the ones to lead Prince Charming to Cinderella.\n\n===[[Cinderella (2015 film)|''Cinderella'' (2015)]]===\nAs seen in one of the film's official trailers, the birds make an appearance in the live-action adaptation of the original 1950 classic, reprising their roles as one of Cinderella's animal friends.\n\n===''[[Ralph Breaks the Internet]]''===\nNetizen versions of the birds appear in the film. They appear when Cinderella inquires [[Vanellope von Schweetz]]'s status as a princess when asked if animals could talk to her. At the climax, they help the [[Disney Princess|princesses]] save [[Wreck-It Ralph (character)|Ralph]] by attaching the ropes made from [[Rapunzel]]'s [[Rapunzel's Magic Hair|hair]] to [[Snow White]]'s dress.\n\nIn the film, none of the birds are blue.
