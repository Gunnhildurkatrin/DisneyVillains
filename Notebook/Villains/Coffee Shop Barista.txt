She has appeared in two [http://clubpenguin.wikia.com/wiki/Secret_Missions Missions], and in ''[http://clubpenguin.wikia.com/wiki/Club_Penguin:_Elite_Penguin_Force Club Penguin: Elite Penguin Force]'' for Nintendo DS.\n\nShe is friends with [http://clubpenguin.wikia.com/wiki/Flare Flare], saying that the [http://clubpenguin.wikia.com/wiki/Puffle Puffle] was the only one who could boil water hot enough to make hot chocolate.\n\n==Involvement in Missions==\n\n*[http://clubpenguin.wikia.com/wiki/Secret_Mission Mission 5] - When [http://clubpenguin.wikia.com/wiki/Herbert Herbert] and [http://clubpenguin.wikia.com/wiki/Klutzy Klutzy] attacked the [http://clubpenguin.wikia.com/wiki/Coffee_Shop Coffee Shop], the Barista believed that they were some type of hairy mutant crab.
