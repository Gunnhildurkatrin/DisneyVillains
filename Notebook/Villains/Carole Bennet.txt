"{{Infobox_character\n|image = Carole Bennet.gif\n|films = ''[[The Love Bug]]''<br>[[The Love Bug (1997 film)|''The Love Bug'' (1997)]] (mentioned)\n|actress = Michele Lee\n|alias = Carole\n|personality = Sweet, smart, level-headed, caring, kind, thoughtful, romantic\n|alignment = Good\n|home = [[San Francisco]]\n|partner = [[Jim Douglas]] (ex-husband)\n|friends = [[Tennessee Steinmetz]], [[Herbie]], Tang Wu\n|enemies = [[Peter Thorndyke]]\n|likes = Cars, the [[Thorndyke Special]] (formerly)\n|dislikes = Thorndyke cheating\n|fate = Marries Jim at the end of the film<br>Either dies or divorces Jim}}'''Carole Bennet''' is the tritagonist and [[Jim Douglas]]' first love interest in ''[[The Love Bug]]''.\n\n==Background==\nJim first met Carole at a car dealership and was originally dating her boss, [[Peter Thorndyke]]. [[Herbie]], who was sold to him by her, felt they were made for each other and tried to match them up by taking them to a drive in and to a romantic point.\n\nThrough the beginning of the film Carole was always beside Thorndyke and was one of her pit crew members. However after she saw what he did to Herbie, she quit Throndyke to help fix Herbie.\n\nCarole joined Jim and [[Tennessee Steinmetz]] as one of the co-drivers in the El Dorado and through the course of the film grew romantically involved with Jim and at the end she marries him.\n\nCarole is last driving away with Jim in Herbie on their way to their honeymoon.\n\n==Trivia==\n*[[Yvette Mimieux]] was originally considered the role of Carole.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=4nnuKQXe1RY|title=Herbie history: 1969 - 2003|website=YouTube|accessdate=March 22, 2019}}</ref>\n\n==Gallery==\n{{Gallerylink}}\n\n==References==\n{{Reflist}}\n{{The Love Bug}}\n{{DEFAULTSORT:Bennet, Carole}}\n[[Category:Females]]\n[[Category:Live-action characters]]\n[[Category:Lovers]]\n[[Category:Disney characters]]\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:Adults]]\n[[Category:The Love Bug characters]]\n[[Category:Spouses]]\n[[Category:Tritagonists]]"
