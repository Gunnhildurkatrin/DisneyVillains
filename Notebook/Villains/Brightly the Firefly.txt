"{{Infobox_character\n|name = Brightly the Firefly\n|occupation = Cubby's nightlight, signal beacon||films = ''[[Jake and the Never Land Pirates: Jake Saves Bucky]]''\n|shows = ''[[Jake and the Never Land Pirates]]''\n|voice = [[Dee Bradley Baker]]\n|personality = Humorous,  friendly, brave, playful, somewhat timid, helpful\n|appearance = Small red firefly in an old lantern\n|home =  [[Neverland]] (formerly)<br>[[Pirate Island]] (currently)\n|friends = [[Jake (Pirate)|Jake]], [[Izzy]], [[Cubby]], [[Skully]], [[Peter Pan (character)|Peter Pan]], [[Peter Pan's Shadow]]\n|enemies = [[Captain Hook]], [[Mr. Smee]], [[Shade the Shadow Pirate]]\n|likes = Playing, exploring with Cubby and his mates\n|dislikes = Captain Hook\n|powers = Flashing light|image = Brightly the firefly.JPG|alignment = Good}}'''Brightly''' is a [[Neverland]] firefly from the television series ''[[Jake and the Never Land Pirates]]''. He is voiced by [[Dee Bradley Baker]].\n\n==Appearances==\n===''[[Jake and the Never Land Pirates]]''===\nBrightly first appeared in the episode \"[[The Golden Twilight Treasure!]]\", bumping into Jake and his crew while they were gathering seashells on Seashell Beach when they came across an\u00a0old lantern. Izzy is the one who named him Brightly because when he is happy he shines brightly He also likes being inside the lantern lighting the way for Jake and his crew. Captain Hook once tried to steal him so that he could light his way to a treasure. But Jake and his crew recovered him with the help of Izzy's Pixie Dust.\n\nBrightlys return in the special ''\"[[Jake and the Never Land Pirates: Jake Saves Bucky]]\"'', when\u00a0Jake and his crew use the firefly to light their beacon in the Never Land sky so they can summon [[Peter Pan (character)|Peter Pan]] to help them get [[Bucky (Jake and the Never Land Pirates)|Bucky]] back from Captain Hook.\n\nBrightly reappeared in the episode \"[[The Lighthouse Diamond]]\" to light the way as the sea pups prepare to see the midnight sunflowers with [[Red Jessica]] on the far side of Pirate Island. Brightly helped find the Light House Diamond after Captain Hook attempted to swipe it and lost it the Gemstone Pit.\n\nBrightly reappeared in the episode \"[[The Never Sands of Time]]\". Cubby reminds his mates not to forget Brightly and his firefly friends will perform a light show on [[Pirate Island]]. Later, Brightly and his firefly friends assist Jake and his crew distract the [[Sand Serpent]] allowing the pirates the chance to restore the [[Never Sands of Time]] back to its stand.\n\nBrightly reappeared in the episode \"[[Pirate Fools Day!]]\".\n\n==Appearances==\n*\"[[The Golden Twilight Treasure!]] (first appearance)\n*\"[[Jake and the Never Land Pirates: Jake Saves Bucky]]\"\n*\"[[The Lighthouse Diamond]]\"\n*\"[[The Never Sands of Time]]\"\n*\"[[Pirate Fools Day!]]\"\n\n==Gallery==\n<gallery position=\"center\">\nBright01.jpg\nBright02.jpg\nbright03.jpg\nbright04.jpg\nbright05.jpg\nBright06.jpg\nSmeeHookwith Jake&crew-The Lighthouse Diamond.jpg\nJake&crew withBrightly -The Lighthouse Diamond.jpg\nJake&crew withBrightly -The Golden Twilight Treasure!.jpg\nJake&amp;crew withBrightly -The Golden Twilight Treasure!02.jpg\nJake&crew-The Sands of Time01.jpg\nbright07.png\n</gallery>\n{{Jake and the Neverland Pirates}}\n[[Category:Jake and the Never Land Pirates characters]]\n[[Category:Males]]\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:Insects]]\n[[Category:Characters who fly]]\n[[Category:TV Animation characters]]\n[[Category:Disney Junior characters]]\n[[Category:Pets]]\n[[Category:Silent characters]]\n[[Category:Disney characters]]\n[[Category:Animated characters]]"
