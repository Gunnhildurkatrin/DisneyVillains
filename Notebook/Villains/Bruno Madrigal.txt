He is the estranged \u201cblack sheep\u201d of the Madrigal family, with the gift to see into the future.\n\n==Background==\nBruno is Mirabel\u2019s maternal uncle whose gift is the ability to see the future. His gift caused misery to his hometown and it\u2019s due to this that he is considered the black-sheep of the Madrigal family. Due to unknown reasons, he mysteriously disappeared prior to the events of the film and his name is considered taboo among his family and the townsfolk.\n\n===Physical Appearance===\nBruno is a tall, unkempt looking middle-aged man with overgrown curly back hair, a thin mustache and beard and brown eyes. He wears a green poncho and carries a walking stick around.\n\n===Powers and Abilities===\nBruno has the ability to see the future. \n\n== Trivia ==\n\n* The name Bruno was actually chosen by Lin-Manuel Miranda, in order for him to fit the lyric \"Bruno, no, no\" into one of the songs. \n*Unlike his sisters, he is the only Madrigal in his family generation to not have a family of his own.\n\n== Gallery ==\n<gallery>\nFile:Encanto-PromoPicture-06.jpg\nFile:FunkoPOP-1150-BrunoMadrigal.png|[[Funko]] [[Funko/Disney/Pixar|POP!]] Bruno Madrigal\n</gallery><br />\n==References==\n{{reflist}}\n<references />\n{{Encanto}}\n[[pt-br:Bruno Madrigal]]\n[[Category:Males]]\n[[Category:Siblings]]\n[[Category:Animated characters]]\n[[Category:Disney characters]]\n[[Category:Characters in the Disney animated features canon]]\n[[Category:Encanto characters]]\n[[Category:Upcoming characters]]\n[[Category:Hispanic characters]]\n[[Category:Magic Users]]\n[[Category:Uncles]]\n[[Category:Twins and Triplets]]"
