Now he drops the Smokejumpers at [[Piston Peak National Park|Piston Peak]]\u2014it\u2019s a lot like combat, but nobody\u2019s shootin\u2019 at him. With a payload of 10,000 pounds and a maximum takeoff weight of 74,000 pounds\u2014Cabbie can carry the smokejumpers up to 2,000 miles away.<ref name=\"DIMeetChars\">[http://blogs.disney.com/insider/2014/03/10/meet-the-characters-from-planes-fire-rescue/ Meet the Characters from Planes: Fire & Rescue]</ref>\n\n==Appearances==\n===''[[Planes: Fire & Rescue]]''===\nIn ''Planes: Fire & Rescue'', Cabbie is a\u00a0[[Smokejumpers]]\u00a0member, an organization working to fight against wildfires. He first appears at [[Piston Peak National Park|Piston Peak]]\u00a0with [[Lil' Dipper]],\u00a0[[Windlifter]], and the [[Smokejumpers]]. He is later seen flying\u00a0Pinecone, Blackout, Drip, Dynamite, and Avalanche inside his cargo hold. When they arrive at the site of a wildfire, Cabbie opens his cargo hold and allows the Smokejumpers to jump out of him while commenting that \"he will never understand why they want to jump out of a perfectly good airplane.\" He is later present in the main hangar, where he, [[Dusty Crophopper|Dusty]], Lil' Dipper, Windlifter, and the Smokejumpers watch an episode of ''[[CHoPs]]'' (parody of ''CHiPS''). When the episode is over, Cabbie comments that the show stinks. He later helps Lil' Dipper, Windlifter and the Smokejumpers tackle additional wildfires and is last seen taking part in\u00a0the [[Propwash Junction]] Corn Festival by racing against Dusty.\n\n==Trivia==\n*He sports a design based on a Fairchild C-119 Flying Boxcar, a transport plane commonly used in aerial firefighting<ref name=\"SK Fire & Rescue\">[http://www.stitchkingdom.com/disney-planes-fire-rescue-fun-facts-70822/ 'Planes: Fire & Rescue' Fun Facts]</ref><ref name=Honeywell>[http://aerospace.honeywell.com/blog/fighting-wildfires-with-second-chances/ Fighting Wildfires with Second Chances]</ref>\n\n==Gallery==\n===Screenshots===\n<gallery widths=\"150\" captionalign=\"center\" spacing=\"small\" position=\"center\">\nPlanes-Fire-and Rescue-4.png\nPlanes-Fire-&-Rescue-4.png\nPlanes-Fire-&-Rescue-5.png|Starting engines\nPlanes-Fire-&-Rescue-6.png\nPlanes-Fire-and-Rescue-8.png|Opening cargo doors\nPlanes-Fire-and-Rescue-9.png|\"I will never understand why you gravel crunchers want to jump out of a perfectly good airplane!\"\nSmokejumpers-8.png\nFire&rescue9.png\nPlanes-Fire-and-Rescue-23.png\n</gallery>\n\n===Miscellaneous===\n<gallery position=\"center\" captionalign=\"center\" spacing=\"small\" widths=\"150\">\nCabbie_Render_2.png\nCabbie_Render.png\nCabbie Planes Fire and Rescue.jpg\nCabbie toys 3.jpg\nCabbie toys 2.jpg\nCabbie toys 1.jpg\nCabbie and Smokejumpers 1.jpg\n</gallery>\n\n==References==\n{{Reflist}}\n{{Planes}}\n[[fi:Rami]]\n[[pt-br:Cabbie]]\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:Disney characters]]\n[[Category:Planes characters]]\n[[Category:Vehicles]]\n[[Category:Aircrafts]]\n[[Category:Males]]\n[[Category:Animated characters]]\n[[Category:Characters who fly]]\n[[Category:Anthropomorphic characters]]"
