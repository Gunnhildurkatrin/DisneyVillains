He is an imaginative young boy. He has a few pets: a goldfish named [[Dennis (Stanley)|Dennis]], a dog named [[Harry (Stanley)|Harry]], and a cat named [[Elsie]]. He has an older brother named [[Lionel Griff|Lionel]], who enjoys playing his electric guitar, a [[Mark Griff|father]], who stays at home and draws cartoons, and a [[Joyce Griff|mother]], who is a dentist.\n\n\n\n\n==Background==\n===Powers and Abilities===\nWhenever Stanley enters his book, he transforms into any animal he can change into with amazing skill and ability. Even though he can speak to animals, the animals rarely talkback, the general exception being his pets Harry, Elsie, and Dennis, with whom he converses freely.\n\n==Disney Parks==\nStanley appeared as a meetable character during the time of the show's release but is considered retired.\n\nStanley appeared in the stage show ''[[Playhouse Disney: Live on Stage]]'', but was removed in [[2008]] during the re-imagining of the show.\n\n==Trivia==\n*It is shown in the episode \"Look Who's Helping\" that Stanley has a cousin named [[Max (Stanley)|Max]], who owns a Golden Retriever named [[Molly (dog)|Molly]], but it is unknown which of Max's parents is related to which of Stanley's.\n*It is also shown in the movie ''[[Stanley's Dinosaur Round-Up]]'' that he has a great-uncle named Stew, who owns a dude ranch.\n*In the pilot, Stanley had 4 fingers on each hand and eyebrows.\n*Just like [[Doug Funnie]] (the star of another [[Jim Jinkins]] [[Doug|production]]), he has a few strands of hair on his head.\n*Stanley is one of fewer male characters in the series to be voiced by a female, counting a friend of his named [[Lester]].\n\n==Gallery==\n<gallery spacing=\"small\" captionalign=\"center\">\nStanley 1.jpg\nStanley Griff Chameleon Form.PNG|Stanley's chameleon form\nIMG_7696.JPG|Stanley and Dennis with a North American Bison herd\nStanley.JPG|Stanley at one of the Disney Parks.\n</gallery>\n\n{{CharacterStub}}\n{{Stanley}}\n[[Category:Kids]]\n[[Category:Playhouse Disney characters]]\n[[Category:Heroes]]\n[[Category:Males]]\n[[Category:Singing characters]]\n[[Category:Siblings]]\n[[Category:Magic Users]]\n[[Category:Transformed characters]]\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:Animated characters]]\n[[Category:TV Animation characters]]\n[[Category:Disney Junior characters]]\n[[Category:Stanley Characters]]\n[[Category:Disney characters]]\n[[Category:Characters in Disney parks]]"
