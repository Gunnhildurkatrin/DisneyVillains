She is a young girl, who is friends with an animated cat [[Julius the Cat|Julius]]. She was played by four actresses over time the first being [[Virginia Davis]], then [[Margie Gay]], next [[Anne Shirley]], and finally, [[Lois Hardwick]].\n\n==History==\nAlice is the main protagonist of the ''[[Alice Comedies]]'' series. She first appeared in \"[[Alice's Wonderland]]\" as the daughter of a animator. She was played by [[Virginia Davis]] for a long time, her last film as the character being in \"[[Alice in the Jungle]]\". [[Margie Gay]] would be the next person who played Alice, although [[Anne Shirley]] played Alice in one short. [[Lois Hardwick]] took the role in \"[[Alice's Circus Daze]]\", although most of her films are lost.
