"{{Infobox character\n|image = Dhakksgskcigns.jpg\n|shows = ''[[Doc McStuffins]]''\n|alignment = Good|voice=[[Audrey Wasilewski]]}}'''Penny Possum''' is a stuffed possum from the animated Disney Junior show ''[[Doc McStuffins]]''. She is a loving mother to her babies, Pip, Flip and Trip. They appeared in the episode \"[[Awesome Possums]]\", while [[Dottie McStuffins|Doc]] and her friends were painting a picture for them, and when Penny woke up from her nap, she realized she has lost Pip. Doc, [[Lambie]], [[Stuffy]] and [[Chilly]] go downstairs to find him, bumping into [[Donny McStuffins|Donny]] and [[Dr. McStuffins]] along the way. In the living room, Stuffy roars to call Pip, and Doc finds him in the floor lamp and the gang bring him back to Penny. Doc finds that one of the patches to hold Pip on Penny's back has fallen off and tells her how she wandered off from her mom at the shoe store.
