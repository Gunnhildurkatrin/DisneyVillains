Slick<br>[[Leech (Goof Troop)|Leech]]<br>[[Pete]] (to a degree)\n|likes = Honesty\n|dislikes = Criminals<br>His car being hijacked<br>Practical jokes }}\n'''The Chief of Police''' is the police chief of [[Spoonerville|Spoonerville's]] police department from [[Disney Television Animation|Disney's]] 1992-1993 animated series, ''[[Goof Troop]].''\n==Background==\nHe appears in only a few episodes, arresting criminals, or in such minor roles as being in television commercials. He is considered one of Spoonerville's top celebrities.\n\n===Personality===\nThe Chief of Police is a grave man who does not have any patience for nonsense. He takes everything very seriously and does not appreciate others wasting his time.\n\n===Physical appearance===\nThe Chief of Police is a heavy-set anthropomorphic dog with black fur. He wears a light brown coat, a white collared shirt, a black tie, blue jeans with rolled-up cuffs, brown shoes, and a fedora over his eyes.\n\n===Role in the series===\nThe Chief of Police appears in the episode \"[[Counterfeit Goof]]\" where he arrests the criminals Mr. Braxton and S. Slick for making counterfeit money. He also appears in the episode \"[[In Goof We Trust]]\" where he supervises [[Pete|Pete's]] used car business for honest practices. He then appears in \"[[For Pete's Sake (Goof Troop)|For Pete's Sake]]\", telling Pete about a murdered man. He later appears in the episode \"[[Maximum Insecurity]]\", arresting [[Leech (Goof Troop)|Leech]] for framing [[Max Goof|Max]] and [[P.J.]]. His final appearance is a minor role in the episode \"[[Buddy Building]]\" where [[Harold Hatchback]] convinced him to appear in one of his TV commercials.\n\n==Trivia==\n*Unlike wearing a uniform as do most police officers, the Chief of Police wears attire akin to that of a police ''detective'', including a coat and a fedora.\n*His voice sounds different in \"Buddy Building\".\n\n{{Goof Troop}}\n[[Category:Goof Troop characters]]\n[[Category:Males]]\n[[Category:Adults]] \n[[Category:Police officers]]\n[[Category:Disney characters]]\n[[Category:The Disney Afternoon characters]]\n[[Category:Dogs]]\n[[Category:Heroes]]\n[[Category:Animated characters]]\n[[Category:American characters]]\n[[Category:TV Animation characters]]\n[[Category:Mickey Mouse universe characters]]"
