"\n{{Stub}}\n\n{{Infobox character\n|image = [[File:Cupcake (Puppy Dog Pals).png|thumb]]\n|name = Cupcake\n|shows = ''[[Puppy Dog Pals]]''\n|voice = [[Jill Talley]]\n|personality = Snooty, rude, selfish, impatient\n|appearance = Small, lavender and white fur, pigtails\n|alignment = Bad, later neutral\n|friends = Rufus\n|enemies = Bingo, Rolly, Hissy\n|likes = Playing fetch\n|dislikes = Bingo and Rolly\u2019s antics}} \n\nCupcake is a recurring character in ''[[Puppy Dog Pals]]'' and a minor antagonist as well. She is often found at the dog park with her henchman Rufus.\n\n== Appearance ==\nShe is a lavender/white Maltese dog, with cupcake shaped hair ties that hold her hair in pigtails. She has a very small tail and a golden pup tag (also shaped like a cupcake.)\n\n== Personality ==\nShe is rather snooty and also gets annoyed by the pugs' mischievous antics. She shares similarities with [[Hissy]], though she has much less tolerance for [[Bingo_(Puppy_Dog_Pals)|Bingo]] and [[Rolly_(Puppy_Dog_Pals)|Rolly]]. She\u2019s also a terrible singer.
