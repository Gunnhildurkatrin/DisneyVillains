He only makes two short appearances in Season 2 in the episode \"[[Bite Father, Bite Son]]\".\n\n==History==\nHe is [[Brad Morton]]'s father and an officer in the police force.\n\n==Physical Appearance==\nFrom what is seen in the series, Mr. Morton has a strong body and broad shoulders. His hair is blond, just like Brad's, and he sports a short moustache under his nose. He is only seen on the job, so it is unknown what he usually wears aside from his blue police uniform. He is also never seen without his pair of sunglasses.\n\n==Personality==\nFor some reason, he refers to his police car as being female. Mr. Morton is a proud and cocky police officer that would do anything to stop criminals. He is very close to his son Brad and even lets him blare the sirens of his police car whenever he wants. Mr. Morton appears kind of laid-back since he is never seen getting into a fuss. He also appears to be very fond of his job.\n==Episode Appearance==\n*''[[Bite Father, Bite Son]]'' (first appearance)\n==Quotes==\n(about his police car regarding the sirens) \"Make her sing, son.\"\n\n==Trivia==\n*When on the job, Officer Morton has the habit of eating doughnuts and sip coffee;\n*Officer Morton sometimes relies on anonymous tips to catch criminals on the road;\n*Officer Morton wears sunglasses even at night\n*Like his son, he's arrogant and cocky, and he was just like him in his childhood.\n\n==Gallery==\n<gallery spacing=\"small\" position=\"center\" widths=\"185\">\nMorton Police Car.png\nBite Father Bite Son.jpg\nBite Father Bite Son (14).jpg\nBite Father Bite Son (15).jpg\nBite Father Bite Son (100).jpg\nBite Father Bite Son (101).jpg\nBite Father Bite Son (102).jpg\n</gallery>\n\n==References==\n<references/>\n{{DEFAULTSORT:Morton}}{{Template:American Dragon: Jake Long}}\n[[Category:American Dragon: Jake Long characters]]\n[[Category:Disney characters]]\n[[Category:Males]]\n[[Category:Police officers]]\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:Parents]]\n[[Category:Adults]]\n[[Category:American characters]]\n[[Category:Neutral characters]]\n[[Category:Anti-heroes]]\n[[Category:Spouses]]\n[[Category:Heroes]]\n[[Category:Animated characters]]\n[[Category:TV Animation characters]]\n[[Category:Disney Channel characters]]"
