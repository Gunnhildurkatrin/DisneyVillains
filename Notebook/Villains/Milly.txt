She is one of the [[Fireside Girls]] in [[Danville]] and is a member of Troop 46231. She is voiced by [[Isabella Murad]].\n==Biography==\nShe, [[Isabella Garcia-Shapiro|Isabella]], [[Gretchen]], and [[Adyson Sweetwater]] were the background singers for Phineas and the Ferb-Tones as the Ferbettes (\u201c[[Flop Starz]]\u201d, \u201c[[Phineas and Ferb Musical Cliptastic Countdown]]\u201d). She promoted the Brawl at the Mall by passing out fliers (\u201c[[Raging Bully]]\u201d). She, along with [[Ginger Hirano]], [[Holly]], and [[Katie]], help [[Phineas Flynn|Phineas]] and [[Ferb Fletcher|Ferb]] create S\u2019winter (\u201c[[S'Winter]]\u201d). Another time, she and [[Adyson Sweetwater]] were trying to explain the T.V. concept to [[Isabella Garcia-Shapiro|Isabella\u2019s]] pet dog, [[Pinky]] (\u201c[[Interview With a Platypus]]\u201d). \u00a0 \u00a0\u00a0\n\n==Appearance==\nMilly has curly brown hair, blue eyes with a twitch, freckles, and has a yellow bow (not always completely visible). Her uniform has a red collar, red circular pattern on the sleeves and the bottom, and black Mary Jane shoes, but when it's winter, she wears an orange sweater with a bell, leggings under her skirt, orange boots with white trim and a red Santa hat.
