"{{Infobox_character|alias=|affiliations=The Adventurers Club|home=9910 Primrose Path, Sandusky, [[Ohio]]|family=Emil Bleehall, Sr. (father)|inspiration=Robert Cox|rides=[[Adventurers Club]]<br>\n[[Jungle Cruise]]<br>\n[[Trader Sam's]]|occupation=Junior Adventurer (formerly)|image=Emil Bleehall.webp|fullname=Emil Bleehall, Jr.|friends=[[Pamelia Perkins]]<br>\n[[Trader Sam]]}}\n\n'''Emil Bleehall, Jr.''' is a character from [[Pleasure Island (Downtown Disney)|Pleasure Island]].\n\n== History ==\n\n=== Background ===\nEmil Bleehall, Jr. was the son of Emil Bleehall, Sr. in Sandusky, [[Ohio]] and lived in the early 20th century.  Emil became a member of Tribe 602 in the, \"Junior Adventurer\" with dreams of joining the Adventurers Club, a group of big-name globetrotting explorers.  In [[1937]], Emil travelled to the group's base on Pleasure Island, [[Lake Buena Vista]], [[Florida]] for an open-house on [[December 31|New Year's Eve]].  Via competing in the annual Balderdash Cup, Emil won over the club and their crowd and became an official member of the Adventurers Club.\n\n=== Development history ===\nEmil Bleehall's character was inspired by the Adventurers Club's head writer, show producer and show Director, Roger Cox.  Notable elements taken from Cox is that he is an awkward, modest and crowd-pleasing little guy rom Ohio who wins over the big-name group which he joins.\n\n== [[Disney Parks]] ==\n\n=== [[Disneyland]] ===\n\n==== [[Trader Sam's|Trader Sam's Enchanted Tiki Bar]] ====\nIn this bar, Emil is referenced in a letter sent from [[Pamelia Perkins]] to Trader Sam.  It mentions Emil as having left his canteen in the possession of Trader Sam with hopes that it would be refilled upon his return from an adventure with the club.  During the holiday seasons, Emil has a Christmas stocking hung up in the bar.\n\n=== [[Walt Disney World Resort|Walt Disney World]] ===\n\n==== [[Adventurers Club]] ====\nEmil was the effective protagonist of the Adventurers Club in Downtown Disney's Pleasure Island.  The story of the attraction had him winning the Balderdash Cup and becoming an official member of the Adventurers Club.\n\n==== [[Jungle Cruise]] ====\nA removed detail on the [[Magic Kingdom]]'s Jungle Cruise had a luggage tag belonging to Emil in the possession of the [[Jungle Navigation Company]].\n\n<br />\n\n== Trivia ==\n\n* Animatronic [[Donald Duck|Donald]] plays the role of Emil in the ''[[Epic Mickey]]: Tales of the Wasteland'' story \"The Rubbish Cup\"\n\n== Gallery ==\n<gallery>\n\nFile:Adventurers Club Cast.jpg\n\n</gallery><br />{{Society of Explorers and Adventurers}}{{Jungle Cruise}}\n[[Category:Theme park characters]]\n[[Category:Jungle Cruise characters]]\n[[Category:Males]]\n[[Category:Pleasure Island]]\n[[Category:S.E.A. characters]]\n[[Category:Disney characters]]"
